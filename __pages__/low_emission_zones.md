> Description of the solution

Milan's Low Emission Zones, "Area C" and "Area B," are initiatives to reduce vehicular emissions and promote sustainable urban mobility. These zones regulate vehicle entry based on emissions, with stricter controls over time aiming for a fossil-free city center by 2030.

> Socio-economic effects

Enhances air quality, promotes healthier lifestyles, reduces traffic congestion, and incentivizes cleaner transportation methods.
