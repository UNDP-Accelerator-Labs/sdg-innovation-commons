## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights) 

In conflict, women return to rebuild.

Healing and rebuilding social cohesion begins with programming that targets women beyond the humanitarian and life saving interventions that sister agencies provide.
