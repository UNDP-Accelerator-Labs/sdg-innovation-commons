> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem &amp; solution description)

It solves the problem of wastage of energy as it saves up to 80% of your fuel! With the clean cooking basket, you first pre-cook the food for a few minutes, then finish the cooking from the basket.  With it, you will never burn rice or beans again! Spend less time cooking!
