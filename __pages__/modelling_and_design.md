> Brief description of the innovation

Many local companies import items which can even be produced locally e.g Chutes,pans,screens cylinders  e.t.c.We provide a service to the companies and sole traders to formulate solutions
by inventing, 3D modelling and 
designing of Manufacturing Plants, products, 
software, systems, models 
to improve their lifestyle 
and operations. With this designs a local company can manufacture products instead of always importing, this will allow a local company to employ more local personnel
