## Solution holder: Mefleh Al-Mahasneh

Beit Al-Reef Jerash Eco Camp beckons you to discover its hidden charm, where nature, culture, and community intertwine to create a truly memorable journey. Immerse yourself in lush forests, hike scenic trails, and enjoy captivating performances by the "Al-Waradat" folkloric band. Delight in authentic Jordanian cuisine and get ready to embrace the extraordinary where every moment is filled with wonder and adventure. Experience the harmony of nature, culture, and community at the camp.
