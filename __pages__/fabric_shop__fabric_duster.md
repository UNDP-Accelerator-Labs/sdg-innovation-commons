> TITLE: Please provide a title for the solution you observed.

Fabric Duster

> DESCRIPTION: What did you observe, or what did people write or say?

Entering the fabric shop, I noticed an object placed in the door handle. I asked the vendor what it was. He explained that it was a fabricated duster which he made out of the excess fabrics attached to a piece of wood. It was also interesting how this hack fit perfectly in the hole of the door handle which could be considered as a second hack; the fabric duster holder.

> NEED: What need does this answer?

Cleaning and maintenance: clearing the dust from the fabrics, especially the ones situated outside.

> INSIGHTS: What lessons can be learned from this observation?

The importance and benefits of upcycling. With simple solutions, one can not only cut costs but more importantly avoid waste.
