> Title of the innovation

Kapenta wholesale

> Brief description of the innovation

Ordering of Siavonga Kapenta and fish and sell to neighbouring boarder countries  like Congo and some other part of Zambia at an affordable price. Whereas retailing at an affordable price in order to improve our diet in Zambia and promote good health among zambians

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Most people would like to sell Siavonga Kapenta but they can't manage to buy  from the source, hence the people who buy direct from there inflate the prices such that the profit made is minimal. Introduction of wholesalers in Kitwe can be a very viable business. Pre packaging can also increase the profit and help those who can not manage to order in bulky

> Technical Feasibility

The business is very viable as most families in Zambia are extended families hence the availability of kapenta as relish provide so much relief. Kapenta being food is on daily demand

> Anticipated Social Impact

Most of the young people can be buying at wholesale from other provinces other than southern province at an affordable price and resale in their respective communities, hence making income to improve their lives and make the Kapenta affordable for most families. This can help improve health among zambians as  Kapenta is a natural food
