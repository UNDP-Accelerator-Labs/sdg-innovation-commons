> Brief description of the innovation

Mireyly Natures Soap is a company which was founded in 2020. We are manufacturers of natural skincare products. In recent years, there has been a growing concern regarding the side effects of chemicals on the skin, causing skin irritation, allergies, and skin dullness and this has been one of the key factors fuelling the market growth of natural skincare products. Up to 50 percent of women seek out all-natural or organic ingredients in facial skin-care products and buy those that are free of chemicals like phthalates and sulfates, according to a 2017 survey from the NPD Group. Dissemination of knowledge related to the benefits of natural skincare has led consumers seeking eco-friendly, sustainable products that come with greater product ingredient transparency. The global natural skin care products market size was valued at USD 10.84 billion in 2019 and is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.0% from 2020 to 2027. Mireyly Natures Soaps makes a wide range of 100% natural skin products i.e., liquid and bar soaps, body creams and lotions.
