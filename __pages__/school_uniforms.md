> Title of the innovation

School uniforms

> Brief description of the innovation

It's a school uniform, we also design other clothes for women, children and men.
We make these uniforms out of suiting materials and poplin or polyester for both male and female pupils.
We design clothes for women and men out of the Chitenge materials and Satin.
Something we do tie and dye for both women and men suits.
We also make traditional clothes when there is Kuomboka ceremony like Misisi, Liziba and Transitional caps called Lishushu.
Lastly we design Working suits for builders and other general workers.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Sewing can be seen as a low income job but it is a high income business especially when you buy materials and design clothes and school uniforms.
School uniforms have a continuous demand when schools open each term especially when pupils shift from one grade to another.
PPE the is very important in constructions companies as a means of safety. there fore the work suits that we design help a lot of people to work protected and dressed as the company requires.

> Technical Feasibility

We cut materials wirh a scissors and sew them with the sewing machine according to the standards and the type of a cloth and according to the style or fashion.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It can provide job to a number of Youths who studded sewing at trades and also to salesmen.
