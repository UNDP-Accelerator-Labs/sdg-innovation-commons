> Brief description of the innovation

Siwila corp is a company aimed at different aspects of human development helping bring abt Employment through farming various crops some of which are: Soya beans, Maize, Garlic, Cabbage and Sunflower as it's major products.
Siwila corp will also aim to provide user  products suchs as phones, laptops and many other electronics that maybe needed for national development.
Lastly Siwila corp will have real estate business in order to satisfy the accomodation demand among people.

The company is build around a system that doesn't not require an individual for it to function but requires everyone to work as one. Siwila corp's main priority Will be Farming operation this will open doors for many things. Because it is intended that everything done by the company Will be dependent on one another, meaning each product or service requires another service or product to be available.
