> Description

Leafplus is a socio-economic enterprise that aims to have an impactful contribution on environment and society by producing and promoting areca leaf products manufactured with direct engagement of womenn, marginalized communities and farmer groups in the production chain. The products are made from fallen leaves -100% natural through eco friendly process (no chemicals or additives used) and is biodegradable and compostable. `
