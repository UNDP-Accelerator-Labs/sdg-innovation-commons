> Brief description of the innovation

The Innovation is about agriculture, both animal and crop. Rearing of chicken (poultry),;fish farming and animal husbandry.
The , innovation would be made possible by the provision of a water source such as a borehole. The agriculture innovation project is dependent on water, as crop farming will run through out the year. The orchard, banana plantation will be better off with plenty of water. The innovation, as a project running more than one activity, will need the installation of energy, this will power the activities through out the day and night. A variety of equipment and tools also need to be provided for the smooth running of the farm activities. Human resources, and skilled is also vital for the success of the innovation. In addition, regular supervision is the most necessary as it calls for commitment and accurate results of the products.
