> Description of the solution

The C40 Cities Finance Facility  supports cities in developing and emerging economies to prepare and implement climate mitigation and adaptation projects. It provides technical and financial assistance to help cities develop sustainable infrastructure projects, such as low-carbon public transport systems and renewable energy installations, contributing to significant reductions in greenhouse gas emissions and enhancing urban resilience.

> Socio-economic effects

The CFF promotes sustainable economic growth, reduces urban poverty, enhances public health through improved air quality, and creates job opportunities in green sectors.
