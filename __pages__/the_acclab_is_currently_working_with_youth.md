A virtual marketplace for local producers: The AccLab is currently working with youth innovators (Technomath) in the development of an e-commerce platform meant to link producers, street vendors/retailers and customers. The platform is available free of charge, for both producers and buyers. The application is currently in testing stage, and this will be following by the deployment of the application for piloting
