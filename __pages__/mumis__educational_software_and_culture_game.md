> Title of the innovation

Mumis, Educational Software and Culture game

> Brief description of the innovation

a)	Mumis-  Mumis  is a computer software program that makes the job easier when it comes to identify Gram negative bacteria(Enteriobactericea). The identification of enteriobactericea microbes using the manual methods makes the job harder and you might also miss out other potential probable microorganisms.
Mumis helps with the quick and fast identification of enteriobactericea compared to the manual methods therefore contributing to early release of release of patient results and also attend to more patients samples within the quickest possible Time.


b)	Educational Software- We have come up with different types of educational software from Preschool to grade 12 especially now that there is covid 19 learners can either download The software on their smart phone or have access of the educational software on their Computer through CD. The names of the educational are follows Catherine Phiri Punchout Balloons to Learn(for Preschool), ItestpaperMCQsocialstudeis(Grade5-12), Ipracticalbiology(grade-grade12), ItestpaperMCQbiology(Grade10-12) and  Car ride to learn(For Preschool).

C) Koumboka and Ncwala game - Due to Covid 19 many traditional ceremony have been  canceled which is one of the Tourism attraction in the country in view of this Challenge we have developed Kuomboka and Ukusefya pa Ngwena mobile and Computer game
