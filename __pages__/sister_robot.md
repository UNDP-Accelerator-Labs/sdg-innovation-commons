> Organization

Sister Robot

> Description

Frontline health workers are at a high risk in the fight against COVID-19 due to direct exposure to patients. In order to motivate and boost the confidence of health workers, Robotics Association of Nepal (RAN) desinged and deployed a remotely-operable robot that can perform non-clinical tasks such as delivering medicines and food to COVID patients in isolation wards. The robot if made of wood and can carry 30-35 kgs of materials at a time and operate within an approximate range of 300 meters. The cost of manufacturing is robot is less than US $ 2000.
