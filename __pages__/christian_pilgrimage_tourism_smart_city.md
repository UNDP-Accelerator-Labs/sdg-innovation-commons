> Title of the innovation

Christian Pilgrimage Tourism Smart City

> Brief description of the innovation

The Project aims at providing spiritual, moral, peace, mind transformation and counseling to people from unstable nations around the world. The cities will have recreational facilities, accommodation facilities, training facilities, shopping centers and spiritual interaction sites.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The cities will collect 10% taxation from all business activities. the city will fund innovations from the citizens who cannot fund their enterprises and own 51% share holding to ensure sustainability of start-ups. the city administration will appoint credible and experienced mangers to manage the enterprises while training apprentices.

> Technical Feasibility

1. Acquire the Land
2. Legalize the Land
3. Develop City Plans while consulting Environmental Management Authority
4. Construct the Solar and Wind Power Plants at the Mountain tops
5. Develop the city in Phases starting with service industry infrastructure.

> Anticipated Social Impact

1. Reduce the religious radicals
2. increase peace loving people through relevant training and counseling
3. Reduce poverty level among the orphans and windows
4. increase economic activities and entrepreneurship skills
