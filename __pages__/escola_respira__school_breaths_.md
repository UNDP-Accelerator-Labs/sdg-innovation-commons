> Description of the solution

Escola Respira (School Breathes) is a municipal programme of initiatives for improving air quality in school environments such as awareness-raising actions among the entire school community. It revolves around four core areas: the urban space, classrooms, recreational spaces, and cross-cutting and educational aspects. The programme includes 2 lines of action for actively working with schools ‚Äì air quality eco-audits and awareness raising ‚Äì and 4 more lines of action, including promotion of green walls, increasing safety, sustainable mobility and urban model initiatives.
