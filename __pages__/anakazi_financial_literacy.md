> Title of the innovation

Anakazi Financial Literacy

> Brief description of the innovation

Educating women about financial literacy. Most women know how to make money but have no idea how to save and invest. The idea is to teach a woman to guide them into investing into projects that will change their future and lives. The emphasis is teaching them the importance of investing into education for whoever they are responsible for i.e children and dependents.  And for those women who do not know how to make money, we teach them how to make money then advise on what investments to take on.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation is firstly providing cheap finance from K100 to K2500 at 30% interest paid daily. For instance  K1000 can be paid off at K44 daily for 30 days. Provide finance for the informal sector. The target market is anyone who can sell vegetable to shop owners. Teaching women how to access good finance, save and invest will enable them to buy plts, build houses, take children to school and in turn improve their livelihood

> Technical Feasibility

Find customers (educated and uneducated women)

Fund customers.
Monitor their progress

> Anticipated Social Impact

By teaching a woman, you teach a nation. A family will have improved livelihood which will improve communities
