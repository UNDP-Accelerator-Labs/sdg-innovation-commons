> Title of the innovation

Village Hope

> Brief description of the innovation

It’s a program targeting to help rural youths in the North Western and Western provinces to acquire skills that will help them to gainful employment or start their own businesses.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Upon graduation, students will have the skills required to start their own businesses.

> Technical Feasibility

We are building a trades school in Kasempa where hands on  training  in small business entrepreneurship for North westerners and westerners will be taught  to train youths who can’t afford to pay to go to college  in computer skills, knitting, carpentry, welding ,and farming . These are skills needed for youths to be gainfully employed or start their own businesses.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The innovation will help to alleviate and break the cycle of poverty. It will provide youths with the much needed skills that will make them to be marketable.
