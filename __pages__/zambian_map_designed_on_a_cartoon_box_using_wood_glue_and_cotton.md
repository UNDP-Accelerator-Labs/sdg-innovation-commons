> Title of the innovation

Zambian map designed on a cartoon box using wood glue and cotton

> Brief description of the innovation

This is an art work done by collection of different materials in order to come up with our Zambian map.
You have to look for a cartoon box then buy cotton in different colors for easy identification of your cotton color just use the colors on a Zambian flag.
Thereafter, buy wood glue then make a Sketch map of Zambia on a cartoon box then start applying wood glue with your cotton.
Then the design of your color outcome will depend on you the designer.
That is how I came up with this design.
