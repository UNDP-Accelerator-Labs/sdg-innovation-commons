  Problem that the solution is trying to solve; The need for street cleaners hired by municipalities to use a cart that contains amenities for the worker’s comfort, but without the need for costly means of energy. Solution: Designing a cart that contains solar cells that feed the fan that can reduce the heat and without the need for a source of energy consumption.  Stage of development: The prototype   was designed
