> Brief description of the innovation

We recycled plastic wastes to create backpacks,travelling bags,handbags etc that are waterproof,durable,eco-friendly and sustainable.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Will generate income from each sale of the bag made. Our Target market include women fo handbags and school backpacks for students or pupils from low income communities. Our innovation protects the environment such that it makes the use of plastic wastes collected from the environment into something sustainable. Whenever one buys a bag they are helping in the fight against plastic pollution.

> Technical Feasibility

Our innovation involves the use of already available materials such as plastics and we use a technique that allows us to fuse different plastics together using heat to come up with a leather like material that is ideal for our durable bags.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Our innovation has the capacity to create employment for most local women and youths out there. We are trying to give customers another option for bags which is made from recycled plastic wastes.
