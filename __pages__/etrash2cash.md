> Description of the solution

The project incentivizes recycling by buying waste from the public, focusing on transforming it into recyclable products and organic compost. This initiative not only aims to reduce environmental pollution but also offers financial benefits to low-income earners, promoting sustainable waste management practices.

> Socio-economic effects

Reduces waste and provides income opportunities for low-income communities
