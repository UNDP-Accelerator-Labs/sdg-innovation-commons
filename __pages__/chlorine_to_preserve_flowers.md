> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

Preserving the flower stem by applying chlorine to the water, for the flower vendors to sustain their business and avoid profit loss.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

The need to be able to extend the storage or shelf life of flowers.

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

For entrepreneurs selling flowers.

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

The observation is effective and at the same time, the water will not become mosquito hatch because of the pressence of chlorine. Chlorine is economical. 

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

Extend the storage life span of flowers in the flowershop.
