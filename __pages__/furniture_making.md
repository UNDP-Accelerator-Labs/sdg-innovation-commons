> Brief description of the innovation

Sofa making is a rewarding business but it requires capital ,commitment ,faithfulness skill and endurance.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

It can reduce lazynes and it helps me take my children to School. It also act as employment to myself and other guys who assist me in manufacturing. My target is to start competing with the the Chinese companies that make sofas bring to Zambia. My few customers that I have are the local people in my locality (Chambishi). I am thankful to them.

> Technical Feasibility

I wish to have a full set of carpentry tool and enough capital so that I can employ permanent guys to assist me do the job.

> Anticipated Social Impact

People will learn a skill from it and start there own carpentry work shop.
