Retaining walls help to enhance overall property value, they are durable and low maintenance, and extremely functional. Stone walls are eco-friendly and optimal because they use minimal space and prevent soil erosion during natural disasters such as flooding. Not only is it used to protect or enhance the value of a property but the most common types of stone walls are used in interior design such as limestone and sandstone.
