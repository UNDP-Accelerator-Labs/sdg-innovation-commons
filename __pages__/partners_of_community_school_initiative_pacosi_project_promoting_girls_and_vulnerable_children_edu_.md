> Title of the innovation

Partners of Community School Initiative-PACOSI Project-promoting girls and vulnerable children Education and Health through skills training and empowerment initiatives.

> Brief description of the innovation

community schools were created out of the need for continuous education both in rural areas.our project,the pacosi project seeks to reduce the rates of school drop outs especially of the girl child through skills training of guardians e.g. tailoring,bricklaying,poultry etc so as to stimulate a steady source of income to enable them buy books and other necessities. In the area of Health, we seek to enforce community interventions through counselling in order to avoid contangious diseases,sexually transmited infections etc because a health populace is  a productive population.Our goal is to promote awareness of the need for entrepreneurship skills as the cornerstone for skills transfer and self reliance hence promoting Health and Education in the communities.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Do to the high number of unemployed Trained Teachers in the country,we hope to build community schools,3 to 4 per selected districts.These will generate income as learners will be expected to pay at least a k50/month multiplied by at least 5000 pupils by 3 months gives about k750 000 per term in some selected areas.We also hope to purchase about 500 sewing machines with the view to generate over k3m per month in some selected districts.Also not over looking campentry and bricklaying which can generate about k5m in some selected areas.These funds will be revolving so as to empower as many groups as possible.

> Technical Feasibility

The design of innovation is based on enhanced survival skills among the vulnerable people. To that effect, the innovation is composed a 10 member committee with variant skills capable of imparting survival skills in different disciplines. We aim to implement the plan in about 5 years time ignored to meet all the developmental goals as outlined in the plan. Other than committee members, we hope to recruit other staff members for outreach areas. These will include community Health workers,Teachers,Secretaries and other personnel.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Reduced school drop outs, especially among the girl child.Improved school attendance due to School Feeding Programme.Enhanced survival skills for these guardians of vulnerable children.Improved economic activities for the poor.Supplemented government efforts in reducing deforestation in the country due to alternative methods of making money for survival.Imroved Health among the target groups. Reduced Shortages  of school desks in selected areas as these will be supplied by the local people.
