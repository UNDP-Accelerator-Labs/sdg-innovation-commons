> Brief description of the innovation

Dijifuel system is a fuel card based micro loan product offered by Great North Credit (GNC) to Taxi and Mini Bus drivers who require money to buy fuel.  Dijifuel system is a protected trade mark. The product is aimed at promoting the Smart Zambia Policy( by minimising physical cash handling in a Covid-19 environment ) and also to foster the Financial Inclusion Policy by lending to market segments  which are excluded by mainstream Financial Service Providers.
GNC is a Micro Finance Institution (MFI) which was recently issued with an MFI license by Bank of Zambia.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Great North Credit Limited (GNC) offers Dijifuel loans. This loan facility targets Taxi and Bus drivers with amounts ranging from ZMK100 to ZMK800 at a low interest rate of 4% plus other charges. These loans have a maximum tenure of 5 days and customers have a revolving access to the loan immediately after repaying. 

The number of public taxi and mini buses are in the range of 100,000 countrywide and so far we have enrolled over 3000 taxi and bus drivers without counting private fleet owners and private other motorists. Further, the product can be franchised in other countries as well.

The customers are issued with Dijifuel cards at on boarding, after a rigorous credit vetting process on which all disbursements are made. The card is a property of GNC and it is valid until expiry or termination of relationship.

GNC has wallets with Airtel and MTN, which are used as repayment platforms for all the customers, therefore, repayments are very convenient.

GNC has spend over USD 60,000 in building the system and in the procurement of Card and Point of Sale (POS) machines. 

The prize money will be used to; procurement more POS machines, fuel cards, marketing and training, research and development, promoting financial literacy/inclusion and engagements with Visa or Master Cards embedment.

> Technical Feasibility

The Dijifuel system is an environmentally friendly  software with primary server hosting by digital ocean based in USA and the secondary server hosting by amazon located in Germany with auto back up processes.
Our Dijifuel  solution is called All-in-One (AiO) and consists of three modules. These are AiO FCS, which is the Fuel Card System, AiO Digital which is the Mobile and Digital Banking Suite necessary for fuel dealers to open new revenue avenues and AiO Loyalty which is the Loyalty Platform that enables fuel companies build a loyal customer base. 
The payment system is approved by Bank of Zambia (BoZ. It meets stringed 
requirements by BoZ to protect data and integrity of the financial sector.

AiO FCS is an enterprise ready NFC (Near Field Communication) Fuel Card Issuance and Management platform which meets rigorous operational and reporting requirements for any fuel company. It is approved by the Bank of Zambia (Central Bank) as a payment system in line with best practice. It meets the strictest privacy and security requirements as defined by the operator and dictated by ISO 8583 Standard. The system can be integrated to third party systems to provide a truly
omnichannel ecosystem. The AiO card is a prepaid card platform.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Public and private taxi and bus drivers have one common challenge according to our research findings; start up fuel for the day after  cashing all previous dayâ€™s money. Usually  the fuel light is on red.

In addition, private fleet owners and private motorists are faced with fuel challenges from time to time thereby affecting their operations and mobility. In other ways, our target market is faced with a problem of lack of cash to buy fuel for their errands, preclusion from accessing credit from mainstream financial service providers and exposure to the risk of contracting Covid-19 due to frequent physical cash handling.

Fuel is a life blood of any economy. It turns the wheels of the economy. Therefore, our innovation of the All-in-One (AiO) Dijifuel card system is aimed at ensuring that public taxi and mini bus drivers and others have access to fuel money at an affordable interest rate to support the business operations. furthermore, our innovation ensures that our target market is financially included into the financial mainstream, and aslo reduces the risk of contracting Covid-19 by promoting digital transactions.

Our innovation is relevant to the target market in that we are offering affordable micro loan facility through a card based system. We are also promoting financial inclusion. Our target market has been outside the mainstream financial service providers. Our product is an answer to Governments Policy on financial inclusion and Smart Zambia Policy. Our innovation reduces physical cash handling in filling station (Public Fuel Stations) thereby reducing the risk of spreading Covid-19 through frequent physical cash handling. It also reduces cash pilferages at the filling station and financial loss to armed robbery. 

Our research has also shown that most of the taxi and mini bus drivers are not adequately insured. Our innovation provides micro insurance life covers through PARTNERSHIP with a leading insurance company as an added value.
