> Brief description of the innovation

We are into support of winter and irrigation farming,promoting and sensitising communities about Climate change and energy. We promote afforestation, value additional to our crops  on the market. Our aim is to make sure that youths and women are fully participating so as to reduce illegal poaching, prostitution and zero hunger by 2026.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The population  is growing rapidly in our district, therefore ,there is high demand in variety of food. By growing and supply on the market we will be making a lot money for the family, it will be the source of employment creation which will also boost the living standards of others when they paid their wages. There will  be a higher competition at the market which  helps levelling prices as to compared where is monopoly of prices because of low supply  and high demand.
We are embracing afforestation, using of inorganic fertiliser and practice arable agricultural farming method. We introduce a revolving funds amongest women, youths and differently abled persons. This will promote self-reliance.
