A start-up that ensures that the electronic waste generated in your home or
workplace is taken from your home and delivered to licensed electronic waste
collection facilities. By this way, it aims to eliminate the logistical burden
created by WEEE (Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment) for both parties.
