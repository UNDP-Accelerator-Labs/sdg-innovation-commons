PDMx, the active ingredient in surface cleaning products, is a unique blend of plant-derived compounds and has won numerous local and international innovation accolades. The formulation is proven to be non-irritant, non-corrosive, non-toxic, biodegradable and ecofriendly. Most similar products in the market are flawed with health and environmental issues due to their micro-biocide content. Skin dehydration, intoxication, respiratory problems, hormonal imbalance and carcinogenic effects, among others, have been associated with the usage of alcohol, hypochlorite and triclosan, as micro-biocides. PDMx, or the active ingredient is a proprietary blend of plant derived compounds proven to be effective in eliminating 99.99% of germs including the infamous superbug, methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus (MRSA). The products are laboratory proven to be non-irritant and non-toxic with low-volatile content, and is biodegradable in the environment. All in all, the nature-inspired formulations eventually lead to its remarkable safety features.
