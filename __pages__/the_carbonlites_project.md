> Description of the solution

The Bangalore-based startup's initiative, Carbonlites, converts organic waste into biogas, effectively turning garbage into a renewable energy source. This innovative approach not only reduces the volume of waste in landfills but also produces a cleaner alternative to liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) and generates organic fertilizer, addressing waste management and energy challenges simultaneously.

> Socio-economic effects

Provides cleaner energy, reduces landfill waste, supports sustainable urban development, and enhances local air quality.
