> Description of innovation

Blue Sky Analytics are building a Geospatial Data intelligence platform to provide ambient air quality status across India using various government monitors and satellite data. Their vision is to solve the menace of air pollution using data and analytics approach thus transforming the whole process of environmental monitoring in India.
