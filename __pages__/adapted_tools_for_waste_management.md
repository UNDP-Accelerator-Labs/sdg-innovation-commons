Augusto Gomes has developed a collection of waste management tools for making recycling easier the city. Due to the poor infrastructure, and excess waste in the region, the inventor has adapted the items, so they can be used by bikes and for different weather conditions which may cause the waste collection to be more arduous. Working with youth of the region, they take time everything to clean the city of Gabu.
