## Solution holder: Emad Haddad

Welcome to the enchanting Mount Burqesh eco-tourism camp nestled amidst the breathtaking green hills of Ajloun. This sustainable haven is a sanctuary for nature enthusiasts and those seeking an authentic cultural experience.

The accommodation facilities, designed with sustainability in mind, seamlessly merge with the natural surroundings, allowing visitors to reconnect with nature without compromising on comfort.

One of the highlights of Mount Burqesh is undoubtedly the mesmerising view of Ajloun’s verdant hills that unfold before the guests’ eyes. The culinary experience here is a delightful journey into the rich flavours of Jordanian cuisine. Traditional dishes, prepared with locally sourced ingredients, satisfy taste buds and transport guests to a realm of culinary indulgence.

To complement the journey, the camp offers traditionally flavoured tea infused with a medley of aromatic herbs, carefully selected from the surrounding hills. This invigorating beverage invigorates the senses and provides a refreshing respite during leisurely walks through the camp's lush surroundings.
