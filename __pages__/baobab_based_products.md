> Title of the innovation

Baobab based products

> Brief description of the innovation

We are Fidanna Group Limited a Zambian owned startup processing baobab powder and churning it into various different products. We currently have 4 types of products, Baobab powder, Baobab spread which comes in 2 variations; Baobab Dark chocolate (baobab, cocoa, Moringa, honey, lemon juice) and Omega BaobabPlus (baobab, Moringa, ginger, lemon juice, honey) and Baobab freezits.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We have already been able to sell over 2,000 packs of baobab powder and we've sold over 50 jars of our spread which have only been on the market for about a month and we're looking at commercializing. Baobab freezits we sell about 20cases each week of 50packs each.

> Technical Feasibility

We produce healthy, natural and highly nutritious foods. All of which are baobab based bringing value addition to the fruit of life. We have already started selling our line of products and are looking at expanding our product line.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Our products are known to improve immune health, digestive health, blood sugar regulation, maintains safe blood of pressure levela, high in electrolytes for mental health and recovery, high vitamin C and essential minerals. Etc.
