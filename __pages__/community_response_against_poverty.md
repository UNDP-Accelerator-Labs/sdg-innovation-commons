> Title of the innovation

Community Response Against Poverty

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation is to set up an agricultural value addition small community business. This will involve setting up a business to make cooking oil and stock feed using locally grown crops and employing local people.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The business will involve setting up  a cooking oil plant using a small industrial presser and production of stock feed using a hammer mill. As a community based business market will be small traders and community poultry farmers who will a have cheap source of commodities.

> Technical Feasibility

The project will involve renting a disused beer hall by installing a cooking oil presser to produce cooking oil and soya  or sunflower cake. The the cake will be used to make stock feed using locally grown maize.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The innovation will create employment to 12 people who will work in two shifts, produce cheap and quality stock feed to small scale poultry farmers . It will be a model for others to start value addition small scale businesses using locally grown agricultural materials.
