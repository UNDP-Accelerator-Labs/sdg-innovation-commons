> Title of the innovation

Capital hill outdoor adventures

> Brief description of the innovation

Capital hill outdoor adventures is a youth centered camping venue, where kids/youths come to build values, character, and team skills which are required to function In the real world. (It is an outdoor camping experience with a variety of activities for everyone to enjoy)

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The target market for this would be schools, church groups, companies, and families, offering this type of experience to kids from different types of backgrounds giving them a surrounding where they get to challenge themselves and grow their friendships, company team building and giving church groups a place where they can worship in nature and for families a place where they can unwind. Existing businesses will be given a platform where we bring in sales and business professionals that will teach their employees on improving their sales. The challenge prize money will be used to buy equipment like tents and prepare.
The potential profit from a school group with the existing market conditions would be 200 per person from an estimate of 100 students with gives 20 000 per group

> Technical Feasibility

Their is no prototype for this. The idea is to start off with school groups and church groups once the place has been set up, we would have to start off with getting tents enough for 100 people, and offer less activities at first them as things progress use the profits to get more equipment and workers

> Anticipated Social Impact

The youth are the future and giving them a platform where they can unleash the leader in them and an environment that teachers wrong and right will be beneficial, Currently they are no outdoor adventure places that offer this. And it will create employment and beautification for the surrounding areas.
