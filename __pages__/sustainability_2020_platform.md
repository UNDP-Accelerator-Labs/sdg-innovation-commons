> Brief description about the identified local solution:

A social media page, on Facebook and Instagram that publish a lot of awareness posts that related to climate change and its effect on our lives with catchy photos. The Added value of this page is all the posts are written in Iraqi language specially in Mosulian language. Also, the way of writing the posts depends on the human centered methodology that get the reader's emotions while reading.
