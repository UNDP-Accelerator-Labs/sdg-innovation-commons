> Title of the innovation

Nchito

> Brief description of the innovation

Nchito is an online platform that will work at connecting the under utilized skilled human resources of Lusaka to employment opportunities in a target market group of middle to high income households and companies, organizations or corporations on a full time, part time and hourly basis for all forms of work in real time at your doorstep.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Nchito will generate income from a set commission collected as it connects thousands of potential skilled employees to recruiting companies and individual for part time jobs, permanent jobs and even works on an hourly basis through its online platform that works in real time. The challenge prize will go towards setting up this platform and have it accessable to the thousands unemployed yet trained and skilled human resource in an unbiased manner.

> Technical Feasibility

Nchito is designed mostly as an online base tapping into human resources closest to the need of recruitment. Nchito untilizes the ever expanding technological advancements to creat opportunities for employment.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Nchito is an unbiased platform recommending recruitment purely based on merit, qualification and ability. This evens the field for every recruit will unveiling them to the opportunities within their locality.
