The Challenge:

Iraq as a post-conflict country has been suffering from instability for decades which has been hindering the implementation of the national development agenda. The root causes of this situation are linked to economic and social problems among individuals, groups, and institutions. Therefore, the development challenges the solution seeks to address are linked to two main interlinked areas; Economic Development and Social Capital1/ Social Cohesion.
 
Innovation and entrepreneurship and the associated terminologies and concepts are relatively new in Iraq especially when they are linked to social impact, SDGs, and the development agenda. Thus, data and statistics in this regard are limited.



Brief description of INPoint Platform:

INpoint is a virtual environment for entrepreneurs, communities, companies, and institutions that provides an innovative space for youth and entrepreneurs to interact with other stakeholders of the ecosystem (private and public sector) is based on the gamification system. This platform consists of E-learning, profile system, team management, events management, success stories of startups, and data that could be used by the investors and institutions aiming to support and work with youth. 
The Platform was developed by service provide and delivered website platform and mobile application (Android and IOS).
There's Two kind of users in the platform you can register as a normal user or association account, there's a few changes in the association account that permits them to talk more about their company/institution, branches, and present their products.
The Associations account is also capable to request to offer a course to be posted in the E-learning module by their name.



Objective:

The platform aims for:
(1) Establishing a virtual open space for individuals and institutions interested in innovation, entrepreneurship, and SDGs to build their capacity and coordinate to build teams and partnerships for sustainable start-ups and business.
(2) Transforming Collective Patterns of Action and Thinking, resulting in enlarge and enhance the entrepreneurship community and ecosystem.
(3) Changing the mind-set of individuals within institutions of the entrepreneurial ecosystem.
(4) Helping to manage and monitor the activities of the project to measure the impact and the results.

The activities of this project include meaningful engagement of stakeholders (governmental and non-governmental institutions). This engagement aims to enhance the coordination and building partnerships, therefore enhancing the ecosystem including providing policy recommendations to key stakeholder (especially governmental institutions). Moreover, to keep stakeholders informed about the development of this sector using different tools and activities (meeting, reports…etc).

The platform contains a set of Module that was created to achieve the Platform objective and facilitate using the platform by users
 
•	The Profile Module allows the platform users to create their own profiles, access different platform features/modules, and collaborate.
•	The Newsfeed and posting Module allows the platform users to create a regular post, articles, jobs announcement, support, and events. 
•	The Teams Module aims to provide users with collaboration space for project management.  
•	The Project/Business/ Start-up (PBS) Module aims to provide the user with a microsite for their projects, business or start-ups. The purpose is to grant the user with marketing and advertisement capacities.
•	The Events Management module aims to provide the user with an option to create a micro-website for their event.
•	The gamification module is the core added value and the differentiator component of the platform that aims to reward set of desired actions through points and badges (to level up in this virtual gamified environment). 
•	The reporting module is essential to summarize the activities of the platform, therefore the business-related activities in the community. This module will help decision-makers in different public and private institutions to take actions. Moreover, this page and the data generated will inspire individuals, institutions, and investors to be part of the economic development movement.
•	The E-Learning module is composed mainly of two main components: Online courses and Publications that aims to provide users with training and learning materials in addition to online courses.
