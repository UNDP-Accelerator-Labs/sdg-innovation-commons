> Brief description of the innovation

Bongopad is an online music distribution platform that aims as creating a more transparent way to sell (distribute) music created by different artist to their desired clients. This System is aiming at improving sales while monitoring all distribution point as also  a way to reduce piracy.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

As a distribution channel that will carry the reponsiblility of marketing and distribution of musical content, Bongopad will have specific percentage share with its  stakeholders  for been responsible for the process.

> Technical Feasibility

Bongopad is an online distribution system. this is a web based application build with full and proper management system.  the development of this system is at 90% with only about a 10% before it is fully implemented. Hoping to begin the marketing process with we take advantage of the social network  as our starting point

> Anticipated Social Impact

This system will provide a more secure finical flow for the content makers(Musicians). The system will provide an alternative for passive income generation for the artists
