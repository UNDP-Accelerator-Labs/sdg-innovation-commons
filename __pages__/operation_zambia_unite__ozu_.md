> Brief description of the innovation

Encouraging local based solutions to personal, community and national problems. With entreneurship as a driving force, we want to bring out the best in everyone's life. More of turning Hobbies into careers.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Being the pioneers of the innovation WEB TYCOONS (webtycoonsenterpri.wixsite.com/contractor) stand to benefit by offering intermediary services startups and existing businesses. 
Existing businesses stand to benefit from our time management skills where innovation, creativity, efficiency and effectiveness would be the order of the day. Reducing wastages and improving output.
This innovation open to every Zambian who yearns to see things improve for the better.
The prize will help to establish a network of community development clubs national wide. Former school mates associations, to champion the cause.
Since the initial activities of the community development clubs would be farming, recycling and tree planting, the environment stands to benefit a great deal. For more info, view: yodeco.wixsite.com/youthdevelopmentcomp and www.kabsyouth.wixsite.com/kabulongayouths

> Technical Feasibility

This innovation aims to involve everyone who is willing and able to contribute to personal, community and national development.
Everyone is good at something but there's no system for them to make a living from it. People are busy doing courses and jobs they even love just to survive. We want everyone to be able to monetarize their hobbies.
Growing an inclusive local economy and reducing on externalization of resources for the benefit of all involved.
Letting people know that culture is an integral part of creativity.

> Anticipated Social Impact

People will be consuming products and services produced by themselves.
