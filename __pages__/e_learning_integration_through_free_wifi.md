Providing E-Learning to select schools by providing free connectivity and data through partnership with a local Internet service provider, ThinkWifi (formerly a Google Station), that distributes free Wi-Fi. Once the schools are connected, Google will offer its G-Suite for Education platform at a subsidized cost. G-Suite for Education provides a suite of cloud-based educational offerings and tools that can be utilized to integrate E-Learning into any curriculum. The tools and services include but are not limited to, messaging and collaboration apps such as Gmail, Google Drive, Calendar, and Google Classroom.
