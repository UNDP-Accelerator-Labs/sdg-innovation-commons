Radio Mulher Bafata, is a radio complete 'wo'manned by young women. They were trained by a Spanish NGO and they work around their university schedules to provide content for the radio. They invite, inspirational women and entities that focus on the protection, empowerment and success of women to come and do awareness campaigns on different topics surrounding gender and gender inequalities in the Region.
