> Brief description about the identified local solution:

Is the first Iraqi media platform that provides Arabic content on entrepreneurship, Business and economics in an easy, Friendly and professional way. lead by Iraqi youth to highlight the importance of this topic among the Iraqi Youth and to help them in decide the career they want for their life. Specially during quarantine, the Iraqi youth interacted with this platform and successfully build partnerships and expands their network.
