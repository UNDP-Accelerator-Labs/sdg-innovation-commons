> Title of the innovation

Clean Zambia For All

> Brief description of the innovation

Garbage is an issue that my people have been crying out to the government for years now and as an Innovator, I intend on tackling this issue by setting up "Skip Bins" all over the city and country to ensure that people have somewhere to throw out their garbage since digging of pits has been announced to be illegal.
