> Brief description of the innovation

Journey Search is a platform to help traveller's with no desire to organize their own tours by providing them with great tour  offers from tour operators.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

It's based on the P2P Model. Tour operators will connect to the system and enter data on available tours, the user then selects the best offer and buys it. And we as Journey Search we get  commission on every bought offer. This innovation will give an online presence to a lot of tour operators. This will in turn help boost their businesses and the tourism sector in the country. Winning the prize will help me accelerate the developing process of the platform taking into consideration the technical aspects of getting the system up and running on the web.

> Technical Feasibility

My innovation will comprise of a web app and mobile app. The web app will provide a dashboard to allow operators enter tour data. Travellers will mostly make use of the mobile app when buying the tour offer and other services.

> Anticipated Social Impact

My innovation will improve the lives of others through;
Providing travellers with the luxury of comparing tour offers and the best possible offers from Tour Operators. 
As Tour Operators, listing your tour offers on the platform will create credibility to your business. 
Provide source of information to travellers.
Will boost the tourism sector in Zambia.
