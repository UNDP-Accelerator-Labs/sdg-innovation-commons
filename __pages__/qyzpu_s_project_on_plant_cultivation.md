> Affected Sector

Improve Air Quality

> Description of the solution

The project by scientists from QYZPU in Almaty, Kazakhstan, focuses on improving urban air quality through the cultivation of plants that can absorb harmful substances. This green initiative will be implemented in densely populated areas of Almaty after coordinating with local authorities.

> Socio-economic effects

Aims to improve air quality and public health in Almaty, creating green spaces that serve as natural air filters.
