> Title of the innovation

Mwekere Beekeeping Educational Forum

> Brief description of the innovation

To reduce poverty levels through beekeeping practices by enhancing value addition on honey bee products
To provide a credible link between the college and the Local community on matters of sustainable development
To Promote skills development among the learners and the local community in apiculture
To enhance forest ecosystem protection through Beekeeping
