> Title of the innovation

TULIME SOYA GROWERS

> Brief description of the innovation

TULIME SOYA GROWERS IS AN INNOVATION THAT SEEKS TO GROW SOYA BEANS ON A COMMERCIAL LEVEL IN MUMBWA DISTRICT OF CENTRAL PROVINCE.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

SOYA BEANS HAS A READY MARKET IN MUMBWA DISTRICT. WE TARGET TO PRODUCE ABOUT 400 BY 50 KG BAGS ON A 40 ACRES OF LAND. THE EXPECTED BAGS CAN BE TRANSLATED INTO ABOUT K240,000 AT THE CURRENT MARKET PRICE OF BETWEEN k550-k580 PER 50KG BAG. THIS WILL GENERATE ENOUGH MONEY FOR DIFFERENT WORKERS WHO SHALL BE INVOLVED AT ALL LEVELS WHICH INCLUDE PLANTING, SPRAYING, HARVESTING, CLEANING AND PUTTING IN BAGS. THE GENERATED INCOME SHALL ASSIST IN IMPROVING OPERATIONS OF THE EXISTING BUSINESS.

> Technical Feasibility

THE INNOVATION IS DESIGNED IN SUCH A WAY THAT IT SHALL BE A YEARLY ACTIVITY WHOSE PREPARATIONS SHALL BE STARTING IN SEPTEMBER BY WAY OF PUTTING IN PLACE ALL FARMING REQUIREMENTS IN READINESS FOR THE ONSET OF THE RAINS IN NOVEMBER. A VARIETY BY THE NAME OF DINA SHALL BE PLANTED WHOSE MATURITY PERIOD IS FROM NOVEMBER TO APRIL. MAY AND JUNE SHALL BE USED FOR HARVESTING WHILE JULY AND AUGUST SHALL BE FOR MARKETING OR SELLING. IN SEPTEMBER, PREPARATIONS AGAIN COMMENCE TO START THE CYCLE

> Anticipated Social Impact

MANY PEOPLE SHALL BENEFIT BY WAY OF SECURING EMPLOYMENT AT DIFFERENT LEVELS OF THE SOYA BEANS GROWING PROCESS AND THEREBY EMPOWERING THEM ECONOMICALLY
