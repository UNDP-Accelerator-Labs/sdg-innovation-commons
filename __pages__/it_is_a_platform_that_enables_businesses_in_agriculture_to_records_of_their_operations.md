> Title of the innovation

It is a platform that enables businesses in agriculture to records of their operations

> Brief description of the innovation

It helps agribusinesses to keep track of their business operation by helping them keep records of their transactions. This makes the farmer to know their the position of their business accurately

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Mode of revenue is subscription mode. In order to use the system the users will have to paying a monthly subscription. Our system will assist agribusinesses without proper book keeping skills. They will able to easy know the position of their farming business, thus helping in making business decisions and promoting accountability and transparency 
The prize money will be used to help the our business get started.

> Technical Feasibility

Our platform runs in the web browser. Our platform is also compatible on different devices including mobile phones. Our mobile app version is also compatible with android handheld pos devices for faster way of uploading the record. In September we are set to test the prototype with 50 more agribusinesses

> Anticipated Social Impact

Our platform will simplify record keeping for Agribusinesses. You need good records to monitor the progress of your business. Records can show whether your business is improving, which items are selling, or what changes you need to make. Good records can increase the likelihood of business success
