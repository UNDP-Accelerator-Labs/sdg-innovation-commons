> Brief description of the innovation

The Web Application of Alumni management system is used to maintain data of Alumni and to provide a platform where Alumni can see the progress of an institution and also participate in improving institution condition with help of donation.

ICU Alumni Management Software deals in providing each and everything you need to achieve alumni data on website &amp; mobile devices to build a strong alumni community. Both alumni and faculty can do online registration and after that administrator can approve registration and permit alumni to intermingle and acquaintance with their institution for years to come.
