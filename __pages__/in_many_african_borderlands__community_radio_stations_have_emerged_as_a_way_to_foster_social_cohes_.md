> Innovation Solution Description 

In many African borderlands, community radio stations have emerged as a way to foster social cohesion and promote local cultures. 

> Qualitative Results of the Innovation

 1. Fostering cross-border dialogue: Community radio stations can provide a platform for people from different sides of a border to communicate with each other. This can help to break down stereotypes and build understanding and trust between communities.
2. Promoting local culture: Community radio stations can showcase local music, stories, and traditions, which can help to reinforce a sense of identity and belonging among local communities.
3. Providing information: Community radio stations can provide important information about health, education, and other issues that affect local communities. This can help to empower people and enable them to make informed decisions.
4. Encouraging participation: Community radio stations can encourage participation in local development initiatives and provide a platform for people to voice their opinions and concerns.
5. Creating employment opportunities: Community radio stations can create employment opportunities for local people, which can help to reduce poverty and promote economic development.
