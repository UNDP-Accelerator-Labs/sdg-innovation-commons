> Title of the innovation

Agricultural Information and Innovation Hub

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation is an agricultural service and facilitation hub that will provide various services as well as facilitating agricultural information dissipation and engagement through technology and networking.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation will generate income through consultancy fees, by facilitating workshops and networking events, by providing marketing services to agricultural companies for their products and services as well from subscription fees from individuals paying for access to the various platforms. It will also be providing training services for agricultural investment.
It would improve operations of other businesses by linking them to prospective clients and vice versa by listening their services on our platform.
It will provide value addition to the environment due to providing information as well as training on eco-friendly and sustainability.

> Technical Feasibility

Initially due to limited resources, the hub will hire freelancers to cover various roles. Additionally,  due to the fact that we are looking to be a technologically forward company, we will ensure that most of our services  as well as products can be accessed online. This will increase our coverage to not only distant places around Zambia but also to other countries around the world. The services to be offered include agricultural consultancy, farming produce marketing, agribusiness training which can all be accessed online.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation will provide agricultural information, statistics and networking opportunities to all members of society. The whole essence of the innovation is to make Information accessible to individuals from all walks of life with a major focus on low income areas in which it will give them access to agricultural opportunities. 
Also it will also be providing information and guidance on eco-friendly and sustainable methods of farming such as aeroponics which does not require pesticides and uses less amounts of water.
