> Brief description about the identified local solution:

University of Basrah in Iraq took an extraordinary place in responding to COVID19 and in supporting the Iraqi government to tackle this pandemic through their local activities. One of these activities is manufacturing local Ventilator Devices inside the university with international standards and in cooperation with the university academics and local private sector. These devices got the official approval of Basrah Health Directorate and asked the university to provide them with 10 ventilator devices and here where the UNDP is looking forward in supporting the Academic Sector in achieving the required objective through providing the raw materials.
