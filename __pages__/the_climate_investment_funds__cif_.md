> Description of the solution

The initiative provide financing to developing and emerging countries to support large-scale projects that mitigate and adapt to climate change. The CIF operates through targeted programs, such as the Clean Technology Fund (CTF) and the Scaling Up Renewable Energy Program (SREP), which fund initiatives including renewable energy deployment, energy efficiency improvements, and sustainable transport systems.

> Socio-economic effects

The CIF promotes sustainable economic development, reduces poverty, enhances energy access, and fosters climate resilience, improving quality of life and creating green jobs.
