> Title of the innovation

Fish Growth Production

> Brief description of the innovation

We will be raising quality tilapia fish that will be of international standards. Our fish will weigh 300 grams and kept under clean and healthy environment. This product has market in Zambia and is a healthier cheaper source of protein .The stocking rate will be five fingerlings per square meters,and the amount of feed that we will use in kgs will be (60 bags) 3,000kg in one circle.
The  farm will raise and supply Oreochromis macrochir and tilapia rendalli of recommended sizes e.g. that of 300 grams inorder to survive the competition in the industry. Additionally, the quality of fish provision cannot be ruled out as one of the services that will  be delivered as it is the key in fish industry.in order to achieve the quality of international standard, the fish will be kept under clean and healthy environment. The farm is located in chasato area at Kayankude farm,  Lundazi District in Eastern Province. The source of water will be the bollhalls that will shall construct.
