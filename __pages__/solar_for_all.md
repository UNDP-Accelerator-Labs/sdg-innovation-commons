> Description of the solution

Solar for All is a New York State utility bill assistance program (financial support scheme). New York State is funding solar farms to benefit nearby homeowners and renters who may not be able to access solar (clean energy). Through Solar for All, vulnerable households prone to energy poverty can get the benefits of clean energy while lowering their energy costs.

> Socio-economic effects

Reducing energy poverty
