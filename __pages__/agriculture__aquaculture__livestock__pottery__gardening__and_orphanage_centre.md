> Title of the innovation

Agriculture (aquaculture, livestock, pottery, gardening) and orphanage centre

> Brief description of the innovation

Farming which involves aqua farming, livestock keeping , pottery and gardening to sustain the land and people around. Also to put up an orphanage centre for orphans to stay and find a home that they can be a part of and still have food and basic needs provided for them.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This will be a farm business which will be able to grow crops and keep livestock. This will help generate income to sustain the complex so that the orphanage centre can be kept running by providing them with food as well as finances from the farm produce sales. 
This will help reduce orphans with no homes, reduce poverty within the area as well as hunger among orphans.

> Technical Feasibility

The plan is to use my 10hectres farm land to start gardening, livestock keeping and pottery farming, with the cash flows from the mentioned, will be able to put up an orphanage centre and expand into aqua culture. The whole essence is to have a place for orphans to call home and have something to work on. And still have time for school and nature as a balance.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This will be able to create jobs for people around. 
By providing a larger market of food stuff, will help reduce poverty and hunger.
