> Title of the innovation

Strengthening entrepreneurship through building of local livestock market in central town district of rural areas.

> Brief description of the innovation

The project intend to increase and strengthen business interactions with farmers in livestock emphasize diverstification of Zambian economy through agriculture sector. The project will involve livestock animals village chickens, goats, pigs sheep's and other demanding birds needed for consumption to the public. The project will have sensitization and advertisement programmes of the animals to local rural towns once the farmers bring the livestock to the market. The local people in rural to urban areas in formal and informal employment will be targeted to buy local animals for consumption in order support farmers having wider markets for their livestock. This innovation program will also target local and international meat, chicken, goat, pork and sheep suppliers from Lusaka Central and Kasumba Lesa Boarder.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation project will generate income for the implementor youths and the rural-urban farmers and increase the operations through the following. Management of the markets fees affairs will establish the fee charge per animals and the birds too.
1. A goat or pig and sheep will have a fee-paying charge of 10 kwacha each after sells for storage and trading space.
2. A chicken and other birds will have a fee-paying of 2 kwacha after sell for storage and trading space.
3. The other way of generating income through the innovation will be through provisions of accommodation to farmers and people with small guest rooms within the markets at 100 kwacha after sell of all the livestock.
The generated income will assist in the operations of the innovation to paying market utilities, market workers, market security and renovations and developing the market standard. 
4. The innovation will increase environment protection through the launch planting trees in rural areas where the markets will be established.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The innovation project will improve and benefits farmers through:
1. Farmers will have good and easy accessable markets for their livestocks.
2. Farmers will be selling their livestocks at a good price because they are able to negotiate with customers and farmers will establish markets price.
Farmers will be motivated to do animals farming and invest in the health of livestocks.
4. Poverty levels in rural areas will be reduced as farmers won't just depends on crop farming but also motivated in livestock farming.
5. Because livestock markets have been created, farmers will save time and also transportation cost for long distance will be the thing of the past.
