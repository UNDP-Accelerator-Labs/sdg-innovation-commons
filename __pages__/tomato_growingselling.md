> Title of the innovation

Tomato growing/selling

> Brief description of the innovation

Tomato is one of the most important vegetable crop in zambia grown both commecialy and small scale. Highest yields are obtained in the dry season under irrigation and lowest during rain season from the end of October and march.
It is important to choose the right tomato variaty because this will affect the fruit quality, adaptability and reliability disease resistance, plant growth habit , market preferences and planting time.
Timing and site selection, tomatoes are hardly annual, warm season crop that are sensitive to frost. Average daily temperature of 20'C to 24'C is optimum for growth, yields and Fruit quality and we pick a sunny spot.
