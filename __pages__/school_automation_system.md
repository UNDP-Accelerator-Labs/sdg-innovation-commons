> Title of the innovation

School Automation system

> Brief description of the innovation

School Automation system is a software that has been designed to store and retrieve data or information based on educational facilities (schools).this software will store information kept on paper or books in schools and retrieve it anytime if needed, the information that can be stored include: pupil's results, payments, registers, school activities (calendar), teacher's details and any other information kept by schools. The main aim of the innovation is to reduce the work of teachers and save time.
