> Title of the innovation

Community school teacher and self business of selling chinkondia

> Brief description of the innovation

It's a community school located in komboka area in CMML Church, it's name chawama christian school. Business self marketing in community of commodity chinkondia soap.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

For school: trying to cata for all parents and those incapacitated and trying to employ even those without good education background. For business: wanting to reach as farequested as chipata, Ndola, Livingston and many parts of Zambia selling chinkondia soap and when expanded employ others, being that it has good income.
