> Title of the innovation

Community Media and outreach project

> Brief description of the innovation

A familiar, extremely simple, cost effective and easy to access platform with unlimited mass capability that can be accessed using any internet enabled device.
A fitting alternative to social media that provides all the benefits and more, whilst eliminating all the undesirables such as misinformation, congestion, cyber crimes and bullying etc.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The platform will be used for marketing, advertising,media publishing,data collections, jobs and opportunities creation, resources identification and mobilization etc. 
Prize money to be used for further company formalization, project marketing and advertizing,purchase of equipment and content creation etc.
A fitting alternative to printing paper, easing and Increasing organizational public engagement,helping to Spare trees, reducing pollution and littering etc.
A Percentage of company earnings will be reinvested into corporate social responsibility and investments such as referbishment of public facilities, clean up campaigns and other creative public benefitial projects etc and prioritize vulnerable groups.

> Technical Feasibility

It is a platform that mimics the most popular and familiar social media platforms, yet maximizing positives and minimizing negatives.
We had approached and discussed with the private sector and are now at the far end of 18 month spent engaging and presenting to the Public sector(GRZ) to sign on and use the platform as an official government platform as it meets all the criteria, we are currently awaiting feedback from smart zambia.
We will then engage the public on joining and benefiting from this free, easy to use platform and project that truly leaves no one behind.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will create and support livilhoods, directly by creating jobs and opportunities, and indirectly by easing access to useful and necessary information that helps our society.

When properly analyzed,all our SDGs are hinged on information.The project looks to address our SDGS using information as the backbone and creativity as a driving force that keep people engaged and progressing accordingly.
Our goal is then to develop an App solution different to anything currently on the market,for Africa,by Africa.
