As part of the PPE waste initiative/behavioral insights experiment, AccLab Uzbekistan in partnership with one of the largest Supermarket Chains in the country and Waste collection agency created an awareness raising video on good practices to prevent the spread of virus: social distancing; correct/safe use and disposal of PPE; protecting people at risk such as elderly; shifting to non-cash transactions, etc.
