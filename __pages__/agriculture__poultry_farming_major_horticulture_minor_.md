> Title of the innovation

Agriculture (poultry farming major,horticulture minor)

> Brief description of the innovation

Poultry farming being the engine of the economy of Zambia, it has emproved livelihoods of many people, therefore, as a supplier of chicken we came to realise that we need to supply both broiler and village chicken to sustain the market and improve the economy of our people as well as tomatoe production and cabbage.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We plan on start producing broiler chicks and supply to our local farmers as we are running out of chick supply in the country especially muchinga and northern province, this will help generate more income so that we also start supplying the feed to our farmers, not only that but also helping sausage and and beef companies Bering supplied with our products.

> Technical Feasibility

Our innovation is designed into two groups poutry as major as well as horticulture as minor

> Anticipated Social Impact

Our innovation helps small scale farmers with the manure as the prices for fertilizer has been skyrocketing, we also plan to help poultry farmers access chicks without moving in other far provinces of districts doing so we are lifting small scale farmers, as well as create informal and formal employment to the youths and mothers in society who don't have what to do.
