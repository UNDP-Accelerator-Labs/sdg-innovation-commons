The project is about explaining the perceptions of the Z generation in Turkey regarding the concerns, expectations and emotions related to sustainable development, through research in the mobile neuroscience laboratory to be established. Thus, it is aimed to use the research results of the project to develop the quality of the plans and programs that should be run regionally, and the program communication based on scientific data, at the point of sustainable development, education and cooperation that can be implemented with these young people.
