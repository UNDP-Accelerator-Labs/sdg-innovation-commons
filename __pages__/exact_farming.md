> Description of the solution

A platform that unites digital agronomy solutions for agricultural producers, manufacturers and sellers of fertilizers, agrochemicals and seeds, financial institutions and other participants in the agricultural industry, allowing them to effectively manage agribusiness, make timely decisions, reduce risks and increase the transparency of interactions with each other. ExactFarming combines satellite imagery, weather data, and soil analysis to offer insights that improve crop yields, resource efficiency, and overall farm productivity.
