> Description of the solution

Academic Institutions can assist in leading research for local government to make evidence-based choices on heat thresholds, interventions and guidelines. Integrating information on heat risk management into urban planning courses, public health courses and disaster management course can also help to train the next generation of city leaders in key heat risk management concepts.
