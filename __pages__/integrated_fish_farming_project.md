> Title of the innovation

Integrated Fish Farming Project

> Brief description of the innovation

Integrated fish farming project shall comprise of a solar borehole, fish pond and a garden. We shall use the nutrient rich water from the fish pond to irrigate our fruits and vegetable garden.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Our main income shall come from fish sales, vegetable and fruit sales. our target market is Mongu and nearby Districts like Senanga, Kalabo and Kaoma. Our project will assist improve operations of other businesses like Restaurants, offtakers. Our project will help reduce the current pressure on capture fisheries and allow the fish in natural water bodies to breed.

> Technical Feasibility

The project comprises of a fifty (50) metres by twenty five (25) metres fish pond and a vegetable/fruit garden. We have the land on which we intend to start production, we require seed money for initial start up, construction of solar borehole, fish pond,fish feed, fish farming accessories, irrigation equipment, light truck (transport), seed and other agriculture inputs.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The project will output more fish on the market. We project to create 3 direct jobs, 6 indirect jobs. The project will support other businesses like Restaurants.
