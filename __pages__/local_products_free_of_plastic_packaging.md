Al grano is a store that provides alternative solutions, experiences and quality local products to reduce the use of plastic and daily waste. They sell in bulk according to the weight that the customer really needs. The products sold in the store are mostly organic and fresh, in addition to being purchased from networks of local producers. They have a home delivery service and also have a physical store where you can bring your own containers.
