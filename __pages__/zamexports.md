> Title of the innovation

ZamExports

> Brief description of the innovation

ZEXPORTS is an online web portal/modile app that is built on improving the export sector of the country (Zambia). It is meant to serve as an educative/business service to enable manufactures and commercial businesses to operate localy and internationally.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

ZEXPORTS shall play as a vital part in the business sector nationally and globally by bringing to light all the raw materials/products that are locally produced and indigenous to the international market making a step forward to the eliminations of product shortage.

> Technical Feasibility

ZEXPORTS is to  work as a digital marketplace which connects customers and suppliers to a national and manufactural level as an ecommerce platform.
it is to work similar to eBay with an AI system and UI interface tailored to suit the needs of the users.

> Anticipated Social Impact

it will help improve the supply chain and the amount of value to be added based on the improvement of the products reaching the market, and bringing more goods to light.
