Paperman is a social enterprise with a mission to accelerate the Trash Economy by investing in game-changing leaders and ideas, creating an enabling ecosystem through research and facilitation, and designing behavioral change interventions through collaboration.  The portfolio of solutions includes different systemic solutions that enable efficient collection of waste, avenues for commoditizing waste thereby creating livelihoods and income for waste collectors, using the revenue from waste for social programs like healthcare and education.
