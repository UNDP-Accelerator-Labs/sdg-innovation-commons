> Title of the innovation

EDUCATION THE FOUNDATION OF A STABLE, MORAL AND STRONG NATION

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovative idea focuses on the education of the nation, as education is definitely the key to success. It focuses on providing a practical, interesting and moral education that will empower learners to reach high standards and enable them to have broad ideas that tackle today's and tomorrow's problems.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Income can be generated by establishing a centre to tutor children in various skills and disciplines of study. The target market is to provide this opportunity to children in both rural and urban (small towns) areas, by establishing institutions with competent people as instructors.

> Technical Feasibility

It is designed to reach the all classes of society, and conduct experiments in a practical but cheap way which will not harm the environment.

> Anticipated Social Impact

By making education afford to the poorest and wealthy, families will be empowered and not only the family but the nation at large. Will also elevate the status of individuals and Zambia as a nation.
