> Description of the solution

Is an essential tool for gathering and disseminating data related to agricultural markets. Launched to improve market efficiency and provide farmers with timely information. Aims to enhance transparency in market operations by providing real-time information on prices, supply, and demand for various agricultural products. This system plays a crucial role in supporting farmers, traders, and policymakers in making informed decisions based on current market conditions
