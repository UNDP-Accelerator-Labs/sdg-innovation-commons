> Type of the Solution

Community Engagement

> Affected Sector

Energy, Education, Public Policy, Community Development

> Description of the solution

The project aims to transition Kutaisi City entirely to renewable energy by 2050. This comprehensive project includes strategies such as enhancing solar and wind energy capacities, improving energy efficiency in buildings, and integrating energy education in schools. It emphasizes community involvement.

> Socio-economic effects

Promotes local energy independence, reduces carbon emissions, educates the community on sustainable practices.
