> Brief description of the innovation

Bethel African Shop has a vision of working with school leavers and windows. from the training of fabric printing like tie dye and batiks, from the same fabric we make african outfits like kafftains' african shirts for men and women, we will also make african bags , chitenge beddings and scatter cushions.
