About the Challenge:

In Iraq, many people face many difficulties in obtaining high-quality education for several reasons, including: (1) High costs of tuition fees, transportation, and living costs. (2)There are no universities and institutes in most villages and cities. (3) People with disabilities cannot go to education centers. (4) Many women are prevented from going to education centers because of customs and traditions. (5) Many young people are deprived of education because of the need to work. (6) The displaced and refugees in the camps are not able to go to the education centers. (7) Poor quality of education in most universities and institutes. (8) Many graduates face difficulties in gaining hands-on experience. (9) There are no courses (in Iraq and in most Arabic countries) related to Programming, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Web development. All these reasons result in more unemployment, deteriorating living conditions, poverty, ignorance, and deprivation of education.

Goals of Code Khana Platform:

Code Khana platform seeks to achieve the following GOALS: (1) Providing free, and high-quality education (%100 online) in Programming, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, and Web development. (2) Qualifying Youth for the labor market to get jobs in the top companies. (3) Supporting the private sector by helping young people build their own projects and encouraging them to build tech companies, applications, and smart systems. (4) Encouraging students to learn by offering jobs and prizes for outstanding students.

Code Khana Main Features:

Code Khana platform seeks to achieve the above goals by providing the following SOLUTIONS: (1) High-quality online courses and educational programs (for free). (2) Anyone can register and learn via smartphone or via a simple computer, and there is no need to purchase any hardware or software. (3) Providing equal opportunities for all students (in cities and villages) to learn without attendance. (4) People with disabilities can register and learn from any place. (5) All women can register and learn at home. (6) All young people can register and learn on their own time and without having to leave their work. (7) All refugees and displaced are welcome to register without the need for documents and without the need to leave their camps. (8) Code Khana platform offers a high-quality curriculum (certified by the best educational institutions in the world). (9) Code Khanna platform provides hands-on experience and real projects for all students to gain real-world experience.
