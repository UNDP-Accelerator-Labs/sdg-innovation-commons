> Brief description of the innovation

The name of the innovation is called "Ending Sexual Discrimination in Schools". The innovation is aimed at fostering the much needed health services for school going child.
Research has recorded that Zambian community is among the most documented regions with poor health services especially among school going children and physically challenged people.
Research has also indicated that school going children have been sexually harassed by their teachers, school administrators as well as their fellow school mates. This situation has contributed to poor academic performance and bad health among the victims.
Thus, the organization aims at supporting sexual harassment policy by helping learners report sexual discrimination cases to relevant authorities. If this situation left an attended to, it may lead to learners skip or drop classes due to psychological effects i.e inability to concentrate in class and loss of interest in regular activities.
