Name of solution owner: Arena Recycling Industry co-founded by a young girl Hellena Sailas.

Arena Recycling Industry is a social business that collects plastic waste from beaches in Dar es salaam and produces building materials such as Eco-Bricks, paving block, and tiles out of recycled plastic waste for
construction of affordable houses, toilets, and other buildings in rural areas.

More about the Solution;
This solution not only contributes toplastic-free beaches and waterways, but also helps bridge the water and
sanitation gap by constructing water tanks and latrines with our eco-bricks. We
educate and promote the change of behavior in waste disposal, byinvolvinge
community recycle’s into logistic and improve their income by buying collected
plastic waste fromthe  environment, the collected plastic waste is then sorted
according to plastic waste composition (density) melted and mixed with sand to
produce bricks covered during courses.
