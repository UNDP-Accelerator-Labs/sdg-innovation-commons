> Title of the innovation

My Transport

> Brief description of the innovation

My Transport is a transport peer-to-peer application that connects car owners looking for hikers to people searching for rides to nearby cities

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The app will help people earn an income by offering paid rides on their vehicles. Also our organization will get a commission per ride. It will also provide an alternative to people using who use buses to move between cities.

> Technical Feasibility

It is an Android application that users will be able to install on Google play. They can either sign up as a driver or passenger.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The app will provide an alternative to people that use buses as they will now have an option of easily finding a car to hike in. This will also help driver lower their transportation costs and earn some money as they travel between cities. Our solution will make car hiking easier and simpler.
