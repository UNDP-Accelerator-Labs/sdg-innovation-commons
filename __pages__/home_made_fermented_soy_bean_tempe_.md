ALMA sister (nunnery ) making Tempeh using local soy beans. This activity is done in the morning and sell only to few consumers who visited their convent. The idea is to support young girls with basic skills so they can create their own job later on after they already master the skills of making tempeh. Soy bean can be found anywhere in the markets around Dili and it is affordable to people with low income. Tempeh is a nutritious food for children and families.
