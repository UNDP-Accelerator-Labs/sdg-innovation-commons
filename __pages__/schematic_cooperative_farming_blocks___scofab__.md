> Brief description of the innovation

Schematic Cooperative Farming Blocks. Has the following functions in order to spearhead substantial  productivity in Zambian Small scale farming:-
√. Acquisition of land from a particular local chief in a district.
√. Mobilize the bush clearance personnel.
√. Provide means of field cultivation.(Loan)
√. Provide seed for that planting season. (Loan).
√. Provide fertilizer to farmers in scheme blocks. (Loan).
√. Provide technical knowledge and supervision through an Agriculture extension officer.

> Technical Feasibility

IMPLEMENTATION PLAN.
√. Acquire 2 tractors per a scheme block.
√. Acquire 1,600 acres land from the local chief or Ministry of lands per scheme block see
√. Target 2 scheme blocks for starters
√. Distribute 30 acres to each youth between the age of of 25 to 35 years (allowance 40 years).
√. Bush clearance personnel at a minimumal wage.
√. Use Cooperative tractors to till land for sitting members of each portion of land at K1,000 per acre. (Loan).
√. Provide seeds at K1,000 per acre. (Loan).
√. Provide fertilizer at K2,000 per acre. (Loan)

> Anticipated Social Impact

Each Scheme farming block would empower 40 youths from that particular chiefdom in agricultural jobs. For starters two scheme blocks in 2 different chiefdoms per a district.
