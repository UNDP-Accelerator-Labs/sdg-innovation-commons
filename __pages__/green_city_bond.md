> Description of the solution

Green City Bonds provide a financial mechanism for cities to fund sustainable infrastructure projects.  The approach allows cities to raise capital for projects that improve environmental sustainability and resilience, such as renewable energy installations and energy-efficient building upgrades.

> Socio-economic effects

Increases funding for green projects, boosts economic growth through infrastructure development, and promotes sustainable urban planning.
