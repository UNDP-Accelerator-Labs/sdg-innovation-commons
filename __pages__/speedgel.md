Description: 
The contactless hydroalcoholic gel dispensing terminal using an intelligent proximity detection system, sized for places with high flow with a capacity of 2 liters for more than 3200 doses per filling, with an intuitive design allowing an immediate understanding of the system.

Origin of the solution: 
Algeria Startup Challenge/Coronhackathon. A partnership between UNDP Accelerator Lab. and Cap CoWork Incubator.
