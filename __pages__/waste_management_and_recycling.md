> Title of the innovation

Waste Management and Recycling

> Brief description of the innovation

The projects' main focus is on improving the environment through management of waste. It's aim is utilise on the wastes around the community and make new products out of them through recycling. The main targeted wastes include, plastic, sacks and grass bottles.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The project will create jobs for the locals and at the same time enhance supply of plastic products to the local market. It has been observed that main local market in kalulushi and the surrounding areas, lack the source needed plastic products, hence this project will benefit both the market and local people. In addition the project help in ensuring clean and green environment which is essential for climate control.

> Technical Feasibility

The collection of plastic gabbage from dump sites and local houses and recycle them to me new products. Separation of plastic and glass bottles.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Create market to the local interprises through supplying them with plastic products, create jobs to the locals who will be collecting gabbage and working at the company.
