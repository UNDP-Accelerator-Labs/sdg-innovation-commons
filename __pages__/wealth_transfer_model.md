> Title of the innovation

Wealth Transfer Model

> Brief description of the innovation

This project is one that could help educate the masses on the transfer of wealth. It involves a bus that will be used to aid the wealth transfer. All parties involved do benefit in a time period of one year six months or one year eight months atmost.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Well in the area I live in, people always travel. This bus project will provide a daily job to a conductor and driver that will have a fixed pay at the end of the month, with the bus owner servicing the bus as they work for it for the specified time and educating them financially. Then when the duration expires, the driver and conductor can then get ownership of the bus. They as well can become employers if they wish.
