Ana Bavoro started her business “Helpmate Fiji” after she lost her job during the COVID19 pandemic. Helpmate Fiji offers errand run services which includes grocery shopping, pick-ups, deliveries, bill  payments, admin work, research, and even delivery of goods to people in isolation/ quarantine.
