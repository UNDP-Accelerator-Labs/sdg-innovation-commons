> Type of the Solution

Policy/Regulation

> Affected Sector

Transportation

> Description of the solution

This solution establishes a designated urban area where vehicles must meet a minimum emission standard to enter. The LEZ aims to discourage high-polluting vehicles and improve air quality within the zone.

> Socio-economic effects

Improved air quality, potential reduction in respiratory illnesses, discourages high-polluting vehicles, may require short-term economic adjustments for businesses/individuals
