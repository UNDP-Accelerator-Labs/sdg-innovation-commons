MARN Campaign for waste and waste sorting: written and audio translation in 4 main Mayan languages to disseminate on community radios and social networks. This campaign aims to reach more people and help them comply with GA 164-2021.  


The 4 languages chosen are the 4 that have the largest number of speakers in Guatemalan territory.
