> Title of the innovation

Liuwa Community Ecotourism subsector Plug In.

> Brief description of the innovation

Offer opportunity to Liuwa communities to earn direct tourism revenue through the ecotourism sub sector by leveraging on community events that attract tourists such as ceremonies, village courts, indigenous fishing events and others. 30% of revenue is given to the participating village.
Without taking time off their other livelihood activities, villages will earn extra revenue.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The service will attract a fee of $50 per family that will take on this exciting adventure of learning more about the people of Liuwa in addition to the wildlife and exciting landscapes.
The innovation will add to the existing pool of tourism services already existing in Liuwa Plain National Park.
The target market is the tourists that visit Liuwa Plain National park.
The challenge prize will assist us establish an ICT infrastructure that will coordinate our various activities and connect us to various partners.
This innovation will reduce peoples' dependency on natural resources harvesting for livelihood as well as growing local economy.

> Technical Feasibility

As the name suggests, it is a Plug In meaning we're connecting the innovation to the already existing tourism infrastructure and packages in Liuwa Plain national park.
The implementation plan involves taking inventory of exciting events in the nearby villages. These activities are listed and explained on our portal so that interested tourists  can sign up. Once this is done, we coordinate with the villages on the preparation up to the actual day when the visitors will come. The payment is made through the tour operator who then remits to us.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Participating villages will be given 30% of the earnings from the visitors. This will help them cushion their financial budgets.
This innovation is relevant to this market because Liuwa Plain national park is a treasure of history and exciting activities. Being a park with people living legally in it, many tourists would love to interact and learn from these people.
