> Description of the solution

Policies such as the proposed City Council bills which codifies the Citys cooling center program and the City Council law 1960-2022 in New York City, and which requires the city to submit a heat plan each year, need to be implemented with a core equity focus in order to allocate permanent resources and institutionalize these crucial-life saving programs.
