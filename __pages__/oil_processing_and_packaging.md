> Title of the innovation

Oil Processing and packaging

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation will be to Extract, process and package cooking oil. Sunflower, soybeans and groundnuts will be used as raw materials. These will be sourced from local farmers. Modern Machines will be used during the production process so as to produce products of high quality that will meet the market standards.
