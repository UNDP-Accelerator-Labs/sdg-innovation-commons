> Title of the innovation

Aquaculture (Fish Farming) Project

> Brief description of the innovation

Aquaculture innovation is an agricultural innovative that will enable Zambia produce adequate fish to mitigate the high demands of fish in the country.
This will also enhance revenue generation to the country, uplift the living standards of people in the community through job creation and also reduce people's attitudes of poising fish and other aquantics  from the streams for relish due to unavailability of affordable fish.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Our innovation will generate income by selling fish to the near by community, shops, restaurants and many other retail and consumers.
The initiative will improve the operations of existing businesses especially agro dealers by purchasing project's products from them.
Our target is mainly the surrounding retail customers (shops), restaurants, schools, households, hospitals and malls.
The initiative will favorably compete with the existing ones by providing quality service and products to the community, engage the community through open door policy to make them feel part and parcel of the initiative and we shall also survive on low profits for some time to attract many people to the business. 
This innovation's advantage on environment is water conservation, less mowing, fewer pollutants, fewer pesticides and fertilizers, supports local wildlife and creates environmental awareness.

> Technical Feasibility

Our innovation was designed to enable our innovation be effectively implemented starting with the project description and it's  over view, marketing trends and strategies,  feasibility studies, executive SWOT analysis and many others.
A great team coordination, community involvement, focus, and determination will enable us run a very successful innovative ever

> Anticipated Social Impact

It shall improve the lives of other people through job creation, empowering the vulnerable youths and women, educate the public about the importance of fish Farming project, impart them with skills and knowledge and inspire them to start up businesses to contribute to the growth of our economy.
