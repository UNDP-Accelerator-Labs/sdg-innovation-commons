Bukalemun (Chameleon) is a platform project that offers multi-stage digital Turkish Sign Language (TÄ°D) training and tests to 984 users via fixed videos. It is aimed to develop necessary digital tools for deaf individuals and to provide accessible vocational training for their participation in full employment as instructors.
