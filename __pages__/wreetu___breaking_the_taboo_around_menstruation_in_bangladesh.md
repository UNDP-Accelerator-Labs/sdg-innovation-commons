In Bangladesh, menstruation is still a taboo topic and as a result a lot of adolescent girls often do not have access to proper information regarding menstruation. To address this challenge, Wreetu - a Bangladeshi MHM platform - offers menstrual education, MHM products and health assistance via Chatbot. This social enterprise that aims to create a community of health-conscious adolescent girls in Bangladesh. Since 2016, Wreetu has provided menstrual education to 19000+ adolescents, their parents, teachers and community people. Wreetu offers comprehensive menstrual education to community through interactive session and comic book, manufacture sustainable menstrual hygiene product that is reusable, eco-friendly and provide MHM consultancy to organization and individuals. Through Wreetu Comic Book, an illustrated and story based book on puberty and menstruation, Wreetu has been unique in providing MHM content and education in Bangladesh. The Reusable Pad made by Wreetu is an added value to help every girl and woman to enjoy their healthy period. Wreetu also provides training to individual MHM activist and activists from NGOs. Wreetu is working towards building an empathetic and a friendly environment for every adolescent in Bangladesh. Wreetu consists of three layers: firstly, awareness building through workshops, the second layer is the book that offers useful knowledge.
