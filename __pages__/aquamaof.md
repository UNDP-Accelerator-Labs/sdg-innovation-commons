> Description of the solution

Aquamaof offers closed loop aquaculture solutions using Recirculating Aquaculture Systems (RAS). Key features:
Closed loop: Efficiently treats and reuses water, reducing consumption.
Monitoring: Uses sensors to monitor environmental conditions in real time.
Sustainability: Minimizes pollutant emissions and risk of disease.
High productivity: Allows for dense fish farming at lower costs.
Flexibility: Suitable for a variety of aquatic species.
