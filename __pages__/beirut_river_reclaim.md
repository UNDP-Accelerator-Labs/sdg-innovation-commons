Beirut River less

> How would you describe this solution in under 300 words? 

Local architect partnered with the Indian social enterprise Afforestt @afforestt to employ the Miyawaki method of afforestation, developed by Dr. Miyawaki in Japan, and which works in any environmental condition, from humid environments to deserts. Miyawaki forests are ideal solutions for cities as they can be grown fast (3years) in very small (10sqm) to very big plots of degraded land, such as leftover spaces, roundabouts, road medians...
