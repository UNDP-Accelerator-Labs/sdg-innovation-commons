Activated charcoal is a bio product by a Lao Bio Energy Group. They turn leaves into charcoal by adding natural ingredients such as cassava flour, water and fragrance powder. The activated charcoal is a great solution to solve issue of burning leaves which can be seen everywhere. The charcoal helps reducing bad smell in wardrobe, refrigerator, toilet and car.
