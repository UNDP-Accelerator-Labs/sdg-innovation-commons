## Solar powered back pack made from waste plastic and electronic waste

In Sierra Leone, the lack of electricity in remote areas limits students' access

to reliable light for extended study time. Growing plastic and electronic waste pollution worsens environmental challenges. These challenges affect both educational opportunities and environmental

sustainability.
