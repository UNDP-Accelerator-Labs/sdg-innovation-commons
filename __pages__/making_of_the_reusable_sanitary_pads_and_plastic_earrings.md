> Brief description of the innovation

It helps the orphan and Vulnerable children especially girls to be economic strengthened by empowering them with the skill for making reusable sanitary pads and plastic earrings. This in returns end period poverty especially in Misisi compound where this innovation is and will be implemented.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

It will generate income because once these items are made when all materials are in place, they can be sold to the larger market such as Comesa (common market for Eastern and southern Africa). The prices target K150

> Technical Feasibility

It's design using plastic and bottle lids with fabric...The proposed implementation plan is to start as early possible once materials are available.
The will be need to train more of the girls to handle such innovation so that they can sell to their friends

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will improve their need to buy more sanitary pads instead they can make for themselves and in ready to plastic earrings it makes their lives easy and affordable. It also improve their skilks.
