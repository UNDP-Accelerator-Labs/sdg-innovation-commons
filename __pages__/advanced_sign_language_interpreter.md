> Title of the innovation

advanced sign language interpreter

> Brief description of the innovation

In this project, sigh languages are programed and translated into English and Bemba ( a local language spoken by 80% of the people in Zambia and parts of southern Africa). A Teacher who has speech impairment was to teach a class of normal people with sigh language without the need of anything but this technology. This followed up with normal interaction between the students with the help of speech to text translation software and both old fashioned typing method and handwriting recognition software.
