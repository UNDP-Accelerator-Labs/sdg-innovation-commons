> Title of the innovation

Art(traditional dance,afro fusion, craft and theater)

> Brief description of the innovation

The aim is to have traditional and afro dance,theater plays and craft shop all in one location.All these tourist attracting activities will give jobs to many young talented citizens as it will attract the attention of tourists.
Almost every weakened there are entertainment demanding gatherings that take place around the country,continent and world at large.Because of poor marketing the client fail to access these services.
Therefore will hire bussines agents and advertise on screens and 
on the  largest billboards in town.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

*The dances will attract tourists who will buy the crafts.
*The dancers will be performing in hotels, Lodges and other places.The charges will be k1200 per hour
*Watching a theater play will attract an amount of k30 per person.
*There will be Art classes that will be meant for  sharing  our skills with tourists.This will actract a fee of k200 per session (two hours)

> Technical Feasibility

Unlike Kabwata cultural village,we will be charging K5 as entry fee.We have have all the different tourist attracting activities in one location.

> Anticipated Social Impact

These activities will not only provide employment to young energetic youths but also help them abstain from drug abuse and criminal activities. These activities will also act as recreational activities. Dull to poor marketing the market is failing to have access to these products and services.There fore the hiring of agents and advertising on screens and billboards will definitely bring business.
