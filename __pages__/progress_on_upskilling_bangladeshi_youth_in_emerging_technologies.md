## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights) 

1. There is definitely demand for local human resources with skilled in cutting-in edge technology from local private sector companies in the technology industry. 


2. Skilling youth in technology is a priority of the government and they are positively exploring innovative methods to deliver the training.

3. The government partners are actively involved in scouting for skills demand and designing the most apt courses for students.

4. Harvard university is very interested to collaborate on this initiative given the promise of its impact in this region. 

5. The country office is interested to pre-invest in the pilot of this initiative from Trac sources given its potential to mobilize additional finance and impact.

6. A pilot is being designed focusing on female students only to gain insights into the unique challenges faced women that may be causing the gender digital divide in Bangladesh

> Can you provide more detail on your handover process?
