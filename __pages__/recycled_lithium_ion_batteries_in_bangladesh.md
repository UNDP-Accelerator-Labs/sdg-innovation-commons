Borac Energia is a Li-ion Battery and Electric Vehicle manufacturer. They make their batteries by scrap Li-ion. They are replacing pollution creating Lead-Acid battery with environment-friendly Li-ion Battery. Lifespan of their battery is double compared to lead-acid batteries, enabling renewable energy companies to spend less money on battery, thus contributing to achieving SDG-7, making Clean Energy more affordable.
