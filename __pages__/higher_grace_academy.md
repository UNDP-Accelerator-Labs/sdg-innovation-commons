> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Higher grace academy is the name of our innovation. The institution will draw it's income through school fees and it will other unemployed teachers to have a salary at the end of the month. The target is to bring competition to other existing schools. Our target areas are Kamuchanga, Kalukanya and chibolya.

> Technical Feasibility

Our plan is to start with early childhood education and go to primary. We have a small piece of land so we propose to start with the baby class in 2022.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This initiative will help reduce unemployment as it will employ at least a good number of teachers in a long run. This innovation will help other existing schools to offer better services
