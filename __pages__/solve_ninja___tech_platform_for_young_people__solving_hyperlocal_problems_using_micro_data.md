Solve Ninja is a platform by Reap Benefit that activates young citizens to solve small problems that leave a big dent on their local communities. Reap Benefit is a social enterprise based in Bengaluru, India. Reap Benefits works with young people, enabling them to solve small local community problems. This not only helps young people in developing changemaking skills but also sow the seeds of active citizenship at a young age.
[https://reapbenefit.org/about/](https://reapbenefit.org/about/)


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2022/blobs/solutions/fcb77c6f71d3db2b811889a3c8531e24.png)


By using relatable graphics and language, Reap Benefit makes solving local problem engaging, fun and cool for young people.  The Solve Ninja platform is user friendly and taps in the natural curiosity of the technology native young people. 


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2022/blobs/solutions/06ad20543bf6a50c2080031f7a53db88.png)


Solve Ninja does not promote any one way of solving a social problem. Instead, it acknowledges that each person can contribute in problem solving using their unique strength. This gives young people the self-permission to take charge of their local problems and come up with their unique solutions.
