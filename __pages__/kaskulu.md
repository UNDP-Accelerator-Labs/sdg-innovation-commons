> Title of the innovation

Kaskulu

> Brief description of the innovation

Kaskulu is a mobile application developed to enable scholars to prepare for their exams through interactive quizzes and revision materials prepared by qualified teachers.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Kaskulu will generate income for both the teachers and developers through subscriptions and third-party monetization.

> Technical Feasibility

The application is to be developed as a cross platform application available as a web app, mobile app and desktop app. The mobile and desktop application are to developed with flutter as we are familiar with dart and have been using since 2020 for mobile developed while the backend (REST API) will be implemented in python (FastAPI) with a frontend being built in Javascript (SvelteJS).

> Anticipated Social Impact

At present, the mobile application that provide learning materials for student either do not get the users engaged or are limited to certain platforms. 
We intend on building Kaskulu as an edutaining, user-friendly platform for both scholars and instructors.
In addition, involved of the Examinations Council of Zambia is part of the project roadmap, thereby ensuring quality education is delivered through the platform.
