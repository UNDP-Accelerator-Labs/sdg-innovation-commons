A sensor controlled hand washing station which prevents the spread of covid-19 and is user friendly for disabled people. Soap and water is dispensed when a hand is detected.Â  A hand drier is available to dry the users hands. It also has a voice alert function that asks people to wash their hands to prevent the spread of covid-19. The station also dispenses hand sanitizer.
