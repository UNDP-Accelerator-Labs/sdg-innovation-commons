> Description of the solution

The users of the trade portal are farmers, agricultural producers, large clusters, agro-logistics centers, processors, small and large retail chains. "Marketplace" is especially relevant for residents of settlements with few financial services and low-mobility categories of agricultural producers. In the future, this platform will be used to conclude export contracts and develop agricultural exports.
