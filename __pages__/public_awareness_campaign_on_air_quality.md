> Description of the solution

Educational campaigns designed to raise public awareness about air quality issues, their health impacts, and potential solutions.

> Socio-economic effects

Increased public understanding of air quality, behavior changes that improve air quality (e.g., reduced pollution), potential economic benefits from cleaner air
