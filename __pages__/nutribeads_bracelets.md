> Description

Nutribeads Bracelet: Is an educational tool that is used to make mothers aware about the nutritional requirements of the children in the first two years of life. The beads contains a set of colorful beads with number which indicate age interval of the child and each color of the beads represent specific type of food that needs to be incuded in the child's meal. This innovation is a life hack for mothers in rural areas of Nepal who dont have sufficient access to information and education on child nutrition.
