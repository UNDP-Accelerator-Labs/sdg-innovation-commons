Description: 
The online platform that connects people with specific needs with volunteers through a sharing economy. With a view to the integration of people with specific needs into society, and equal opportunities, professional integration and mainly connectivity.

Origin of the solution:  
Algeria Startup Challenge/Coronhackathon. A partnership between UNDP Accelerator Lab. and Cap CoWork Incubator.
