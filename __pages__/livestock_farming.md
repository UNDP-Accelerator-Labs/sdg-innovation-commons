> Title of the innovation

Livestock farming

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation is about livestock farming particularly goats and chickens. Buying, keeping and selling them at the ministry of agriculture and fisheries, Zambeef company and at Kasumba Lesa boarder. Every year the ministry of agriculture and fisheries buy livestock from livestock farmers which they later use to empower cooperatives country wide. Therefore it is a good innovation for Zambian Youths.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

My innovation will generate income by selling the livestock at a reasonable price at Kasumba Lesa boarder, the ministry of agriculture and fisheries, Zambeef company, as well as in our Zambian markets. 
   The innovation will increase food production and availability in the market, increase employment opportunities for Youths regardless of gender, hence reducing unemployment rates and poverty.

> Technical Feasibility

For this innovation programme to be executed there is a need for a small startup farm,at least 3 livestock shelters, grazing land and a reliable source of water.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Livestock farming will increase job opportunities for everyone regardless of gender and education background. 
    Livestock farming will reduce poverty by increasing food production and continuous supply of food in Zambian markets.
