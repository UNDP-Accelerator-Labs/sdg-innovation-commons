Transporte
Komunidade (TraKom)
is a local initiative which provides free transport for community in need of medical emergencies
support in rural areas of Suai (Covalima) Municipality. The initiative was part of USAID project activities in 2019 "Hakbit Servisu Saude Baziku" shown in the picture here a woman namely Camelia and her son who used TraKom service to go to the hospital when he could not breath. She admitted that this approach helps the community in rural areas. Many people are still living on the hills and difficult to reach clinics and hospitals by foot. Those who have vehicles are encouraged to help those with emergency needs to go to the hospitals immediately.
