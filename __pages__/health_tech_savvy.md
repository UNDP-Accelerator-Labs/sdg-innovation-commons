> Title of the innovation

Health-Tech Savvy

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation will help individuals positively cope with their stress by accessing emotional and mental health services at their convenience using either a mobile phone or computer

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

My innovation will make money when people download the app, request for mental health services and through adverts made on the application. It will help existing businesses by providing them a wider coverage for advertising their goods and services.
Our target market is children, men and women of all age groups. The prize will help us in setting up the software application and website.
The value addition is to help individuals easily acesss emotional and mental health services at on the- go.

> Technical Feasibility

This will be designed in such a way that it will be connected to Facebook, and enable individuals download it thereafter they will be connected to an individual who will guide them through a mental and emotional wellbeing conversation.
This will be done by first setting up a website then an app and link it to Facebook for easy accessibility

> Anticipated Social Impact

This software application will help people easily access mental health services by simply using their phone or laptop anytime.  This service will be a 24hr service and will help in reducing mental health related illnesses and deaths.
