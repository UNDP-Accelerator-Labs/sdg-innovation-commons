> Brief description of the innovation

our innovation is were we came together as youths and decided that we come up with a brick/block making site so that we can at least lessen the transportation  cost of our potential offtakers.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

it will generate for me and others after the bricks/blocks are sold to our potential offtakers , our target market are the farmers within our locality who have to travel distances to go buy bricks/blocks to aid their projects completions. if we get the challenge prize  it will boost the amount of human resource and  advanced machinery will used hence reduction of accidents.
