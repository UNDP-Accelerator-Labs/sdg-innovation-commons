> TITLE: Please provide a title for the solution you observed.

Shipping container transforming into small store

> DESCRIPTION: What did you observe, or what did people write or say?

In capital Bissau I observe recycling use of Shipping container to transforming into small store, for Olivio Costa it's opportunity to have a business without need to Built a store.
In this store you can:
1- buy food products
2- Transfers Money
3- pay electricity bills


> NEED: What need does this answer?

It responds to the need to construct and build a store and up serving population in more remote areas



> INSIGHTS: What lessons can be learned from this observation?

Recycling can be a good opportunity for economic growth
