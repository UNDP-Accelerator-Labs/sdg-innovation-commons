> Brief description of the innovation

This is an innovation aimed at enhancing the health sector in mental, social, sexual and reproductive health,  according to statistics of 2019 about 700,000 people died of suicide,  we have cases of high depression and stress especially in the covid-19 period,  people are so depressed but they can't find anyone to talk to, thus, starlight clinic will be providing, mental and social health counselling online, it will provide expert consultations in matters of sexual health like family planning and contraceptive. this will be done online at the convenience of the patient

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

it is a viable project because there are many young and old people that need counselling services but they can't access them , mental and social health have not been given the real attention in zambia, issues of early and unplanned pregnancies are emerging rapidly, starlight clinic will be able to bridge and enhance the lives of people.

> Technical Feasibility

The means of physician/counsellor interaction are very easy for both the members of staff and clients, many people today have access to the internet and in cases were the client needs physical meeting  they will be linked to the nearest health centre on the platform.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will help many people especially the youths, there are alot of cases of depression, suicidal thoughts, unplanned pregnancies, through starlight clinic people will be helped in a great way the social impact starlight clinic has is very huge.
