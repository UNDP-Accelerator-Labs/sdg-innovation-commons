> Title of the innovation

Intemba v2

> Brief description of the innovation

intemba v2 a localised ecommerce business that allows buying items from the black market electronically using a local terminal in a compound. a terminal is a fabricated shipping container fitted with a computer and a big monitor where custermers shouild see and choose item to buy. after choosing they pay throgh a mobile money payment gateway.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Explain how your innovation is designed and what prototype exists if any. What is the proposed implementation plan? 
This innovation is designed in such a way that a local terminal (shipping container) will be fitted with a computer installed with software that will be taking orders which should update the town terminal about the orders placed. The workers in town will order on behalf of the customer then a registered taxi driver operating in the route where the order was placed will be sent to deliver to a local terminal.

> Technical Feasibility

It is designed like an eCommerce business , except that this will be localized and specialize in black market products which can not be easily found in local compounds.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will create business for many marketeers and buying items at the black market will be modernized.
