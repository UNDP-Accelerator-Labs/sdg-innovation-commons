> Brief description of the innovation

It will support a stable supply to the lack of agricultural products in the region as well as providing jobs to local residents for employment in farming. Further it develops into one large living the perfect town by providing residential facilities, health and schools, also other welfare facilities for residents

> Anticipated Social Impact

The innovation is going to create about 6,000 (six thousand) employment opportunities directly and a population of about 30,000 is going to be directly or indirectly involved. The innovation will build permanent houses for it's employees. The economic effects will be more than $300,000,000. This will increase the tax revenue for the government. The complex will be a tourist attraction and activate the local economy.
The complex will be Eco friendly producing 4 crops in 1 year. Other farming products will include fish, fruits, poultry, livestock, etc and educating 200 youths in South Korea in different areas to run the complex
The complex will generate 5,000 KWh of electricity from solar energy for it's energy needs and the surrounding areas.
