Grain aggregation and input financing solution.

> Qualitative Results of the Innovation Solution 

1.ECA facilitates access to credit and financial services to smallholder farmers, allowing them to invest in their farms and adopt new technologies. 
2. ECA established market linkages for farmers, connecting them with local and regional markets, thereby enabling them to sell their produce at better prices and increase their profitability.
3. Promoting sustainable agricultural practices in the region. To educate farmers on best practices in soil conservation, water management, and pest and disease control. helping farmers adopt sustainable farming practices, leading to improved soil fertility and reduced environmental degradation
