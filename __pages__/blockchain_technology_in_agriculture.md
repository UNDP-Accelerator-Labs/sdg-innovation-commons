> Description of the solution

It is the use of advanced blockchain technology in agribusiness to enhance traceability, transparency, and efficiency across the entire agricultural supply chain. It enables sustainable business practices, reduces waste, and facilitates consumer purchasing decisions and smooth transactions. Blockchain in agriculture addresses key challenges by streamlining data handling for informed decisions and better collaboration, empowering smallholders with direct market access and fair prices, ensuring food safety and quality via data recording and rapid response, and enabling supply chain transparency and traceability for consumers to track product details.
