Communication technology that uses ultrasoni sound to enable any device with a microphone or speaker to exchange data.

Sound-based payments are not only a transaction between two smartphones. In reality, our technology can sound-enable a variety of everyday devices(ATMs, Parking Meter, Public Transport Terminals) enabling more use cases for digital payments.

Stage: Validating:  Iterating and testing for a validated solution to demonstrate initial user growth and revenue.
