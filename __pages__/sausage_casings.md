> Title of the innovation

Sausage casings

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation is based on agriculture and health. Keeping pigs which can improve food production in the country,the pig intestines will be used to make sausage casings targeting small butchery owners furthermore, the intestine products can be used to the health sector for the production of drugs such as liquid paracetamol for kids. The pig waste can be used for making fertilizer.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This will help in food production such as meat,it will grow the agriculture sector with enough fertilizer farmers will be able to grow enough products.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed in a simple way which uses human labour, spoons, water salt and a table are some of the equipments used in this design.buying a casing machine can help with the production of casings at a larger scale and also it will be a quick and fast way of supplying to customers on time.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Employment to the youths,it will be able to help young people out there to work and feed their families unlike them spending time drinking alcohol.it will also improve agriculture sector in terms of food production and also the health sector.
