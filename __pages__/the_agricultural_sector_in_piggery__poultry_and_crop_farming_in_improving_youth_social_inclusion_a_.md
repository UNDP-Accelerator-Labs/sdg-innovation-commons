> Title of the innovation

The agricultural sector in piggery, poultry and crop farming,in improving youth social inclusion and economic opportunities in the agriculture sector.

> Brief description of the innovation

Palamambwe farm was inaugurated twenty two years ago in mutenda area along mutenda swamp in lufwanyama district.the main products are maize, groundnuts,beans and soya beans,we also produce chicken village and goats.the challenge we have is the selling of our products because of the swamp as most of the customers come from the other side of the swamp hence the products must be ferried from the farm to reach out customers coming from Chingola and muchishi area.this was causing sometimes our products like fresh maize to lose its test which falsed us to make sure we take our products to Chingola and that was adding some more expenses and causing our business to be very slow in selling. Our Main desire is to reduce an employment among the youths and the general public at large and promote the youth to be preoccupied with something duty so that they can not be busy to involve themselves in crime activities like theft,beer drinking, smoking and so many vices in the community.
