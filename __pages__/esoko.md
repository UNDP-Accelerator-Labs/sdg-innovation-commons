> Description of the solution

Esoko is an agricultural technology company thta provides tools and services to hep smallholder farmers connect to markets and access information. It provides up-to-date information on market prices for crops, and also gives access to accurate weather forecasts to help farmers plan their harvesting nd planting schedules. Esoko also provides expert advice on best practices for farming, including crop management, pest control and soil health. Through Esoko, farmers can receive important updates and alerts via SMS, voice messages or interactive voice response (IVR) systems.
