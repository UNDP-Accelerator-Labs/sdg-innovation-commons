> Affected Sector

Education

> Description of the solution

Supplying citizens with testing kits to learn more about the air quality in their local areas. The kits include a monitoring tube and an easy-to-follow guide, so concerned citizens can get accurate answers about the air they breathe.

> Socio-economic effects

By educating people on the importance of clean air, what they can do to lower their emissions, and how to be aware of the air quality in their area, the pollution problem can be better addressed.
