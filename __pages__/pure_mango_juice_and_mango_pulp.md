> Brief description of the innovation

Zambezi pure Mango Juice is an innovation by Ammy Heinstain Investments.

Our vision is to create a market for local fruits to reduce on waste and create a value chain for many communities in our country.

We have a vision to scale production with a niché approach of sticking to 100 % pure juice and Mango pulp.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Zambezi pure Mango Juice will benefit communities and enhance the quality of life for many people as it will create a value chain.

The value addition to the readily available fruit will offer employment to many women and youths.

Or market segmentation and positioning is well thought out. 

The demand in many chain stores, local supermarkets and households gives the product a fair share on the market.

The product will also help existing wholesalers and retailers as we scale our production.

The mango fruit waste is degradable there for making it an organic waste that can be processed into organic fertilizer .

We are hopeful the challenge price will help us aquire the equipment we need to scale production .
