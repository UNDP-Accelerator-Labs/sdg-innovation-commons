> Title of the innovation

Goat farming

> Brief description of the innovation

This is the rearing of goat either for food or for sale and use their skin to make bags and shoes.Goats are kept in the shelter and feed from there and the shelter need to be cleaned.Goats are sociable animals to people.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Goats rearing can generate income by selling meat to the restaurant holders and skins to the companies which make shoes and bags and my target market are restaurant holders and the environment is beautified as goats walk around it.They can also provide manure to the garden Farmers.

> Technical Feasibility

You make a shelter using trees or metals if available and a wire fance around the shelter for the goats to move around but not going very far from the shelter.you make a water pool where goats can drink water from and create enough space where they can feed from and rest.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Goat rearing is beneficial to the community as it provides meat and skin to make drums and shoes where everyone can be entertained by drums and ware quality shoes.
