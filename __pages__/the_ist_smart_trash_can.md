In prototype, we present to you the intelligent trash can designed by the students of the IST of Mamou. Beyond the normal function of trash which is used to collect garbage, our students have just set up a formula that allows to aggregate the automation of opening, closing, transmission of alert sms with automation of the extinction made available against probable fires.
