> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem & solution description)

Profile Innovator, Mr. Parvez Ahmad Ahanger is from the Baramula district of Jammu & Kashmir. He works as a blacksmith. Purpose Mr. Parvez has developed a portable cooking stove that can be used for domestic purposes and can be carried during picnic and camps. Technical details This stove is made of square-shaped iron sheets. One inlet is provided to feed the charcoal inside the stove over the iron mesh and an outlet is provided to collect the ashes. An inlet cave is also provided at one side of the stove for ventilation that helps in the proper burning of the charcoal. Benefits This challah is portable and can be used as a room heater during winters. Firewood can also be used as fuel. Suggested Reading(s):   Barbieri, J., Riva, F., & Colombo, E. (2017).
