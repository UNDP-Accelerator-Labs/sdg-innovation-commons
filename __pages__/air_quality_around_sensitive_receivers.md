> Description of the solution

The Netherland's Sensitive Receivers Decree (Besluit gevoelige bestemmingen (luchtkwaliteitseisen)) prohibits buildings designated as sensitive receivers from being built near provincial roads and motorways. Sensitive receivers include schools for primary and secondary education, buildings for childcare, nursing homes, and elderly care. Furthermore, such buildings can only be built or expanded if the limit values for air quality are not exceeded.

> Socio-economic effects

Avoided health impacts on vulnerable groups.
