> Title of the innovation

Cattle Rearing Project

> Brief description of the innovation

Cattle rearing Project shall comprise Beef and Milk production. Our animal shall be feed on natural grass, rice hasks, maize bran and molasses.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Our income shall come from sale of Beef, milk and hides. Our business shall assist other business like Restaurants, Butcheries,super markets by supplying them with beef and milk. our target market include Zambeef, parmallat,local butcheries and super markets  in Mongu and other surrounding towns. Our innovation shall help improve soil fertility and carbon capture and sequestration.

> Technical Feasibility

We have the land start our project on. Our project shall comprise a Solar borehole, 10 heifers, 10 steers, fencing wire, feeding and watering troughs, Calving shelter, spray race, light truck, milk cans. We intend to start our project with the seed money from the challenge prize.

> Anticipated Social Impact

We shall create permanent employment to two (2) people and 4 indirect employment.  We shall increase beef and milk output on the current market
