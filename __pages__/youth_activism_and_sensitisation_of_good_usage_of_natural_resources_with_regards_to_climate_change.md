> Title of the innovation

Youth activism and sensitisation of good usage of natural resources with regards to climate change

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation is a field related program mainly carried out with a view of finding out how people are using the natural resources.
Educating and sensitising people on climate change.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will equip farmers and marketeers with the necessary information on when different types of farm products are to be grown and expected on the market.

> Technical Feasibility

> Anticipated Social Impact
