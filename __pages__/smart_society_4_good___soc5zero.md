"SmartSociety4Good" is a digital platform that aims to provide an environment conducive to cooperation and coordination in terms of creating social impact and brings together all relevant actors (institutions, non-governmental organizations, social entrepreneurs and individuals). This platform, which combines the crowdfunding system with the corporate social responsibility framework, provides institutions with social responsibility projects. It also provides transparent and reliable support to social entrepreneurs and charities in finding funds.
