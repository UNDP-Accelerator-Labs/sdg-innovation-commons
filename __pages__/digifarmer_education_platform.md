> Description of the solution

This is an online educational platform that focuses on developing the digital skills of farmers. It aims to help farmers adapt to the digital age and keep up with technological advancements by providing them with the necessary knowledge and skills related to using digital tools and technologies. The platform is likely to offer courses, training materials, and resources to enhance farmers' digital capabilities, such as using smartphones, computers, and other digital devices for various agricultural and business-related activities. It is part of a project funded by the Erasmus+ KA2 program for adult education, which emphasizes strategic partnerships in the development of educational initiatives.
