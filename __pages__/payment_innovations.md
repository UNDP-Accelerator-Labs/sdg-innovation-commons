> Qualitative Results of the Innovation

1. Increased financial inclusion: Flutterwave payment infrastructure increased financial inclusion by enabling people who were previously excluded from the financial system to participate in global commerce.
2. Reduced transaction costs: Flutterwave has reduced the cost of transactions, particularly for small businesses. 
3. Improved security: Flutterwave's payment infrastructure is secure and reliable when making and receiving payments. 
4. Enhanced customer experience: Flutterwave seamless payment experience offers a range of payment options, including credit cards, mobile money, and bank transfers.

> Quantitative Result of the Innovation

 1. According to a report by TechCrunch, Flutterwave processed over $1.2 billion in transactions in 2020, up from $221 million in 2019.
