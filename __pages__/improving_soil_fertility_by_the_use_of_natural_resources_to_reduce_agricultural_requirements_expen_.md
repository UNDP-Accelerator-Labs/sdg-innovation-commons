> Title of the innovation

Improving soil fertility by the use of natural resources to reduce agricultural requirements expenditure.

> Brief description of the innovation

This is the method of the manufacture of  organic manure instead of using artificial fertilizer. We make this manure using tree leaves and grass. We first dig a pit of about 1.5 metres according to the size of your field and then we put a layer of some leaves in the pit and a layer of some soil on top, then there comes a layer of some grass and then soil on top of the grass. And we put another layer of leaves and soil on top of a layer of leaves and another layer of grass and add soil on top. After all this, what follows now is watering everyday to make the grass and those branches to decompose easily. We do this for 3-4 months for the organic fertilizer to be ready for use. After this method of reducing farmer's expenditure then we have also to take a step of teaching farmers about crop rotation.
