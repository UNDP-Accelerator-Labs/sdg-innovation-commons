3 Link Communication uses broadband satellite technology manufactured by Boeing and launched by SpaceX. The internet connectivity service is powered by a generation geostationary satellite that operates in the Ka-band frequency spectrum. It has a wide coverage that extends to over 25 countries reaching many islands, mountainous and rural regions. Coverage is everywhere in Vanuatu...
