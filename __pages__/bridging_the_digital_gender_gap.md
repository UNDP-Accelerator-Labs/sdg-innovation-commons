In February 2021, Microsoft Airband partner New Sun Road (NSR) began implementing a gender-focused connectivity program in Alta Verapaz, Guatemala. To address this lack of connectivity, NSR is using a USAID grant under the Microsoft Airband partnership to build ten Stellar Ixq-Saq'e Digital Community Centers in ten non-electrified rural communities in the region.
