> Brief description about the identified local solution:

ITLive is a yearly congress gathers all the IT related heroes in multiple fields, it consists of presentations, panel discussions, training sessions and success stories. They have a wide group of followers through their Facebook pages and group more than 100K. one of the most important events that are make the youth wait it. Also, it encourages youth to start their own IT projects in order to participate in this event. This initiative encourages the volunteerism among youth since each year more than 300 volunteers which make them gain experience and relationships. Beside that, the volunteers and the organizers are using this platform and support the Iraqi universities to be a technology based and building the students and graduates capacities in the field of IT to open new market for them and to build their career.
