> Title of the innovation

Package of Chicken Manure for vegetable and fruit gardening. Vegetable and fruit gardening is a complex process whose possible outcome are typically uncertain. By identifying innovative ways to increase and improve production, this reduces dependency on external inputs and improves the quality of life for gardening as well as help with climate change especially on seasonal crops. The use of chicken manure will help to grow healthy vegetables and fruits as they provide a slow release source of micronutrients and acts as soil amendments.

> Brief description of the innovation

Better Choices Gardens wishes to package Chicken Manure for easy access for vegetable and fruit growers. The use of chicken manure is ideal because it posses compact structure and is convenient and cheaper to use. The reason for packaging is to encourage small scale vegetable and fruit growers to use this organic compound known to provide nutrients for crop growth and improve the soil. It also reduces the production cost because it will be easy to access.chicken manure for vegetable garden fertilizing will produce excellent soil for vegetables to grow in because it is very high in nitrogen and also contains a good amount of potassium and phosphorus.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Better Choices Gardens will be specialized in packaging the chicken manure into afford kilograms starting from 5kg, 10, and 25kgs respectively. This innovation will generate income from the sales of this product. It will improve the operations of existing businesses as the other alternative from the fertilizer being used to grow vegetables and fruits for it will be cheaper and easily accessible to the farmers. Our target market are individual vegetable growers for home consumption, small scale vegetable gardens as well as commercial growers this can be delivered or be purchased from Agro-shops. Our objective is to create a product based company whose goal is to exceed customer expectations and increase the efficiency of our production by 10% a year as well as develop a sustainable farm inorder to survive off its own cash flow. Chicken Manure as an innovation alternative will add value to the environment because it produces excellent soil for vegetables and fruits allowing crop rotation and no harm to the soil.

> Technical Feasibility

Through a feasibility study, vegetables and fruits have become expensive to the consumer because of a number of factors such as high prices of seed, chemicals for treatment and aswel as the fertilizer used to grow these crops. We would like to bring a cheaper product on the market.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This will create jobs from the company and will also improve the quality of vegetables and fruits that will be grown locally by the farmers and us. This innovation is relevant as most people grow vegetables in their backyard and also for the small scale farmers to reduce on their production cost
