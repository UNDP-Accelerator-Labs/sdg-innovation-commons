The Iraqi community lacks awareness in terms of climate change. We have seen a well-noticed ignorance of the environment from the individuals in the local community and the stakeholders. Not only that, but there is no specific space that is interested in educating individuals about the importance of protecting the environment. As a result, the Iraqi citizens are negatively affected by the current climate change crisis. For that, O2IRAQ is a platform that focuses fundamentally on climate change in IRAQ. For now we call it the next LinkedIn for climate. It is a new space for everyone to discuss environmental issues happening in our region and a space to build and connect the ecosystem related to climate and environment. Nevertheless, it is a space to record the movements and the efforts in this field, gamified system, and the destination for the investors and donors in this field. O2IRAQ aims to build an educated audience about the significance the climate change and its impacts on human-being life.
