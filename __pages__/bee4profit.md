> Title of the innovation

Bee4Profit

> Brief description of the innovation

Bee4Profit is a community development initiative empowering rural based bee farmers with the needed resources to practice sustainable bee farming, to increase incomes, reduce dependence on natural resources and improve biodiversity in forest environment. We aim to set up an outgrower scheme in mbala district northern province with a provision of 1000 low cost concrete beehives with an average production of 50kg to 75kg per hive producing organic certified honey for the local and export market . The farmers will have hands training in bee hive management and comb honey harvesting . Our model will incorporate 70% of hands on learning experience.20% learning from others and 10% guided training experience.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The project will engage the rural farmers in entrepreneurship, operations and management training.Addressing poverty, particularly in rural areas, is one of the main challenges facing the Zambian government. Currently rural headcount stands at about 77 percent(CSO, 2016), with women making up 64 percent of the rural population and approximately 80percent of food producers. The poverty levels in rural setups have led to over 50 percent of the youth aged 20-24 year migrating from rural to urban areas in search for alternative non-farm jobs(Iapri,2019). Bee4profit recognizes that the most vulnerable population in rural areas is our women and youths. Beekeeping is not labor intensive and gender sensitive, making it suitable for women to get actively involved. This in turn increases income, the well-being of the family as well as the self-esteem of women and youths.
