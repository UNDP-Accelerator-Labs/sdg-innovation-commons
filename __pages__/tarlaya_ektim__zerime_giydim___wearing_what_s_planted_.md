The solution has been developed within the scope of responsible production and consumption relationship for effective and efficient use of resources. The aim of the project is to produce various textile products by obtaining yarn from the corn plant, which is an ancient agricultural product and a food source for many living things. Thus, by improving the usage area of the corn plant, which is widely used as table and animal feed, ecological and sustainable production will be supported.
