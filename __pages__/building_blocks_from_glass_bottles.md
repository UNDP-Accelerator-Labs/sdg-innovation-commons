Domingos da Silva began an initiative to collect glass bottles which are causing harm to the enviroment and physical harm to citizens. Using his knowledge, he has began to garner support from volunteers to gather bottles ready for his collection. Going through a process to building these blocks and selling these, more resistant blocks.
