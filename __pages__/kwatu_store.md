> Brief description of the innovation

Kwatu Store is an online Retail platform that allows customers to buy goods and earn commission for every purchase. Its comes with social features such as Messaging, Stories, Postings, Music, Videos, Documents etc. Each purchase saves you money with you can withdraw.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Kwatu Stores generates income through sales made on the platform and through advertisements to its customers/ users.
Kwatu Helps users to generate income for themselves by buying on the platform. each purchase earns them a commission.
We are targeting students and Youths between ages 16 - 35. as they are more likely to buy online than any other target group.
The Challenge prize will help us order more stock, get office space and improve the platform technically.
The innovation is environmentally friendly as most of the transaction happen online.

> Technical Feasibility

The Kwatu Store platform is currently a mobile application built with React Native. We plan to integrate Mobile Money payment system soon.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Kwatu Store will help its customers to save money for every purchase. Customers earn commission which they can withdraw after reaching a withdrawal threshold.
This will undoubtedly improve the lives of our customers
