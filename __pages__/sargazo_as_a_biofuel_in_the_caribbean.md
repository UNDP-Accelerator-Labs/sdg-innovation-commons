> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem & solution description)

Researchers of Mexico transform the sargazo in Mexican beaches in the Caribbean Sea to produce biofuel. The burning of this fuel would be used to heat water in order to obtain vapour for the hotel industry in the state of Quintana Roo.
