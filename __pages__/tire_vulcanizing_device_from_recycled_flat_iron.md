> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

They used an electric powered iron for more precise melting of vulcanizing patches. It  also reduces the time needed to melt and bond the vulcanizing patches to the interior of the wheel.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

Affordable vulcanizing device

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

Vulcanizing shop owners

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

Utilizes old flat iron modified for melting and bonding vulcanizing patches.

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

Solution can be enhanced and redesigned to come up with a cheap alternative to current vulcanizing machines
