> Description of the solution

An European Union (EU) funded programme implemented by UNCDF in partnership with the ASEAN Secretariat in Jakarta, Indonesia. It  promotes sustainable urbanization in ASEAN by investing in green and smart city solutions.

> Socio-economic effects

Reduce the environmental and carbon footprints of selected ASEAN cities through green and smart solutions, promote energy efficiency and renewable energy and reduced air pollution, and contribute to the development of a circular economy.
