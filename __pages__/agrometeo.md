> Description of the solution

The Agrometeo system is a weather and disease forecasting tool that serves the regions of Kochani, Strumica, and Gradsko in North Macedonia. It provides real-time information on weather conditions, issues alerts for the development of specific crop diseases and pests, and offers basic data on several prevalent plant diseases.
