> Type of the Solution

Infrastructural

> Description of the solution

It produces as much renewable energy at it consumes annually thereby eliminating its dependency on conventional energy systems. These buildings are characterised by reduced energy requirements and use of renewable energy systems to meet the reduced needs.

> Who lead the solution
