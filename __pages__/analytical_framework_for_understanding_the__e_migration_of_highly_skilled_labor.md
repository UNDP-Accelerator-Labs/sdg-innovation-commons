Analytical framework for understanding the (e)migration of highly skilled labor is an analytical framework and methodology for working on the unified data for target groups of organizations, researchers and other individuals interested in the depopulation process using as data sources social networks, traditional institutions data and surveys and all in order to better understand the emigration of the population, primarily the dynamics, volume and causes of emigration of highly skilled labor.
