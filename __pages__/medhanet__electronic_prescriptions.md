MedhanET: Electronic prescriptions 

> Solution description

●App/online platform streamlines
access to prescription Medicines from across medicine distributors and
pharmacies,  
●It includes prescription
follow-up/reminder (e.g. for diabetics patients), home delivery service, free
medication review and overall health tracking/monitoring system 
●Also provides a mechanism to give
check up reminders so patients visit their doctors
