> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

This is about being resilient during onset of outbreak like African Swine Fever (ASF).

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

How to be resilient during the ASF outbreak

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

For pork retailers and wholesalers in the market

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

The strategies to beat effects of African Swine Fever (ASF) to the pork vendor was remarkable. Theresa Blanco resorts to the following strategies to compensate income losses due to ASF. These include: business diversification, reducing input cost (labor) and sticking to policies or laws related to meet quality output.

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

Other pork vendor may benchmark on the coping mechanisms and strategies and also government support.
