> Description of the solution

Farrelly Mitchell believe in the transformative impact that smallholders and SMEs can have on the food supply chain and the wider agrifood industry.  Their consultants have extensive experience nurturing the growth and development of smallholder farmers and SMEs. They work alongside governments, NGOs, and private investors to introduce modern technologies, promote sustainable practices, and connect small-scale businesses with global markets.
