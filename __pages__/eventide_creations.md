> Title of the innovation

Eventide Creations

> Brief description of the innovation

A startup company in the business of events planning, decorations and coordination to ensure that our clients know only the best will be delivered at whatever event they are having.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation will create income through client bookings and confirmation of events.
It will improve operations of other businesses when we engage other small companies to hire a number of equipment for use at a clients event.
The challenge price will help the business grow because is will be invested in purchasing goods solely for use in the business to reduce extra hiring costs.
Generally, this innovation will create an opportunity for school leavers as well as university students to earn an income.

> Technical Feasibility

Due to the fact that we are a startup company, our short-term plan is to begin with small events such as picnics and birthday parties then progress to weddings and corporate events. The reason being that we are trying to get known in this industry so starting with big events will prove as a challenge because of competition. Once our customer base starts to grow, then we will begin employing more people to assist as well as start accomodating bigger events.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation will benefit others because it will create jobs for a number of people and ultimately improve their living standards because they will be able to earn an income. Which will be a boost on the economy and the youths employed in this innovation will become self-reliant.
