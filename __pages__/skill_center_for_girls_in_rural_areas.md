> Title of the innovation

Skill center for girls in rural areas

> Brief description of the innovation

The levels of unwanted pregnancies and early marriages are common in rural areas, this is due to alot of extra time after school. This is where we come in with a center that allows for young girls to be kept busy with constructive skills that help them not only sustain themselves but also occupied with useful things. These will be sewing, cooking to mention but a few.
