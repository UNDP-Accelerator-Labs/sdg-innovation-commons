> Description of the solution

A platform for Agricultural Risk Management (PARM)'s activities in Ethiopia which is organized by International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD), aiming to enhance the country's agricultural risk management. It includes knowledge sharing and learning platforms: the use of blended (physical and virtual) learning cycles and workshops for capacity development and knowledge sharing. It has brought multiple benefits to Ethiopia, such as strengthening agricultural risk resilience, aiding knowledge sharing and capacity building and facilitating policy integration and practice
