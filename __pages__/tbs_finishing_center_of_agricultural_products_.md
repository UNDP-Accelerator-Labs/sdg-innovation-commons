> Title of the innovation

TBS FINISHING CENTER OF AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS.

> Brief description of the innovation

TBS FINISHING CENTER FOR AGRICULTURAL PRODUCTS is a center which is aimed at providing convinient and affordable finishing services to communities for a wide range of agricultural produces.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Finishing services are to be provided for an affordable price.The Provision fees from customers shall in turn be used in operations of business activities and as a reward for interprenueral resource.the provision of secondary industry will be forcing the primary industry to expand and increase production.Further,communities shall enjoy lower prices for agricultural raw materials as a result of increased production prompted by the availability of secondary industry.moreover,this innovation shall absorb unemployed youths who will be required to assist in operations.targeted off takers are farmers and the general community.Other businesses may be improved through social interaction with stakeholders of the business.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed in such a way that it should have the production section, processing department and the marketing department.The production section shall be responsible for producing required raw materials.The processing department shall contain required machines to turn raw materials into finished products.for instance if soya-beans is produced from the production section,the processing department should have machines to turn the soya-beans into its finished products such as cooking oil and other food stuffs.The marketing department shall be responsible for selling and buying of finished products and raw materials respectively at affordable prices.The design is so wonderful such that business activities can operate smoothly.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation has positive social impacts on the society and can improve the lives of others through the Provision of required finishing services to agricultural raw materials.The innovation can bring employment to the local unemployed residents in societies thereby exposing them to incomes which inturn may be facilitating their expenditure against basic and other needs.The proposed innovation is also relevant to the current market in order to improve efficiency and effectiveness.Through research,it has been found that people do not operate at full capacity due to lack of facilities like the proposed innovation.Hence,there is need to establish this innovation in societies so that people can afford these services.This can reduce dependency on imports for finished products thereby supporting and improving the local industry which inturn shall contribute to the growth of both the GDP and GNP at large.
