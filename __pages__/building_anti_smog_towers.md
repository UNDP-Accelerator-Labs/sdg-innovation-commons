Infrastructure

Infrastracture construction

> Description of the solution

Buildings with the purpose of removing pollution particles by sucking in polluted air at the top and releasing clean air at the bottom. It can reduce PM2.5 (fine suspended particles) concentrations by between 10 and 19 per cent over an area of 10 square kilometres.

> Socio-economic effects

Improve air quality
