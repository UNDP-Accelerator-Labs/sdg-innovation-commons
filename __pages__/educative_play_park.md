> Title of the innovation

Educative play park

> Brief description of the innovation

This will incorporate basic education like reading and writing, survival skills, as well as day care for children. Over time the plan is to help even women discover their talent and help start up and build up on it. To provide social and education activities for youths and children, encourage sport and talent... Eventually grow and spread to other parts of Zambia.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

It will create employment opportunities for the trainers and staff, as well as keep children out of the streets at a very low charge. Give everyone child an even opportunity to learn the basics skills needed to survive and in the process discover and develop their talents and or passion and potentially build an income out of it. Through sport and talent we shall create employment opportunities for the youth and women.

> Technical Feasibility

For a start, to find a place that can accommodate at least 50 people per session and put in place the basics needed to startup. While In the process advertise and encourage citizens to enroll. Inspire volunteers to help the team.
