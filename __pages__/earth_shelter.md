> Description of the solution

Building structures partially or completely underground to take advantage of the earth's natural insulation and stable temperatures. Earth shelter is also called an earth house, earth bermed house, or underground house, is a structure usually a house with earth (soil) against the walls, on the roof, or that is entirely buried underground.
