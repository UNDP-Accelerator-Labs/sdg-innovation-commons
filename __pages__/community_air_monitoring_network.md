> Type of the Solution

Citizen Science &amp; Data Collection

> Affected Sector

Environment, Public Health

> Description of the solution

A network of low-cost air quality sensors deployed by community members to collect and share real-time air quality data. Programs may involve citizen science training, data analysis tools, and collaboration with environmental agencies.

> Socio-economic effects

Increased public awareness of air quality issues, potential for data-driven advocacy, citizen empowerment
