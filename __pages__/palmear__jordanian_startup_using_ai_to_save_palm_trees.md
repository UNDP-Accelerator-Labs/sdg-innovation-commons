## Solution holder: Zeid Sinokrot 

Palmear combines big data, artificial intelligence, and audio engineering to solve a major challenge affecting farmers globally through the early detection of pests.

Our platform enables an Integrated Pest Management approach in monitoring and controlling pest and disease on a farm and country levels.
