> Description of the solution

Roco is a Roving Personal Comfort Device, which provides ultimate personal thermal management for people in inadequately or in un-conditioned environments. It provides 12% to 30% energy savings compared with typical conditioned building systems, and better thermal and mobile comfort to occupants.
