> Organization 

Uneako

> Description of innovation

Uneako, an eco brand , with the moto of "Sharing Sustainable Happiness" is striving to create an aura of cautioness for the problems of environmental destruction and climate change and  by introducing an exclusive and innovative range of ecofriendly product solutions. They offer a range of sustainable products in different domains like utility, gifting, visiting cards, stationary items etc. Alongside they are also trying to support the marginalized sections of our society like the village women, artisans and craftsmen, especially abled people, and also jail inmates under trial by employing their skill sets in the production and value addition of their products.
