> Title of the innovation

Kwacha Rooms

> Brief description of the innovation

Kwacha Rooms aims to create a platform that offers last minute booking low cost budget  accommodation to short stay travellers. The platforms aims to help hotel/owners to occupy empty rooms and connect travellers to hotels that offers the best deals.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Kwacha Rooms will generate income by creating an application and.website that will  charge a small monthly subscription fee to hotel owners that lists its services on its platforms, it will help improve operations of existing business by offering them an excellent innovative platform that connects hotel owners to its clients at just a click of a button. Potential clients are hotel and lodge owners and the prize money will help me to build the platform and also to market the platform to clients through various social  media platforms.

> Technical Feasibility

The platform will build a brand around its name Kwacha Rooms which will be the number one top search for anyone planning to travel to any part of Zambia,. It will create demand for accommodation seekers looking to save by staying at places that doesn't break their bank accounts. By setting itself as an industry best, kwacha rooms will make use of all social platforms to build its brand and attract clients.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Besides helping business owners accumulate an income on its empty rooms, it will help accommodation seekers save income and find targeted places at just a click of a button without wasting loads of time searching different platforms. The proposed innovation is relevant for the current tourism market as more people are slowly starting to recover from the pandemic and Kwacha Rooms will be able to offer them a platform to book accommodation without having to worry about their budget.
