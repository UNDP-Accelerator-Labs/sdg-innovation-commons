> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem & solution description)

To reduce the amount of wood used for cooking in large institutions such as schools and neighborhood care points. Although this solution was found being implemented by the above institutions, it was first pioneered in the country by some of REASWA's member organisations, including New Dawn Engineering.
