> Title of the innovation

Long life education policy that meets the needs of our younger generation.  The current education policies are donor orriented and they don't answer our questions to Foster innovations that limits the gap of unemployment in the country.

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation points at creating the education system that provides skills to the learners at an early stage putting in consideration the demands of different localities. Today's education system is the colonial system that do not demands the changes in the technological world.upon entering school learners needs to be introduced to different skills that needs to Foster development in their localities and be assessed in order to group them in areas of their stength so that as they are trained they will go straight to offer expertism in the skills they have been trained into to contribute positively to the development of our country. The skills may be in the areas of agriculture, tourism and mining as these are the critical  areas our country can benefit from.
