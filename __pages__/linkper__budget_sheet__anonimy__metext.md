> Brief description of the innovation

Linkper, Lets users keep URLs for sharing with others. Budget Sheet helps users make home budget and check prices from known stores. Anonimy lets users express themselves anonimously. metext lets users chat with disappearing chats.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The Innovation has four ideas but put together.  (1)..Business owners can post their products on Budget Sheet for customers to see prices. This will help customers to have an informed decision before they even go to the store. (2). Students and Researchers can share internet based information with friends on Linkper. This will help improve the quality of Learning.  (3). On anonimy People can contribute a solution to society without being afraid of anyone pointing at them. (4). on metext, users chat and chats get deleted just after logging out. This promotes privacy and helps people to freely discusss topics with others without being afraid of anyone looking into their conversation later. 

Income can be generated by running Advertisements on the Web Apps.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed to be running on web servers so that users can access them from anywhere at any time. There is also need to keep maintaining the source codes on Computers for system Improvements.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will help in product show casing for business owners. People can contribute solutions to topics openly and freely. Students and Lecturers can share knowledge with other students.
