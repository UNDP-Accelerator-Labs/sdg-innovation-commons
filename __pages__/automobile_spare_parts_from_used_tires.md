> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

Production of different automobile spare parts from used tire.  

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

Automobile parts are expensive and not easily available in Davao City, hence, this stimulated the innovator to fabricate automobile parts out of used tires. 

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

His clients are vehicle drivers or owners who value practicality.

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

You can utilize the available waste to provide a cheaper product substitute, you just have to be resourceful.

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

The practice of the innovator can be emulated or improved on a more commercial scale. More materials out of used tires or other wastes can still be produced if proper training is extended.
