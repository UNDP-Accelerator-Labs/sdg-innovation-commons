Surviving skills club

> Brief description of the innovation

The  In school  club whose purpose  is to equip leaners with different  surviving  skills so as they leave school, they become responsible  and independent citizens, with interpreneurship skills.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Having understood that the club deals with many skills people survive on, like making smart modern doormats artistic crafts gardening etc. The programs will help both community and learners to be product and self reliance.

The innovation is designed  to help the community and as trained learners leave schools they are encouraged to come up with groups in community and Robby for fund from the government. This will provide employment opportunities especially  to the physically and hearing impaired .

It will help a lot of youths because they will be self reliance and they will be productive  using the available materials. This will also promote farming in different  communities of our mother Zambia because young legends will be equipped with practical skills on different farming methods and crops easy to grow and earn income out of it.
