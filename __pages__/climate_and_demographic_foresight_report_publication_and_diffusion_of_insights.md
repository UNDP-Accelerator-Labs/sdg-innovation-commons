## Learnings on your challenge
****

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights)Â 

- Foresight can be done with breadth (geographical and type) of stakeholders to provide more complete scenarios or future implications

- It will take extra effort to mainstream foresight by talking to marginalized groups like indigenous people, but efforts in validating using national language and come down to their settlement have grounded the findings of the foresight report

- Due to huge volume of input from stakeholders, and amount of policies to be stress-tested the length of the report tripled - prompting dilemma of choosing which information to be included or excluded

- Doing joint-publication due to lack of capacity in the CO pose risks of delayed publication. CO should seriously consider increasing the technical expertise of own staff to carry similar projects in the future. Although there are huge merits partnering with local organizations, such as the ease of doing logistics, some progress were beyond UNDP's control
