This DIY motor-cycle used oil stove was created to reuse used oiled motor-cycle from motor cycle workshops nearby. the person (not to name the inventor) who created this stove said that it save the hours to make fries ready for breakfast and can be used to boil water in few minutes compare to normal stove. it also help them not to use kerosene and reuse tools that that can be reused such as air compressor.
