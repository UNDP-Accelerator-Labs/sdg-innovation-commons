> Title of the innovation

Digital Skills For Young Innovators Replica Facility

> Brief description of the innovation

The facility that facilitate the diffusion and emboding of digital skills  in young Innovators using digital garage system.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

By creation of innovation digital contents that can be  accessed on online platforms  thus increasing young Innovators  ability 
to optimize their innovations using innovative digital skills.

> Technical Feasibility

It's  designed in form of an online course in all Zambian languages  and composed of 26 modules. It is planned to engage young innovator in the the following ares:
1 social Entrepreneurship
2 Arts industry
3 Education
4 small scale manufacturering
5 Agriculture etc

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will close the digital divide especially among the low status  digital users such rural based young Innovators and it will also  reduce poverty by generating jobs, care and   income for upcoming and existing young innovators.This facility is  most needed in this era of digital revolution .
