## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top 5 key insights you generated about your frontier challenge during this Action Learning Plan?

External partners may change their priorities which affects the implementation of the project. It is vital to keep the balance between connecting with interested and supportive government partners and performing work independently so that you are not completely dependent on your partner's decisions.

> Did you experience any barriers or bottlenecks when impacting the system, working on your frontier challenge respectively?

The main barrier was that the challenge was linked directly to the activities of the Ministry, which decided to postpone the project. It was not possible to carry out the project independently, so we had to put it on hold for an indefinite period and move on to other projects.
