> Brief description about the identified local solution:

Code Lab is a local community led by D3 Startup training people in the coding and development realm, their main focus is mobile development, backed development and web development.
They are creating a friendly environment for learning using peer to peer or Youth to Youth. After completing the course, the students are required to present a graduation project using the programming languages that they learned. in addition to programing they teach them the principles of design thinking in order for them to come up with a human centered graduation project. Some of their activities are for women only.
