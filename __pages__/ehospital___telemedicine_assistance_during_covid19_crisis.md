eHospital is an AI powered telemedicine platform created by Intellysis and PUCMM university to offer free consultation for people in need of medical care, who were not able to obtain it due to the impact of the COVID19 crisis on the health system. The platform offers both mental and physical health consultations. The solution can be accessed at www.ehospital.do
