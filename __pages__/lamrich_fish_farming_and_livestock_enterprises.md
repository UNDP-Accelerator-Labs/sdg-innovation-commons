> Title of the innovation

Lamrich fish farming and livestock enterprises

> Brief description of the innovation

Lamrich fish farming and livestock started as the individual operation by the leadership of Lameck Kabwe and letter partner with Richard Samangwe the best friend to Lameck Kabwe and this business located in Mwense District Luapula Province in Mwense Constituency, Mwense ward. Our aim is to create the good environment and create job opportunity and empower our fellow youths in the community with knowledge in farming and livestock skill to be self sustainable and liable and take farming as the business to change the life and change the living.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We have the great potential in the generation of income though our self contribution and well wishes such as NGO, GRZ support and empowerments funds... Market is suitable for fish we targeted restaurants, schools, individuals as well as international market like congo and other places... We are luck that  we have good road network and good network for communication, this will help and contribute to the reduction of food prices on the market and national, 

Marketing we will conducting packaging and door to door delivery and take the opportunity of social media marketing by presenting our products on social media.

We have been in existence for two years in fish farming business we started with one pond now we have five and the money we generated had helped Us to start pig farming and chicken as the fish and livestock are daily consumption.

> Technical Feasibility

We Lamrich fish farming and livestock enterprises we are located in Mwense District Luapula Province and we are currently implements five fish ponds, and two pigs as well as vegetable production... We set the structure on starting from Manager, we deployed some one who did fish farming, and we also deployed someone with the knowledge of vegetables farming is managing the produce... We aim to open branches where we be selling our products such as pig meat, fish, vegetables and other food staff..
