In cooperation with the UNDP IPSD Project, AccLab set up experiment of finding local solutions in rural areas of the two pilot regions in Uzbekistan. In partnership AccLab aims to test out approach of collecting local grassroot solutions/innovations at the public service agency in rural areas. Two consultants are supporting rural populations in formulating innovative ideas, search innovative solutions among different groups of people – youth, local leaders, farmers, and etc for further possible test out within the AccLab team.
