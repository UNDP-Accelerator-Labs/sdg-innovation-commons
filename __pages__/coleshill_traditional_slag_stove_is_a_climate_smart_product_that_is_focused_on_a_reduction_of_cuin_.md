> Innovation Solution Description 

ColesHill Traditional Slag Stove is a climate smart product that is focused on a reduction of cuing down of trees (deforestation) by using Copper Slag (a by-product of copper extraction by smelting) as the heat source, thereby contributing to climate action and an action to land pollution caused by industrial wastes. The innovator is a beneficiary of the Experimentation program with support of USD10,000 for material procurement, techncial support from the Copperbelt Univiersity and training in business development. More on the innovation on the following Sites: https://youtu.be/Ed2YD8o1QSM; https://web.facebook.com/search/top?q=coleshill%20zambia
