> Title of the innovation

Prison Rehabilitation Welfare trust

> Brief description of the innovation

The prison rehabilitation welfare trust was established in 2011 with the aim of helping prison, victimized, widows, orphans and old aged and also to fight child abuse, drugs and crimes and well as to sensitize the community on HIV/AIDS and other related illness.
