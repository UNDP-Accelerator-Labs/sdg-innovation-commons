> Title of the innovation

Simple water purifier ( SWP) this is a simple machine that is hand Crafted it's going to be used to to purify water and improve the water  and sanitary sector  it's going to Carter for the rural areas so as to improve the livelihood .

> Brief description of the innovation

My machine was made out a plastic bottle and some straws and some cloths which act as the Main water purifiers and so water containing dirty particles is poured in bottle and the water moves down the system and when it reaches the last phase it is purified for drinking and the water (H20) becomes useful  for the community and this machine can contribute to the economy of our nation in which machines like it and more improved can be sold to other countries with issues in the water and sanitary sector e.g Zimbabwe and it can also be a project to provide jobs for the youths on a small scale base.
