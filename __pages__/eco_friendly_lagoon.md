> Description of the solution

The Eco-friendly Lagoon was created by Crystal Lagoons in South Florida. The seven-acre lagoon uses 50% less water than that needed by a park of the same size, 100 times less chemicals than swimming pools and 50 times less energy than conventional filtration systems. Applying safe additives and different ultrasonic waves in the water, which causes the contaminating particles to agglomerate into larger particles later removed from the lagoon.
