> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

The device is a portable lathe machine that is primarily used to make billiard cue sticks but has been used to create small wooden furniture pieces as well.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

Alternative, portable and affordable machining devices for use by carpenters and craftspeople.

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

The solution is for carpenters and craftspeople who work with wood, mostly in machining cylindrical surfaces.

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

This is an alternative way to machine wooden cylindrical surfaces that can be sold as a component to furniture and general carpentry.

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

The device has the potential to be redesigned and reproduced to make it portable, accessible and affordable to carpenters.
