> Title of the innovation

Life In Diversified and Motivated Agriculture (LIDMA) Grow a LIMA and save a LIMA.. A farmer should be Motivated and empowered at small scale level as to be able to grow what they can consume at domestic level and sale the othe lima at and acceptable market value

> Brief description of the innovation

The Life in Diversified and Motivated Agriculture (LIDMA ) innovation is an initiative of empowering small scale farmers at chiefdom level. Grow a LIMA and save a LIMA. When a small farmer is motivated and empowered to diversify and produce enough for domestic consumption and the other LIMA for sale to Government at an acceptable market value.  This initiative can be effectively be implemented through chiefdoms. A chiefdom has land, water and people, that is where it should start from. Help the chiefdom develop their own strategic plan and Foster their own development. A chiefdom with a strategic plan has a leadership structure within their Kuta which can Foster agricultural policies
