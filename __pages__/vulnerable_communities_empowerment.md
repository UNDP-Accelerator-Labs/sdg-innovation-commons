> Brief description of the innovation

I work with a team in the missions of identifying vulnerable families then mobilise support and empowerment for them. My works are on christian basis. We impower the poor with a variety of projects beneficial for them.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Engage youths in construction skills like building and painting piece works.

> Technical Feasibility

Fundraising projects such as purchasing and selling of Kapenta and rice to generate income to empower the vulnerable communities. And doing construction piece works to help young people raise money for school and business capital

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will benefit the poor especially the old people in supporting them with basic needs and empowering the youths with skills, start up capital for business, and support for school. This includes the widows.
