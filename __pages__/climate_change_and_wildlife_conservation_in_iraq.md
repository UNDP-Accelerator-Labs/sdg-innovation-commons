> Brief description about the identified local solution:

Non Governmental Organization, managed by Iraqi ecologists professionals and wildlife experts, works on climate change, biodiversity, wild life conservation, and ecotourism in Iraq. This community driven initiative highlight the local stories that related to climate and environment and transfer the message to Decision Makers through storytelling and in localized way. Besides, they localize the global researches about climate and environment to fit the Iraqi situation. Finally, they highlight the climate and environments effects on our lives through interactive videos and from day to day situations, (they increase the Iraqi's awareness with localized materials)
