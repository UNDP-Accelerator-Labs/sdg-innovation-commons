> Title of the innovation

value addition-cooking oil processing at medium scale level  to reduce poverty and unemployment

> Brief description of the innovation

the innovation shall involve cooking oil processing and  sales basing on value addition to take advantage of produces like Soyabeans, groundnuts and sunflower which are locally grown. Soyabeans in particular has turned to be major cash crop. here is a business continuity plan to improve productivity and build resilience in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic  stapes: 
1, identify key products and services
 2  establish the objectives of the BCP
 3. evaluate the portantial impact  of disruptions to the enterprise and workers 
4. list actions protect the business and minimize risks to people, processes, profit and partnership.
5. establish contact lists
6. maintain, review and continous improvement strategies.
