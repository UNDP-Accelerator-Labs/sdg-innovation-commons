> Description of the solution

The festival aims to promote and develop renewable energy projects independently of state support in urban areas and beyond. It focuses on sharing successful, economically viable projects in solar, geothermal, and biomass energy. The event encourages entrepreneurs and activists to explore sustainable energy innovations and offers discussions on practical applications and cooperation without government involvement.

> Socio-economic effects

Promotes economic opportunities in renewable energy, reduces reliance on traditional energy sources, and fosters local innovation, initiatives coming from private and non-profic sectors minimizing reliance on the government funding.
