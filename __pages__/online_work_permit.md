ONonline is an online cross platform that provides online documentations for new, renewal or replacement of work permit where foreign nationals can easily request for a work permit wherever they are” Key features  · Online documentations for new, renewal, clearance/ return and replacement of work permit card. · Time and energy saving · Digital data base rather than paper based one · Can accept digital payment systems as well as local bank transfers · Physical Contact free documentation · Better accuracy and security
