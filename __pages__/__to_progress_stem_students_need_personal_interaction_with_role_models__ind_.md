## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top 5 key insights you generated about your frontier challenge during this Action Learning Plan?

- To progress STEM students need personal interaction with role models, individual approach 
- While advocating for women in STEM, need to include men into the dialogue
- Quotas for women in STEM could be applied, but there should be development work behind
- We have to be careful with positive discrimination issues,

> Did you experience any barriers or bottlenecks when impacting the system, working on your frontier challenge respectively?

Cultural barriers. People generally have very negative experience with so-called "feminist topics" and were very negative about it in the beginning. We shifted the focus by engaging more men into conversation.
