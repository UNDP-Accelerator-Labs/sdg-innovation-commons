> Title of the innovation

CREATING THE FUTURE WITH THE PAST AND THE PRESENT . A COUNTRY THAT IS SET TO TAKE OFF FROM THE GUTTERS OF IRRESPONSIBILITY AND LUCK OF VISION HAS THE ABILITY TO CHANGE IT'S PARADIGM WITH THE OLD ,THE HOMELESS AND IT'S PRIVATE SECTOR WITH INCLUSIVENESS. OLD AGE HOMES , HOMELESSNESS SKILL TRAINING

> Brief description of the innovation

A COUNTRY'S IDEA OF DEVELOPMENT STABILITY IS BASED ON ITS INNOVATION ABILITIES, BUT THIS SHOULD EXTEND TO THE FACT THAT SOCIAL WELFARE  CAN PROVE TO BE A VIABLE COMPONENT OF ANY GROWING SOCIETY, WITH THE GROWING TREND IN POVERTY ALLEVIATION PROGRAMS BASED ON THE PRESIDENT'S OVERVIEW OF A COUNTRY'S PARADIGM IN THIS ERA, MY VIEW OF A BETTER ZAMBIA IS TO STRENGTHEN THE SOCIAL WELFARE SYSTEM LIKE SUPPORTING OLD AGE HOMES, HOMELESSNESS INCLUSIVENESS WHICH INVOLVES THE PRIVATE SECTOR LIKE ME WITH THE IDEA AND A PIECE OF LAND THAT CAN BE DEVELOPED INTO A MORDEN STRUCTURE  FOR SKILL SET. IN THIS CASE THE DISABLED CAN BE ASSISTED, THE OLD CAN HAVE A PEACE OF MIND, THE YOUNG MEN AND WOMEN WILL HAVE HOPE GOING INTO THE FUTURE, THE STREETS WILL BE FULL OF HOPEFUL PEOPLE. THE GOVERNMENT IN ITS EFFORT TO REDUCE POVERTY WILL BE SUPPORTED BY WORKABLE STRUCTURES THAT ALREADY EXIST IN THE CULTURAL SECTOR OF OUR GOVERNANCE SYSTEM

> Technical Feasibility

THE FIRST THING THAT NEEDS TO WORK OUT IS TO HAVE A BUILDING READY SINCE A SMALL PIECE OF LAND EXISTS.  THE PIECE OF LAND IS IN THE OUTSKIRTS OF TOWN, ACTUALLY, THAT IS WHY IT IS SIGNIFICANTLY A CONVENIENT AREA TO MAKE USE OF. THE 2 HECTARES PIECE OF LAND IS NEAR ELECTRIC CABLES FOR ZESCO. THE BUILDING SHOULD HAVE A CLEAN TOILET AND BATHROOM. THE CLINIC IS ALSO ALREADY NEARBY. THE PLACE SHOULD HAVE RELATIVES AND FRIENDS VISITING EVERY WEEKEND OR ONCE IN A WHILE TO CATCH UP.  THIS IDEA IS A REINVENTION OF HOW SOCIAL WELFARE IS SUPPOSED TO BE, A WAY THAT WILL MAKE PARTNERS AND RELATIVES EXCITED AND REALLY BEGIN TO FEEL ALIVE AGAIN, WE ARE GOING TO HAVE TO MAKE THE GOVERNMENT ENTHUSIASTIC TOO AND BE ABLE TO HELP OUT.  I PERSONALLY FEEL THAT WITH A CARING GOVERNMENT IN PLACE IDEAS LIKE THIS ONE CAN LIVE. WE CAN LOBBY IN ORDER TO EMPLOY SO AS TO BE ABLE TO PAY SOME PEOPLE  AS WELL AS PROVIDING TRANSPORT THAT CAN STAND DUE TO THE GRAVEL ROADS ESPECIALLY IN RAIN SEASON. WE WILL HAVE TO HAVE CLEAN WATER AND PROVIDE LEADERSHIP BECAUSE IT'S NOT EASY DEALING WITH PEOPLE.
