> Description of the solution

Smart Paddock is a GPS technology used to track cattle. The Pulse Dashboard translates data captured by Smart Paddock products into actionable insights about your animals, assets and farm. From there, one can send alerts to their team, update statuses and attend to any urgent tasks.
