> Description of the solution

The course "Digital Agricultural Technologies" offered by the World Bank Group's Open Learning Campus provides an in-depth exploration of how digital technologies can revolutionize agriculture. It delves into the concept of Digital Agricultural Technologies (DATs), their potential benefits, and real-world applications. The course highlights the role of data platforms, including open data platforms, in agricultural decision-making. Additionally, it sheds light on emerging technologies like AI, remote sensing, crowdsourcing, and big data analytics, and their transformative impact on the agricultural sector.
