NoWAIT: NoWAIT is the third place winner of the UNDP Barbados and Eastern Caribbean Accelerator Lab #IslandHack Hackathon challenge. NoWAIT is an app that attempts to provide a solution to long lines at various establishments following COVID-19. By reducing and controlling queues, safer physical distancing measures can be observed as well as improved, safe access to goods and services.
