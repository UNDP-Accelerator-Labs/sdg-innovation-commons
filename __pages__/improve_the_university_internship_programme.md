## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights) 

Internship program should be regularly modified to ensure that it could achieve intended outcome and that students are equipment with labor market's skills demand. Collaboration with private sectors should be increased to ensure that they contribute to building the potential workforce and that internship program outcome goes in alignment with market demand. Co-create is an important aspect for problem solving and project sustainability and this project is no exception.  

> Considering the outcomes of this learning challenge, which of the following best describe the handover process? (Please select all that apply)

> Can you provide more detail on your handover process?

Materials and knowledge under this project have been transfer to the faculty. The lesson learnt, key successes and challenges from this experimentation has been discussed and shared amongst UNDP and the faculty who may then utilize the learning to further improve the internship program at the faculty and beyond.
