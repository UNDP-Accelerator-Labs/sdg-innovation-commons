> Description of the solution

Airly addresses air pollution through a network of sensors providing real-time air quality data. This initiative empowers individuals, communities, and policymakers with actionable insights to combat pollution and enhance public health.

> Socio-economic effects

Improves public health awareness, supports informed decision-making for cleaner air, and promotes environmental sustainability
