> Description of the solution

The concept of climate budgets focuses on integrating climate action into the financial planning of cities. By allocating specific portions of the budget to combat climate change, cities can systematically address environmental targets in line with their economic strategies.

> Socio-economic effects

Promotes sustainable development through strategic financial planning, potentially reducing long-term climate-related costs and fostering economic stability.
