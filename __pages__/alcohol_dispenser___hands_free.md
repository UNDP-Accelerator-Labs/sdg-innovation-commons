> TITLE: Please provide a title for the solution you observed.

Hands free alcohol dispenser

> DESCRIPTION: What did you observe, or what did people write or say?

This type of solution has been implemented by local businesses and local markets mainly, figuring out, how to provide touchless alcohol (gel) due to Covid 19. Many have come up with this handmade mechanism, as a pedal you mush press with your foot, to obtain place alcohol in a touchless way in your hands.
