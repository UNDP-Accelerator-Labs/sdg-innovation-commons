## Food Safety and Quality traceability

Decapolis is a Jordan based leader in Food Safety and Quality traceability.Decapolis provides a platform; Decapolis Food Guard (DGF), for ensuringthe safety and quality offood worldwide.The mission ofDecapolis is to help the food producer to provide premium goods for which the end-to-end supply and production chains comply with strict safety and quality control standards.
