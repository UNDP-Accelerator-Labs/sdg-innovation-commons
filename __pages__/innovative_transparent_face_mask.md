The proposed Project will aim to prevent the transmission of covild 19 virus (i) envisages the establishment of a plant for the production of  Transparent mask with a capacity of 25,000 pieces per annum (iii) The 
basic a im of these transparent masks is to allow deaf and hearing 


impaired people to read the lips of someone speaking to them
(iv) Pilot scale plant with community-based efforts in to useful transparent mask.  
The aim of this work is to develop an eco-design actions guide that supports the design of dedicated transparent masks
