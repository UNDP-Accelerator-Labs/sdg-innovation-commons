> Title of the innovation

LINE AGE GROUP OF COMPANIES

> Brief description of the innovation

The CEO of Line Age Electronic Gadgets store is Arnold Kalepa, it was registered      under PACRA in 2019 Electronics and creative development thereby. And received mentorship and seed funding from the Tony Elumelu Foundation ,we provide a one stop shop for quality electronic gadgets.
 The business is currently running  and intends to provide its customer with an A plus store and later an online local mobile shopping application.
The business objectives over the next 3 years is to establish ourselves as a fast, easy delivery services to our customers, individuals to use our products to meet their needs. We aim to grow the business to an advanced Electronic gadget store that will be on top of the needs on a wider note in the Zambian market in 3 years. Line Age aims to break-even in 18 months from launch through the sales spread across the identified revenue streams .Our Electronic gadgets will mainly come from United Kingdom, USA and China as these are the leading producers of good quality electronic gadgets and also we have direct contact with suppliers, line age will order in bulk at cheaper prices and the goods will be shipped to Zambia.

> Technical Feasibility

Operations Plan          
The demand for electronic gadgets are high so operations will be throughout the year. Our operation program cycle around bulk order from UK, USA or China depending on the exchange rates and 10 days wait for shipment to arrive, clear shipping costs and Zambia Revenue Authority then to the point of sell. The license required for operation are the Patents and Companies Registration Agency (PACRA). For business name registration and the Zambia Revenue Authority Tax certificate 
Key resources  
For the business to have a legal status on the market, the business has a business registration certificate in order to function as a commercial entity by law. In addition the business also registered for tax purposes.  
The key resources of our operations is the Management Team, workforce team, Sales and marketing personnel transport and premises. 
•	For the Management Team, we have a team of diverse backgrounds involving Accounts, Sales and marketing and Information Technology among others 
•	For the workforce team, we are empowering the unemployed men, youth and women from local communities
 
From the empowerment we are receiving ,we are looking at how best we can grow the company to multi-million dollar electronic gadgets store country wide.
