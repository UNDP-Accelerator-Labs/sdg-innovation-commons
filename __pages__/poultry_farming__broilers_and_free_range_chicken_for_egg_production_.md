> Brief description of the innovation

Dealing in poultry rearing by being a leader in poultry growers by the year 2025. This will be by diversifying into egg production and increasing production broilers broilers to 1,000 birds every six weeks. Once we are funded, we intend to begin with 500 day old chicks of rose broilers that will be graded according to weight  (1.2kg-  2kg) and according to customers preferences;  packaged, labelled, priced and sold to the potential buyers.
