> Title of the innovation

Rearing village chicken and making village chicken sausage

> Brief description of the innovation

Rearing village chicken which is gaining more demand on the market for it's chemical free meat.And to add value to this ,I thought of bringing to the market a village chicken sausage which I believe will have more demand on the market .

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

It will generate more income for me and others involved in the production , for it will bring more profit and the market is available locally and internationally with minimal advertisement.winning the challenge money will help me and my team to aquire needed machines for making sausages.And it will enable us to purchase a vehicle for transportation of our products to our would be customers.

> Technical Feasibility

We are planning to be rearing more village chicken and buying from our neighbors as it is our raw material,and be able to produce 50000 pieces of sausages per day.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will create employment to the locals and provide easy market for village chicken farmers in our community.there is high demand for village chicken so rearing more village chicken and introducing village chicken sausage on the market will help meet the demand for the village chicken meat on both local and on international market.
