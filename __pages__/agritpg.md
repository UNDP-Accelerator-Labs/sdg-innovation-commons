## AgriTPG of Topogis.

Providing plantation statistics using satellite and drone imagery. They are launched in 2019 with a group of young Angolans. Their solution tries to close the knowledge gap. Challenges that they are facing during implementation were: Understanding the market and user needs, Procurement of technology vendors, Data collection issues.
