> Description of the solution

Urban Innovative Actions is an initiative of the European Union that provides urban areas throughout Europe with resources to test new and unproven solutions to address urban challenges. The UIA funds innovative projects that can demonstrate a positive impact on urban development, focusing on themes such as sustainable urban mobility, energy transition, and urban poverty.

> Socio-economic effects

Enhances urban living conditions, promotes social inclusion, reduces environmental impact, stimulates economic growth, and fosters innovation in urban areas.
