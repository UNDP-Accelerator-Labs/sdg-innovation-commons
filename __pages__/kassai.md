## The "Kassai" platform is an online application where health technicians can receive training, resilience courses, publish work experiences in the fight against malaria and interact with other professionals across the country.

Self paced platform, where healthcare professionals can take courses in their own time. All contents are approved by the Angolan Ministry of Health.
