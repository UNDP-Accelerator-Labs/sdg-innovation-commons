> Title of the innovation

THE USE OF TECHNOLOGY TO ADDRESS HEALTH EMERGENCY AND RESPONSE IN ZAMBIA

> Brief description of the innovation

The inmovation is the application of the technology to address health emergency and responding to patients needing urgent assistance. The technology is the mobile app that i have called 'Mylife App' which  will function as a response App and can be on mobile phones, computers and ipads. Lusaka and most parts of Zambia has challenges  responding to emergence and urgent health situtaions as there are no ambulances in most clinics and hospitals, and many people die on the way to the hospital and there has been an increase in Brought In Dead (BID) cases as most people run of options to rescue their beloved ones. Hand held mobile phones has become nornal and at least with a presence level in huge numbers. The Mylife App will just be a matter of pressing a button and receive immediate intervention from health practitioners, clinics and hospitals. The Mylife App will be equipped with names of clinics, hospitals, ambulance providers, doctors, and all connected to the communication response centre where information will be proceased cordinated within few minutes.
