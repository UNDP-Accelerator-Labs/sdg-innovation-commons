> Description of the solution

FarmERP is an agritech platform that offers end-to-end farm management solutions designed to improve the efficiency and productivity of agricultural operations. The platform integrates various features that support farm activities, including crop planning, pest and disease management, weather analysis, inventory and financial management, and sustainability reporting.
