> Title of the innovation

Women doing poutry for village chickens and Gardening

> Brief description of the innovation

Our plan is to empower women to do poutry for  village chickens and gardening  in chibombo and be able to find them customers for them in LUSAKA town if possible even others surrounding districts of LUSAKA.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Village chickens especially are on demand on the market,by empowering women with these skills we are going to create employment and this will help reduce poverty levels at house hold level and community level.our target group are marketeers,supermarkets, restaurants and individuals.

> Technical Feasibility

We plan to work with women in rural areas of chibombo at community level,headmens and other gate keepers,our work plan we intend to start implementation in January 2021.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Will create employment and empowerment among women,and if women are empowered financially it will reduce poverty levels at house hold level.
