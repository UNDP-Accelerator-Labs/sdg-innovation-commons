While conducting an ethnographic study on flood management across seven cities, UNDP Indonesia Accelerator Lab team identified several products sold by local entrepreneurs. One of the highlights was a female entrepreneur who processes plastic waste into prayer mats and carpets. MSMEs in Indonesia have increasingly integrated waste-generated and environmentally-friendly products such as this, embracing the notion of circular economy.
