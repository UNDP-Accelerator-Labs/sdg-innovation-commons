> Description

We are an organized group of women and men, where we work together to strengthen our union, and in an organized way obtain greater benefits. We are more than 70 associated families belonging to the Mbocaya Poty-Ayolas Company, where we are dedicated to agricultural production and small animals for self-consumption and sale of farm products.
