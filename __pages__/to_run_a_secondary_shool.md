> Title of the innovation

TO RUN A SECONDARY SHOOL

> Brief description of the innovation

A secondary school consisting of pre, primary and senior education levels would mean inclusive for a wide range of learners' needs and the community. It will Carter for community development as it will call for employment of staff.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation will generate my income as it will become my work place and others will benefit as teachers, general workers, health workers and many more others through engagement.Existing businesses will benefit too, like markets, where school material will be bought from, carpenters,etc who will be supplying the school with a lot of commodities.
The target group is the community around,and all provinces around Zambia as the school will provide accomodations to all of it's clients.
Value addition will be witnessed through quality provision of education which will cause many people to bring in their children for learning, nevertheless, sensitisation programs to the community will be carried out on different areas of concern to the public. The school will also become the center for all the community around to use for different developmental meetings and seminars.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed to meet every individual's need, including the disabled.
This is to be implemented as early as possible, provided all necessities are made available.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Lives of others can be improved through this innovation because,many teachers can access employment, starting their own business through empowerment by the school, hence employing their own workers.
The proposed innovation is relevant to the current market because of the recent pandemic (CORONA VIRUS) which has affected the education of many people in the country, bearing in mind that education is currently spuring on the market.
