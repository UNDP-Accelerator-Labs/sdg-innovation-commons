## Turning biowaste into energy


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2023/blobs/solutions/05b28b710a4446894087614c57e87df9.jpg)


Pellet Mexico addresses the problems linked to reducing CO2 emissions, taking advantage of the energy potential of residual biomass and taking it as a substitute for fossil fuels.

Biomass is used for the renewable generation of thermal energy, also contributing to the proper management of forest, agricultural and organic waste.
