> Title of the innovation

E-consult

> Brief description of the innovation

This is an agribusiness consultation that allows people to access information and guidance virtually no matter where they are. The platform offers courses to members of society that have very little knowledge on how to conduct agribusiness

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

While the platform has some free resources, other resources such as courses and online consultation are charged and this makes it possible to make an income

> Technical Feasibility

The implementation plan includes the design of a user friendly website that makes it possible to access information on mobile phones. Mentorship, training and consultation can all be obtained online.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Agribusiness is an important sector that has the ability to provide employment to thousands of people in the country. The information provided will make it possible for people to have the information needed to execute agribusiness more effectively therefore leading to an improvement in their livelihoods. By introducing this product, a number of skilled expert will be employed to provide relevant information.  By creating employment,  this innovation will be making a direct social economic impact.
