> Title of the innovation

Explosive Arts Zambia

> Brief description of the innovation

Explosive Arts Zambia is a wholly Zambian owned company.
Our company business is focused on delivering professional products and services which include applied arts that range from visual arts, performing arts and literature. We are skilled in a wide range of artistic spheres to meet and exceed our clients ever growing requirements for all kinds of occasions and activities.
Explosive Arts Zambia plans on strategically selecting and bringing on board talented youths who are unemployed or run small scales artistic businesses in community and introduce them to a platform where their skills shall be sharped through industrial exposure and will enjoy monetary gain.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Our company is Business oriented and focused on income generation through advertisement of our products and services, sourcing and procuring of business contracts that evolve around our specialties. The income to be generated will not only benefit I as an innovator alone but also the various artists that will be brought on board and also be used to grow ourselves as a company.  
Our Services will also help already existing business in terms of beautifying their working environment and also help their operations in terms of supplying them with products they need. 
Our market target varies between our three departments of applied arts which Visual arts, Performing arts and Literature.
Visual arts:-there is a wide clientele target here that includes both Individual/Households clientele and corporate clientele.
Performing arts:-The clientele target varies from personal events to corporate events.
Literature:-The clientele target varies from personal events to corporate events.
The challenge prize will really go a very long way in helping us secure land for the company, get equipment that can be used to increase and secure our equipment and capacity hence increasing service delivery.

> Technical Feasibility

This innovation is dynamically designed into departmental operations sequence, where our operations differ with respect to our three departments which are Visual arts, Performing arts and Literature departments.  Our Visual arts departments offers products that are marketed and sold to clients from all walks of life. Our Performing arts department offers service to mostly corporate clients and lastly but not the least, our Literature department offers writing services that range from academic, business, corporate, aesthetic etc paper works and the like.
