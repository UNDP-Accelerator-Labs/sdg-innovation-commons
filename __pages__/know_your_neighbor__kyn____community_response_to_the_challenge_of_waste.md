The KYN initiative is being implemented in one of the high density areas of the city supported by the Lusaka City Council. It is a community-led model called that aims to strengthen the social fabric of the community to address issues development issues namely waste management, Gender Based Violence (GBV), substance abuse. 


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2020/blobs/solutions/13fb9fbf3f1fa6f1720f446082403d7b.jpg)


In response to calls from the local Council to promote Clean Green and Health communities, the community discussed testing incentives to separate waste at source with the aim of reducing the amount of waste that ended up in the landfill.
