Agripredict’s Information Communication Technology (ICT) application  addresses lack of access to timely information on farming conditions, weather, pests and their control measures. It's a mobile Application that gives timely information on farming conditions, weather, pests and provides agricultural advice to users. 

The App offers farmers an easy-to-use way to diagnose plant diseases, predict weather patterns, identify pests and provide advice on general agricultural related topics. It offers benefits of improved yields, easy to diagnose plant diseases at an early stage to help improve a farmer's planning for their crops
