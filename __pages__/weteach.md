Description: 
WeTeach is a start-up, created by students from the School of Higher Commercial Studies and the Higher School for Computer Science in 2019. WeTeach is an intermediation platform (teacher / student) which offers the possibility to connect and book WeTeachers of all specialties, at any time and according to the needs of the students.

Origin of the solution:  
Algeria Startup Challenge/Coronhackathon. A partnership between UNDP Accelerator Lab. and Cap CoWork Incubator.
