> Title of the innovation

COVID-19 Diagnosis Expert System

> Brief description of the innovation

The Innovation aims at alleviating the continuous shortages of COVID-19 test kits in hospitals through the use of a mobile based application to enable people conduct a self test. All one needs to have is just an Android device.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The Innovation will help the health facilities and the general population at large to save on money required to purchase the COVID-19 tests kits. It will be on sale and the license can be renewed each time there is a new COVID-19 varient.

> Technical Feasibility

Its Designed using Phython. The implementation will start with Kapasa Makasa University before being rolled out.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The Innovation is relevant in a sense that people can conduct a self test of COVID-19 whenever they suspect any symptoms. It provides quick remendies before one is presented to health workers for further diagnosis.
