> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

Mobile hot chocolate vendor where customers can buy cacao beans, and have them roasted and grounded. The vendor delivers the hot chocolate to his customers within the vicinity.  The vendor was able to manufacture the tablea or cocoa pellets themselves and made this ready to drink beverage for the customers.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

Accessible and mobile vending to busy market vendors.

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

The solution is for busy market vendors who have no time to go around and buy food in nearby shops or restaurants.

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

It was a good experience to see people doing diskarte for a living. Fresh and hot chocolates are availble at a cheaper cost. 

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

Once this person makes friends and customers, he may influence someone else to do the same. Mobile and delivery of fresh hot chocolate beverages.
