## Using clean energy for ice production and maintenance


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2023/blobs/solutions/8ba7d00a8aedd539cbda4bd3595571a9.png)


Solar Ice is an ice factory, whose main value offer is the commitment to the environment, focusing on the energy efficiency with which it operates its ice manufacturing plant.Located in the city of Chilpancingo, Solar Ice's manufacturing, storage and distribution plant is equipped with the latest in solar energy production technology, making it an energy self-sustaining factory.The water with which the ice is made is extracted from a spring, free of toxic agents, which is transported and treated under the highest quality standards in the industry.
