## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top 5 key insights you generated about your frontier challenge during this Action Learning Plan?

- Women entrepreneur's qualitative research showed that women regard their sense of financial literacy in book-keeping as "Basic"  - Programme-level training in a number of observed "entrepreneurship programs" is not consistent  - Programme-level training in a number of observed "entrepreneurship programs" is time-consuming and inaccessible to most women given the variable schedule of women  - current inflation crisis in Lebanon demands better book-keeping to keep up with fluctuating prices  - Getting into the habit of updating the books can give both clarity and confidence for women business owners
