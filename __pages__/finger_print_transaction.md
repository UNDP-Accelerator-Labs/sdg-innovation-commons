> Brief description of the innovation

Biometric payment is a device more like many money transaction machine out there, but with a finger print provision on it, that enables people to make payments on the go using their finger print and pin to complete verification process. With it's two steps verification process, makes it more secure, faster and easy to use for any service provider and buyers to make payments ,that is since everyone has a unique finger print and pin that will be required to complete verification and transact.
after a successive payment the account hold will be sent with a notification sms on their phone, making it even more secure and reliable
