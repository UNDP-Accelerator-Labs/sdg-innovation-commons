> Title of the innovation

Kanchele Fish Farming

> Brief description of the innovation

Kanchele youth group is among the beginners initiative group. For decades, our plans to farming fish is the main target to improve livelihoods and zero hunger through empowering youths, women and vulnerable children in our societies via education, technology, health care agricultural, aquaculture creative industry and housing.
Besides our organization formed by vulnerable students with the aim of helping 9ther vulnerable people in various communities across the country.

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

In the first place, this youth group is formed by the local members and loan repayment schedule will start soon after the business is established preferably in the 2023 January.
The accountant will take an active role of tracking Cash flow with a supervision other committee and the financial management.
Every quarter, there will a given report 8n financial services. Other projects will merge from the main operation of services such as gardening, poultry Farming and so on.

> Technical Feasibility

This innovation is designed to have 10 fish ponds and it's necessary equipment, infrastructures for storage, office room, guard room, fanced, cctv camera, electricity and transportation. The above mentioned sets of tools will enable the fishing project to running smoothly and earn its own income.
Kanchele Fish Farming will be managed by the board of directors as follows: project coordinator, finance, sales and marketing and it's committee members.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Local government will capitalize on the efforts and initiatives in this project to copy or extend the knowledge and skills to other indirect beneficialies and therefore rise economic of all the communities through increasing people's food, nutrition and income at household levels.
This will have positive results in many individuals to grow new business, going to school be competitive in market places.
Members within the project and their families will benefit as well through monthly payments
