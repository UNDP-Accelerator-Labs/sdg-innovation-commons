The application of fertilizer especially by subsistence farmers is a very long and tedious process with potential to cause long health ailments. The process also lacks standardization in the amounts of fertilizers each plant receives. To ease and increase efficiency in the application of

fertilizer by subsistence farmers, an applicator was designed to speed application of fertilizers in large fields and is easy to easy to carry since the fertilizer is carried in a correctly measured quantities in a chamber as one does not need to keep going to refill basins (which are commonly used) every now and then from a sack.
