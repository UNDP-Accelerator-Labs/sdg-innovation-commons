> Type of the Solution

Technological

> Affected Sector

Transport

> Description of the solution

The ANTISMOG project by Net Sas uses an innovative hydrogen-based technology to reduce emissions from combustion engines by up to 80%. This open-source technology enhances fuel combustion, leading to fewer emissions and improved engine efficiency.

> Socio-economic effects

Decreases vehicle emissions, enhances fuel efficiency, reduces maintenance costs.
