> Title of the innovation

Tikumva

> Brief description of the innovation

Tikumva is a Chewa word for we are listening, this is an online application that will be used to offer counseling services to abuse victims and offer them counsel, as well as police intervention if need be. The counselling sessions will be private and are aimed at protecting victims of abuse, equally an SMS platform on a toll-free number will be available in cases of those without access to a smart phone

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The business will generate income through a minimal registration fee to be paid by the phsycosocial counsellors and therapists attached as counsellors, carrying out workshops in educational institutions and training young pupils and students in counselling. Through the downloads of the application , conferences where the software will be promoted and running essay competition and other diaspora of art to expand the financial base of the company

> Technical Feasibility

There isn't a prototype of the software yet, however the proposed implementation plan is to promote the launch of the software at a conference addressing abuse and counselling , several prominent person's will be contacted to try and get the software promoted on various platforms including TV and radio

> Anticipated Social Impact

The software Tikumva will allow for persons who have been abused to open up to a licensed counsellor, and be offered help needed. As well as job promotion for counsellors at high school level, tertiary and those working as agents on the  ground 
Equally breaking the stigma and falsities attached to abuse
