Pakomak is a nonprofit company. Pakomak’s mission is to ensure responsible, efficient and most economically affordable management of customers packing waste in accordance with their legal obligations. They have placed collection points through out the city, as well as they make specific agreements with businesses, and other places that are frequented by people, and place containers that ease at selecting at source.
