> Title of the innovation

Helping Zambian people access quality health through a one to one stand with the community and the nation at large this will help us follow people than waiting for them to come

> Brief description of the innovation

My project is based on having a team of health workers willing to travel round the country to identify and deal with problems affecting the country. It helps us to be on the ground to see what areas us the health care providers need to take to the people, to achieve this it must be supported by having a chemist that will generate income which in turn will be used as means of moving from place to place, issues such as cervical cancer Tb and other serious sicknesses are a serious challenge to people in distant areas. But with this programme we wish to put that to a stop. A team of clinicians,nurses, ehc and others will be put together to meet the target of fighting disease
