Kyiv entrepreneur launched Christmas tree rental business. In order to minimize the risks for the tree, it can be rented for up to 3 weeks. The tree is provided in a box and the new owners may keep the tree and plant it in their gardens, or return it for a cashback (25-30% of the original value).
