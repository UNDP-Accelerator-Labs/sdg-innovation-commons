Tour and Travel in Uganda
Hapi Tours Uganda is perfectly placed to arrange your ideal Gorilla and Chimpanzee Tracking in Uganda as well as Wildlife Safaris. Our staff has worked and traveled extensively in the region commanding a wealth of experience in booking the right accommodation, road networks plus meals of all travelers in Uganda and East Africa In general.

Hapi Tours Uganda is an inbound and outbound Safari Agency based in Uganda with extensive experience in arranging both long and short, individual or group memorable Safaris to the unspoiled Ugandan wilderness and beyond.
