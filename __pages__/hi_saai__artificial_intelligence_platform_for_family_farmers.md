> Description of the solution

Hi Saii is a platform designed to help farmers by providing instant answers to their questions via WhatsApp. The platform uses a vast amount of data to provide comprehensive advice on various farming topics, including business planning, financial management, crop production, and more. Saai aims to make this technology accessible to farmers worldwide, particularly in developing countries. While the platform is still in its development phase, it has already proven to be a valuable tool for farmers.
