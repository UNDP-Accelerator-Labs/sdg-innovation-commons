> Description of the solution

AgriFin Accelerate is a US$25 million, six-year initiative funded by the Mastercard Foundation. It aims to help smallholder farmers in Kenya, Tanzania, and Zambia access financial and information services. The program works with farmers and various stakeholders to identify barriers to access and develop solutions to overcome them. It utilizes technology and mobile banking to improve farmers' access to financial products and services, ultimately increasing their productivity and income.
