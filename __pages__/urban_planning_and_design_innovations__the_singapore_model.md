> Other Notes

Singapore Universities 
Among the push towards more 'green' buildings and the implementation of the Singapore Green Plan 2030, this is a move towards science-based policies in reducing urban heat risks. Scientists in several universities in Singapore have been leading research into the Cooling Singapore Initiative since 2017.
