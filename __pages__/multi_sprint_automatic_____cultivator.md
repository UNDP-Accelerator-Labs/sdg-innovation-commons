> Description of innovation

Grassroot innovator Ghanshyam Jadhav has successfully developed a low cost and low maintenance multifunctional tillage equipment with mechanical sensor. 
He developed a Tractor  PTO operated multipurpose equipment having attachments like plough, disc harrow, weeder, grass cutter and furrow opener. The implement can be attached to any tractor of 22hp and above, and can cover an area of 1 acre in an hour.
