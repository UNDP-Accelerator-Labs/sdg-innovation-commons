> Innovation Solution Description

Digital Service to hire a tractor or other equipment inputs. 

> Qualitative Results of the Innovation Solution 

1. Increased Access to Tractor Services: Smallholder farmers access affordable and reliable tractor services.  Farmers can rent tractors on an hourly basis, enabling them to complete their farm work faster and more efficiently.
2. Improved Yield and Productivity: With access to tractors, farmers can cultivate their farms in a timely and efficient manner, resulting in higher yields and increased productivity.
3.  Reduced Labor Costs: By using tractors, farmers can save on labor costs, as tractors can do the work of several laborers in a shorter period of time. 
4. Enhanced Data Collection and Analysis: Hello Tractor provides real-time data on tractor usage, which can be used to track the performance of tractors and identify areas for improvement. This data can also be used to monitor the impact of tractor use on crop yields and productivity.
5.  Increased Adoption of Technology: By providing a user-friendly platform that connects farmers and tractor owners, Hello Tractor has demonstrated the benefits of using technology to improve farming practices, leading to increased adoption of other agricultural technologies.

> Quantitative Result of the Innovation

1. 250,000 smallholder farmers across Africa have access to tractors.
2. Increased crop yields of up to 200% for farmers using Hello Tractor
3. Reduction in labor costs by up to 40% for farmers using Hello Tractor.
4. Studies have shown that farmers who use Hello Tractor can increase their crop yields by up to 200%, leading to higher incomes and improved livelihoods.
