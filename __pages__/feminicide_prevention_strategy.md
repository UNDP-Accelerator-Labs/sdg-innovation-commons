> Description

During lockdown gender violence increased and women´s possibility to report became more chanllenging. This was a finalist in Women´s Hackathon during lockdown co-organized by Impaqto, EcuAcclab and other partners. The idea builds networks and tries prevent gender violence by sending discreet messages with help numbers in menstrual pads.Messages signals where local help is available.
