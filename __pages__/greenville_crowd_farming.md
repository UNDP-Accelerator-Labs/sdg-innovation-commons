> Title of the innovation

Greenville Crowd farming

> Brief description of the innovation

Greenville Crowd farming platform will connect small scale farmers that are mostly unbanked with sponsors who are excited about impacting the lives of the farmers, these sponsors will also earn returns at harvest when the food cycle is completed. In a nutshell, the platform will enable people get the opportunity to invest in agriculture without getting their hands dirty

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Some of the Off- takers are companies that commit to a contact to purchase the farm produce from sponsored farmers after harvest for example milling plants in the case of maize. They make suggestions to farmers and field experts on crops that are in demand on the market for each crop cycle. This will enable our platform to be able to determine potential returns on sponsors investment. Insurance companies will play a major role  because farming involves a significant risk; from unpredictable events like weather. The insurance  covers farm infrastructure and farmers sponsors capital.

> Technical Feasibility

The platform will allow sponsors to sign up and log in and check the farming initiatives, the number or size of the farm they wish to invest in. Sponsor is anyone with interest and means to invest an amount of money to support a farmer on the platform. These are generally individuals with strong interest in agriculture but lack the time and skill to enter into farming. To boost their confidence in the model, sponsors will receive bi- weekly updates about the farms progress including pictures and videos.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Due to challenges like climate change, lack of start up capital, poor pricing, pest infestation, and government Inaction, many individuals are opting out of agriculture business thereby posing human, food security and employment threats In the nation. Small scale farmers are mostly affected by these challenges as their livelyhoods largely depend on agriculture. These farmers constitute over 90% of players in the crop production segment of the industry. The platform will help curb these problems
