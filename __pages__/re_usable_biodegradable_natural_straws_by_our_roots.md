Ouroots is Partnering with the Earth. Ouroots grows 100% biodegradable & compostable plant based drinking straws. All natural, grown hand-crafted & sanitized in Uganda from the Luseke Grass:
a wild grass found in Africa, designed and crafted by nature , used by ancient Ugandan tribes as drinking straws since hundreds of years.
