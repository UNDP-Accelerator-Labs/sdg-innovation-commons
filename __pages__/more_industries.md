> Title of the innovation

More Industries

> Brief description of the innovation

It has been noted that Zambia does not produce many exports, which is one of the reasons for our economy's struggles. The government will spend more in industry machinery that will turn our raw materials into completed products, allowing the country to export finished and processed items, bringing in more revenue.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

If the government invests in purchasing machinery that assemble automobile parts, it will create jobs and encourage more people to buy cars because they will be purchased locally with our Kwacha currency, allowing the Kwacha to become more stable.
