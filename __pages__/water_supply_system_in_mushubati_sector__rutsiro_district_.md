Karambi village had water supply crisis and residents used to walk many miles to get water. A resident (Mr. KAGEMANA Naphtal) initiated water collection system using his own financial means amounting to 1,600,000 Frw /1,500USD. Almost 100 households benefit from this water supply system 24 hours, 7 days a week free of charge. For the sustainability of the initiative, the beneficiaries are planning to pay100 Frw per month to afford maintenance costs.
