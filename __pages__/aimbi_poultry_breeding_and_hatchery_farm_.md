> Title of the innovation

Aimbi poultry breeding and hatchery farm.

> Brief description of the innovation

Breeding stock for broilers and layers.
There will be pens for broiler parent stock and pens for layer parent stock.The other part will have incubators for hatching eggs.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

There are many poultry farmers in Luano and parts of Mkushi districts who have difficulties to get day old broiler chick.They currently get them through hatchery agents in Mkushi and have to wait long periods before their orders are honoured
Sale of day old chicks and excess hatching eggs bring income
Additional capital would help to convert broiler housing to breeding pens and get standby generator.

> Technical Feasibility

The plan is to start with young parent stock and grow them to maturity.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The youth will benefit from easy access to day old chicks.The deficit in broiler supply in the community will be addressed.Retaurant owners will benefit.
