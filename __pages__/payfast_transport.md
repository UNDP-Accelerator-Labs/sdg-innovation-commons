> Title of the innovation

PayFast Transport

> Brief description of the innovation

This is an annovation designed specifically to reduce congestion  in Intercity bus terminal due to covid and easy payment of bus/tax fee using a mobile app and  USSD code. Using fastPayTransport ,one is able to buy the ticket of the bus at the comfort of the home without been physically going to the station and know the seat number and where the seat is and when the bus will start off.
