> Title of the innovation

maize production as commercial farmer

> Brief description of the innovation

Subsistence farming in which we dearly all of the crops or livestock raised are used to maintain our farms families. And sometimes we have surplus for sale or trade. As a farmer wish to increase the produce into commercial farming so that we have manage to supply produce to large scale. Commercial farming is about growing of crops and the rearing of animals for raw materials, food, or export, particular for profitable reasons. Example of subsistence farming is where family grow crops of 1 to 5 hectares of maize and is for good consumptions. Commercial farming can be more from 10 hectares and above.  Mainly commercial produce is for selling within and exported outside the country. We use maize for food such as Animal food and human consumption. Other way is a large scale intended for widespread distribution to wholesalers or retail outlets. In commercial farming crops such as maize are harvested and sold in the world markets.
