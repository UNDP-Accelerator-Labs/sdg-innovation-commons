> Title of the innovation

Agrivest

> Brief description of the innovation

It is a platform that will let people invest into farming projects and they will get a certain  percentage of profit on the money they invested after the farmer finally sells the produce.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Farmers will be able to have access to funds and able to help them grow and people investing in them will make some profits on the money they invested. The main target is small scale  farmers who are in cooperatives. This will help people have financial freedom while empowering farmers.

> Technical Feasibility

This will be a web based or app based platform and farming projects will be posted on the site or app. People be able to invest in the projects and after investing they will be given a weekly update on how the project is going and even go to physical check on them.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Farmers will be able to get funded and people investing will be making profits on the money they invested.this will increase the food production in Zambia and reduce hunger in Zambia.
