> Title of the innovation

Community garbage collection and recycling of non degradable substances in the plastic manufacturing industry and connecting the pipes of gasses released during the processing to other less harmful gasses

> Brief description of the innovation

Our communities In the compound have poor environment, most of the compounds are dirt or the land is polluted by the plastics and other non degradable materials that can be collected and converted to other substances in the industry and those that are degradable their life span can be measured as to when they can decay or others be converted into other forms of agriculture manure as the way to clean our environment and save it from land pollution.

> Technical Feasibility
