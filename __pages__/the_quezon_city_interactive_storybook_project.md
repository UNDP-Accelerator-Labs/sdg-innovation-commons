> Description of the solution

The project aims to educate children on climate action, enhancing awareness and participation in sustainability efforts. This initiative creatively presents the city's climate policies through accessible formats like braille and audiobooks, ensuring inclusivity.

> Socio-economic effects

Raises climate awareness among youth, promotes inclusivity in education, supports informed future generations, empowers young citizens.
