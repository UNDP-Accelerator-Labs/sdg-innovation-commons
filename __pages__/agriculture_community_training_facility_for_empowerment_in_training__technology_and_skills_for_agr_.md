> Title of the innovation

Agriculture Community Training Facility for empowerment in training, technology and skills for agriculture entrepreneurs in the rural district with over 70, 000 people. This would reduce poverty, disease disease, early marriage, social vices due to productivity among the youths, women and men.

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation is intended to setup a hands on agriculture community skills training facility in Rufunsa district. The target groups are primarily the youths and women. To train them and equip them with skills and capital empowerment to reproduce such enterprise in their locations across the district. Activities areas would be in aquaculture, poultry, piggery, vegetable gardens, processing of agriculture products etc. market linkage and financial support to be inculcated in the participants.

> Technical Feasibility

the land facility of about 35ha in size shall be used for production and hands on training of participants. these participants shall be enrolled on merit across the district. minimal participation fees shall be payable but their work during training shall be credited to the training cost. after training the participants shall be loaned funds to go and startup their own agriculture enterprise. officers shall give support technically as well as financial management. these shall be linked to good off takers and markets.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Sustainability of communities in income generation, households will have decent livelihoods and be able to provide for education and health requirements. job creation will ensure wealth creation and empowerment. reduction of criminal offenses and diseases.
