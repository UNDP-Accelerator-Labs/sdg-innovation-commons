> Explain what is it about

SMEs can benefit from a wide range of services provided by remote accountants. Thus, if you operate in a big city, you can hire an accountant from smaller town at a lower price. Monthly fee is around 18 USD. Remote accountant manages all documents and submits them to the tax office.



This helps SMEs to save time and money
