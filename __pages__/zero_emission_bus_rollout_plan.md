Infrastructure &amp; Policy Intervention

> Affected Sector

Transport

> Description of the solution

Los Angeles County Metropolitan Transportation Authority (Metro) is developing a plan to convert its entire bus fleet to zero-emission buses (ZEBs) by 2040, prioritizing disadvantaged communities.

> Socio-economic effects

Reduced emissions, improved public health, especially in disadvantaged communities

> Other Notes

Public Authority (Los Angeles County Metropolitan Transportation Authority) leads the solution. 
Requires investment in electric buses, charging infrastructure, and grid upgrades.
