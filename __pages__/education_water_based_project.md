> Title of the innovation

Education water based project

> Brief description of the innovation

Selecting ophaned and vulnerable pupils , employing their Guardians on part time in the water based agriculture project . The profits realised will be used to feed the Guardians and pay for the pupils school fees and requisites.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

To employ locals to keep goats, pigs and have garden. 
To supply food to the locals and nearby supermarkets.
To pay school fees and other requirements to the pupils and
Provide food to them and their Guardians

> Technical Feasibility

Select ophaned and vulnerable children from schools
Involve theirs parents or guardians to work in the water based agriculture project. 
Use the profit to provide food for the Guardians or parents and the pupils.
The project will be land bore hole and tank. To keep goats, pigs chickens and have gardens.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will provide employment and food to the community. 
And provide the ophaned and vulnerable children with school requisites.
