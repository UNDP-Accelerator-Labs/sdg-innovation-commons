## Travel &amp; leisure guide

Ziyara is a new tourism app in Jordan that helps users discover cultural, historical, and natural attractions across the country. The app highlights authentic experiences, promotes hidden gems in different governorates, and supports local tourism by connecting users with communities and unique activities.
