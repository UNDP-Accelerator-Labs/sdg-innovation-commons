> Brief description of the innovation

An online student portal information system is a web based application that will allow prospectus students to apply online, register for a semester/term, course registration and exam registration. The system will also help institution conduct online exams, display final exam results which will be downloadable and most importantly revenue collection. The system will help in data centralization and be able to allow the institution to deliver lessons to students online in the absence of physical classes.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will be sold to several institutions that will be interested both private and government. The institutions will have increased student enrolment levels hence increasing the income generation. The target clients will be the higher learning institutions in Zambia, the system will reduce on paper work, easy access to the information and manage student data and integration of the banks with the accounts systems in the institution to facilitate quick update on students payments.

> Technical Feasibility

The system is designed with a database that will need to be hosted online and the implementation will be done using the pilot implantation method. The prototype exist and is currently hosted temporarily on https://school-online-portal.herokuapp.com/.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The innovation will improve the way higher learning institution provide their services and reduce physical interactions when neccessary.
