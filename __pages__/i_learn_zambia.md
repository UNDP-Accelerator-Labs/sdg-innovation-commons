> Title of the innovation

I Learn Zambia

> Brief description of the innovation

I Learn Zambia is a different kind of a school, where the learners needs are met. 
As we know all learners are different, and hence we need to teach them in a special way.
Thus I Learn Zambia focuses most on the learners capacity to learn so as to teach them rightly.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

I Learn Zambia will generate income by offering tuitions to learners. It’s a different kind of a school, this fostering on the learners growth in understanding and implementing educational theories.
The target market are learners who want to excel in their academics.
The challenge prize will help make I Learn Zambia start it’s full operations by acquiring all the needed materials it needs.

> Technical Feasibility

Its designed to tutor learners, offer them library services. And examine them to see how far they’ve understood the acquired information.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It’s relevant as it focuses on the learners ability to learn.
It’s not like the common education set up, where teachers just teach and give exercises then that’s it. It’s mission is to see and know how different learners learn so as to help them in understanding and remembering what they’ve learnt.
