The Fijian Government together with the Ministry of Education, Fijian Broadcasting Corporation (FBC) and WALESI has made available a free to access educational channel to assist and encourage educational continuity while schools are closed during the COVID19 lockdown. Educational programs were available for children in Kindergarten up to Year 13.
