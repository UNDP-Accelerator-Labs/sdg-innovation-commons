DTN Based Communication platform for Ethiopian Health Institutions”

Key features

 · Provides an Alternative Communication Platform for enhancing Reliability

· Addressing Problems with TCP/IP and give an Alternative Communication. 

· Promotes ease delivery of Sensitive information to organizations in charge. i.e. Health Institution.

· The Communication platform depends on DTN network which tolerates any delaying condition. 

· The DTN technology is well-suitable in providing network access to people in rural areas of the specified environment.
