Sunilesn is a company that collects, stores and recycles used cooking oils from hotels, restaurants, fast food companies, big and small food producers and consumers of vegetable cooking oils from all over the country. When they collect the used cooking oil they pay compensation and provide special containers, drums or barrels for collection. Upon collecting the used oil, they convert it bio-fuel.
