> Title of the innovation

McCloud Home and eHealth Care

> Brief description of the innovation

McCloud Home and eHealth Care intends to be a nursing and midwifery practice that brings healthcare services as close to the people in a convenient and professional manner. It intends to offer home based care to patients who would prefer to be cared for in the comfort of their homes.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The services offered by health care professionals will be charged. The clients will bear the cost of the services, as such revenue will be gained which will ensure the sustainability of the project.

> Technical Feasibility

In the western world, nursing agencies are common. A selected number of licensed personnel are contracted. When a client demands a service, a given professional is assigned to fulfill the task. The client then pays for the services. Part of the money is allocated to the company, and a specified percentage is given to the health care personnel.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Such a project is very relevant, especially during a pandemic, it eases the congestion of clients in public health care settings, allowing for quality care. Also it allows those who prefer private services to ha e their needs met at their convenience.
