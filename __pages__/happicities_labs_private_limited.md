> Description of innovation

HappiCities Development Model is a model & framework to plan, design, fund, build and operate clusters of connected greenfield micro cities that are sustainable for food, water and energy, rural incubated, smart-by-default, energy efficient, net-zero, mobility efficient, future tech ready, lifestyle oriented, accessible, affordable, inclusive, clean, green and beautiful. We propose that these micro cities be developed under a PPP model by crowd sourcing and funding from various private land owners/poolers, real estate & infra developers and smart city tech providers to create a MVP of sustainable and happy future cities of 21st Century.
