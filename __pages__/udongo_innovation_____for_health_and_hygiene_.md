Udongo innovation.... For health and hygiene.

> Brief description of the innovation

An upcoming company that focuses on health and hygiene. Offering cleaning services and delivery and supply of cleaning chemicals. We also supply sanitary products, perfumes and other cosmetic products. Further, we aim to curb climate change by Educating the general public on the importance of hygiene and proper disposal of waste.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will generate income through the cleaning services we are going to be offering. Our target market for this one include households, companies, hospitals, schools and  Markets. Further, the selling of cleaning chemicals, sanitary products, perfumes and cosmetics will generate income and provide job opportunities for a lot of people.
