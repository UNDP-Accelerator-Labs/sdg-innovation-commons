> Title of the innovation

PULLET ENTERPRISE

> Brief description of the innovation

This start-up Enterprise chiefly to produce eggs, will have three pens each measuring 42m² giving us a total surface of 126m².this Enterprise will accommodate 900 Layers.
PULLET ENTERPRISE is a partnership comparing of five persons namely Sitende Mufaweli (Chairperson) Mr Nyambe Sydney( vice chair person )Mr Mukonka Lastan (marketing manager) Mis Anayawa S Theresa (Treasure)and Mr Lubinda S Kennedy Security officer.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Four outlets places  were identified namely Limulunga, Mongu Central, Mongu Town and Mandanga markets.Furthermore, mobile number sales will be considered to ensure eggs are sold freshly.This will reduce possible losses resulting from stale and cracked eggs.The business will further expand into broiler production that will be marketed in various forms such as live chickens, dressed chickens packaged in pieces and to be stored in freezer boxes and also processed into sausages upon buying a sausage mechine.
Eggs will be provided to the community for relish as a source of proteins.
Chicken manure will be used for growing vegetable production such as tomatoes,rape and cabbages to cushion expenditure of feeding and treatment of the chickens.
Community members will also fetch water from the borehole.

> Technical Feasibility

This innovation is designed to have two of the five persons namely Mr Mukonka Lastan and Mis Anayawa S Theresa went through a formal training and therefore have academic qualifications in general agriculture.
In this first consignment members will work voluntary without a wage to ensure that the business gets rooted through expenditure reduction.
The following are you some of the business implementation plan.
Good housing such as space,ventilation system, equipments, brooder, light and cleanliness are essential for proper growth of chickens.
Feeders and drinkers are also needed for the well being of chickens.
Nest boxes are needed for chicken laying of eggs and safety in compartments.
A vitamin stress pack and vaccination records are to be administered for the good growth and against infections.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation plan will improve lives by increasing the levels of proteins intake which is necessary for the body building and repair experciary for children,expectate mothers and the eged.
The innovation is embarked on expansion which will create job opportunities for the youth in the community.
Lastly, we do not have credible competition in those areas mentioned places.Long distance walking will be stopped because eggs will be sold nearby in the community.
