Description: 
Filahati is a digital platform that links farmers with actors in the field of agriculture, where the platform provides many solutions to actual problems faced by the sector such as the problem of production and agricultural services in addition to marketing and export. 

Origin of the solution:
Algeria Startup Challenge/Coronhackathon. A partnership between UNDP Accelerator Lab. and Cap CoWork Incubator.
