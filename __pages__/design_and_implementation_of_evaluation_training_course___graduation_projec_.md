> Brief description about the identified local solution:

Gaimified Design Thinking Methedology by using electronic platfrrom. Encouraging the introduction of new ideas and helping to evaluate them through international experiences as well as choosing the best of these ideas in order to encourage and support them (training and financing), To work on building the capabilities of youth and choosing constructive ideas and solutions and sorting them according to multiple levels, Supportive institutions that have reached these projects provides the market with youth entrepreneurs who have contributed to increasing job opportunities in the private sector, Diversifying the economy and moving it towards solutions that adopt modern technology and creative human resources. 

OBJECTIVES

1- Giving young people an opportunity to implement their innovative entrepreneurial ideas, improving living standards and creating the revolution, is not only an entrepreneur, but rather it helps in employing some young people, that is, it reduces unemployment in society and their great lack of interest in government work.
2- provide a suitable training environment for young people in analyzing the problems of society and working to find appropriate solutions that help develop the mindset of thinking and innovation in design for Iraqi youth, from which to create an educated, developed and purposeful society.
3- The group training workshops help in identifying different cultures and personalities in the labor market and establishing job relations in addition to exchanging ideas.
4-Working with a different people and use brainstorming will make you get different solutions for one problem.
