> Title of the innovation

Redesigning  of health care books or files at health care facilities

> Brief description of the innovation

Redesigning  of health care books or files at health care facilities that do not use smart cards 
Amid the Covid 19 pandemic, health care givers should be protected, there for the files to be designed must have a column that enables the patient to explain or write down their symptoms and the clinic officer collects files that have all the necessary data and is able to come up with a diagnosis based on the information given by the patient, a one on one interaction can only be for better understanding.
