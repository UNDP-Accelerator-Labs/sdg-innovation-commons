Follow the money'(Sledigiparite) is an application that enables converting municipality budgets into open data formats, visualisations, and downloads for further analysis. Too add and browse a new budget go to the 'My Budget' tab. To see the data in the archive, browse, and plot go to the 'Explore available data' tab. On the 'Custom download' tab you can choose to download the whole archive or a filtered set.
