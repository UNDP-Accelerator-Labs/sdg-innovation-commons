A learning and experiencing space for children and adults on ecology and sustainable life. For a more liveable world and healthy individuals, it aims to inspire people to initiate an ecological transformation that starts from individuals and spreads to the whole society with the principle of working in harmony with nature. Organizing workshops and providing consultancy on permaculture activities for houses, institutions, schools, and, providing consultancy on creating urban gardens.
