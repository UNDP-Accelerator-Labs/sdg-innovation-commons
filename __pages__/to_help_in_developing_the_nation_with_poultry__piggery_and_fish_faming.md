> Title of the innovation

To help in developing the nation with poultry, piggery and fish faming

> Brief description of the innovation

Looking at the demand in food stuffs with proteins and the importance of it's health benefits, we have thought of starting this innovation in poultry, piggery and Fish farming in order to supply our country with quality and some food stuffs, also to help with developmental projects as well as source of employments locally. Our group would like to do this on a large scale if only provided with the enough resources financially and others.
