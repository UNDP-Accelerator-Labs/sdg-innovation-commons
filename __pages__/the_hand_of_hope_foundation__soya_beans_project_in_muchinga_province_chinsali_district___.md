> Title of the innovation

The Hand of Hope Foundation (Soya beans Project in Muchinga province Chinsali District) )

> Brief description of the innovation

Through Policy making Government to join hands to strengthen commercial and small scale farmers this will help meet the High demands for Soya products and the link in collaboration will provide at commercial and small scale levels sustainable livelihood as farmers will meet the demands towards the product in Zambia and Food security will be enhanced
Through planting new varieties that yields healthier and incorporating  sustainable practices to protect land for future generations .
