> Brief description of the innovation

youth agents for change multipurpose cooperative is the registered business entity under the registrar of the societies and the Zambia revenue authority ZRA .we aim to certify the business and the financial needs of the public and empower those entrepreneur fraternity of Zambia with information and knowledge . this will help them to meet both local and international demand and private as well as competitive market . we believe that for the national to develop it requires the youths and the pubic to have information . we want to empower the youths and the women with practical self sustaining skill , financing and give them professional technical support and create market platform locally and internationally. this will create direct and indirect employment and eradicate poverty levels and early marriages in both rural and urban set up .in order to the healthy nation and we would create the activities ,that will supply the needs locally and increase agricultural and livestock activities to strengthening Zambia economy hence the reduction of early marriages  and gender based violence .
we intend to offer the trainings and entrepreneurship skills . we aim to employ at least 25 employees in the first year of operation .
