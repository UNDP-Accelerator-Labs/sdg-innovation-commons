> Title of the innovation

Fresh farm produce and aquaculture

> Brief description of the innovation

The inovation is aimed at producing and providing fresh farm produce and fish to the ready market. In so doing to help with nutritional values of the population. Especially the malnourished children. Hence we produce and sale soya, ground nuts rice and maize, which we also pound into powder form to make porridge for children. We also grow fruits and vegetables. And provide fish through aquaculture.

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The inovation will generate income by selling the products to the locals and also delivering to shopping malls at affordable prices. This will also create jobs and also improve nutrition value for the masses especially for the malnourished children.

> Technical Feasibility

The crops are grown nearby and some bought from fellow farmers, then the fish is collected from the fish farm. All these are nicely parkeged and delivered to the ready market. 
Meal combination are also made to facilitate nutrition supplement for the malnourished children.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will improve the nutrition status of the population and also create jobs.
