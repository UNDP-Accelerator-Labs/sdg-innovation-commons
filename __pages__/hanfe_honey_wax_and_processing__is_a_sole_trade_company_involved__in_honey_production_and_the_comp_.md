> Title of the innovation

> Brief description of the innovation

the company was registered in July 14/ 2017 in choma southern province and, the aim of the company is to  take honey industry to anther level where we can start producing products from beeswax and by doing so we believe in job creation in kabompo district of north western province of Zambia.
as Hanfe shifted to north western province because of the dream we have in the honey industry,       
one of the products we want to start making is soap, lotion, heir food,honey wine and other beeswax products, and this industry will creates employment to youth, persons with disability and in the district in kabompo district of north  western province and this honey industry will not just help people in the district but the country as  a whole.
