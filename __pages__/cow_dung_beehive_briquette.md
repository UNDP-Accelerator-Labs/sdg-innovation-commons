> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem &amp; solution description)

The briquette is made out of cow dung and mixed with clay, which will easily burnt on any type of cook stove. The cow dung that could have gone wasted the environment, could be turned into a biomass energy, which is also affordable, and accessible mainly among the rural farming community.
