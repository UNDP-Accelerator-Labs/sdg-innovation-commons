‘Jiana&Me’ is a local Fijian business that sells high end purses and bags made from ‘voivoi’ (pandanus). The products are weaved by women from the village of Nakorosule, Naitasiri. It is generating consistent income for these women, and their products are also being sold internationally.

The products are 100% organic and bio-degradable, and can be used as a plastic alternative that is environmentally friendly.
