Description: 
Revadex is a digital marketplace for waste. A platform for connecting waste generators with recycler buyers in order to increase the circularity of secondary raw materials in the industrial segment.

Origin of the solution:  
Algeria Startup Challenge/Coronhackathon. A partnership between UNDP Accelerator Lab. and Cap CoWork Incubator.
