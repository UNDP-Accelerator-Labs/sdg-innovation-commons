> Description of the solution

Jelbi aims to reduce car traffic in metropolitan areas by providing a seamless mobility service. Launched by Berlin's public transportation agency, BVG, and developed by Trafi, a Lithuanian startup, Jelbi merges public transport, shared vehicles, and on-demand services into one app. With over 25 transportation partners, Jelbi enhances urban liveability by improving air quality and reducing CO2 emissions through decreased car use. The service also plans to introduce dynamic pricing and uses data to aid city planning, such as preventing traffic jams and creating new bike lanes.

> Socio-economic effects

Improves urban liveability, enhances air quality, fosters efficient city planning and traffic management, reduces CO2 emissions.
