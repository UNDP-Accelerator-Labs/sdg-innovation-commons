> Solution name

Technology and Employability for Women Builders

> Description

A mobile application, called 'Women Builders,' available on Google Play, aims to connect the supply and demand for labor in the construction sector to promote greater participation of women in this predominantly male-dominated field. The initiative includes training in the use of digital technologies, including the use of the app. Initially, it works with 180 empowered women builders who have strengthened their skills in using and sustaining the application, thereby expanding their job opportunities in the sector
