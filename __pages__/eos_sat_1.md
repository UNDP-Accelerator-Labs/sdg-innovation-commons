> Description of the solution

EOS SAT-1, a satellite by EOS Data Analytics, focuses on multiple applications. It has advanced features like 11 bands, super-resolution, and can cover a large area daily with 1-day revisit. It's used in environment, urban, agriculture, and forestry monitoring. The process involves upstream, midstream, and downstream operations. It benefits various sectors and offers satellite-as-a-service with steps like imagery, analytics, and product integration. Launched in 2023, it helps in making informed decisions and has wide-ranging impacts.
