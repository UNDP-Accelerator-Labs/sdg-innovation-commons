> Title of the innovation

Turkey Rearing

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation involves the raising, processing, and marketing natural free-range Turkeys on a large-scale in Zambia. It aims at providing nutritious Turkeys at an affordable price for many households, Hotels and restaurants. it not only supply free range Turkeys but also day-old chicks and eggs.

> Technical Feasibility

The business will aim to rear Turkeys from day-olds to point of sale. The eggs laid will be sold while others incubated for hatching of day-old chicks for sale. Distribute to customers on a delivery basis based on the quantity purchased. To attract new customers, Promotion strategies will be used. Social media Pages will be used for advertising to reach more customers. Word of mouth marketing (referrals) with a bonus will also be encouraged. TV, Radio, and newspapers will be used for more visibility. To encourage loyalty, discounts for bulk purchase and free delivery will be offered. The business will establish partnerships with Sales agents who will be placed in different towns to increase customer base.
