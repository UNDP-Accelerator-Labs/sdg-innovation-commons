## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top 5 key insights you generated about your frontier challenge during this Action Learning Plan?

- the importance of enabling SSPs to have access to the market
- the importance to use a digital platform to speed up their integration
- the importance of going digital for promoting the agriculture sector in Mauritania 
- the importance of long terms strategic partnerships to promote the agriculture sector in Mauritania.
- the importance of providing more income-generating activities opportunities for disadvantaged farmers and small producers.
