Izbor association consists of multiple program that support the community in Strumica. The programs consist of prevention programs that offer jobs and job related education to individuals at risk, harm reduction, programs for sex workers, program for acceptance, treatment and healing, program for rehabilitation, resocialization and reintegration and a program for free legal assistance
