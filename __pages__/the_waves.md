> Brief description about the identified local solution:

The Waves is a tree-based knowledge sharing community. Their initiatives are called waves and structured as group of trainees enlarging. Their idea is simply that they build a circle from trainers in different fields then these trainers will train other trainers and the last will train another group until they will complete five waves. each wave will carry different topic and with each wave there is a new startup idea are come up. at the end they will come up with startup ideas and link them with potential stakeholders, this will encourage the youth to enter the field of entrepreneurship.
