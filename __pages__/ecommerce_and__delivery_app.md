## Send Me is a collective of diligent and enthusiastic young entrepreneurs hailing from Sierra Leone. With an unwavering belief in the technological advancement of Sierra Leone, we have established a platform that facilitates daily online transactions for buying and selling. Since its inception in November 2019, our company has been dedicated to the creation of a comprehensive system that integrates an online marketplace, a secure online payment system, and an efficient delivery service tailored for the African continent. We remain steadfast in pursuing this trajectory.
