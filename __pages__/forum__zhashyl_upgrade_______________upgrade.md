Urban Planning, Environmental Conservation

> Description of the solution

The Forum aims to enhance urban sustainability through three main goals:

Educational Enhancement: Increasing citizen awareness about the importance and maintenance of urban greenery through ecological, biological, and legal education.


Collaborative Tools Development: Working with decision-makers to develop initiatives for preserving green spaces.


Community Consolidation: Building a unified network of organizations and individuals focused on the conservation of urban greenery, fostering stronger community involvement and resource sharing.


These objectives collectively promote sustainable urban development by emphasizing the preservation and importance of green spaces in Bishkek.

> Socio-economic effects

Enhances urban livability, promotes environmental awareness, and fosters community participation in sustainability initiatives as well as civil society and local government collaboration.
