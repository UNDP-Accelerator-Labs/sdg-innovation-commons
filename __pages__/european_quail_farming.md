> Title of the innovation

European quail farming

> Brief description of the innovation

Business ventured on raring European quails for meat production,  European quails are ground nesting birds that are a source of cheap or affordable protein to the communities and it's price friendly. This agricultural based venture should be based in mumbwa district where no potential competitors are dense. The major idea is to favour the community to have access in form of benefiting from this innovation via the meat products of these birds as well as their eggs

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation will generate some income when sales are made after the maturity of the European quails. Since the quail meat isn't common in Mumbwa district, many shopping centers demand for quail meat products which implies that the establishment and efficient operation of this innovation to sustain the market at large requires high production of this European bird. This innovation will income even to some youths via employment and attaining some skills for future benefit, this business is environmentally friendly meaning it doesn't contribute much for it to cause a synergistic effect of climate change.

> Technical Feasibility

The design consists of a single building for poultry farming which can accommodate quiet a good number of quails, the plan is to have a larger market share in five years time from now. The building is situated at a suitable place where water is not an issue, hence the major issue at hand is the challenge of financial services to sustain the project from the first day of introducing the chicks, feeding and providing necessary vaccines until the time they become ready for market sales

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will improve the lives of many in such a way that, those who will attain employment and have some skills will be capable to start a related business some day, it's relevant to the current market because quail meat is a very delicate and price friendly source of protein for many.
