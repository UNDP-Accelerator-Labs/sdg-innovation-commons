> Title of the innovation

Spiru-Nutri

> Brief description of the innovation

Spiru-Nutri is a super nutritious powder made from spirulina that is added as a food additive to snacks, junk and fast foods in order to promote wellness and good health to consumers.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Spiru-Nutri sells powdered form of the spirulina to food manufacturing industries, pharmaceutical companies as well as small scale home-based businesses that sell food products.

The challenge prize will ensure continued production of the product to reach a higher market value and create more jobs for qualified members of the public.

Over 45% of children in Zambia are malnourished, Spiru-Nutri contains a high proportion of nutrients that combats against malnutrition, thus adding it as a food additive especially to snacks and junk food that many children love eating will drastically reduce the prevalence of malnutrition in Zambia. It also has additional benefits to adults such as reduction in Inflammation, heart-disease, cancer and has immune boosting properties which consequently can increase the life expectancy for many Zambians.

> Technical Feasibility

It is in powder form, and the proposed implementation plan involves using it as a food additive to snacks, beverages, junk and fast foods.
Spiru-Nutri later intends to produce it as a standalone super food for sell.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Sipri-Nutri made from spirulina has various potent benefits that will help reduce malnutrition in children as well as promotes wellness in adults. Current research shows that it also slows down COVID-19 aside its immune boosting properties. (https://www.nutraingredients.com/Article/2021/03/02/Algae-extract-may-act-to-slow-down-COVID-progression)

It will also create jobs for qualified members of the public thus contributing to the growth of the economy in the long run.

The product is very relevant to the current market, especially now that diseases and other health problems have been on the rise in recent years in the country.
