![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2021/blobs/solutions/0ce48020a46b5ab98b56002b33a30473.jpg)


Like a simple letter box, with a empty ballon stuck on the back orifice,  please see the local respirator invented to support the ministry of public health to secure and save lives of millions of citizen. the box itself is a 25cm side cube in what you have a system realising all the mecanism on the respirator as we see in the hospital. The respirator is powered by a 12V source. It is equiped with an electrical switch and a tube that will be linked to the patient for him to breath easily
