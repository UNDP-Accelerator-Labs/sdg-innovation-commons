> Description of the solution

Smart Watering: Irrigation Assistant is a digital solution designed to monitor and optimize drip irrigation and fertilization practices. The platform integrates advanced data collection and analytics to ensure precise water and nutrient delivery to crops, enhancing efficiency and sustainability. Tracks water distribution and ensures uniform irrigation across the field. Prevents overwatering or underwatering by providing real-time data and alerts.
