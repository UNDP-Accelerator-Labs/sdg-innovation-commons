> Description of innovation

Kriya Labs provides Packaging Solutions using only eco-friendly value added materials from waste natural materials or fibers.

Their strategy, broadly, is to assign a real economic and commercial value to the agricultural residue and make it an asset to the farmers by creating markets for rice straw.
