> Description

My Community is a software solution t facilitates the process of management and maintenance for the managers of residential buildings, and provides the tenants with transparent information about the financial situation, current activities and problems of the community of owners. 


Tenants have the opportunity to actively participate in the management of the residential building, with the ultimate goal of facilitating community living. 


The software helps save time, maintain and improve neighborly relations. It is a digitalized process. This is a commercial service
