> Title of the innovation

Eco- Friendly Alternative to charcoal

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovative is about creating an eco friendly alternative for charcoal to mitigate deforestation and increasing the awareness of climate change and the importance of sustainable development.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

1. Our innovation will generate income for the organization through sales that we will make from selling the charcoal. 
2. Our target market is every household in the country because at least 85% percent of Zambian household use charcoal to make meals. 
3. The challenge prize will help grow the business because at the moment this is just a business idea and we are hoping this prize will help us kick start our innovation. 
4. We aim to reduce the carbon footprint of the traditional charcoal because our solution is an eco friendly alternative that is independent of tree cutting. There for will be mitigating climate change

> Technical Feasibility

Our innovation will design a simple, cost effective  and easy to adopt cooking blocks for the countries most vulnerable families. Through our proprietary process, we will create valuable energy blocks from biomass waste that will will address the specific needs of our customers.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Currently Zambia is facing a pandemic of deforestation and unemployment. This innovative will improve lives by job creation and will enable people to breath clean air and live in a sustainable economy. 
Given the high level of climate change, and the current formation of the new ministry of green economy, this innovative is very relevant as will be addressing a specific need of our customers.
