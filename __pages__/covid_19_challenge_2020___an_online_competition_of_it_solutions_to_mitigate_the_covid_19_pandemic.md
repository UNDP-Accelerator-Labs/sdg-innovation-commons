          AccLab in partnership with the IT Park, Ministry of Employment and Labor Relations, Ministry of Innovative Development, Youth Union, Youth Academy under the Ministry of innovative development etc.

The team of mentors who acted as experts to assist in the direction of activities and feedback between several project teams. The purpose was to help teams create solutions that are both meaningful and implementable. For each direction, 4-5 mentors were involved. The contest is held in the following tracks:

1 - HEALTHCARE
2 - HELP TO VULNERABLE POPULATIONS
3 - SMALL BUSINESS SUPPORT
4 -COMMUNITY SUPPORT
5 - EDUCATION
6 - IMPROVEMENT OF AWARENESS AND BEHAVIORAL MODELS
7 - LEISURE



600 applications of solutions were submitted. Experts selected 5 finalists in the area of Health Care, Education and Community support.
