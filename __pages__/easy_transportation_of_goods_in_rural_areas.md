> Title of the innovation

Easy Transportation Of Goods In Rural Areas

> Brief description of the innovation

Zambia is rich in the Agricultural Sector and I have just noticed that there are a lot of Farmers out there with better goods in Rural areas that would really benefit not only them as Farmers but also Mother Zambia at large. However, there is lack Transportation in terms of how they can move their products to various required destinations henceforth My thought that with a number of Canter Trucks and other Transportation machines I would help those who lack deliver their goods and lay them before the Table of Mother Zambia.
