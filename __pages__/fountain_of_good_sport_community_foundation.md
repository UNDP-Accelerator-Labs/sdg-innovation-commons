> Title of the innovation

Fountain of Good Sport Community Foundation

> Brief description of the innovation

Fountain of Good Sport Community fo Foundation aims to inspire and empower young people to take charge of their lives and create their own futures. 
This PROJECT will train 4000 young people by the year 2030 to engage and practice organised sports and Educational programs.

> Technical Feasibility

This innovation will build a multi-Sport sports complex center where all project activities shall be taking place while working with partners to implement our curriculum and make things happen to  create impact. 
 Participants will graduate and take on the leading role in their community. This will lead to a massive job creation.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Through our train the trainers program, 4000 young people will be empowered with leadership and intreprenuerships skills so that after they graduate from our program, they can be able to start their own clubs and implement our curriculum. 
For the young boys that will be participating in organised sports, we will ensure we provide the right opportunities for all talented indivinduals so that they can make a living out of their own talents which will improve the social status and wellbeing of their families.

More than half of the countries population are young people especially children who do not have access to quality programs sports and Education programs especially in rural areas. These are children who, if well trained would go on to become prominent leaders. This initaive will give each participant an equal opportunity and ability  to get involved in our mission of human development  through sport.
