Ms. Hong, a woman from Da Nang, came up with an idea to turn organic waste into money when the neighbourhood's garbage truck encountered a problem. She collected plant residue and food waste to create environmentally-friendly household cleaning products. Eventually, she made it into a business with a mission to reduce environmental pollution.

One of her products is dish soap made from vegetable peels containing enzymes. When used, this enzyme removes odour in the air and its residues purify water in rivers or seas.
