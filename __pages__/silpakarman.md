> Description of innovation

Silpakarman is a for-profit social enterprise where a group of craft enthusiast work with clusters and artisans in the rural parts of India. Their aim is to innovate contemporary products by following the traditional crafts of these craftsmen while providing them with livelihood.Silpakarman is focused to bring the best out of natural fibers. They are working across 3 states in the north-eastern part of India, innovating products made from bamboo, cane and other grasses.
