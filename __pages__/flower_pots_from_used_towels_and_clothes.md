> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

Quarantine restrictions due to the COVID 19 pandemic, forced people to stay at home. While staying at home, they attended to growing plants and flowers. Hence the need for flower pots. There came a time that flower pots are out of stock in the locality, hence the need to offer alternative design and source for pots. The idea to use rags, clothes and towels and mixed with cement to form pots of various sizes.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

Aside from addressing the need for pots, it also addresses the need of people to maintain good mental health and divert focus from pandemic challenges

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?
