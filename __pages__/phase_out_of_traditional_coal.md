> Type of the Solution

Policy/Regulation

> Affected Sector

Energy

> Description of the solution

A gradual ban on the sale and use of traditional coal for domestic heating or industrial purposes.

> Socio-economic effects

Improved air quality, potential economic impact on coal industry, transition challenges for users

> Type of Measure

- 3. mitigation measures

> Type of sub-measure

- 5. (none)

> Who led the solution

- national government

> Timescale of implementation

- long-term (5 and above)

> Other Notes

Requires clear phase-out plan, support for transitioning to cleaner alternatives, and public awareness campaigns. The pace of phase-out may vary by country.
