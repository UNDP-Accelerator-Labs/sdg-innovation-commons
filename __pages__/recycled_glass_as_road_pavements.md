# Recycled Glass as Road Pavements

[[sdgs:12. Responsible consumption and production]]

[[country:Malaysia]]

Glass bottles were crushed and and restructured to pave roads. The initiative was done in a rural community that practices circular economy and prevent pollution of waste in rivers. It is organized by Ripple or Persatuan Kitar Semula Sabah. The chairperson is Mdm Anne Lasimbang. Many are interested to give their bottles for this initiative.
