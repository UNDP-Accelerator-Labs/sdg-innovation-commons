> Title of the innovation

Eco-heroes of environmental and climate change actions

> Brief description of the innovation

Our innovation is picking liter and reusing to pack our plants

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Our target market are farmers, we will improve peoples lives by offering employment to the people who will assist us to water our plants

> Technical Feasibility

We pick liter from landfills, then use the to pack our plants

> Anticipated Social Impact

By reducing carbon emissions, by making Lusaka clean and green, the trees will filter the water we drink, they will clean the air we breathe
