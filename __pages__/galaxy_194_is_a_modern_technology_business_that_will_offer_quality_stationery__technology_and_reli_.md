> Title of the innovation

Galaxy_194 is a modern technology business that will offer quality stationery, technology and reliable education  services to the local residents and online clients..

> Brief description of the innovation

For many years,  Mwanjawanthu has not had an established stationery shop. Residents have been traveling long distances just to access stationery products and services. In the last 12 months, our learners have  suffered to enjoy their educational rights because of the COVID pandemic. Responding to this tragedy,  a new initiative is underway with a view to keeping learners glued to their ambitions as they shall have a virtual school at fingertips. 
Farmers and other businesses men will also find it easy to market their products through the company's advertisement options and promotion.
