> Title of the innovation

Buying of scrap metal for refining.

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation deals with the buying of scrap metal and taking it for refining in order to make new metal products from the old metal.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The scrap metal innovation plan is a plan that will not only the owner of the business but the community and the nation at large in the sense that it'll lessen theft in a community and will help the nation make new products out of wasted metal thereby avoiding much imports but rather making exports of steel products. It'll also help keep Zambia clean.

> Technical Feasibility

The plan is to find a balancing place for the business, example: a warehouse or a fensed yard. Of which at the moment the place is ready available. Making sure it doesn't affect the living of others.

> Anticipated Social Impact

As a lot of youths are having a hard time finding jobs, this plan will help them earn a minimum amount of money depending on how much the metal weighs on the scale and how much the price is per kilograms(kg).
