> Title of the innovation

Mushroom production

> Brief description of the innovation

Mushroom production is the innovation that aims at producing mushroom seeds and kits to the market places.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Mushroom production will generate income through selling the mushroom seeds. The business can assist improving existing business in supper markets and shopping malls through supplying them with mushroom seeds and kits. The target offtakers are  already there buying the same product from Unza agriculture science especially the white people on a daily basis. This will reduce poverty levels especially on the side of unemployed youths.

> Technical Feasibility

The mushroom production needs about k10000 kwacha to buy suitable equipment for the process. It requires a staleliser, a gas cooker, a cooking drum, the gas silinder with gas, a 500kg of sorghum for a start, a 1kg of aluminium foil, a 200kg of lime, a 1kg of cotton wool and 5000 bottles of 750mls each for a start. The business will sustain itself.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This can reduce dependence tendency on white collar jobs for the youths and reduce poverty levels because this business can glow big and youths can be learning how to glow mushroom and supply on their own.
