> Brief description of the innovation

Mmeg Media is a Lusaka based company that is creating a platform for talent exposure and empowerment. Our goal is to bring talented and skilled people in one environment and work with them effectively for their exposure for profit making, giving the talented youths a living and empowerment. 
Each talent will be placed in a specific department. We have department such as art department, music department, fashion department, movie department, photography department, as we created entertainment for our clientels.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We will generate income by offering services such artistic services, photo shoot and video shoot, dance group hire, live stage plays, film and music production, decor and wedding planning, master of ceremony, musice system hire, and variety and entertainment shows.
Out market consist of large youth enterprises/youths, firms, government,  non-governmental organisations.  Our target market large up takers in zambia include youths between the ages of 13-35
The challenge prize will help as a company to buy more and better equipment to continue our quest in putting Zambian on top of the world as the most talented.
This innovation will reduce the number of pontetial in youths from dying untapped.  This platform will be accessed from people in the village,ghetto and town, keeping the youths busy with what is productive hence contributing positively to the community economically.

> Technical Feasibility

Mmeg means coming together as one, we believe in team work. The innovation has been designed to work with talented youths. We have actors, dancers, producers, directors, fashion designers( we have some shoes,handbags,ear rings, tables  that we make) and we have many more talent.
We have talented young people who can amaze Zambia by their ability if given a chance. We have been working tirelessly to create such a platform as we have created a community of talented youths but we are only lacking proper equipment to bring them on top of Africa.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Mmeg will create employment for many youths, we are creating a platform that will empower many as we will be on a search for talent in zambia.
The entertainment industry is ever on demand, hence this proposed innovation is on demand.
