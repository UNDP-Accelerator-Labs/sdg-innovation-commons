> Description of the solution

This solution provides farmers with services such as agricultural market information and agricultural product sales. Its digital platform can help farmers understand the market price trends of agricultural products, obtain suggestions from agricultural technical experts, and at the same time provide an online sales channel for agricultural products to help farmers expand the market.It features products, aims to cut costs, boost profit potential, enable informed decisions, offers shopping/delivery, financial services, sustainability options, and shares relevant news.
