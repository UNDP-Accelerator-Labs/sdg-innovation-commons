> Title of the innovation

HAZARD RISK REDUCTION

> Brief description of the innovation

My innovation aims at reducing hazard risks in the country and world over.  The main focus will be directed at the most vulnerable people in society such as those in informal unplanned settlements with great exposure to both natural and anthropogenic hazards.  This will be achieved through establishment of a proactive framework that will encompass preventive, resilient and coping strategies.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation will generate income for me through the cooperate world and and other well wishers who will appreciate the value of the innovation and the people's income will be generated in the sense that their businesses or capital will not be lost or disrupted due to disasters.
