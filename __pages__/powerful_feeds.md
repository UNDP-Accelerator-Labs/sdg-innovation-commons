> Brief description of the innovation

Powerful feeds is a process of making feeds the easiest way and affordable for all sizes of farmers. 
Raw materials used: Black Soldier flies
Maize bran
Or still chicken manure 

Which enables all kind of animals to consume starting from Chicken, pig, fish and goats, it is rich in protein quantity.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovative solutions will enable farmers to make their own feeds at home as the prices of feeds keep on adjusting the struggling farmers are unable to purchase these feeds at a very high cost, this innovative will bring more income and help people build up their poultry businesses. The targeted market are the farms manufacturers of feeds. This challenge prize will enable our team to successfully build a bigger maggot production plant and employ more than 30 youths. And lower the cost of rearing chicks thus our farmers making more profit which will enable our families to have excess money to take care of their families.
