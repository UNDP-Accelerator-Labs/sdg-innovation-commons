This solution analyses emigration trends, including industry breakdown of emigrants, by collecting Facebook Ad data. In addition, it explores the connection between the number of emigrants and scientific research output of domestic scientists, using scientific publishing data through the Scopus library, a huge multidisciplinary abstract and citation database of peer-reviewed literature.
