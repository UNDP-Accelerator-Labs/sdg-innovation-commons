> Description of the solution

The New York City Clean Energy Fund, managed by the New York State Energy Research and Development Authority (NYSERDA), supports initiatives aimed at increasing energy efficiency, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and promoting the adoption of renewable energy technologies in New York City. The fund provides financial incentives and technical assistance to projects that contribute to the city's clean energy goals and climate resilience.

> Socio-economic effects

nhances energy security, reduces utility costs for residents and businesses, creates green jobs, and improves public health by reducing air pollution.
