Network of women initiatives that support rights and social securities of household work. Their objectives and lines of work are:



- Make household work visible and to whom it is carried out.

- Generate a public debate on the value of this work and raise awareness about its important economic, social and social welfare functions in general.

- Promote the labor rights of domestic workers. Raise awareness that households are active agents to promote social justice.
- Promote that employer households guarantee the labor rights of domestic workers.

- Show that Mexican society is ready to take a step that the Mexican State has not yet wanted to take.
