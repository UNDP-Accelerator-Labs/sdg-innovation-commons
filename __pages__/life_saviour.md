Description: 
Life Savior is presented as "a simple, intuitive and innovative response" to address the issue of access to care and the endless expectations among doctors. Thus, she unearths all the appointments available with doctors in order to offer you a panel of flexible hours and thus reduce the waiting time for making a medical appointment and of course without forgetting the various services offered by the solution.


Origin of the solution: 
Algeria Startup Challenge/Coronhackathon. A partnership between UNDP Accelerator Lab. and Cap CoWork Incubator.
