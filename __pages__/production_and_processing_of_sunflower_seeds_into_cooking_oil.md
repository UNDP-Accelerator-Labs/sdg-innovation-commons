> Title of the innovation

Production and processing of sunflower seeds into cooking oil

> Brief description of the innovation

An outgrower business venture that engage small scale farmers to grow sunflower seeds and buys the produce at the end of the season. The sunflower is later processed in to cooking oil and sunflower seeds cake. The making of these products will create revenue and employment for many.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will generate income through the sale of it's major products (sunflower oil and sunflower cake). The target market include; household consumers, hospitality industry players, restaurant business owners as well as livestock farmers. The challenge prize will grow the business in that better machines will be procured to increase production, more sunflower seeds with higher oil content will be produced and bought from small scale farmers. The value addition of the innovation will improve the social and economic status of all the players involved from farmers to consumers of the final products.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed in such a way that an agricultural extension service is given to farmers to ensure that quality sunflower is produced. A ready market at the end of the season is provided. A processing plant is located right where the raw materials are produced. The whole idea is to produce the final products at a reduced cost by cutting down on transportation costs.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Sunflower seeds processing is of great benefit to many. Farmers are making profits from the sale of their produce, many people are employed in the process of value addition, sunflower oil and cake are made available to consumers with special health needs and livestock farmers will have the feed for their animals. The innovation will improve the supply of the final products onto the market and the scarcity problem will be solved. With the improved productivity due to funding, the business will also increase its contribution to Gross Domestic Product (GDP).
