> Current status of experimental activity

> What portfolio does this activity correspond to? If any

"Monthly Memos" highlight the latest trends in innovation and development relevant to the UNDP country office focus areas. These memos are circulated on a monthly basis and aim to highlight significant advancements, insights, and opportunities that could influence our projects and strategies. The initiative promotes a culture of continuous learning and adaptability, ensuring that the CO team remains at the forefront of development practice.

> What is the frontier challenge does this activity responds to?

Embedding innovation in the UNDP country office projects

> What is the learning question(from your action learning plan) is this activity related to?

Can we embed innovation in the UNDP county office projects and processes?

## Design
**Explain the design of the experimental activity. In general, experimental activities consist on trying to learn how results are connected to a stimuli.**

> What is the specific learning intent of the activity?

Can we embed innovation in the UNDP county office projects and processes, through providing reliable insights on new tools/ideas and innovative thinking for development?


> What is your hypothesis? IF... THEN....

If UNDP colleagues have the latest tools, processes and innovative new ways of thinking about development, then they will start implementing it in their projects/processes.

> Does the activity use a control group for comparison?

- No, it does not use a control group

> How is the intervention assigned to different groups in your experiment?

- non-random assignment

> Describe which actions will you take to test your hypothesis:

Engagement with the content we have produced [readers, feedback, willingness to co-create the product with us]

> What is the unit of analysis of this experimental activity?

Monthly Memo.

> Please describe the data collection technique proposed

Emails as the distribution channel for the Monthly Memo's with follow up discussions when there is interest.
