> Title of the innovation

The technology can replace the Combustion Chamber in a conventional engine with an electro-magnetic induction Chamber.

> Brief description of the innovation

The technology is novel and it is 100% electrical. Meaning it is a 100% Zero Carbon emission technology. Energy can be decarbonized by the innovation in a sustainable way while increasing the role of domestic and industrial sources in the energy mix.
The design is a unit called a Dynocell, a drive-train that is electromechanically driven by a technological innovation called the Linear Electromagnetic Dyno-cell (The L.E.D Technology).
It relates to the generation of electric power using purely electrical engineering principles. The L.E.D Technology is replacing the Gasoline Combustion Chamber in a conventional engine with an electro-magnetic induction Chamber. Thus the arrangement can provide electromechanical drive train to power automobiles, trucks, trains, ships, earth moving equipment, aircrafts and space aircraft.
