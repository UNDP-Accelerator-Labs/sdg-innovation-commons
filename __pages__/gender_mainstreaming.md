## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights) 

women empowerment that enables women to be more proactive in protecting their rights, self-courage to understand other's needs, access to supports is very important and willingness to lead when there is no leadership action is very crucial

> Can you provide more detail on your handover process?

The Gender Mainstreaming activity was led by UNDP Justice program in collaboration with Acc.lab team. The activity is now part of the country office ongoing commitment to foster safe space for all staff particularly women staff on the importance of reporting any cases of GBV.
