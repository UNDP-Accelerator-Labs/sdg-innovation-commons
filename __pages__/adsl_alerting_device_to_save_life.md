> Title of the innovation

ADSL-Alerting Device to Save Life

> Brief description of the innovation

The ADSL is a plug-in module for all sorts of vehicles ranging from cars to mobile machinery and heavy equipment. ADSL stands for Alerting Device to Save Lives. The ADSL is a versatile tool that can be used in countless scenarios where safety is of prime importance. This module was designed to overcome most of all human error-induced accidents in places where mobile machines are used. Human limitations such as poor hearing in noisy environments, poor vision in dusty environments, and blind spots caused by obstruction by obstacles are just a few of the many causes of accidents in the workplace. The ADSL is an embedded computing system that has been equipped with enough computing power to detect the presence of another vehicle within a given proximity and to alert the machine operator or driver of a possible collision by means of an audible sound. It has also stereo intercepting system to priorities warning sound. Incase of breakdown along the way, it has warning emergency switch to warn other vehicles.
