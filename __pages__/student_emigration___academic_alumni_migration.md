This idea was sourced through the Depopulation Data Challenge and was not awarded in the final evaluation round. 

Student emigration - Academic Alumni migration is a solution that uses the methods of advanced analytics based on data on alumni members of various universities in Serbia show the attractiveness of countries for work and life, using data from LinkedIn about alumni to university members, and open data on living standards, in order to better understand emigration of students.
