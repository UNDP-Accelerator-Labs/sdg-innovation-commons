> Title of the innovation

> Brief description of the innovation

Voice Me Out is a non profit organization that focuses on helping, supporting and protecting the welfare of a teenager in society.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The organization is a non profit so it will generate income through fundraising and donations from well wishers.
Out target market is the teenagers.

> Technical Feasibility

It is here to offer help to the teenagers through reaching out to them in schools, hospitals, streets and orphanages. Through counseling and sensitization in situations affecting them and where possible provide basic needs.
Support them by listening more and talking less. Support and embrace their ideas, opinions and creativity. What changes they want to see and their generation to come, positively. 
Protect them against violation of their rights because it is in their rights that everything is found. Educate them and remind them of their duties an responsibilities and speaking out for them on things affecting them.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will improve the lives of others specifically the teenagers because it is at this stage that one starts to grow mature physically, mentally and emotionally , this is where we come in as a voice for teenagers in helping, protecting and supporting them in society. It is at this stage that attention is required because it determines how that child is going to be after turning 20. A lot of physical, mental and emotional changes prevail at this stage whilest ones mind is still active thus guidance in the safety of the child needed most.
