> Description of the solution

The Beskol School in Kazakhstan has implemented a UNDP-supported initiative featuring a biomass boiler, significantly reducing heating costs and cutting CO2 emissions. This project, part of a broader effort towards low-carbon development, not only saves the school approximately $5,000 annually but also reduces yearly CO2 emissions by around 900 tons. The initiative underscores a practical application of sustainable energy solutions in educational settings, enhancing environmental and economic sustainability.

> Socio-economic effects

educes operational costs and greenhouse gas emissions, enhances comfort and safety in school environments.
