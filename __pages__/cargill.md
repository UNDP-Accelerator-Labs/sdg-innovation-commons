> Description of the solution

Cargill is an American company that has implemented difital systems all throughout its supply chain. This includes: Automated Rib-Chine Saws, Foreign Object Detection Technology, Paperless Food Safety, Quality, and Regulatory System, or Automated Box Handling Systems. For instance, they implemented AI-powered systems in its meat processing plants to optimize operations. The system uses machine vision and AI to monitor the quality of the meat and ensure compliance with health standards, improving efficiency and product quality. Another example is their use of automated meat processing equipments (chainsaw lines) to help separate meat from bone.
