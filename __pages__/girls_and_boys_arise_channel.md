> Title of the innovation

GIRLS AND BOYS ARISE CHANNEL

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation is aimed at :
i) Creating a better and more innovative,creative, independent,realistic and interesting way to face and look at Life as a whole.
ii) Improving the hands on entrepreneurship of youths.
iii) Grow a business that will be sustained to live and support our community, society and our country Zambia

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation will precisely provide the practical entrepreneurship that our youths can embark on ,inclusive of the necessary skills and trainings needed.
-The training and registration will be done from one of our local school premises.
a)-A shop will be rented in a market area,ovens ,water ,as well as utensils will be provided so that girls can engage themselves into baking Buns ,Cakes , Cupcakes , scones etc.
These food products will then be sold in different places within our various communities.
b)-Boys on the other hand will get their hands on agricultural activities every favourable season to plant ,grow,and weed.
*In the first case ,an income will be generated after the sells have been made.This though will require each girl to develop a minimum amount of income to provide for necessary ingredients, prior to which, ingredients were provided for during training.
-All that will be required of our boys is to be readily available for trainings and work for the most part.
Once the team works on a specific land ,then each boy will be paid accordingly.
-A certain percentage of the money generated in both cases A and B will be withheld as per agreement to be deposited into the business account while the rest will be despatched to the deserving persons.
*The challenge prize will serve as our main source of income to empower us to commence this journey of transforming our youths' idleness into fruitful,life building efforts.This in turn will reduce chances of having gangs ,drug addicts as well as early girl child pregnancy among others.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed to start up a motivational business center in that ;the center will provide ovens and necessary utensils to equip,train, and orient girls to have hands on food business that can grow to their independence and sustainability.

In like manner,the innovation can also support boys to improve their agricultural skills by venturing into agricultural activities that this innovation is definitely providing.

Firstly advertisements will be done in our various communities as well as sensitizations  to call upon a mass meeting to be held on the 7th day for more information plus registration in order to enroll.

The trainings will then commence two days after the meeting, followed by the compilation of the final list of qualified students to begin the practical entrepreneurship part.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Many lives especially of our youths will greatly be enreached with not only knowledge but most importantly the Skill that can empower them for a whole life time.
This can also divert our dependace  on government employment to a bigger and even better independent way of earning money.
That's by equipping, training and orienting our society to a broader way of empowering oneself.
Having increased man power on work and less idleness will improve the levels of productivity in our country which in turn can can emprove our economic status.
