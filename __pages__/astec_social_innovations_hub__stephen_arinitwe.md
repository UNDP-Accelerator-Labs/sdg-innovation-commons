ASTEC Social Innovations Hub is a youth led social enterprise Founded in 2008 seeking to create a generation of youth and women that use their passion, strength and skills to create work that solves shared problems in their respective communities. We believe that everyone is born with passion, abilities and a heart that thirsts for purposes. ASTEC plans to reach at least 1,000,000 Young People with possible real hands on and practical related income generation skills |
