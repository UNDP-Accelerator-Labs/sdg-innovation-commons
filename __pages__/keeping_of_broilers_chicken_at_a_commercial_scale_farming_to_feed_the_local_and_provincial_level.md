> Title of the innovation

Keeping of broilers chicken at a commercial scale farming to feed the local and provincial level

> Brief description of the innovation

The Youth Union Club is here to start keeping broilers chicken that will help to solve and create jobs for the Youth of Shiwangandu District and other districts. The innovation will mainly concentrate on the broilers because they have market and they mature fast.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation or the keeping of broilers will generate income through selling of chickens when they get ready. Also by supplying feed to the locals those in need this increase the income of the broilers chicken keeping innovation. These chickens will be sold to different customers such as hospitals, boarding Schools, Clubs /parties such as weddings and many more.

> Technical Feasibility

To start keeping broilers and commercial scale farming. These chickens will be kept in a very good environment to reduce diseases

> Anticipated Social Impact

The keeping of broilers chicken in Shiwangandu is a very good idea because this innovation will create jobs for many youths in the area as you may be aware that Shiwangandu is a District with no jobs no companies so most youths will find jobs in the keeping of broilers chicken.
