## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights) 

1. The majority of our respondents on (81%), regardless of gender, had a poor perception towards public transportation in Lebanon.

2. Respondents with a higher level of education are more likely to commute to Beirut, whilst those with no formal education are more likely to go to North Lebanon. 3. Female respondents and LGBTIQ+ respondents use PT slightly more frequently. 4. Female respondents usually have a more favorable opinion of all means of PT than male respondents.

> Considering the outcomes of this learning challenge, which of the following best describe the handover process? (Please select all that apply)

- Our work has not yet scaled

> Can you provide more detail on your handover process?

The Sustainable Transport Project within the CO is utilizing the interactive data dashboard to inform their project. We are also working with them to fill in data gaps and identifying experiments to validate the hypotheses derived from the research.
