> Description

The Cadaster Agency provides the following: 
1.Building pre-registration sheet
 2. Excerpt from the cadastral plan
 3. List of indications for cadastral parcel.
 4. Certificate for historical review of the performed entries. 
5. Certificate that the person is not registered in the real estate cadaster. 
6. Insight into the data in the collection of documents from the Geodetic-cadastral information system in written form. 
7. Property list for infrastructure facilities This system has a digitalized process
