Based in Sabak Bernam, Selangor, the innovation of foklic trolley is used to carry heavy loads.  On the front side of the trolley is a forklift moving the trolley upward and downward. This innovation can lift loads to a higher level up to 1.5m high without the need for many workers. The trolley requires 12V battery and is rechargeable.
