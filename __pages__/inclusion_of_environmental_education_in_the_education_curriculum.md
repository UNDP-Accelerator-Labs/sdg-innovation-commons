> Title of the innovation

Inclusion of environmental education in the education curriculum

> Brief description of the innovation

The problem of climate change and it's impact on the entire population can not be overemphasized, as it is a well know fact of the effects it has on everyone regardless of their social status and this has highly impacted on our food base, sanitation and health among others. 
Therefore, the inclusion of environmental education in the education system at every stage is cardinal in helping address issues associated with climate change on the environment.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Environmental education boarders on principles of sustainability and every economic activity should be environmentally friendly. 
Through community sensitisation campaigns, creation of eco clubs in schools (from kindergarten to grade 12) and incorporation of environmental sustainability principles in every field of profession. 
These activities will attract funding from the relevant stakeholders to help it run effectively.

> Technical Feasibility

It  will have to take place in phases as a pilot project in about four schools and one community will be identified. 
The time line would be for six months. 
Kindergarten - 1
Primary school - 1
High School- 1
College- 1
Community sensitisation campaigns - 1

> Anticipated Social Impact

The innovation will help increase environmental awareness, climate change impacts , how to adapt to several changes that occur as a result of environmental degradation and knowledge of sustainability principles.
