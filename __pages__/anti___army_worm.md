For the past four years farmers in Zambia have experienced crop attacks by army worms. This problem has resulted into poor harvest thereby posing a threat to food security in the Country especially for the rural population that is highly dependent on small scale farming for their livelihoods and food security.

The innovation provides a chemical liquid that farmers can use to kill Army worms that are prone to attack the country's staple crop - maize
