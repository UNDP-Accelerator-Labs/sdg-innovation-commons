> Brief description of the innovation

Alternative Tertiaries  is an innovative startup project offering  free learning courses for knowledge and skills throughout the globe. It is a technological project committed to creating a way for anybody, anytime, anyhow, and anywhere to have access to an alternative higher education. We are a force for greater accessibility, setting up and managing, growth, and equity to everybody through our project. 

We provide technology programs, business, humanities, art and design, fashion and more. To help them better accomplish their ambitions, including massive, element, and simple opportunities on site and online, students can select from a variety of formats and modalities.

More than anything, we agree that free access has the potential to clampdown walls and improve people. 

The platform is designed and hosted by the Centre for eLearning and Information Technology Research, a Zambian  registered Information technology social enterprise company. operated and run by dedicated Zambians, with support from overseas mentors, advisors, donors and investors.

What is driving this project?

We are motivated by our confidence in the ability to improve peoples choices lives in some way through free skills and qualifications development, and are focused on delivering an integrated educational environment that addresses their goals and requirements them reach career aspirations.

We would never be limited by something that already happens and will therefore pave the world in adopting new approaches to accomplish our goal. Everyone's Genetic information is revolutionary and the adventurous attitude that inspires other pioneers is recognized and welcomed.

The comprehensive suite of courses of the Alternative Tertiaries covers all the basic foundations of creativity to provide many options for growth and advancement for individuals and teams.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Firstly, we will be able to generate income from the subscriptions per semester. Then We will also have other direct sales of materials such as exam preparedness, identity kits, and notes. On top of that our website is running google AdSense, which is going to be earning us over $7,000 per month from google depending on the keywords that will be used. We also have additional youtube income on the impassive income plan, by simply producing youtube videos and sharing videos. This will be earning us an additional of $10,000 per month. Our aim is to make tertiary education affordable, by getting leading of lecturers and other unnecessary admin costs. 

Our target market is directly the school leavers, any person who has completed grade 12, and has difficulties attaining tertiary education due to lack of funds or due to high poverty levels. The indirect offtakers are any working professionals who would want to either study further or simply have dropped out and would want to continue with tertiary education.

The challenge prize will help our business to grow and earn leverage in the Zambian market. This is possible through marketing, admin logistical costs, and also establishment of digital inclusion centres in selected provinces. The Prize money will help us employ the right staff in selected position for better growth.

The value addition of innovation on the environment is that it is the first mover, designed in both Web app, and mobile application, with an interface that offer modernity. Our platform creates a free way of learning without any payment at all. the only thing students or any participant would pay for is subscription.  Assessment, in terms of exams and any other form of continuous tasks are handled by the University of Zambia (MOU being signed). This platform will offer a chance to any person, anywhere, anytime and anyhow to access tertiary education regardless of the status quo.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed in form of an online SaaS, optimized with online courses that run in conjoined efforts with the Massive Open Online Courses offered by top leading universities and organizations around the world. Our students will learn freely, from the audited courses provided by foreign universities, and our local team will provide continuous assessment through our chief examiner office. 

The platform is able to offer the best courses ever that will prepare an individual for the future job market, though our curricular emphasize personal growth rather than job seeking. 

The prototype exist and you can access it on https://www.alteriatires.org
