> Title of the innovation

Ticket Express

> Brief description of the innovation

What is Ticket Express ?  
Ticket Express is a website and mobile application that provides event marketing, purchasing, registration and online ticketing services.
Ticket express can be integrated for musical concerts, football matches, sports events, online events  and more.

How it works?
Customers get to buy tickets, food and merchandise, for an event, at the comfort of their home using either the ticket express website or application.
 Payments can be made by either mobile money or bank debit card;payment is secured by Flatterwave(An online payment proccesor).
After the transaction customers are sent a QR code,which will act as a digital ticket for the event, to customer's ticket express account's and email's.
This QR code can be scanned at the event with a scanner.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

HOW PROFITS ARE GENERATED
Ticket express charges a low five percent fee from event owners , for every ticket bought on the website or application.

ADVANTAGES TO THE BUSINESS
Free Digital marketing, as ticket express promotes events for event owners on social media plateforms: Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and Google ads. In addition, engage with customers forming a brand name for event owners.

Lines at the event are reduced, as customers do not need to buy tickets, food or merchandise; because, these are bought in advance in the comfort of their home or elsewhere.

Less staff is needed at the checkout , reducing costs to doing business.

Reduction to the cost of printing tickets , as QR codes are used instead of physical tickets.

Ticket express sends out data matrices, showing customer's interest in an event; consequently, enabling event planners to make informed decisions about:seat allocations-which can be useful during COVID times-,merchandise,food,drinks or whether to raise awarness for an event,if ticket sells are low.

ADVANTAGES TO CUSTOMERS
Booking and buying of tickets is more coinvent for customers;as they can easily make payments at home, rather than going to retail stores or the event sellers.
Customers do not have to stand in long lines, in order to buy tickets, food or merchandise before an event.

Customers immidaitelty know if seats to an event are available.

Customers benefit from frequent or early purchase discounts.

> Technical Feasibility

THE DESIGN OF THE WEBSITE AND APPLICATION
The application and website is made using the following programming languages:Python , Html , CSS and MySQL. It is hosted on Google Firebase - a cloud platform for hosting websites and applications that provides serivces such as customer database management and authentication and emailing- which eliminates the cost of purchasing servers,since the website and application runs on the cloud.Furthermore, as the business grows and more customers use the website and application Google Firebase automatically scales the serivces the business requires.
