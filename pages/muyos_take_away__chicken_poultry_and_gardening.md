> Brief description of the innovation

Muyos take away is dedicated in providing customers with decent foods ranging from baked, roasted and fried foods as well as nshima. To reduce on coast we have embarked on chicken rearing as well as garden of fresh vegetables to supplement the take away

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We will sell various food products to our customers  (students, lecturers and the community at large). The profit raised will be invested in chicken rearing. The manure from birds will then be used in gardening to beef up the take away. Chicken reared and vegetables will be sold to other take aways and the community at large in serenge and chitambo districts. Women and youths will be engaged as a way of empowering them. The challenge prize will help us to bring these ideas to fruition. Once the business Brosom, will empower the venerable children by sending them to school.
