The first learning with entertainment platform of its kind in Iraq and the Middle East, which teaches programming in a simple and fun way for children by designing and programming games, interactive stories and animation, as well as building engineering projects, artificial intelligence, principles of software and algorithms. Nevertheless, some of the courses targets the climate issues and renewable energy in fun and creative way.
