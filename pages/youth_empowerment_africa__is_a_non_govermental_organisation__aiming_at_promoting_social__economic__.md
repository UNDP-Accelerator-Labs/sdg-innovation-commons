> Brief description of the innovation

The youth empowerment Africa abbreviated as (YEA). As a national professional based youth organisation, does hereby dedicate itself to increasing the participation of young people in the fields of socialeconomic development, professional careers as well as giving back to the community. As a part of the YEA's mandate, programs are initiated both within and outside the school community, which serve to strengthen relations between industry and the community. Members of this organization are encouraged to join and participate in activities of their schools/communities and those professional societies advocating their respective discipline of their careers. This organization endeavor to provide a nurturing atmosphere that foster the growth of members professional, academic, leadership and social skills. With the aim of promoting knowledge transfer to the prospective less previledged youths.
