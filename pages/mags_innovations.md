> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

if electricity supplier where to reach the furthest parts of Zambia,things like climate change and the over whelming cutting of tree for charcoal would by reduced e.g Mobomshi dam project of Chisamba district of Central province.As that more customer base for Zesco and employment for the local masses.

> Technical Feasibility

when you consider line work,observe tree lines so that trees are not cut unnecessarily and line through boundaries thus in fire traps.it is done in three phases.phase one-bush clearring and excavations
            phase two-electing and stringing
            phase three-connecting to the national grid

> Anticipated Social Impact

Since we live in a advanced era,access to information and education as gone to the world wide web.Electiricity is cardinal in our daily lives hence every Zambian should equal access to Electricity, in town or village.leading to improved health care to all and service delivery.
