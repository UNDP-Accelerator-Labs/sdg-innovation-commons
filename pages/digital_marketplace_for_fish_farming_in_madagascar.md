> Description of the solution

The Sustainable Aquaculture in Madagascar project has launched a digital marketplace to connect fish farmers with buyers. This marketplace uses a hotline and SMS notifications to facilitate transactions. The project has also launched an awareness campaign to promote the use of the hotline.
