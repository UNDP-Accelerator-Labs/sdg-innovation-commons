> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Besides improving social economic position, a higher income allows for greater access to goods and services that provides health benefits such as, Better foods and housing, additional health options and greater choice in health pursuits.
Money allows us to meet our basic needs to buy food and shelter and also pay for health care. Meeting these needs is essential and if we do not have enough money to do so, our personal well being and the well being of the community will all suffer . 
Income is associated with mental health compared with people from families who earn more than $100,000 a year, those with family income below $35,000 per year are four times more likely to report being nervous and five times more likely to report sadness .
