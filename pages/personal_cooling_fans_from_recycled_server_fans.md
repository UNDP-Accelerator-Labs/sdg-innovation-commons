> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

These are upcycled server fans that are made into personal cooling fans. Innovators rigged the power input to allow easy operation by anyone without having to overheat the fan motor.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

The need to have affordable personal cooling device.

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

The solution is for those who live in small cramped rooms and apartments in the warm Manila City.  This is a good alternative with a lower electricity consumption to the expensive  air conditioning units.  The device is also small in size which makes it portable.

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

This means that access to cooling devices in a hot and humid Manila is needed by people. 

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

There is potential in having these cooling device to be produced en masse providing a cheaper alternative to existing appliances such as electric fans and air conditioning units. It is also more portable and more affordable than the existing solution.
