> Brief description about the identified local solution:

An Iraqi Platform that provides multiple services to the students and graduates in different fields such us; providing virtual training on 3D printing by using the virtual reality technique also building the capacities of students on the latest programming languages. Their goal is to support the graduation projects of students and encourage them to use the 3D printings. in other words is to build new ecosystem for 3D printing users. They are finding worth solving problems and provide a proper solution for it by using the 3D Printing.
