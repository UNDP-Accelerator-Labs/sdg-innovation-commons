## Design
**Explain the design of the experimental activity. In general, experimental activities consist on trying to learn how results are connected to a stimuli.**

> What is the specific learning intent of the activity?

 “How might we have learnings on career counseling so as to inform the overall career guidance strategy?”

> What is your hypothesis? IF... THEN....

IF the "short term guide" is developed and made accessible to youths inclusive of school students THEN the youths, parents and community will  have a sense of alternate success pathways to future careers. 

> Does the activity use a control group for comparison?

- Don't know

> How is the intervention assigned to different groups in your experiment?

- other

> Describe which actions will you take to test your hypothesis:

1. Develop capacity of newly formed "Information and Career Guidance Unit" employees, MoLHR  2. Short term guide developed by using knowledge gained from capacity building 3. Get buy in from Ministry of Labour & Human Resources to experimenting short term guide 4. Rapid assessment of the career 

> What is the unit of analysis of this experimental activity?

youth groups 

> Please describe the data collection technique proposed

Observation, crowdsourcing and sense making
