> Title of the innovation

Fish farming and food production .our aim is to recycle everything in a systematic way so as in to make agricultural revolution in Zambia.

> Brief description of the innovation

Systematic desensitization of fish ponds placed in a way that will provide the drained waste water from the pond to the vegetable gardens and the orchard as well as the lawns
