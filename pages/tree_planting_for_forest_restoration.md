> Title of the innovation

Tree Planting for Forest Restoration

> Brief description of the innovation

Raising of tree seedlings is an innovation that will in turn create environment that will be conducive. At the same time it will be an income for all those who Will be involved. Denude forest areas will be rehabilitated.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Income will be generated through the sell of seedlings to farmer and at the same time those who will be involved will earn an income.
If warded the innovation will be upscaled and a variety of tree seedlings will be raised
Target market will be small and commercial farmers and institutions involved in environmental  management.
With the ever increase in environmental degradation tree planting will assist in the  reversing the trend
