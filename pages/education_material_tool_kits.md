Teach for Malaysia (a Malaysian Non-Profit Organisation) sends monthly box of resources to hard-to-reach students in rural areas. Purpose is to invest in educational recovery to ensure that the long term effects of the pandemic is minimized, especially for rural and remote communities that lack digital access.
