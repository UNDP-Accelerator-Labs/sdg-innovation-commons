> Description of the solution

MAP is positioned as an "agricultural value chain co-creation and sharing platform", building a bridge between consumption upgrading and new-type farmers, and providing the whole industry chain service that combines online and offline and covers the whole process of agricultural production and sales for the vast number of growers and food value chain partners.It helps farmers increase income, improve industrial efficiency, and bring benefits to consumers, leading the development of agricultural modernization and digital innovation.
