> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Tamarind is a naturally grown plant which can be a value addition to the Zambian economy.Potential target market are retail outlets that stocks various beverages. It will be as good as a first or second best choice of beverage that can be enjoyed anywhere at anytime which can also be taken as a cider. The value addition is to process the tamarind pods into a refreshment beverage that can be consumed by anyone.

> Technical Feasibility

Since tamarind is a naturally grown plant,it needs to be sourced from different parts of Zambia and processed. The implementation plan will include hiring the right labour to work on processing the pods and buying a juice making machine. Work on the branding and partner with different retailers to stock the commodity.

> Anticipated Social Impact

There is a potential for business growth as that tamarind contains amazing health benefits that can boosts the immune system and combat diseases such as diabetes,flu and coughs and help lower blood pressure and also help reduce weight.  It is relevant to the current market in that it will provide a second best choice for an healthy beverage to those that are currently on the market.
