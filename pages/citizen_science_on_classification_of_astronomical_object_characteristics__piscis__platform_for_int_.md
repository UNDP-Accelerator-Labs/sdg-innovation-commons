## Overall goal

Classify collaboratively visual information about astronomical objects and phenomena. 
## Specific goals
## Description of citizen participation

Conduct and validate surveys on the classification of astronomical objects and phenomena to promote citizen engagement in scientific research in the field of astronomy. 

Develop training strategies for the general public to participate in a collaborative classification of astronomical objects and phenomena.
