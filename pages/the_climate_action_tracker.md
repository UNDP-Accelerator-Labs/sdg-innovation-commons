> Description of the solution

The Climate Action Tracker (CAT) is an independent scientific tool that assesses government climate actions against the Paris Agreement's targets. It evaluates national climate mitigation efforts, predicts future emissions impacts, and identifies emissions gaps to guide global warming mitigation strategies.

> Socio-economic effects

CAT informs policymakers and the public, promoting informed decisions that drive effective climate policies, potentially reducing global warming impacts and supporting sustainable development
