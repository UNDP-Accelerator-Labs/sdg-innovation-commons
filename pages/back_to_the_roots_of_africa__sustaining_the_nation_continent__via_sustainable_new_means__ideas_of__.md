> Brief description of the innovation

It has been noted with no doubt , that many people , have interest in venturing in agriculture , but not without difficult. Our innovation is centered on tackling these failures, doubt and fears encountered by people due to non sustainable means of agriculture. Our innovation , has new sustainable , satisfying ideas of farming, beginning from the soil  and plants. This is  by use of already known  resources to create something new. For example , special tree chips to improve both soil and plants health.
