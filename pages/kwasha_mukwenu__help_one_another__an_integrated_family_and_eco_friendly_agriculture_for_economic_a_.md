> Title of the innovation

Kwasha Mukwenu (Help One Another) An integrated family and Eco friendly Agriculture for economic and environmental security for the women, youth and the poor but viable communities.

> Brief description of the innovation

Kwasha Mukwenu (Help One Another) will be a local initiative in Kalumbila District in Zambia established to improve the welfare of the local people especially the women youth, people living with disadvantages through income generating enterprises.
The initiative will be implemented alongside Alemu Ecological farms and Milling Limited with the assistance from National innovation initiative challenge.
Alemu Ecological farms and Milling Limited was formed in 2017. Now the farm is under its development phase-:
a. Construction of the farm infrastructures e.g Crop fields, Piggery, vegetable garden, fodder, forage and pastures, poultry, apiary, fish ponds, vermicomposting e.t.c.
b. 20 Ha land have been bought on traditional land.
With the establishment of Alemu economical farms and Milling ltd, the company sees women, the youth and the disadvantages people in Kalumbila District who are the main care takers of the families hope to increase their family income and supporting their children.
Over 80 percent of women in Zambia are small scale farmers who are under absolute poverty but have the drive to work with restricted capital.

THE STRATEGIES OF THE KWASHA MUKWENU (HELP ONE ANOTHER) INITIATIVE 
-Improve the economic conditions of women youth and the viable but vulnerable people in the Kalumbila Community.
-Train the women, youths and the vulnerable but  viable people income generative activities in order to generate enough money to support their families.
-To set up a demonstration farm where all the beneficiaries will be trained in the management of the various farm activities and seek information.
-Train all beneficiaries to acquire more efficient management skills in Agricultural production, harvesting, processing and marketing.
-Help women, youths and the viable but vulnerable farmers to objectively focus on the needs of the family and the demands of the market and promote high value market oriented crops and animals.
-Providing improved varieties of planting materials and breeds of animals that quick maturing and high yielding under average systems of management.
-Community Mobilization and Sensitization of the people to actively participate in the various project components. In particular to encourage women and the youth farmers to voluntarily form small farmer group/associations and societies based on the interest  themselves have in each enterprises.
-Alemu Ecological farms and Milling Ltd will also organise the farmers to bring their produce of the Farm Centre and organise marketing.
-These activities will assist the government and non governmental organisation in the campaign in relation to-:
