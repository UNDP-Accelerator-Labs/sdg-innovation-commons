> Title of the innovation

The Big Idea company

> Brief description of the innovation

The Big Idea company is a tech and innovation hub that has produced 2 software product, brainbox and penda.cloud. Brainbox is an e-learning platform for professionals and penda.cloud is a crm developed for Zambian business owners. TBIC is looking to create more innovations aimed at making life easier for Zambians

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The hub will nurture tech entrepreneurs and help them get their product to market. We have successfully sold our inhouse products to more than 10 clients.

> Technical Feasibility

We have designed it in collaboration with partners in south Africa, India and the USA. We have 2 working systems available for showcase

> Anticipated Social Impact

We have the potential to create employment and offer technology training to thousands of citizens. We have the capacity to educate millions through our systems and help thousands of businesses manage their finances.
