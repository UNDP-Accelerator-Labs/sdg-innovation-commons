> Title of the innovation

LIMAHS MENSTRUAL HYGIENE PROJECT

> Brief description of the innovation

Limahs menstrual hygiene is a project that is aimed at creating a clean health environment for women in terms of their menstrual hygiene. 
Education and health are part of this project as the team will have educative programs in various schools in solwezi. 
Giving out of pads will be pad of the project as to reduce the gab between social economic factor and menstrual hygiene.
A radio programme will also be part of the project so as to reach out to alot of people in the community 
Its a non profitable project with a long term plan.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Limahs menstrual hygiene is a non profitable project which has a long term plan hence there will be need for a source of income that is we will have shop outlets specifically for menstrual hygiene products with prices that will be affordable to everyone.
Our targeted market is the community in particular the female sex as the project is particularly for females.
Menstrual hygiene also looks at climate change as it is important for women to know how and where to expose used products of menstrual hygiene such as pads.
