> Description of the solution

Ice Bank Energy Storage offers a range of Ice-based Cooling System Thermal Cooling System energy storage systems for commercial and industrial applications. The systems can help to reduce energy costs and peak demand, and are suitable for a range of industries, including data centers, hospitals, and food processing.
