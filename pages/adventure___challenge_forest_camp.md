## Solution holder: Thaer Ayyash

Jordan Ranger camp is your nature escape, offering a wide spectrum of overnight accommodation in wooden cabins and treehouses.

The camp also offers traditional food, yoga sessions, and outdoor adventures including zip-line, tower climbing, horseback riding, and campfire squire.

Revive your adventurous spirit at Ranger Camp!
