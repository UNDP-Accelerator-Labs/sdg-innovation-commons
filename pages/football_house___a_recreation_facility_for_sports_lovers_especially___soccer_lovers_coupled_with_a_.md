> Title of the innovation

FOOTBALL HOUSE ; A RECREATION FACILITY FOR SPORTS LOVERS ESPECIALLY , SOCCER LOVERS COUPLED WITH A REFRESHMENT COKETAIL BAR FOR SOFT DRINKS TO BE BOUGHT ON MANDATE .

> Brief description of the innovation

THIS IS A SOCIAL FACILITY  CALLED A FOOTBALL HOUSE THAT IS MEANT FOR SOCCER LOVERS WITH A TASTE FOR A NEAT AND SOOTHING ENVIRONMENT FOR WATCHING SOCCER OR OR SPORTS OF THEIR CHOICE   ON DSTV WHILE ENJOYING COOL SOFT DRINKS OR REFRESHMENTS AS THEY RELAX AND SUPPORT THEIR TEAMS PLAYING WHILE THEY SOCIALISE.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

THE BUSINESS WILL BRING INCOME BY CHARGING A FIXED FEE PER MATCH, THUS IF THE FIXED FEE IS K5 FOR EXAMPLE , AND HAVE 40 CUSTOMERS ON A PARTICULAR DAY  AND  FOUR BIG TEAMS WILL BE PLAYING, IT MEANS THEIR WILL BE TWO BIG MATCHES AND EACH CUSTOMER WILL HAVE TO PAY A K10 FOR THE TWO MATCHES , NOW 40 CUSTOMERS MULTIPLIED BY 10 =400  ON A PARTICULAR DAY. THE K400 MULTIPLIED BY SAY 4 DAYS OF GOOD SOCCER MATCHES IN A WEEK =1600. PROFIT SHALL ALSO BE MADE BY SELLING SOFT DRINKS ON MANDATE TO CUSTOMER WHO WISH TO WATCH SOCCER .BUSINESSES SUCH AS THE TRANSPORT BUSINESS WILL BENEFIT BY KEEPING CUSTOMERS ENTERTAINED AS THEY WAIT AS WELL AS THOSE WITH PRIVATE TOILETS  AND RESTAURANTS AND LODGES. MAIN TARGET CUSTOMERS ARE LOCAL PEOPLE  , THE YOUTH AS WELL AS THE ELDERLY WHO LOVE SOCCER, AND ALSO TRAVELLERS  LIKELY TO BE AROUND TOWN FOR SOME TIME .CHALLENGE PRIZE WILL ASSIST IN FULL FILLING THIS INNOVATION AS WELL AS EXPAND AND IMPROVE THE BUSINESS TO VERY ATTRACTIVE AND CONDUSSIVE ENVIRONMENT FOR CUSTOMERS.

> Technical Feasibility

THE INNOVATION SHALL BE THE FIRST IF ITS KIND IN TOWN AS THERE IS CURRENTLY NO LEGITIMATE PLACE FOR WATCHING SOCCER  EXCEPT FOR SMALL BARS  WITH LIMITED SPACE AND LESS REGARDS TO PUBLIC HEALTH GUIDELINES DUE .THIS IS AN ADVANTAGE TO THE BUSINESS BY CREATING A STRESS FREE SPACE WITH ENOUGH ROOM FOR CUSTOMERS  IN A FAST GROWING TOWN .

> Anticipated Social Impact

THE BUSINESS SHALL SERVE AS A PLACE OF SOCIALISING PEOPLE , ALLOWING THEM TO EXCHANGE IDEAS , MEET NEW PEOPLE AND SHARE THE JOY OF WATCHING SOCCER IN A MORAO BUILDING ENVIRONMENT THAT LEAVES THEM NOSTALGIC. A  PLACE THAT DISCONNECTS THE MAJORITY FROM THE STRESS OF THEIR DAILY ROUTINE.
THIS SOCIAL AMENITY SHALL ENHANCE OTHER BUSINESSES SUCH AS RESTAURANTS  , PRIVATE TOILETS THAT ARE FEE PAYING AS WELL AS MAKE IT EASY FOR CUSTOMERS TO SEE AND NOTICE OTHER BUSINESSBUSINESSES NEARBY AS THEY PATRONIZE THE AREA.
