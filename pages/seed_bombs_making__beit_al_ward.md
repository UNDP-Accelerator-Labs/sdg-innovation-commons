## Solution holder: Wijdan Malkawi

Beit Al Ward - translates to 'House of Flowers'. Your journey will begin in Beit Al Ward, where a couple had decided to transform their own garden into a beautiful greenhouse with over 400 different types of plants.

Spend an hour with Wijdan - your host, and the owner of Beit Al Ward - where you will be welcomed and briefed about this wonderful initiative. This will be followed by a tour of the lovely home garden and an informative activity on how to make seed bombs.

You may take the seed bomb up on a hiking/biking trip and leave it in the soil of nature to grow once it has rained, or take it back home and plant it!
