## Tambran by Tamara is an eco-jewelry brand, based in Grenada. In the Caribbean, approximately 350 million tons of municipal waste materials are generated annually, contributing to many environmental problems and increase in climate change issues. Tambran by Tamara transforms waste recyclable materials such as plastic bottles, vinyl, etc and locally-sourced natural materials, such as tamarind and donkey-eye seeds, into one of a kind wearable jewelry and art-cessories.
