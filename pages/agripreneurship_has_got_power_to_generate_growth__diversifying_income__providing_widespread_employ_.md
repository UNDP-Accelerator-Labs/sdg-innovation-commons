> Title of the innovation

Agripreneurship has got power to generate growth, diversifying income, providing widespread employment and entrepreneurial opportunities in rural areas.

> Brief description of the innovation

Agripreneurship refers to entrepreneurship in agriculture. But entrepreneurship, as opposed to self-employment, is also defined by the spirit of the entrepreneurs. Entrepreneurs are usually creative, take opportunities and accept risks, and can quickly change business strategies to adapt to changing environments and Agripreneurship is the key to help local farmers commercialize their products and compete with global market stakeholders and big companies!. It also provides value addition to agricultural resources, farmers by skill-development and information.
