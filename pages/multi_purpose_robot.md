> Brief description about the identified local solution:

Multi-purpose moving on-ground wireless robot, with a multi-purpose attachment for delivery and telecommunication useful for quarantine hospitals and non-medical delivery jobs to provide human distancing. Features: 
-        On-ground moving robot 
-        150 wireless 
-        CCTV & voice wireless communication - +100Kg useful package load weight 
-        User friendly & easy-to-control 
-        Customizable design can be designed to fit with any custom needs (carrying food, medications, goods, etc.) 
-        Lithium batteries for long heavy-duty usage.
For medical staff protection in quarantine hospital delivery purposes, our solution is based on replacing human resource to do simple delivery jobs by robots, with customizable attachments & design to fit multi-purpose & diverse loads, shapes, and sizes, it is easy to control with minimal learning & experience needs, with audio & video communication features.
