> Title of the innovation

Mobile Teaching

> Brief description of the innovation

Mobile teaching is a system that involves a teacher following registered learners to their various homes to deliver effective learning. Mobile teacher generates money through the payments made by the registered learners. Mobile teaching involves everyone who's interested but it targets learners who can not access effective learning due to distances to the tuition center. It also targets disabled learners who can not have access to effective learning due to their conditions. The Money paid by the registered learners helps the teacher in terms of transport of going to and from various homes of the registered learners. The money also helps the business to grow in terms of materials and involving other teachers to become mobile teachers.

> Technical Feasibility

Mobile teaching is designed in a way which involves teachers moving to and from different places of registered learners.
The implementation plan for mobile teaching is that learners are registered from various homes or areas and then the parents for the registered learners are given specific time when the teacher will come and deliver the lessons.
