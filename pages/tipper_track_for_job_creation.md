> Title of the innovation

Tipper Track for job creation

> Brief description of the innovation

Looking at the income generated from the use of the tipper track, I will be able to raise funds for Agriculture Business.  This will in turn create jobs and wealth for the people in the community.  By so doing I will help conquer poverty and hunger and improve the health of the people in the area and finally the country at large.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The tipper business is very profitable.  When contracted to a company, its use or operations can create quite a lot of funds within a short time to enable me to start the said agricultural business.

> Technical Feasibility

A 25 tonner truck. diesel propelled.  Once I get the track, I will apply for a contract use to a company.  The company will pay me for the hire of the track and the driver will get his monthly salary from the generated income. the other money will enable me to buy land where I will begin the agriculture business.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Firstly, the driver and his family and relative will benefit from the monthly salary. Then, the people employed to work at the farm shall also benefit from their wages.  The lasting impact shall be the knowledge they will get through the training they will acquire in agriculture.  Lastly, the people will no longer live in poverty, they will always have food on their tables which will improve their healthy.
