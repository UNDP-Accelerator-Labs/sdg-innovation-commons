> Brief description of the innovation

At the poultry farm we plan to bred broilers and slaughter them take to our Chicken Shack and easily prepare them for our customers to consume.
The oil production/refinery produces sunflower oil which is taken to our fast food and the used oil is brought back for refinery and later packed for sale on the market.
Note: Our oil machines will be making cooking oil for other oil seed farmers and the remaining cake flower is used to feed our chicken.
