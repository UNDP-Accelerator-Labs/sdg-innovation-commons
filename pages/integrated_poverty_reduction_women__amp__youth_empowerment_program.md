> Title of the innovation

Integrated poverty reduction women &amp; youth empowerment program

> Brief description of the innovation

Its a women &amp; youth led group in agriculture &amp; aquaculture aims to improve the living standards of people through job creation, poverty reduction, hunger, climate change &amp; women empowerment by providing sustainable life skills in women &amp; youths
