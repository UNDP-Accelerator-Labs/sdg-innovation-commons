> Brief description of the innovation

WMH is an online Bus Ticket Reservation System which is a Web based application that works within a centralized network. th WMH is an  “Online Bus Ticket Reservation ” that should be used in a bus transportation system, which is used to reserve seats, cancellation of reservation and different types of route enquiries used on securing quick reservations.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

as bus terminals become even more saturated by the day putting our system in place will at least decongest the bus terminals by a substantial amount to reduce the spread of diseases/illnesses furthermore it will help bus coach businesses with how accurate and efficient they run their businesses by making tasks such as record taking and accounting for their money quick and easy moreover with customers or end users they'll be able to swiftly book, reserve and cancel tickets aswell as select a seat of their choosing. this will ensure that all  funds coming towards the business are accounted for.

> Technical Feasibility

fundamentaly our innovation is designed to book, reserve and cancel tickets as well track the current location(live) of the bus that has been booked (if it's not by the terminal at the time it's been booked) however the location will only be available to the staff at their respective terminal offices .
