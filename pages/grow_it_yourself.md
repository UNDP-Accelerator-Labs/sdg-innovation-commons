Grow It Yourself, GIY, is a start-up specialized in soil-less agriculture that aims to improve the yields of sustainable agriculture in urban and rural areas. As an initiator of social innovations, GIY manufactures, installs and maintains turnkey hydroponic systems that enable the cultivation of food products without pesticides. Concerned about the environment, GIY strengthens the skills of local actors through a range of training programs adapted to modern agriculture.
