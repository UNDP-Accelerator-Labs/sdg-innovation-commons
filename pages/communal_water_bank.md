This app creates a personal cabinet for users on water use and also educates them on how to use water responsibly.


By entering the code of each meter and determines the waste of water used during the month compared to other months. Given that the average monthly water consumption is 20 cubic meters (not standard), the subscriber will receive bonuses for each savings in the coming months will be used for other utilities (electricity and gas). 


Also, the service has online payment system and an info center service.
