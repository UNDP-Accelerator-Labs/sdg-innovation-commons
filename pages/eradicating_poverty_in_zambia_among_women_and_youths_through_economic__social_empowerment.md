> Title of the innovation

ERADICATING POVERTY IN ZAMBIA AMONG WOMEN AND YOUTHS THROUGH ECONOMIC, SOCIAL EMPOWERMENT

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation is all about eradicating poverty in Zambia especially among women, firstly like the saying when you educate a girl child, you are empowering the nation. women are care-takers of the nation in the sense that they look after everyone and everything. Therefore, poverty in Zambia can cab be eradicated through empowerment and this an be done by supporting women in any way we can. contribute towards the reduction of unemployment, among the youth and women, through the creation of employment opportunities in a number of areas, including agribusiness value chains. The direct beneficiaries of the Project are the 5,000 women and youth targeted in 72 districts of the country.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Income will be generated through empowerment of loans to do agriculture, fish farming and livestock businesses. Our Target market are chain stores. The challenge prize will help the business grow in the sense that it will cater for another huge target of youths and women who are struggling to earn a living by so doing it will bring about economic, social and culture development in our country Zambia.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed in such a way that it empowers youths and women by giving them loans to uplift their standards. the proposed plan is to return the loans when they start generating income so that another group of 10,000 women and youths can also benefit from the same to eradicate poverty in Zambia.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Empowered youths and women can help to achieve a sustained economic growth and sustainable development, through education, health care and employment. They, also, have the potential to integrate economic, cultural and social policies to achieve a better life for all.
