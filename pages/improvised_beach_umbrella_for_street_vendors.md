> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

Extra large umbrella recycled from old beach umbrellas.  The innovator reinforced the umbrella spine with wires and used sack-type canvas for durability.  Resembling beach umbrellas, these are sold to vendors who sell on the side streets.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

Affordable and durable large umbrellas to protect products and vendor from the elements.

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

Street vendors in the market

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

Recycling is a good way to improve a product from old flimsy beach umbrellas sold in the market. By understanding what the needs of the vendors are, the innovator is able to provide a product that is durable and affordable.

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

The solution can be a way to help recycle "disposable" mass market umbrellas that are currently sold in the market.
