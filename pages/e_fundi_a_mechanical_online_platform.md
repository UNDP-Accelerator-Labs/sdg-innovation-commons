e-fundi provides a quick and easy way to access to authentic and professional mechanical services through an online application.
e-Fundi is an online platform that aims at a world where everyone has immediate access to mechanical services at the touch of a button. We are doing this through creating an online application that connects car drivers and car owners to nearby reliable, authentic and professional mechanics for  mechanical services for car maintenance and repair
