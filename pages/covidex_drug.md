Approved by Uganda's  National Drug Authority (NDA), COVIDEX is a product formulated from herbal plants that have been traditionally used to alleviate symptoms of several diseases including CoViD-19. It is only available by purchase through registered clinics, pharmacies and drug stores regulated by Uganda's NDA, the product should only be used it is unexpired and sealed.   The product which was developed by Prof. Patrick Ogwang, is an oral consumption medication only to be used by adults (6 drops, 3 times daily) children, ages 5 - 12 years (3 drops 3 times daily) and those younger than 5 years (1 drop, 3 times daily). Consult with a medical doctor before use of this product as it is not WHO approved yet.
