Open burning practice in Ukraine is responsible for most of the seasonal air pollution in Autumn and Spring. Valentyn Frechka designed and patented a technology to transform fallen leaves into the paper. It may be one of the reasons for people to shift their understanding of the fallen leaves: from garbage that needs to be burnt to the applicable material.
