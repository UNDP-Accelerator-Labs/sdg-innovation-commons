> Innovation Solution Description 

Digital finance solutions for loans and finance.

> Qualitative Results of the Innovation

1. Increased access to credit: MIGO's digital lending platform provides quick and easy access to credit for individuals and small businesses that are typically underserved by traditional banks. 
2. Increased savings: MIGO's savings product allows customers to save their money in a digital wallet, providing a secure and convenient way to manage their finances. 
3. Increased financial literacy: MIGO's services are designed to be simple and user-friendly.
4. Increased financial inclusion: MIGO's services are accessible through mobile devices, making them available to a larger population of Nigerians, particularly those in rural areas where traditional financial institutions may not have a presence

> Quantitative Result of the Innovation

1. According to the company's data, since its inception,Since its inception, MIGO has disbursed over $400 million in loans to over 4 million customers, with an average loan size of around $80.
