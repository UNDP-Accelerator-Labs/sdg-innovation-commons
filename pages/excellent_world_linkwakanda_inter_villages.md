> Title of the innovation

Excellent World Link/Wakanda Inter-Villages

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation is designed to reduce or eliminate bad loans or debts citizens of Zambia are facing from banks and other private financial lending individuals, entities or institutions which has enabled their monthly income to be suppressed or stressed or reduced and alot of citizens are struggling to survive in homes thus poverty,crime and violence increase in communities (remember crime perpetrator come from family homes). Because of low monthly income manufacturing, wholesale and retail business have been crippled because people are consuming low and the production chain stressed thus low employment for youths and women.

This business project pays off the bad loans or debts on behalf of citizens so that the remain debt free and improve their lives economic thus reducing poverty and violence in communities. Also revamping the chain of production to make more sales, generate more revenue and create employment for women and youths and improving the business for banks and financial lending institutions.
