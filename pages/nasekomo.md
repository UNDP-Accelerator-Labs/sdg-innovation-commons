> Description of the solution

Nasekomo is a company that produces high-quality protein products from insects, specifically the Black Soldier Fly. They use these insects to convert organic waste into valuable resources like protein meal, insect oil, and fertilizer. This innovative approach promotes sustainable agriculture and reduces environmental impact.
