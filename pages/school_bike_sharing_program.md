> Type of the Solution

Infrastructure &amp; Program Development

> Affected Sector

Transport, Education, Public Health

> Description of the solution

Schools partner with organizations to implement bike-sharing programs that provide students with access to bicycles for commuting, recreation, or educational purposes. Programs may involve secure bike storage, helmet use promotion, and basic bike maintenance education.

> Socio-economic effects

Increased physical activity, reduced traffic congestion, potential program costs

> Other Notes

Schools/Public-Private Partnerships lead the solution. Requires secure bike storage facilities, partnerships with bike-sharing organizations, and potential safety training for students.
