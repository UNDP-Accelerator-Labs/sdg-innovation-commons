> Brief description of the innovation

We are fully registered with pacra and the national Art council of zambia.We produce Artworks using different media such as found objects eg tires, stones, Clay and metals.reduce cutting down of trees.we make sculpture, painting, ceramic, crafts and other forms of Art.we also have a group of young ones that we educate on these skills.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will generate income through Artworks that will be sold it will also work closely with ministry of tourism and the municipal council for the purpose of aquiring contract to mount public Art works. The innovation will also work with the private institution in getting contracts and more jobs will be created.

> Technical Feasibility

Our innovation is designed In Such a way that it is condusive to all age group and we work anyway were where our service are needed.

> Anticipated Social Impact

We are community centered in our operation. We create a situation where youths come together and inspair one another through motivation talks and transfer of skills from one child to another through mobilizing an art exhibition that attracts a good number of people even higher profile individuals from government and private sector.
