Creating Employment and Economic Growth. Our goal is to be given opportunities to manufacture locally produced goods using local materials to create livelihood,Employment,financial fitness through this business venture.We hope to tap into the cotton industry and indigenous crop industry to provide exportable products such as locally produced clothes, preserved traditional vegetables and so forth.
