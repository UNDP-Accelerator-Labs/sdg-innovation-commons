> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Through this Restaurant Business, I will need to support my fellow Small,Medium Entrepreneurs who lay chickens,grow vegetables and so on by buying my Stocks from them hence support their business which in turn will build and grow  the economy. My target is to provide good meals to people who are far from, outside catering for weddings, meals for Conferences and other gatherings. Our method of cooking should be Environmental friendly, instead of using charcoal or fire woods which are the by products of the destroyed nature cutting down trees to be specific, I'm looking forward to use Gas and Electricity as our method of cooking. The Challenge Prize will boost my Capital which I have struggled to raise for the past years due to the hardships and low wages we are subjected to.
