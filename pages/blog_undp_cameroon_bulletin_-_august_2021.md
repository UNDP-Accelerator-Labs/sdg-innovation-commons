UNDP Cameroon Bulletin - August 2021
SEPTEMBER 22, 2021
Just in !
Latest edition of UNDP Cameroon in Action. It is a quarterly publication of the United Nations Development Programme-UNDP. This edition highlights UNDP’s key contributions to Cameroon’s development efforts. Amongst other things, it details how and why the second phase of the Regional Stabilization Facility of the Lake Chad Basin has to be scaled up, how life is gradually returning to normalcy in Amchedi. It also peeps into the upcoming Governors forum and how UNDP is providing life-saving assistance to unemployed Youth.
For More information, Contact Fajong Joseph Lereh, National Communications and Advocacy Expert, UNDP Cameroon via email: joseph.fajong@undp.org
