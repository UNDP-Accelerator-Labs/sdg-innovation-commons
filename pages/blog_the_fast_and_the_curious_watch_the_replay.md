The Fast and the Curious: Local innovations to accelerate progress towards the 2030 AgendaThe Qatar Fund for Development and UNDP hosted, on July 14th 2020, a virtual event to reflect on the wild first year of the world's fastest learning network on development challenges. Watch the replay below.To Accelerate Progress, We Need to be Fast and Curious
In this time of planetary crisis, best practices don’t always exist. We need to learn what works and what doesn’t for sustainable development problems. We need to source innovations from many places. We need to experiment. And we need to do it fast.Thank you to all those who joined during the event on July 14th.
