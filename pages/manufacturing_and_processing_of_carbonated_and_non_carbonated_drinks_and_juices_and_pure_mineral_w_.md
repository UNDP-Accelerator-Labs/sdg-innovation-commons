> Title of the innovation

manufacturing and processing of carbonated and non carbonated drinks and juices and pure mineral water

> Brief description of the innovation

Setting up a manufacturing plant for non carbonated juices and drinks, carbonated drinks and pure mineral water at the already secured land in Ndola

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We will wholesale our products to mega supermarkets, retailers country wide and export to the southern region and beyond. We plan to employ about hundred Zambians. We have sources of quality ingredients and franchise to compete favorably on the local and foreign market.
