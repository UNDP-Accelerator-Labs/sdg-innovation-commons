Aelan means "Island" in Bislama, the language of Vanuatu. Aelan Chocolate Makers was born from a passion for cocoa and artisanal manufacturing. The business is based in Vanuatu and uses hand-farmed cocoa that is locally grown. The chocolate bars are infused with a variety of flavors such as Turmeric, Chili pepper, ginger, sea salt, Coconut, Nangai, Coffee and Cocoa nibs.Â
