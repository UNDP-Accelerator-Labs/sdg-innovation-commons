> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation is an online criminal complaint to help the community in filling in their complaints without visiting the police station.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation will bring employment to the ICT engineers and by working in hand with the Ministry of Home Affairs the youths (both men and women) will get employed.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation and prototype is a web-based system that will be accessed from anywhere and at any time.  The plan will only be implemented only if I manage to get a domain name and the Police under the Ministry of Home Affairs are able to support the innovation.

> Anticipated Social Impact

In this time of COVID-19, the innovation will reduce the number of cases and will help/improve the lives of others by increasing the number employment.
