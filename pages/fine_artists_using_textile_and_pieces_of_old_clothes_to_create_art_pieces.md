Hiyam Slaman a fine artist from Latakia, Syria, is using recycled materials and fabric scraps to create her art pieces. she collects leftovers materials from quilting and sewing projects and combines them with hand and machine embroidery to create collaged wall hangings, table cloths, artistic masks, and other artistic pieces. Through her artwork, Hiyam is able to generate an extra income that supports her family. when we interviewed her she said " I believe that anyone who has the passion to create new things from leftovers and who have artistic skills can make a successful small business that can generate a decent income"
