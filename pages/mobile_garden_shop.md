> TITLE: Please provide a title for the solution you observed.

Mobile Garden Shop

> DESCRIPTION: What did you observe, or what did people write or say?

Despite that stores are closed during Sundays due Covid-19, people are looking for creative ways to sell their products and collect to potential buyers in areas frequently visited (Miraflores, Lima, Peru).

> NEED: What need does this answer?

Entrepreneurs are adapting to new ways of working to attract customers. Instead of having a store, they decided to build a wheelbarrow and sell their plants while walking in the streets of Miraflores. 

> INSIGHTS: What lessons can be learned from this observation?

That people will create their own solutions in order to sustain their work despite the challenging times they are facing
