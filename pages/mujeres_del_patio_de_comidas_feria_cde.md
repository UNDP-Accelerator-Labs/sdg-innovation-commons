> Description

We are an organization made up of several producer partners from Alto Paraná, we have a food court in the area designated for the Fair, where all the work is led by female partners, from different parts of the Alto Paraná department such as: Oleary, Yguazú, Minga Guazu, Mallorquín, Presidente Franco, Mbarakayu, Itakyry, Ciudad del Este, where we produce food from the Paraguayan culture (typical foods in general) and put it on sale on Fair days (Wednesday, Thursday and Friday)
