> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

A mobile platform of credit scoring algorithms and machine learning to fast-track and facilitate the corporate credit underwriting and loan origination processes including improved real time credit risk assessment and higher financial inclusion through partial psychometric credit scoring. 
A  mobile application for the central bank e currency to potentially expand financial inclusion, supplement payment efficiency and enhance monetary policy.

> Anticipated Social Impact

A large segment of the population remains at a significant disadvantage for financial wellness because of their lack of credit. Many under banked individuals are actually in decent financial standing, and are well deserved of credit. This mobile app addesses this problem through pychometric questioning for a credit score.
