> Brief description of the innovation

Tembo and family enterprise was established in March 2017.T.F enterprise has three sections.One deals with parkaging materials (plastic bags),the others deals with fresh fruits and vegetables including charcoal the last deals with groceries.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

T.F enterprise focus on making trade between  individuals of defferent beliefs and views possible.The initiative is currently showing positive feedback. T.F enterprise is operated by members of the same family each focusing on their sector but reporting to the family by close of business.Day to day activities are flowlishing smoothly dispite the groceries sector is not functional deal to funds.
   Fully operational T.F enterprise  generated a positive profit result.which was used to expand its infrastructure and fund a  fourth year medical student who has since stopped attending class.The prime focus of T.F enterprise is to provide essential day to day needs.our long term goal is to expand into stockfeed distribution with hopes to employ full time employees.

> Technical Feasibility

T.F enterprise functions as a source of income for a family of eight.It is financed by loans from private financial services.The parkaging materials sector is the most flowshing hence the reason a huge sum of finance is  directed to it.T.F enterprise is operated on two separate locations 10 km apart.The groceries sector is on its knees due to lack of funding and an incomplete trading structure.However the location of the groceries sector is  busy and has the capability to provide the necessary services to the community.The major challenge is funding  no financial entity is willing to provide enough funds to support the initiative.This results into the business suffering heavy loses.Once fully operational all the sectors are able to provide  Tuition fees for the 4 school going members of the family and 2 or maybe more full time employees to help operate days to day activities.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The initiative can help smaller business by providing services at lower and reasonable prices.The groceries sector has the potential to act as the distributor to the final user.The parkaging materials sector has the potential to act as a supplier to other smaller businesses (the current performance caters for 35% to 45% of the market).
