> Title of the innovation

Village Chicken Raring

> Brief description of the innovation

Maximising the raring of village chickens using locally available requisites with environmental friendly impact and/or effects. It's cost effectivity and manageable demands put this innovation a none risk undertaking. The innovation shall be located at my small farm where some of the infrastructure are already in place, thereby making it easy to commence.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Village chickens are fetched at a good price in Zambia, sakes of the same is quite yielding. Target market is nearby by city markets in Lusaka. Manures from chicken droppings add great value to the soil for other agriculture crops.

> Technical Feasibility

The village chickens raring shall be domiciled at my Malende Farm in open fenced and Central roofed structure. Thus it will be a partly freelance setup. Having already the infrastructure the implementation plan stands at mentorship for the innovation, acquisition of the birds and feed stock (financing), sustainable management.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Room for employment for workers at my poultry project is positive impact on the community.
