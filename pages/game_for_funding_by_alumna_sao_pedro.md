Alumni Sao Pedro - Social Project is a community group initiative led by alumni of Catholic High School Sao Pedro in Dili. Five members of alumni HS Sao Pedro 2013 took this social project to support community members in need with funding collected from creating Game for Funding project. The group has abled to link other members of community who are disadvantaged to those who are willing to help others in need particularly women who are single and or abandoned by their family.
