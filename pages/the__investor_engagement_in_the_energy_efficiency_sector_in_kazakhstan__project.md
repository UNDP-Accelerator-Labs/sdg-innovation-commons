> Description of the solution

The project aims to advance the energy efficiency of buildings and infrastructure by attracting investments from private investors, banks, and international financial institutions. This initiative takes a notable place in efforts for transitioning Kazakhstan towards more sustainable energy use.

> Socio-economic effects

Enhances energy efficiency, reduces operational costs, and promotes sustainable practices within the building sector.
