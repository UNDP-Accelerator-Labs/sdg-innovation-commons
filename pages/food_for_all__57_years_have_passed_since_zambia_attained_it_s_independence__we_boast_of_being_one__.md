> Title of the innovation

Food For All. 57 years have passed since Zambia attained it's independence. We boast of being one of the largest copper producing countries in the face of the planet and yet people are still living below the poverty line. It is a surprise that most families I Zambia are unable to afford three meals a day hence the need to help people and families that can't afford these meals by providing them with workable solutions that will help improve their lives for good.That is why as food for all inovations I have come up with the ways of improving people's lives in the simplest way possible so that the food for all project can be achieved in the most economical way possible.

> Brief description of the innovation

I have seen a luck of a constant supply of chicken in my area, there is a great demand for broiler chicken but no one is standing up to the challenge and come up with this profitable project which can improve and change the living standard of people who venture into it within the shortest time possible. 
I would love to venture in chicken rearing  once am impowered because I know the market is readily available for consumption and not only that but also as a form of job creation which will ease the over dependant upon government creating jobs for it's citizens.
