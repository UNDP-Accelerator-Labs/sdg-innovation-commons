> Description of the solution

EH!CO!CHE is a non-profit consumer cooperative that offers sustainable mobility solutions to its members through the use of shared electric cars. It provides a car-sharing service where the vehicle is purchased by the cooperative and rented to members. The cooperation is directed towards all of Aragon, including cities and towns, organizations, companies or associations, and all people willing to change the chip and want to have a car that belongs to as many members as possible.

> Socio-economic effects

Reduced costs for low-carbon mobility
