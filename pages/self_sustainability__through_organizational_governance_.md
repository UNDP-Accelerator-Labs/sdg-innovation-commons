> Title of the innovation

Self sustainability, through organizational governance.

> Brief description of the innovation

Self sustainability through organizational governance, is an innovation which describes a government that functions like an corporation. This innovation, reduces the macro economic environment of a state into the micro economic environment, while viewing the global economy as its macro economic environment. This development will redefine the countries dynamics of the economy, such as; all macro economic indicators fracture together, the lower the output, the higher the rate of unemployment, and business fluctuations are irregular and unpredictable. Thus, help in poverty reduction.
