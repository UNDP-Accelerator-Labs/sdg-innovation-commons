> Description of the solution

The City of Toronto implemented a combined approach to promote green roofs. The Green Roof Bylaw mandates green roofs on new developments exceeding a certain size, while the Eco-Roof Incentive Program provides financial incentives to property owners for installing green roofs on existing buildings.

> Socio-economic effects

Increased green space, improved stormwater management, reduced energy use, potential for program costs
