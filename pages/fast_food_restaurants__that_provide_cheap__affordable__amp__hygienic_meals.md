> Title of the innovation

FAST FOOD RESTAURANTS: THAT PROVIDE CHEAP, AFFORDABLE &amp; HYGIENIC MEALS

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation will be to bring value addition to the farm products that we are currently producing on our farm.we want it also to create jobs for the locals here in kafue especially among the youths who are facing chronic unemployment in kafue.The innovation will create jobs that are decent for those youths that have skills in catering, cashiering and farm management and also women with unskilled labour will be employed.
We want the innovation to help in the  supply of cheap, affordable and high standard hyginic food stuffs in our fast growing town through our restaurants that we will set up in the central business district of the town.
The innovation will be a game change in addressing high levels of unemployment in the town.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will generate its revenue through the selling of fast foods meals that we will be by products of our farm products (chickens, lettuces, baby marrows and spices). The by products to be processed are chicken shawarmas, smoked chicken pieces, spiced fried chicken pieces and chips. All these food stuffs will be sold at cheap and affordable price.
This innovation will assist the locals who are in informal employment, quasi and formal employment within the central business district in kafue to have cheap- afforbable lunch or luncheon for those with conference /meetings in the town. However the main Target segment are the tailors, welders and marketers who find it hard to eat a decent and hyginic meals when running their day to day businesses. Existing businesses like internet cafes, casinos, clothes stores, accessories stores etc these will benefit through offtakes hence this will lower their cost of doing business due to cheap and affordable meals our restaurants will provide.
The challenge prize will help to rise the production and efficiency of our Enterprise hence experiencing economies of scale in the short run because the fund will be used to invest in machinery that will  bring efficiency and lower production cost of running business.
The innovation's value addition will be in line with environmental substainbity and it will encompass conservative farming methods in our poultry and vegetables production hence protecting the environment. We will further support conservation programs through social corporate responsibility that we will engage in as an Enterprise.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is devised in a way that our farm products ( chickens, lectucces, baby marrows and spices) we will be processed into shawarma, smoked chicken pieces, spiced fried Chicken pieces and chips. We want to have three big restaurants situated in the central business district of kafue town and one kafue estates this will bring the total to four restaurants.
The execution of the innovation plan will be as follows;
Each of our restaurant will need a shawarma making machine and some catering dishes, fridges, nice furniture and machines to help in the preparation of fresh chips , fried and chicken pieces.The Enterprise will also need one smoker for chickens that we will be supplied to our restaurants. We will also need a small vehicle preferably a van to help in the transportation of farm products to the restaurants.
The challenge prize will also be used to boost farm production and our Target is to move from 200 birds to 1000 birds. The vegetables production also to increase exponentially from two heactares to five hectares.
In conclusion of our innovation plan is specifically in the agriculture sector to bring value addition, secondly we have done extensive research appraisal in regards to value addition and this makes our implementation plan measurable, thirdly it is achieveable with the knowledge of economics that our CEO has. Fourthly it is realistic because empirical research has shown that it is doable. Last but not the least our plan is time framed due to the objectives we want to Carter.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation will bring new hope to kafue youths because it will create jobs for both skilled and unskilled youths and this will have a trickle down effect on the economic growth of our country as well.
It will also improves lives of people who will be part of this Enterprise through addressing the first, third and eighth sustainable development goals.
The current market is in short of standard and hyginic restaurants therefore this innovation is very imperative in addressing this social economic challenge. We want this Enterprise to be transformed into a multiple million dollar Enterprise that we be in all Corners of the country in the next 15 years and we believe in tenacity and determination to achieve this. This will create more jobs for our country in the near future.
