> Brief description of the innovation

Youths rarely take part in agriculture because of lack of awareness, access to land and credit. Therefore, social media should be linked to agriculture to create awareness, access to land and loans in parts to help youths take up agricultural activities. this can be implemented through involvement of social institutions eg the church who will evaluate and recommend youth to be granted loans and finally the government can set up institution to evaluation competence and progress of those granted loans.
