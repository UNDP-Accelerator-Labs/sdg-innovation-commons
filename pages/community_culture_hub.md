## Stabene Portsaid

Port Said's Culture Hub

Events, Workshops, Heritage tours, Co-working area and Bike Rental

The hub organises heritage walks, musical events and exhibitions, architectural documentation, and also documentation of the local semsemya folk music, musical and other cultural events.

It also raises awareness among residents of Port Said of the importance of keeping their city clean.
