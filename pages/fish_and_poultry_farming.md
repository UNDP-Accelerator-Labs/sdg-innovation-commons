> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Poultry and fish farming can generate income for me as the owner of the project and it will equally benefit the people around as it will be a job creation that will up lift the living standard the people that shall be involved as the workforce. Both fish and chicken are on demand both at local and international markets. Available local markets are such, Kaoma town, solwezi, and Congo DR for international. The two products are makertable as they are the major sources of proteins for mankind.

The project should have three stages.
1. Baby or Nursery
2. Middle (grower)
3. Finisher
Having all the three stage at one farm enables a farmer to a continuous production.
