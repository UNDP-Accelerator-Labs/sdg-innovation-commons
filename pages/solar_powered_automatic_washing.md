> Solution description




●The machine works without hand and foot contact. IT also includes a UV-C sanitation area for personal items like phone and money 
●Uses a small amount of power and can be used for indoor and outdoor applications.
●It can be powered by electricity or Solar power
●The UV-C sterilization station can be detached and used independently in banks or supermarkets
