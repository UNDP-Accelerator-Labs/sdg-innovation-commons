Mahokela (Community Policing Committees) Mobile App Experiment: The Accelerator Lab conducted a training of the peace keeping committees (Mahokela) at Tsakholo in Mafeteng District on the 23rd to 24th Sept. 2020. This is part of the deployment of the Mahokela experiment. A total of 26 Mahokela and 4 chiefs were trained on the use of the application. Each of the participants was

given a gadget they will use to report incidences of conflicts and crimes in their respective villages. The App will run for the first three months and will be evaluated in January 2021.
