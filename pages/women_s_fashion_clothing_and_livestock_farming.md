> Title of the innovation

Women's fashion clothing and livestock farming

> Brief description of the innovation

My innovation includes sale of women's second handclothing (smart pants and denim) and livestock farming which are piggery, poultry farming.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Women's clothing business is intended to improve fashion ideas and skills for myself and those that love good clothing. Target market include all women of all sizes and diversify types of clothing to accommodate men's wear .the challenge price will help with the ability to bulk order and provide consistency to offer good clothing for our working women and women.

Livestock farming on the other hand will help increase employment for fellow youths, agricultural diversity/traction and make affordable protein sources on the table. Livestock farming also improves soil fertility and prevent erosion. Target market include butcheries, local consumers, abattoir and meat processing/packaging companies. The challenge price will help in making possible the building of infrastructure to house livestock in conducive and safe environment and attract attention from the target market.
Value addition of livestock farming helps restore healthy soils,conserve sensitive species and enhance overall ecological function with proper management.

> Technical Feasibility

The linkages for the project are well established as the demand for pork is high and readily available market is present, environment is condusive for pig rearing, there's is ample water supply and expert  veterinary advice is given.thus the project is technically sound and feasible

The project is scheduled to be commenced as per implementation schedule mentioned below.
Land acquisition-owned
Land development-six weeks
Building of appropriate sheds-one month
Water and sanitation/electricity-one month
Procurement of feed, medicine equipment etc-ten days
Training of management- fifteen days
Rearing- first batch will be ready for market in seven months.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Livestock farming will help with food supply, family nutrition, family income, agricultural diversification/traction, employment etc. The innovation is relevant because it will help in accomplishing the no hunger and zero poverty and decent work and economic growth .
