The method is used to cut the wrapping of corn cobs in order to be used for wrapping food, in particular tamales. The leaf is used to cook the tamales (see image below) and sell as unity. Currently, the leaves are also used to explore the production of disposable plates and packaging. The video was produced by implementers (perhaps improvers) of the solution.
