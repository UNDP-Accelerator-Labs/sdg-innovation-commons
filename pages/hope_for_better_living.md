> Brief description of the innovation

Hope For Better Living, is an NGO registered in Ndola, Zambia to help or support the vulnerable, elderly and to advocate for the voiceless and the hopeless through the creative approach, massage of hope etc.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Hope For Better Living is not about the organization benefiting without the community where it is operating from. We are to change the mindset of the people, community and the country at large.
