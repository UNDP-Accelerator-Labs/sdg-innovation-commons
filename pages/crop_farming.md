> Brief description of the innovation

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Soya beans is a source of income when sold.
It is also a source of employment for the local people. 
The main market for soya beans is the FRA.
Additionally, soya beans farming helps in boosting the soil fertility by fixing nitrogen which is good for conserving the land.

> Technical Feasibility

This project is designed to offset towards the rain season. We intend to grow 50 hectares of soyabeans during this coming farming season. 
However, the project is budgeted for k100,000 to be implemented. This budget will cater for inputs and labour.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It is a source of employment to local people, source of income for beneficiaries.
It is relevant to the current market in a sense that it will provide availability of market products.
