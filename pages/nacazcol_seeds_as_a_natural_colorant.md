> TITLE: Please provide a title for the solution you observed.

Nacazcol seeds as natural colorant

> DESCRIPTION: What did you observe, or what did people write or say?

In Guatajiagua, Morazan, there is an artisan tradition to use nacazcol seeds for turning clay into black clay, through a natural process of roasting, grinding and bathing.

> NEED: What need does this answer?

A way to differentiate their product in a saturated market of clay pottery, to increase their economic success

> INSIGHTS: What lessons can be learned from this observation?

They use an abundant, and local resource and turn them into a local industry.  People in neighbor counties often come to Guatajiagua to learn about the process. Their idea went from product to services
