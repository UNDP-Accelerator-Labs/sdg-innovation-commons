## 

Ecoplast is characterized by the creation of furniture and household items made from 100% recycled plastic. One of the outstanding characteristics is the durability of the materials used, in addition to being environmentally friendly. Ecoplast has a wide variety of raw materials of the highest quality for the national and international industry.
