> Title of the innovation

Formation of an NGO to deal with prostate cancer sensitisation campaign among the Zambian menfolk so to reduce the needless loss of life to prostate cancer due to lack of knowledge about the disease.

> Brief description of the innovation

The Prostate Cancer Campaign -Zambia (PCCZ), was created to fill the vacuum of lack of  information on the causes, risk factors and symptoms of prostate cancer. There has been some information dissemination on breast, cervical and childhood cancers but nothing on prostate cancer, despite the fact that all black men are potential victims of the disease in advanced age if they do not lead a healthy lifestyle. The organization aims at reaching out to all men in Zambia through public and social media, as well as, community outreach activities.  The theme of the project is "Sharing Knowlege in order to Save Lives"

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will help to improve the life of Zambian men and their families as it will help to minimize the disease burden and deaths from Prostate Cancer in the country. Thus, national productivity shall be enhanced.
The proceeds from the award if scooped, would be used to intensify the outreach activities across the country so that every Zambian family gets to hear about prostate cancer and possible mitigatory measures in order to save many lives of men.
By helping cancer patients obtain necessary treatment and care, we shall be  contributing to promotion of a healthy environment in the nation.

> Technical Feasibility

The project is  designed to produce and disseminate information through various media platforms, advocate for patients rights and welfare, provide accommodation, food and transport to poor itinerant patients. Research in prostate cancer related issues is  another major objective of the organization
