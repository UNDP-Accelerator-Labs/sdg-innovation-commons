> Brief description of the innovation

Banana Milling is basically focused on processing of banana flesh and banana peel into powder or flour.its high concentration of nutrition is beneficial to human growth and health. It's to be distributed to retailers,stores, individuals, or other businesses with aim of making profits.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The product will generate income through its sales to its target market and individuals who treasure a healthy lifestyle. It will also create jobs for others as well as increase income to banana farmers. It will reduce litering as the who banana will be processed an advantage to inviroment.

> Technical Feasibility

The product is either sun dried or dried in a fruit dehydrator. Two prototype are available, the peel powder and flesh powder. One dried it's processed into flour and packaged. The plan is to process and sell.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The product will create jobs thus adding value to economy through relevant tax returns . It will be a great alternative to our local maize meal and other staple foods already on market.
