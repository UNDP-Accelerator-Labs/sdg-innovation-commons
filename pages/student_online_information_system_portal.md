> Brief description of the innovation

An online Information Management System to allow students apply for school online, take assessments and view accounts balances as well as final exam results.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

It will generate income in the sense that interested clients will have to buy the system to have it installed at their institution. However, the system will improve the businesses operation via revenue generation and tracking and centralize student data to make it more available at any time needed.

> Technical Feasibility

The prototype is currently hosted on a free servers due to financial challenges and the implementation plan will be pilot implementation and three (3) months trial.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The innovation is very relevant as the nation is trying to adopt online management of student data and E-Learning.
