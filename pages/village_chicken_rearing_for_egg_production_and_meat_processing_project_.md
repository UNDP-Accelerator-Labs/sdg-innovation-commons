> Title of the innovation

Village Chicken rearing for egg Production and meat processing project.

> Brief description of the innovation

The village chicken rearing for egg production and meat processing is a small scale innovation in Chongwe district of Lusaka Province, started with the aim of producing excellent village chickens that will be able to meet local and international market standards. It is currently in it's infant with only 20 chickens.
It stands on a 10 by 8 piece of land which has possed as a challenge for further expansion.
The feed used on this project currently is restricted to maize grains and other not-for-commencial feed that is cheap to procure as currently there is no capacity to use Modified feed.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Given capacity to grow commercially, this innovation is virable in teams of business as market for village chicken is readily available both locally and international. It intends to be a supplier of village chicken and eggs to individuals as well as food processing industries, food store chains and lodges.
Given the capacity to produce quality in quantity village chickens, the innovation can be a source of direct and indirect employment while being tax compliant which will then help in the development of the nation.

This innovation is Environmentally friendly as the by products can be used for development of cheap compost fertilizer that will enrich the land for crop production without being a danger to the environment.

As mentioned, the target market include, Food processing industries, food store chains, schools, lodges and hotels and individuals who are interested in the consumption of village chicken.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is desired for chick production, feeding section, laying point, electrified incubator and an output section.
With capacity given for purchase of a bigger land, the innovation can be desired for commercial production which will then give better outputs of the chickens and eggs.

The plan is to grow the innovation to be able to produce quality village chicken upto 1000 chicks per output with an anticipated egg production of 300 a day.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The social impact of this innovation is that it intends to hire the Services of 5 people on a direct full time employment which would then empower them with not only monthly income but also skills that can then be used to develop their own village chicken farms.
The impact at Family level of the people to get involved in the process will then be improved and also will provide market for suppliers of different materials to be used on the innovation such as day old chicks, feed and feeding materials among others.

With the produced compost fertilizers, it will help the crop farmers to produce crops devoid of MOG for a healthy population.
This will also lessen the dependency on white collar job as it will require for total commitment for those to be engaged and will also go in line with Government's desire of encouraging farming for economic growth.
