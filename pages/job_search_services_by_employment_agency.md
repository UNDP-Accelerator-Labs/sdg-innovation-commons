> Description

Offers on-line registration of employment and unemployment for all public sector employees. It publishes vacancy announcements and other services. this is dashboard, as well as a relevant site with various information on employment opportunities that are available. The private sector employers have to publish the call on this site too. The updates are administered by the Employment Centers and the dashboard is often used for reporting purposes.Â
