This is an e-commerce platform solution that intends to connect informal market vendors to online market vendors to trading online with the established logistics and safety standards (first time in Uganda). This initiative is intends to keep supply chains active, Agriculture, trade, transport; Sustain MSMEs, informal businesses; Jobs and Livelihoods; new opportunities for young innovators; logistics and supply chain management; niche markets for vendors & farmers; promoting the digital economy; promoting  trade
(MSME growth) – financial inclusion; building smart cities.
