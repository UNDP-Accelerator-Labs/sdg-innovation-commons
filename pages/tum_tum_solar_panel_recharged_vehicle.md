Innovation Details: Tum-Tum was introduced by Bangladesh entrepreneur who sell Tuk-Tuk transport to local drivers in Timor-Leste. The original Tuk-Tuk Vehicle was transported as Gasol-fueled vehicle but then he converted it with energy coming from batteries which stored energy from solar panel. In Timor-Leste the Tuk-tuk vehicle is renamed and called as Tum-Tum. It is now one public transportation used in Liquica, Manatuto, Los Palos and Oecusse Municipalities which help people generate income while protect the environment at the same time.Â
