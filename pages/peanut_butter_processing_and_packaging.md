> Brief description of the innovation

Etu peanut butter is completely natural with no added sweeteners, sugars, preservatives and emulsifiers, its a healthy energy product.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Etu natural peanut butter is one of the best smooth peanut butter which has a natural test of peanut as such it currently has a demand market demand from mothers with children below the age of 13,  this product directly creates a ready market for women and youth peanut farmers therefore it will lead to improved rural household economy and reduction of poverty.
The business has a potential to supply local wholesalers who are always looking for a cheap source of quality products, wining this prize will help us increase our production capacity which will lead to increased revenue and profit.

> Technical Feasibility

Our product is processed from locally sourced groundnuts, which undergoes for stages, that is roasting,air removing, grinding and packaging.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Our product has a direct impact on local peanut industry which is mainly comprised of women and youths as such, in the immediate it reduces poverty levels among these two categories of farmers due to accessing a ready market.
