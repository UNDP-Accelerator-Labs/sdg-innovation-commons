> Title of the innovation

Help people learn foundation

> Brief description of the innovation

We are aimed at improving literacy and numeracy levels in young children and parents. This is to provide quality and test or applied education through love, honestly and commitment. We help the young child to be able to speak, write and read the English language from young age efficiently. We also help parents who wants to be able to read, write and speak the English and Bemba language. This is to improve the lost competence in language and numeracy.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Preschool, adult literacy and tuitions for school going children. This will help them aquire the best education and will employ teachers who don't have work. It's will also help parents to help their kids in education and help them to trade well at the markets and beyond. The help will help us with resources and structures for a literacy and numeracy school.
