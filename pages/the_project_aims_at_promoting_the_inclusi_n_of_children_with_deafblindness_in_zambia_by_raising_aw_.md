> Title of the innovation

> Brief description of the innovation

Deafblindness being a unique disability which results from a combinación of both visual and hearing impairment affects a person with deafblindness to have access to information, getting around the environments AND making it difficult to participate in family, community AND social servicios.congenital deafblindness Is More complex aspecto of deafblindness which occurs before language aquisition.it also affects mobility and orientation which results in the need for specialized services.A congenital deafblind child required a táctile bodily touch to develop access to information AND communicate ir interact with others.The mental procession of táctile information Is a slow  AND  mental straining process which makes communication a
