> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

It is a fast cooking hot cake mobile cart that is able to quickly produce a freshly cooked perfectly formed hotcake. The device is rotated manually by the cooker and is attached to a motorcycle. Perfect for going to other sites where there are lots of customers.  

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

Provision of hot snacks to customers wherever they are and whenever they want.

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

Solution is for micro-entrepreneurs looking for decent livelihood.

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

This shows how creatitive innovations occured to be able to answer a problem that is unique to them.

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

It can be applied for those vendors of smalltime burger shops who wants to go mobile.
