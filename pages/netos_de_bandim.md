Cultural dancing group who has created a circular economy whereby the children who dance, their parents and any others who show interest are supported by the organisation economically. The children are encouraged to preserve culture through dancing and showing of masks and traditional clothes, in exchange they have their education paid for as well as international travel to represent the country. The parents are employed by the group to manage children if they are interest as well the children being employed if they decide to stop participating in the dancing.Â
