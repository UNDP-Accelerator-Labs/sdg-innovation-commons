> Description of the solution

Gizwits has 16 years of IoT R&amp;D experience and developed a one-stop solution for freshwater aquaculture. It has three layers:
Perception Layer: Features intelligent hardware and sensors like aerator controllers, dissolved oxygen meters, etc., enabling remote control and real-time data collection for farmers.
Platform Layer: Utilizes IoT for data transfer, and big data and AI for analysis. It uncovers key factors and offers suggestions for better decision-making.
Application Layer: Has a mini-program for farmers to manage multiple devices, view data, and receive alerts. The system also provides services for equipment enterprises.
