> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

Piso-PS4 / Xbox machines are game consoles wired to a digital coin slot & timer mechanism and encased in a wooden box. 32â€ LCD TVs are usually included in the package for the best arcade gaming experience. Users also watch preloaded movies and karaoke videos from hacked gaming consoles.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

The need to have access to affordable technology and entertainment.

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

The solutions is mostly for the youth sector who have no access or are not able to afford digital entertainment systems.  According to an interviewee, this is a good alternative for the youth than to go and join gangs especially when they feel bored. 

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

This means that there is not much rest, leisure, and recreation options for the youth in urban poor areas.

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

There is an opportunity for enterprising electronic technicians to earn while providing alternative rest, leisure, and recreation options for the youth in poorer urban areas.
