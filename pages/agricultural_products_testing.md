> Description of the solution

This solution offers an agricultural products testing service. It partners with authoritative institutions, has a wide testing range and strong ability. Serves various clients, provides comprehensive and customizable services including testing, equipment, lab design, training. The process involves multiple steps, results usually in 5 days (with express option), and tests for pesticides, veterinary drugs, heavy metals, additives, etc.
