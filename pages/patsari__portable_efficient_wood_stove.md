The Patsari wood-burning stove, a metal-portable plate-type stove for rural use, which improves the use of fuel (wood and/or biomass), with a design of prefabricated pieces that allow its replacement and easy maintenance, it has a sealed comal that conducts the gases towards the chimney to avoid contamination inside the house.
