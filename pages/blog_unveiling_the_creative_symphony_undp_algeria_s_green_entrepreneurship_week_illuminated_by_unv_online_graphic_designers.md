Unveiling the Creative Symphony: UNDP Algeria's Green Entrepreneurship Week Illuminated by UNV Online Graphic Designers

Unveiling the Creative Symphony: UNDP Algeria's Green Entrepreneurship Week Illuminated by UNV Online Graphic Designers Hanane Kaouane, Head of Experimentation, Accelerator Lab, UNDP Algeria
10 janvier 2024

The second edition of the Green Entrepreneurship Week UNDP Algeria

In the realm of green entrepreneurship, innovation takes center stage, and the recent Green Entrepreneurship Week organized by UNDP Algeria in collaboration with the National Agency of Waste was not an exception. The event, in its now second edition, sought not only to foster a thriving green entrepreneurial ecosystem but also to inject fresh perspectives into Algeria's environmental initiatives. At the heart of this vibrant happening, was a trio of UN Online Volunteers (UNV)(link is external) who, through their online graphic design prowess, painted a compelling visual narrative for the entire event.

As the curtain lifted on Green Entrepreneurship Week, the UNV Online graphic design team took the spotlight with finesse through their creative aspect. This dynamic team, led by the talented Samher Abbessi from Tunisia, played a pivotal role in crafting an identity that breathed life into the event. Their collaboration resulted in with visually captivating promotional materials and presentations that seamlessly blended information with aesthetics, leaving an indelible mark on everyone attending the event.
The Artistry of Logos: Samher, the creative force behind the team, led the charge in conceptualizing the event's identity. A mastermind in design, Samher envisioned a vibrant logo symbolizing the seven Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) related to waste management and the circular economy. The logo, featuring the symbol of circularity, the infinity sign, became a powerful representation of the event's commitment to sustainability and circular practices.
UNV Online - A Global Reservoir of Talent: This success story prompts us to delve into the vast potential of UNV Online, a global reservoir of talent that transcends geographical boundaries. Our team, composed of UNV online from different corners of the globe, showcased the diverse skills and talents available through this transformative platform. UNV Online serves as a gateway for organizations to tap into a spectrum of skills for impactful projects. Online volunteers on this platform generously offer their support for free, gaining valuable experience and a certificate at the end of their service. If you possess skills that can contribute to positive change, consider applying to UNV Online and become a part of this global network of changemakers.
In conclusion, the Green Entrepreneurship Week stands as a testimony to the collaboration and transformative impact power in usingdiverse talents within UNV Online community. Through engaging visuals and thoughtful design, UNV online graphic design team has not only enhanced the event but also demonstrated potentials for harnessing global talent to drive sustainable initiatives. This story invites individuals with skills to explore UNV Online platform, where their contributions can create ripples of positive change on a global scale.
