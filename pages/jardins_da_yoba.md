## Situated in the Huila province of Angola, Jardins da Yoba is an agribusiness enterprise dedicated to sustainable practices.

Jardins da Yoba integrate agricultural and livestock systems prioritize environmental sustainability and responsible resource utilization. Their core activities involve the production and multiplication of seeds, alongside cultivating vegetables and fruit trees. Additionally, they focus on honey and bee product production, as well as the organic crafting of sweets and jams. These endeavors represent their current and future potential for export, underscoring their commitment to excellence.
