> Description of the solution

Founded and managed by Arsht-Rock, EHRA works hand in hand with global leaders and practitioners at every level to increase heat resilience in urban areas, focusing on vulnerable populations. Experts in public health, finance, humanitarian assistance, disaster management, climate science and risk, insurance and public infrastructure, are part of the aliance that tackle the growing threat of extreme urban heat for vulnerable people. Bringing heat resilience solutions to cities worldwide.
