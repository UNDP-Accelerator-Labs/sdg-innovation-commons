African Mall is a mobile application that acts as a locator for businesses and also serves as an advertising platform for business owners. The innovation makes it possible for organizations to be more visible by making it possible to be found through a simple search on an Android and IOS devices. The solution responds to the challenge of a lack of a platform for corporate visibility and online advertising for organizations.
