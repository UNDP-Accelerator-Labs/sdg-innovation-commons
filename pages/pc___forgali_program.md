## 


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2023/blobs/solutions/900acd05f7a809c8bb0a6c14670fc559.png)


This system of payment for environmental services was used in an unconventional way. The funding was mainly allocated to the training of community leaders from various communities in lower Verapaz, with the aim of increasing water resource management capacities so that they themselves could benefit.  

The participating community groups consider that 87% of them have received a direct benefit from the intervention, considering as benefits mainly the obtaining of agricultural, food, financial or material inputs, free of charge.
