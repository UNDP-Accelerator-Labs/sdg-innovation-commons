> Title of the innovation

Poultry farming as a way of empowering youths in order to make sure that issues of SRHR ( Sexual Reproductive Health Right)are achieved

> Brief description of the innovation

We have a vision to make to that we achieve the following objectives:
1. Reduce on the high levels of employment syndrome.
2. We want to empower youths with life skills so as to make sure youths become self reliant.
3. We will give our trained youths with very soft loans after training.
We will use these instructions to discuss issue of sexual health such as:
(i) use of contraceptive if can't abstain
(ii) teaching our young girls how to make reusable sanitary tails.
(iii) Early marriage will be discouraged at all costs.
4. We advocate for every household to have a garden at the backyard so as to utilise the manure from the poultry house and in return get the nutritional value.
5. We strongly believe that that adequate implications of the Above goals will contribute greatly to poverty reduction in the country.
