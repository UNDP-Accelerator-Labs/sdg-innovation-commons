> Title of the innovation

grow Crops, grow community

> Brief description of the innovation

I grow different kinds of crops in buckets and sacs in order to provide food for my community. I grow ginger, lemon trees, potatoes, strawberries, tomatoes, avocado seedlings.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

By selling the plants and also growing the crops on a large scale, it will help in providing income inorder to improve the business and hence provide employment for my fellow youths in the country

> Technical Feasibility

The crops will be sold in plastic bags while some will be planted directly and the products being sold to the market. There are similar innovations but they sell trees in plastic bags. Due to insufficient space to grow the crops mentioned above, I resorted to selling in buckets and sacks

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will help in improving the lives of the community as it will help in generating income and provide employment for all age groups
