> Title of the innovation

Zisunge

> Brief description of the innovation

Zisunge is platform that will make available all needed information in healthy (sexual education, Sexual health, Nutrition Health, Mental Health), Human rights and more issues affecting the youths and the less privileged.
It will also connect to all the health centers' youth friendly corners for effective learning concerning healthy issues.
Zisunge will also host a hub for Therapy, Rehab, Counseling and sensitive cases such as sexual abuse, physical or mental tortures, victims can report through the platform to the police or the supporting unit.  

We will also have a direct chat app for emergency counseling and information to persons in need

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Zisunge will have Ads, and paid services (From both Private and public health centers) for  Therapy, counselling, Rehab at a professional level out of which Zisunge will be commissioned by the center.
Zisunge will also market for different sectors through blogs and articles following our purposed categories which include sexual education, Sexual health, Nutrition Health, Mental Health, Human rights and more issues affecting the youths and the less privileged. 
We will also help organizations who offer professional services on the matters referred, set appointments.  from these funds we will set up our own center that will include, a book and computer  library, arts theater  and sports field, also we will fund more projects and sponsor young people in various dimensions.
The environment will be much safer and cleaner because young people will also learn the issues affection our environment and how to take good care of the environment.

Zisunge is designed to link all the institutions  that look at the issues of healthy and social affairs. for example  the police service for reporting and investigations of referred cases.
