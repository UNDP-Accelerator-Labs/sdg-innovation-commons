> Brief description of the innovation

We aim to bring  for children ,women  and youth  by innovative activities like baking ,entrepreneurial activities - marketing strategies ,skills in farming ,e- learning.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We have a tractor which can  bring in income generating activities  help reduce labour activities for the women also delivery of goods vegetables for marketeers who solely travel long distance to get commodities to sell on the market but because the tractor isn't in good shape we can not help much in the area where transport is concerned ,but we would like to help through other agricultural activities and improve our capabilities to help women ,children and youth in our area achieve the best through other activities we are yet to bring out for the future for example fish farming and chicken raring.

> Technical Feasibility

Irrigation farming - water and irrigation farming is a necessity ,hence here at mukuyu hope farm have facilitated water and a good irrigation system to reduce labour for the women and youth ,outside cookery  using red brick oven for cookery and baking activities,  chicken poultry and fish farming which have not been established but are in the process as trying to raise funds through farming activities

> Anticipated Social Impact

Our innovation will improve the lives of women ,children and youths for better living conditions ,health and wellbeing of of children by establishment of proper transport facilities - reduce long distance travel to attain commodities for sell mainly for marketeers on the market ,help sustain children to healthy living and access to education ,help attain marketing skills ,characters building of youths towards work to make a better tomorrow health ,education ,and well being of women and children .
