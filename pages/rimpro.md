> Description of the solution

RIMpro is a tool that helps manage pests and diseases in vineyards and orchards. By using weather data from Sencrop stations, RIMpro provides real-time risk assessments for various diseases and pests. This allows farmers to make informed decisions about when and how to treat their crops, reducing the need for pesticides and improving overall crop health.
