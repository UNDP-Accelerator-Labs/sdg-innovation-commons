Eco Life local solution is creating, developing, and disseminating eco-friendly lifestyle products made of green, clean, durable, and exceptional materials in the pursuit of a zero-waste environment.  This solution is meant by developing six biodegradable materials that are alternative to plastic and eco-friendly. These materials can be used in packaging, decoration, furniture and many other applications.


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2022/blobs/solutions/1accf853aba1aecb7fe6edb1af42fc87.jpg)

![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2022/blobs/solutions/c7b98c45063dc038d48f918afe2f3206.jpg)


The six biodegradable materials are available in Iraq and it is 100% natural. These materials are: 1) Wheat Waste 2) Wild Herbs 3) Palm Fronds 4) Reeds 5) Barley Waste.


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2022/blobs/solutions/29a19aec42165c5f8eba3bed3856aaad.jpg)


The specifications of this solution is: 1) Natural 2) Plastic free 3) Hydrolyzes 4) Modern durability 5) Free from harmful substances 6) Moldable as desired, either by injection, pressing, or dry molds in order to use it for packaging, decoration, furniture and many other applications.
