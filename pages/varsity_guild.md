> Title of the innovation

Varsity Guild

> Brief description of the innovation

Varsity Guild is an intiative that will bring together like minded students from multiple universities, colleges and trade schools to share and exchange knowledge attained from their respective institution and from personal research primarily to grade school pupils and amongst each other.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Varsity Guild will have an online video on demand service and audio streaming platform to access it's information and more information. These platforms will generate income through channel subscriptions and advertisements.

> Technical Feasibility

We intend on starting our program with free physical classes for all grade schools around Zambia. Then migrate all our students onto our online platform for the premium services.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The main goal of Varsity Guild is to empower young Zambian and eventually African minds to be more economically, financially and technology equipped and skilled at a very young age, to not only sustain themselves at an early stage but also sustain a friend, a family, a community, a nation and an entire continent with their disruptive minds, innovations and abilities.
