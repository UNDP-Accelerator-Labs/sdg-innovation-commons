> Description of the solution

This program provides a comprehensive framework for schools to identify, prevent, and address indoor air quality (IAQ) issues. It equips stakeholders with resources, guidance, and tools to create healthy learning environments.

> Socio-economic effects

Improved student and staff health, reduced absenteeism, potential for increased learning outcomes

> Other Notes

US EPA is leading the solution. The program offers customizable resources and encourages collaboration between schools, facility managers, and health professionals. Specific implementation details may vary depending on the school district.
