> Title of the innovation

metal fabrication youth empowerment and training.

> Brief description of the innovation

To train and equip the youths with new surviving skills.
building and adding value to the new Zambia through structural innovation and add value to metals

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

the company will be making steel/iron structures and sale them for industrial and domestic use, bringing new skills and ideas will push for good competition and innovation, which will result to good quality products for the consumers.
the target market is both industrial (construction companies) and domestics (households). The price strategy will be used to win the market i.e. psychological pricing, market penetration pricing. the project is environment friendly with the idea to go green.

> Technical Feasibility

It is the modification and adding value to the new and existing steel/iron, eg a metal drum turned into a couch/sofa and others.
October 1 2021. the project has to be launched.

> Anticipated Social Impact

it will create employment and skills to the locals.
bringing competitive prices and quality on the market.
