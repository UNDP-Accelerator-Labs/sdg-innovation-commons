> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

NyamukaBooks has a subscription-based revenue model where subscribers pay a 3$, 7$ and 12$ for the basic, professional and enterprise packages respectively.

The solutions will help existing businesses track their income subsequently positioning their business for investment and calling / Growth. Our target market is SMEs.

The challenge prize will help us as a business be able to meet the costs of developing a mobile application that make it more accessible.

> Technical Feasibility

Our solution is built on CodeIgniter Software PHP Framework. It is secured with built-in protection against CSRF and XSS attacks and all data are encrypted with end-to-end encryption security protocol (Secure Sockets Layer).
We have integrated our accounting system with a major payment gateway like STRIPE, PAYPAL using API. We're currently working with mobile money payment i.e., Airtel, MTN and Zamtel mobile money API.
