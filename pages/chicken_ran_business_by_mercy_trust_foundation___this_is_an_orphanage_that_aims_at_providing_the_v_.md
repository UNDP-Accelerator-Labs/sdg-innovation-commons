> Brief description of the innovation

Mercy Trust Foundation - envisions seeing the vulnerable communities having a bright future by providing access to basic needs such as free education, health and food for their national development. These when well looked after would in turn becomes future leaders of the nation. In doing this, the trust will come up with skills that will help in generating funds for sustainance.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The trust will generate fund through chicken run business and other agricultural amenities such as fish farming which would be done at the orphanage premises in Kalulushi and chingola townships.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed to help children learn self gaining skills as mention above.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This will help surrounding communities have access to cheap goods, foods and from the chicken ran business and fish farming it will help children learn about about farming. This business is relevant to.surrounding communities such as Hospitals and house holds.
