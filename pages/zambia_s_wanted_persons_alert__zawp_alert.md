> Title of the innovation

Zambia's Wanted Persons Alert / ZAWP Alert

> Brief description of the innovation

This application will provide Zambians with a list of wanted criminals, in order to help the police to arrest criminals quicker.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation will generate income from the listing fee and a percentage from the reward offered by the listing party, in this case the Zambia police or other investigative sectors. 
There is high potential in that the information gathered will help law enforcers get information that could led to the capture of criminals faster. 
The prize money will help in turning this project from a design into an application that is fully functional. The prize money will also help with employing app developers, offices in major parts of Zambia and some form of data storage platform.
The value addition is that there will be a rapid response in the apprehension of criminals and crime rate in Zambia will reduce by a percentage.

> Technical Feasibility

This is designed to gather information from law enforcement authorities and help them respond quickly. Currently we have a design and are ready to engage developers to start the development. We are looking forward to engaging law enforcement commission to get an approval and work hand in hand to bring another level to justice.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This will help reduce the amount of crime cross the country in that on one will want to commit crimes in fear of being identified or reported by others.
