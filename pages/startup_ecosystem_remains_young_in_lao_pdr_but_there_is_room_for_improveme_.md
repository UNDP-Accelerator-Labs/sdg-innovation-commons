## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights) 

There is still limited support to startup companies in Lao PDR and the involving of government is necessary for startups to grow nationally, regionally and internationally. This includes reviewing the current regulations that restricted growth of startups. 

> Considering the outcomes of this learning challenge, which of the following best describe the handover process? (Please select all that apply)

- Our work has not yet scaled

> Can you provide more detail on your handover process?

The startup ecosystem assessment findings have been shared and validated with relevant stakeholders including those from public and private sectors. Startup company profiles have also been created and shared in order to expand opportunities for scale up.
