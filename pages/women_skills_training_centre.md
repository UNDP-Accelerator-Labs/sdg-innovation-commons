> Title of the innovation

Women Skills Training Centre

> Brief description of the innovation

The idea is to maximize on young women skills in the community of makeni by upscaling or improving and adding value that the society will appreciate thus changing lives and mind sets.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The idea is to have a revolving fund that will help community members to benefit from after they have been impacted with life skills.

> Technical Feasibility

The idea is to build a young women skills training centre in Makeni Nkonga for the drop outs of school who are the majority.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Makeni Nkonga though based in Lusaka is know for high prevalence teen pregnancy and early marriages, thus with this innovation, the level of sensitization will be high to ensure that skills training and education is valued.
