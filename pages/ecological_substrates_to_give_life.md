![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2022/blobs/solutions/3128e45f27a161c8a3a15937ffce1d16.png)


The project seeks to prevent coconut husks and other vegetables from being lost and reaching landfills. Instead, they are recovered with the help of local traders and used to create high-quality substrates and fertilizers for planting all kinds of plants.
