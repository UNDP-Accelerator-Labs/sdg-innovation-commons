> Title of the innovation

FM Mwimanenwa Farming Enterprise

> Brief description of the innovation

FM Mwimanenwa Farming Enterprise is just an initiative created to generate a subsistence income and a small scale business within the community. It started in April, 2019 to alleviate the hardship mainly faced in family homes. The core business activities of the Enterprise revolve around:
1. Village chicken and improved high breed layers for eggs production
2. Deliberate policy for self and other youth empowerment within the community and district.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The FM Mwimanenwa Farming Enterprise may have conducive business environment if the project have minimum financial resources, therefore realizing reasonable profit margins for meaningful progression and development. 
Major Market points are within the community, town, district and the province who are general public.
The Enterprise is vast in the area of operations, thus it will not be at loss because the demand for chicken which is considered to be a staple relish in the area is higher at the helm of the Enterprise.
The Enterprise, if grows as planned will add value from home, family, community, town district and even to the province as it will employ local youths thus helping the government from burden of  unemployment. 
The Enterprise expects to generate total income of at leat K200,000.00 when full expanded.

> Technical Feasibility

The Enterprise is designed is a small manner but intends to grow bigger depending on financial resources. It is determine to raise about K160,000.00
dividends into the infrastructure (K80,000.00), equipment and machinery (K28,000.00), working capital (K22,000.00), skill training/orientation (K10,000.00), and administration support (15,000.00)

> Anticipated Social Impact

The Enterprise will employ many local subjects including unemployed specialists, security staff, and local workers are very potential stakeholders in the success of the Enterprise thus improving their lives.
Looking at the current market and progression level of the Enterprise, it is expected to have measurable profit margin down the line hence it might have minimum workers but employing more in accordance to the growth of the market and Enterprise.
