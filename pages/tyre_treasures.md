> Organization

Tyre Treasures

> Description

Tyre Treasures is a green enterprise that aims to address the ever-increasing pollution caused non-biodegrasable inorganic waste, primarily TYRES. The company follows the principle of "Reduce. Recycle. Recycle" and "Upcycle" tyres into refined products to promote culture of conservancy and judicious use of resources. The company creates green products out of tyres in order to reduce carbon footprint and to advocate for environmental sustainability.
