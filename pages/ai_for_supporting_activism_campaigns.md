## Accionar.io is a multi-channel platform in Latin America that helps activists and collectives build agile campaigns. 


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2025_01/blobs/solutions/a4d3d6d9f06bbcd6ed7c57fccb483446.png)


Accionar.io is a democratic innovation lab that accelerates change processes. Through campaign design, agile methodologies, and technological tools, it strengthens the capacities of activists, groups, and progressive movements (who seek to expand the exercise of rights) to promote participatory democracy.
