> Description of the solution

Climate Budget Tagging (CBT) is a financial management tool designed to help governments track and optimize their climate-related expenditures within public budgets. It aims to enhance the transparency and efficiency of climate finance and is a crucial component in integrating climate change considerations into broader fiscal policies and strategies.

> Socio-economic effects

By ensuring targeted and effective use of climate-related funds, CBT enhances the impact of government spending on sustainable development goals, improves national resilience to climate impacts, and supports strategic climate policy implementations.
