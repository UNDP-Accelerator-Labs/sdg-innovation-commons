> Title of the innovation

Destined youths goat rearing, poultry and fruit tree gardening project

> Brief description of the innovation

We shall engage in rearing hybrid goat s crossbreeding them with local goats to come up with an improved breed . We shall also rear chickens for chick and egg production  as well as utilize a 10 Hector's piece of land for fruit tree gardening. We shall engage the local vulnerable youths and women as immediate beneficiaries
