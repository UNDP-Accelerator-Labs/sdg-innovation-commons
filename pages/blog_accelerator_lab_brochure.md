Accelerator Lab Brochure
SEPTEMBER 13, 2019
The UNDP Accelerator Lab is a new service offering that works with people, government, and the private sector to reimagine development for the 21st century.
Through long-standing partnerships with the government, we are creating a new capability for decision-makers to explore, experiment, and grow portfolios of mutually-reinforcing solutions to tackle complex challenges from multiple angles.
