> Description of the solution

The AATF-led TAAT Maize Compact is spreading climate-resilient maize technologies, including Water Efficient Maize Varieties (WEMA), to 14 African countries. This collaboration between public and private sectors involves seed companies, farmer organizations, commodity associations, and national agricultural research institutions.
