Description: 
A company providing quality services to individuals or companies in several business areas (mason, plumber, painter, designer, electrician ... etc.), the main goal of this company is to create a link between the service requester (customer) and the skilled worker (craftsman) through an online platform (website, mobile app.) with quality assurance through training for employees before starting work.

Origin of the solution:  
Algeria Startup Challenge/Coronhackathon. A partnership between UNDP Accelerator Lab. and Cap CoWork Incubator.
