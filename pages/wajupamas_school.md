> Title of the innovation

WAJUPAMAS SCHOOL

> Brief description of the innovation

A secondary private school based in Petauke District that operates more like a community school to help in the delivery of quality education to the community at a cheap and affordable fee of K300.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will help essentially help many children to be in school thereby improving the lively hood enabling them to be successful in this completive life of which education is the Key for every person to excel.
This innovation will also help in creation of employment to the mass unemployed teachers which will in turn help to improve their social and economic lives.
The challenge prize will help in the expansion of the already existing small school thereby giving chance to a lot of pupils to be enrolled.

> Technical Feasibility

The implantation plan is to continue and expand from the already existing school and if possible open up new branches in different remote areas where there are no schools.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The relevance of this innovation comes with a great vision of ensuring that children are in school and unemployed teachers find a livelihood by getting employed.
