> Brief description about the identified local solution:

Fikra space is an open place where people with common interests --often in computers, technology, science, art , and entrepreneurship can meet, socialize, share their knowledge, build new things or make use of existing things, make workshops and collaborate. Fikra Space is the oldest technology driven youth community founded in 2015 and conducting Fikra Camps, Fikrathons and other technology and entrepreneurship driven activities. They provide high quality camps related to web and app developers, designs, and technology in Entrepreneurship. Fikra Space network reached to 25K.
