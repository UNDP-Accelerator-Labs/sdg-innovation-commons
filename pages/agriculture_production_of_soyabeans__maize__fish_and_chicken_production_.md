> Title of the innovation

Agriculture production of soyabeans, maize, fish and chicken production.

> Brief description of the innovation

Samachake enterprises is a sole owned enterprises registered under Pacra in Zambia aimed at selling agricultural products and machinery at small scale to medium scale level. Deals in retail and wholesale of food stuff and live animals such as Cattle, chicken, pigs etc. The business is located in Mazabuka of southern province, Zambia

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The enterprises will generate income from the sales of farm products such as soyabeans, maize, chickens, fish, vegetables and medium to long term cattle sales. Workers will be employed to help run the facility, hence job creation. The target market is local and also export especially to DRC and other neighboring countries. We hope to put milling plants to process our own mealie.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed in such a way that there's CEO who will be assisted by managers manning various enterprises with some general workers below these managers. 
The implementation plan is that land must be sourced on a small scale and then grow the starting with short to medium term income generating activities such as chicken and piggery production as well as soya and maize production.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Once this has taken off some people will be employed as workers improving their earning power. Also the products will help improve the nutrition status of most consumers. 
This will also help availability of food and agriculture products on the market which at the moment is proving a challenge due to growing human population world-wide
