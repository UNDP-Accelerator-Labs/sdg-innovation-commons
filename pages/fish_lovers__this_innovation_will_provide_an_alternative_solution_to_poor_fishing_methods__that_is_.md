> Title of the innovation

Fish Lovers- This innovation will provide an alternative solution to poor fishing methods, that is poisoning, use of explosives, use of mosquito nets and overfishing in the community, by promoting Aquaculture as the remedy. Hence, this innovation will be addressing SDG# 1,2 &amp; 14.

> Brief description of the innovation

Fish Lovers’ enterprises is a fish farming business that will aim at producing more fish and fingerlings on the market. Development of this business will be an alternative remedy in restocking the depleted fisheries, reduce pressure on natural stocks and contribute to improved food production and nutrition in the community. Therefore, Fish Lovers will be producing quality fish and fingerlings in the community in an environmentally sound and sustainable manner for the present and future generations.
