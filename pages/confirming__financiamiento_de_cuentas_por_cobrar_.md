> Description

The purpose of TASPRI - Confirming is to support businesses and ventures led by women, specifically in the medium-sized enterprises segment, that need working capital to pay their suppliers. This is achieved through loans to women who have contracts that enable them to fulfill those obligations, which in turn ensures the repayment of the borrowed amount
