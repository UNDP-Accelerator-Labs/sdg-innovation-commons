> Title of the innovation

Young and vibrant.

> Brief description of the innovation

Will target mostly the HIV positive youths in rural areas and also involve the HIV negative youths in rural areas by using local methods to empower them and give them hope for there future.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Empower young people through local available resources and find market for them within the community and outside the community, the revolving funds to the beneficiaries and paying of the initial loan to the organization so that others benefit too. 
Constant monitoring and technical advise to the youths as an individual on the gaps and what they need to improve on till they are independent enough to run on there own

> Technical Feasibility

Implemention is dependent on availability of funds . 
If a youth benefits from the initiative of a peanut manufacturing ,the buy ground nuts from local farmers and sell the product back to the community at an affordable price , packaged for customer satisfaction and affordability. Brand it with the initiative name.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will have economic impact to the youths and there families and the community at Large .
It will also reduce malnutrition in the community.
Education impact will also be achieved as the youths will be able to take themselves and there siblings to school.
Poverty will be reduced to greater levels.
Agriculture impact too as the community will be motivated to farm ground nuts as market will be readly available.
Job creation is guaranteed.
