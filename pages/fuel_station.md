> Title of the innovation

Fuel Station

> Brief description of the innovation

Establishment of a fuel station in the Southern Region.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Income will be generated through the sale of fuel such as diesel, petrol and paraffin. This will provide various jobs to individuals such as petrol attendants, shop keepers, managers, security, maintenance personnel and so on. The fuel station will provide an opportunity for other businesses to strive such as a convinient stores, cafeterias, postal services, transportation bays as well as ATMs. Availability of a fuel station will specifically cater to various motorists and other industries that need fuel for their transportation services. Establishment of the fuel station adds value as it not only beautifies the environment but improves the infrastructure of the area.

> Technical Feasibility

The fuel station will take a form similar to those that exist around the region. As there is availability of land, this will require a need to seek an operational franchise within the petroleum industry. For example, Shell, Total, Engen or Caltex which will provide a base to source the fuel for residents in the area.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The fuel station will assist various individuals and industries that struggle to source fuel from different areas. Therefore, this provides a convenient place for residents of the area to source fuel that will aid their easier and better transportation.
