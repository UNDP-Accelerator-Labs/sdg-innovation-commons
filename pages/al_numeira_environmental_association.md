## 

Al Numeira Environmental Association is a local non-governmental Organization based in South Ghor community, Dead Sea, Jordan, founded on 2012 by a group of local people trying to make a difference and managing with their local resources, reporting the Jordanian environmental Ministry.

Our activities are focusing on sustainable development through two processes : raising the environmental awareness of the local community and engaging local human resources in sustainable tourism activities. 

Four main areas of work: grey water, permaculture, sustainable buildings, composting, and bicycle tourism
