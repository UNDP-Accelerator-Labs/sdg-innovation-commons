> Title of the innovation

AiCare

> Brief description of the innovation

AiCare is an agricultural Innovation that tends to solve problems facing by farmers. Farmers are mostly faced with diseases and oftenly use traditional means of diagnosing these diseases. AiCare brings the solution of on spot diagnosis by using a phone to capture the disease and gives you available solutions. AiCare also bridges the gap between the supplier and the consumer by providing e-commerce platform and also get expert opinion and suggestions through the AiCare forum

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

AiCare will require users to pay for Advertising their products and also Agriculture support to their crops

> Technical Feasibility

AiCare is an Android application that will request sign up/sign in users. This application will require users to upload an image to the system so as to diagnose the disease. AiCare uses artificial intelligence for the analysis. This diagnosis takes less than a minute to diagnose

> Anticipated Social Impact

AiCare creates a culture of smart farming and reduces the likelihood of low yields so, this system will change how farming is done and create more time for farmers and consumers
