> Description of the solution

In Hanoi, Vietnam The Red Cross Society and partners are working with the Vietnam Institute of Meteorology, Hydrology and Climate Change to co-create heatwave forecasts for the city. In addition the Red Cross in Vietnam has conducted an extensive knowledge, attitude and practice survey to understand to current capacity of the population to cope with heat risks. The survey was supplemented by a comprehensive stakeholders analysis to identify potential partners as well as with geographic information system mapping to show the locations of vulnerable people.
