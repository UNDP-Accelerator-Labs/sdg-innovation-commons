# NyeKontrol -- Health Monitoring App

Nye Kontrol is a web-based health monitoring application that offers a range of services from health monitoring, personalized health advice, interpersonal discussions with certified practitioners in an effort to bring quality health care services within reach of all. The app is developed by an eponym startup led by a young entrepeneur by the name of Goga Joel.
