> Description of the solution

This service is provided by Aijia Media on Huinong.com. This solution includes short video and live broadcast operation, backstage management, and product promotion for agriculture products. Prices range from 200 - 500 yuan with different minimum order quantities. Live broadcast data shows metrics like viewers, conversions, and transaction amounts. The company, in Hotan, Xinjiang, has been operating for 2 years and offers services nationwide.
