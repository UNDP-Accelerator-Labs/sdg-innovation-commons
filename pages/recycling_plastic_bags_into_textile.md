## Reform Studio

Reform is An International Award Winning lifestyle Design Brand based in Cairo - Egypt. Our aim is to design creative responsible products that benefits the environment &amp; have a positive impact on the society. We provide a whole range of high end eco-friendly furniture, home &amp; fashion accessories.

Reform Studio was founded in 2012 by a team of young product design entrepreneurs, Hend Riad and Mariam Hazem, who were motivated by challenge and had an urging sense of commitment to the world, believing that doing what you love brings out the most of what you can do.
[https://reformstudio.net/](https://reformstudio.net/)

The company works with weavers and local craftsmen to revive the craft of weaving, while also using nwe materials like plastic bags to create the textile.
