The Solution:

Eco Life local solution is creating, developing, and disseminating eco-friendly lifestyle products made of green, clean, durable, and exceptional materials in the pursuit of a zero-waste environment.  This solution is meant by producing implantable packaging paper where the end-user can plant the paper after its used. These papers are customizable where the user can select the type of plant that will be planted (Flowers, vegetables, etc.). These papers can be used to wrap different products. This solution is part of the bigger movement towards sustainable life (eco-friendly packaging solutions). This product is made completely by Iraqi hands.
