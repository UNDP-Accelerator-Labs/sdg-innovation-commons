> Title of the innovation

Fish farming project

> Brief description of the innovation

Production of quality fish seed to the fish farmers/ construction of durable fish ponds for would be client. This will improve fish production in the country as we will also provide technical services. And training our farmers on value addition to our products . our market is readly available to the community and also can be expanded to other major towns. We also intend to use feeds that will be weather friendly and cannot produce  gases that can affect our ozone layer.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will be set as to ensure that income is generated through provision of quality products to catch the eye of our clients market seeking is not a problem as the community exhibits demand for fisheries products .we will produce locally and it will be cheaper for us to produce locally the feeds needed for our fish. Local materials for feed  production is readly available. Our products will be sold at an affordable price as we will lower our cost of production.

> Technical Feasibility

Our plan is being designed to ensure that it is close to major outlets for our products especially that fish farming is growing at a faster rate in our community. Its also near the natural rivers, streams and lagoons where fingerlings can easily be accessed. Brood storks can also be accessed for breeding and sex reversed. Our implementation plan is to have production ponds , breeding ponds, a hatchery and also a reliable water source
