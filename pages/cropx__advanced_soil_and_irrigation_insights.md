> Description of the solution

CropX integrates hardware and software to deliver real-time soil, water, and crop condition monitoring, making irrigation and soil management more efficient. CropX is an easy-to-use yet powerful agronomic farm management system that uses AI and machine learning to connect farm data, real-time conditions and agronomic knowledge, providing guidance for successful and sustainable farming, while aggregating all agronomic farm data in one place for easy tracking and sharing.
