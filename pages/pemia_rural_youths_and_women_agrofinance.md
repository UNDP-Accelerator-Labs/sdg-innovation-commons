> Title of the innovation

Pemia Rural Youths and Women Agrofinance

> Brief description of the innovation

Provision of goats and village chicken meat in all our Zambian butcheries. The gap will be covered by working with out grower small scale farmers who happen to be rural youths and women. They will be empowered with Agrofinance using the group methodology in order for them to produce more in addition to our own farms.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Our target clients are rural youths and women on Agrofinance while on the goats and village chicken meat we target our local butcheries, hotels, restaurants as it is a major gap currently in our country Zambia. A pilot was done a proved to be more viable both at low and commercial level.

> Technical Feasibility

We shall use group of 25-50 rural youths and women (small scale farmers) to supply tools and resources with interest to produce, buy and supply village chickens and goats. Apart from the profit generated on the interest rates, we shall also make profit on the meat supply.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This will contribute to promotion of all SDGS at village and urban level.
