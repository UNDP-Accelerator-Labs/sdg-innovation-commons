> Title of the innovation

1. Decorative basket making ( Art craft)

> Brief description of the innovation

This is a basket making Art craft. Art craft skills are very important in an Education system. In this view, it is very important that learners at all levels of education atain different skills to enable them being selfreliance and avoid over dependace on white collar jobs. Life may not be easy sometimes but when one has aqured necessary skills, he or she is likely to become an entrepreneur and self employer.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation is very attractive and decorative, hence forth it can be used for events decorations such as weddings and parties. It can also be used by a flower girl to put the flowers during the wedding as well as being used as decorative home use. Therefore it's a money making venture. The challenge price will help grow this innovation at large scale and i intend to impart more skills of such in nature to my learner's and the youth  so that they become self reliant after school. Moreover, the challenge price will enable me to buy more materials for more craft work. There is a good market for such innovations, for example in 2020, I became famous  at one of the private schools i worked for because of innovations and craft work skills which i imparted  to learners  grade  6 and 7. In this view, some of the parents to our learners were very happy with innovations and some of them bought our products at a high price. These includes decorative baskets, verses, and beautiful picture frames made from locally available materials and from the market.

> Technical Feasibility

My innovation is made using the following: scissors, glue, colored Manilla papers, a marker and a ruler. I intend to add more value to my innovation.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This is an entrepreneural skill. Therefore, if well used one can make a lot of money out of it. Moreover, as Educationalist, These are some of skills which enables a school to gain popularity because of the Quality of Education, hands on experiments and innovations. Therefore, to an individual you make more money out of it.
