> Title of the innovation

The Market Place

> Brief description of the innovation

The Market Place is a mobile application that allows Zambians to purchase local grocery products from their mobile phones. In this time of the pandemic , staying at home is essential to the safety of one another. We want to promote local products and encourage Proudly Zambian Brands to strive in these times. We will be the link between the consumers and suppliers, while offering an outstanding service that brings innovation and creativity at the core of our service. This will help grow the Zambian economy and benefit Zambian owned businesses and products. We will deliver all goods needed directly to customers once the purchases have been done via the application

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

How the innovation will make money? 

With this approach of purchasing grocery  products from a mobile devices. The market place will enter into agreements with as many local  shop owners within a certain city. This means the number of products being offered on the Application will be higher. This idea will take a step further to deliver these products  to our customers.  All payments made on the application are electronic, and the market place makes money through additional fees such as delivery and a fractional percentage on products purchased through the application. 

How will be Assist in Operations of existing business? 

This application will help local business owners within the community. The market place will be purchasing vegetables and other foods, from local marketers within that city. The market place will be purchasing products from small shop owners. What the application does is expand on the potential customers that shop stores can have and the use of a mobile application ensures we have the ability to use the power of data analysis to help local shop owners grow into nation wide stores. we will be able to analyze which product sells more , when the demand for certain products are high , and keep track of accounts to ensure maximum efficiency within the market. 

How will the challenge price help? 
The challenge price will help fund the production of this innovative application. The mentorship that is being offered will be key to building the entrepreneurial spirit that needs to be cultivated in order to make a great business man that brings innovation to the Zambian Market. 

Value addition on the environment?

With the market place up and running , this means we have less cars on the road , hence reduced carbon emissions. Delivery's will be taken on by us. Another benefit is having less people crowded and congested within the markets, this will help clean up our market place and allow for social distancing in these areas.

> Technical Feasibility

This innovation is designed as a mobile application, and the implementation plan is to visit local shop owners and enter into an agreement allowing for us to place their products on our application. Once we have a wide range of products to offer, we begin to advertise this innovative idea and launch the application.  Zambian owned products will be our priority and reaching them out to as many people as possible. This will not only help the shop owners of these local shops sell products to a larger customer base but help enhance the local businesses that are available in our country but also  promote the production of local products which in turn will benefit our economy.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation allows for local shop owners within the city ,to  sell products to a large customer base. It puts Zambian owned products as the main focus and intends to have them sold throughout the country. The market place application is all about the current market and brining exceptional goods and services to the Zambian people. The use of Data Analysis will help grow the Zambian Market and have a full and complete outlook of demand and supply within the current market. It will  Answer questions like which products are in high demand at the moment? Where do we need to focus our resources? Is there are gap in the Zambia market that needs to be filled? 

This innovation Is about benefiting business owners within Zambia. And creating something special.
