We aim to launch a comprehensive project that combines sustainable development and women's empowerment. In the pilot phase, an online and certified sustainability training and ideation program (SHE LAB) will be created. Through this program, young women will have the opportunity to explore the field of sustainability and implement their innovative projects. The program will be supported by meetings with industry leaders and will feature a hybrid structure. Participants in SHE LAB will form a network of volunteer ambassadors for active citizenship and equality. The project will encourage young women from universities to take steps in sustainability and gender equality. It also includes an intensive communication component aimed at introducing the agenda of gender equality in sustainability to young people and the public.Â 

It is the Head of Experimentation together with the Gender Equality Advisor designed the initiative. Only with this involvement the programme targets a different challenge that other actors do not work on and also the training programme has many pillars of innovation.Â
