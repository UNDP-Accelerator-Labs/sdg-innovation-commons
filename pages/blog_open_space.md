Open Space
AUGUST 14, 2020
In order to make the best use of the public spaces, UNDP Accelerator Lab Nepal explored and mapped the available Open Spaces of Kathmandu Metropolitan City, Lalitpur Metropolitan City, Kirtipur Municipality and Bhaktapur Municipality using ArcGIS in 2020. These maps can help visualize the general features, conditions and utilizations of the public spaces to explore further possibilities on their best utilization.
