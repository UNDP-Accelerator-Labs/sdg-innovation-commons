> Title of the innovation

community harvest register

> Brief description of the innovation

We are a Marketplace. A digital Farmers Market. Where farmers display their produce and buyers can plan, when (farmers submit when they will start harvesting and the quantities) they will purchase produce and where (farmers map their location on registration). Best of all being organised even if we are small we can aggregate, farmers and buyers can team up to do big scale things. Small seeds bring big trees.
