> Description of the solution

This is an online wholesale platform driven by data and technology, integrates regional supply resources, and offers one-stop procurement, wholesale, and distribution services for agricultural products across the region, constructing a fresh food supply chain for a win-win situation. It includes supply from direct-purchase bases and manufacturers, order processing, stocking, group-buying and flash sales through the DC agricultural middle platform, shared logistics scheduling, third-party logistics distribution.
