> Description of the solution

Specific regulations for indoor air quality (IAQ) can be established at the national or regional level. These regulations set acceptable indoor air quality levels for pollutants and may outline monitoring, control measures, and enforcement mechanisms.

> Socio-economic effects

Improved public health (reduced respiratory illnesses), improved occupant productivity and well-being, potential economic benefits from improved building performance and reduced healthcare costs
