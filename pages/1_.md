## Data and Methods

> Relating to your types of data, why did you chose these? What gaps in available data were these addressing?

Consulted students from 9th to 12th standard. Some of counseling is introduced starting 9th standard. However, data on student receiving career counseling is not organized and not publicly available. 

> Why was it necessary to apply the above innovation method on your frontier challenge? How did these help you to unpack the system?

Critical to interact with the individuals who will be impacted by the career counseling strategy. The team visited and interacted with 118 students between 13-18 years old (67 female: 51 male).
