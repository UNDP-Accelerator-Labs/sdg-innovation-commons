> Description

The is a joint platform by all municipalities in the country. It publishes questionnaires  when these are active. Citizens can go online and check if there are active questionnaires and participate in those. The website does not offer the option to subscribe to specific municipality when there is a new questionnaire online. This relies specifically on the will of the citizens to manually check and contribute.
