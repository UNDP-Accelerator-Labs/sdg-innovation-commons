The cosmetic products are made from natural ingredients, contributing towards green and sustainable economy:

1. MGs Young+ : a new green youth-enhancing face cream for made from certified mangosteen peel extract (MPE)  and other natural ingredients. The development of the product supports circular economy through waste reduction in line with SDG 3 and 12 because it uses alternative resources that are fully renewable, including 100% recyclable containers.
2. TomaLight: a cosmetic product made from plant-based sources, with tomato seeds as its main ingredient. The product does wonders on skin brightening. 
3. CuberClearC+: Based on cucumber extract, CuberClearC+ is an eye cream that targets dark eye circles. 
4. CocoameltTabs THC 100mg: an immunity booster for better health and is suitable for all ages. 
5. Mangosteen (0.1% w/v) Face Serum: a botanical face serum from organic components to fight ageing.
