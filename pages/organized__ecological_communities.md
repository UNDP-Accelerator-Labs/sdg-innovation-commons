## 

Utz Che' is a national association that legitimately represents organized communities, recognized by society and the Guatemalan State for their important contribution to development through sustainable forest management. Through reforestation, cultivation and soil irrigation practices, they seek to be an example to protect nature and the communities that depend on it.
