> Title of the innovation

Digital online shop ....selling original Zambian crafts online  ---samples as attached

> Brief description of the innovation

Taking Zambian crafts online , through my online shop to the world
https://digiplatsservices.com and my South African shop; https://digiplats.co.za

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We letting our nationals expand their market place from local to global; Thus increasing their returns in the shortest period possible . As the demand for selected items increases, this may potentially  generate employment opportunities for the local and put our country in turn on the global market place

> Technical Feasibility

Using the android based digital marketing landscape online ......The sample crafts attached are made nationwide; No prototypes exist currently but could be made available with suitable funding. The implementation plan would be what I would call ...back to the basics, Co-operative approach in which several craftsmen are grouped together and implement a revenue sharing arrangement once the made goods are sold.

> Anticipated Social Impact

With increased revenue via global sales, i anticipate improved quality of lives by those directly involved.In turn , these can hire locals as the demand for the craft items increases
