> Type of the Solution

Initiative, Subsidy

> Affected Sector

Households

> Description of the solution

If you are a Massachusetts resident, you might qualify for a $500 rebate from Mass Save on a swap this year from a gas to an induction stove.

> Socio-economic effects

improve health and time savings for households, preserve forests and associated ecosystem services, and reduce emissions that contribute to global climate change.
