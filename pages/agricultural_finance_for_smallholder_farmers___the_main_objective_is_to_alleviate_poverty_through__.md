> Title of the innovation

Agricultural finance for smallholder farmers,  the main objective is to alleviate poverty through agricultural schemes  such as  trainings through cooperatives,  climate change adaptability,crop diversification and also seed mobilisation according to the soil type and rainfall patterns. the objectives can help to address some of the challenges faced with small-scale farmers.

> Brief description of the innovation

Enhanced Agricultural finance for smallholder farmers is an initiative taking project aimed at empowering small scales farmers not only with financial support but with some of the Agricultural technologies which will help them from production point to marketing of their produce.
What to produce? 
How to produce?
When to produce? 
The  above questions are very important in agriculture as a business which the small - scale farmers need to put into consideration.Once the farmers know what to produce,  how to produce and when to produce then it is ease to make assumptions of the profits as well as budgeting.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Agricultural finance for smallholder farmers is an initiative taking project which is based in zimba district, of which the main enterprise are legumes and tuber crops such as irrish potatoes and legumes such groundnuts and soya beans . The target market are the residents of zimba district if the project grows marketing outside the district we will extended . Value addition such as making cooking oil from groundnuts and soya beans, for irrish potatoes  making fast food palace around the district will be ensured so as to buy these products from the small scale farmers. Proper food processing and technologies, food packaging will address the issue of competition.

> Technical Feasibility

The project is expected to be implemented starting in the coming month of September 2021 as we prepare for rain season, farmer trainings and resource mobilisation needs to put into place for project.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This initiative taking innovation will help rural people to generate income through selling of their produce to the project.the project will provide market linkages to farmers. Through the food palace there will be social interaction and refreshment in the district.
