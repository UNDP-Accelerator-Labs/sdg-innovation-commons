'Style Maya' is a locally owned clothing business that specifically targets breastfeeding mothers. The clothing line also targets breast cancer patients as well because it is tailored to provide comfort and confidence to women in these situations.


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2022/blobs/solutions/6e04ece988019057ec7c9222f2fe3ec0.png)


What is the purpose of your business? The owner Cynthia Cebujano, wishes to use her talent and skill to empower mothers to boost their confidence and also provide convenience and comfort in their lives as they carry out their responsibilities. Also, her fashion line is made from environmentally friendly materials and is durable enough to avoid being discarded; she uses her newfound innovation to provide a means of income for women and convenience to those recovering in hospitals etc.
