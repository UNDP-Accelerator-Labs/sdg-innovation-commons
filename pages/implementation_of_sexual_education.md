> Title of the innovation

implementation of sexual education

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation is currently categorized by the aim of helping vulnerable communities specializing the youths, to disseminate information on SRH. Sexual reproductive health rights. This is however a long term project that we have planned under the tisisunge project, ( let’s stay healthy). It’ll allow the less privileged to have access to sexual education through facilitating.  As an organization, a legally recognized organization is willing to help such youths to be informed about the project.
