> Brief description of the innovation

INSPIRED LIGHTS

This is an initiative which will have a web application that will be able to provide a platform for people to share their lights based on their experiences and exposure. The web application will be partitioned based on various topics that are currently facing society to allow people to access their desired content faster. The content providers will be able to share their story and how they managed to traverse through it to inspire those going through the same situations. The shared content will include Mental Health, Social Stigma, Business, Agriculture, skills development and many others. The application will also have an option for people to share information without being known but also provide a platform for them to be able to link up with those who have been touched by their content in order for them to be fully helped and inspired.
The web application will also provide a platform for those going through challenges such as Mental health to come out and will have an option for them to have their identity hidden depending on the users needs. the User will then be linked with someone who can be able to help them.
Inspired Lights will also have a team which will be able to go in communities and share lights on various topics in order to reach even those that are not able to access the application.
This initiative believes that when people are well informed, they can be able make well informed decisions that can propel their lives forward. The initiative will focus not only on enlightening but also empowering, and then those empowered will be able to give back to the in initiative which will ensure sustainability and growth. 
In a world where people are stuck, with cases of mental Health rising every day, with so much ignorance among youths on the best way forward, Inspired dream will be that bridge that will allow people to grow and contribute positively to the world.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Inspired Lights 

This innovative initiative will be able to partner with organizations that will be able to market themselves on the web application, these firms will have to pay based for them to be featured on the site. The initiative will be providing insights on Mental Health which is a growing need and those who will need to be added to by qualified counsellors will be directed and the engaged specialists will have to give back an agreed percentage to the initiative.
The Initiative will allow institutions of higher learning that provide their content on the site to have more enrolments and will also act as a tool of advertisement for these institutions.
The Initiative will promote Small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) and encourage more people to start their own business owing to the business lights and content that will be shared. It will also Provide a platform for people to network and also find potential business clients.
The Initiative will also empower Youths not doing anything in society by mentoring them and also teaching them practical skills such as farming, Painting and others. The Empowered Youths will then start projects and will be able to give back to the initiative.
The challenge prize will grow the impact of this initiative, allow the establishment of this initiative at a physical location, allow it to partner with organizations that will help foster the goal and help with registration and other formalities.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is a having a web based application which can be accessed on any Gadget(Phone, Laptop, Desktop) having a browser and can be able to access internet service. The application has an admin log in platform where the admins will be also to log in from. The admin will be able to control operations of the application, modify content and also approve contents before being shared.
  The application will allow those that wish to provide content to register and also has an option for the other users to register or just view the content.
