> Observation & Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

Repackaging ripened vegetable to make it more presentable and sellable.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

The need to have other means to sell other than the usual selling vegetables.

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

The solution is for micro-entrepreneurs who are looking for ways to repackage or value-add to vegetable products in order to sell better.

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

The concept of repackaging and value-adding is a good strategy for vegetable vendors to sell their goods better and fetch a higher price.

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

This is a potential strategy for micro-entrepreneurs to add value to their existing vegetable products.
