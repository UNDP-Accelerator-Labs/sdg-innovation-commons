> TITLE: Please provide a title for the solution you observed.

Recycled water tank trailer with a drinker for cows

> DESCRIPTION: What did you observe, or what did people write or say?

For some, it may be an old and rusty water tank, for Henry, an opportunity to reuse and recycle, but above all, to solve a challenge in the field.

> NEED: What need does this answer?

This solution allows the cows to drink water regardless of the place and time of year. In this way, Henry can reuse the water and take it or share it where it is most needed.

> INSIGHTS: What lessons can be learned from this observation?

That with a little ingenuity, you can achieve great solutions.
Using recycled materials, it is possible to contribute to better water management in the field.

Never underestimate a solution. This water tank trailer with a drinker could be vital in critical situations in the countryside.
