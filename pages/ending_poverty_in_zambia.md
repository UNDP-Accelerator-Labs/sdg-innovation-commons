> Title of the innovation

Ending poverty in Zambia

> Brief description of the innovation

Ending poverty in Zambia is an innovation that looks at means and ways of eradicating poverty in Zambia. This innovation encourages every citizen to engage themselves in various economic activities such as agriculture and other business ventures that will help every citizen to put a meal on the table. It aims at providing knowledge to members of the community and provide them with skills that they may need in undertaking these economics activities.
