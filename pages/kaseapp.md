## Online Farmer's Marketplace and Agri-Service Portal

The online farmer's marketplace Kasemall.com  allows farmers/food producers to directly sell their agricultural and food products to consumers. The Kasegro Co., Ltd. is also developing "KaseApp", a Cambodia Agriculture Service Portal App, that will bring more agriculture services to users.
