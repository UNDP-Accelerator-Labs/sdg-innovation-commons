> Affected Sector

> Description of the solution

A nationwide effort in Singapore to plant one million more trees across the country over 10 years, increasing the total number of trees to over 8 million. The program focuses on planting trees in streetscapes, parks, gardens, nature reserves, and industrial estates.

> Socio-economic effects

Improved air quality, reduced urban heat island effect, increased biodiversity, potential economic benefits from eco-tourism
