> Title of the innovation

Business loan scheme

> Brief description of the innovation

*As a developing nation,Zambia’s population is mostly youths (fit for work citizens)and this makes it hard for firms to employ everyone.
*providing jobs only will not equal the level unemployment that we have .
*part of the economy should be diverted into business.
*However,the challenge for business venturing by many youths is the unreliability of banks and other financial institutions.(strict business documentations,collateral and high interest rates)
*capital,set up and support also seem to be the challenge as to why many youths can’t venture into stable businesses.
*Bringing a reliable business loan scheme will encourage many youths to venture into business because of flexible terms of access,reasonably low interest rates and no collateral.
*The scheme will facilitate(oil)the running of businesses, such as access of land (farmers and entrepreneurs ),drafting business plans for the clients  and the acquisition of documentations for business ..
*The goal is to bridge the gap that exists between the youths and lending institutions.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

*The business loan scheme will generate income from the interest rates of the loans to its clients.
*The goal is to help our clients businesses flourish which result in more money (interest rates).
*This loan scheme will work with university/college graduates and already established businesses for youths and these are people who the government or private sector may employ one one day.
*Youths in lufwanyama can venture in businesses like (timber processing,agriculture and entrepreneurship) and this would yield more money and employment for the communities.
*The business loan scheme will make it more easier for entrepreneurs to access loans,receive support and continuity,flexible terms and reasonably low interest rates.

> Technical Feasibility

*This innovation will be designed to work like the student’s loan board,with the view of providing reliable financing mechanisms with flexible qualifications  graduates and already established business men to reduce unemployment.
*This scheme will be readily available for youths and other entrepreneurs.
*The scheme will nurture and support small businesses grow into big businesses.
*Government gives reliable school loans to students,the business loan scheme will be like the continuation of empowerment for the youth.

> Anticipated Social Impact

*This loan scheme will help the youths to venture into different businesses and hence create employment for others.
*The loan scheme will encourage more youths into business because of flexible terms and conditions for business.
*The current market needs more citizens to venture into business and balance unemployment.
