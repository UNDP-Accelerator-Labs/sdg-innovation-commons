Project objectives:
The project aims to identify and nurture innovative water-se-
curity entrepreneurs and SMEs in Northern Jordan. This pilot
project seeks to contribute to multiple dimensions of sustain-
able development, including by aiming to create additional
job opportunities for Syrian refugees and Jordanian host
communities in Mafraq and Jerash.
