A youth initiative media house setup to ensure full accurate and unbiased informative delivery to the Zambian people in all areas

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

First and foremost the creation of this media house is to increase employment for the youths that don’t have qualifications to join big media houses. It also acts as an income generating platform through marketing and advertising hence contributing greatly to Country’s economy cause we will be marketing local made products and increase market chances for small scale businesses

> Technical Feasibility

The media house is designed in a way that it accommodates the aspect of marketing and advertising which in terms generates revenue for the media house as a way of contribution to the economy. Through various shows we host much is achieved and done that contributes greatly to the environment and country

At all cost. Reviews show that our platform has contributed greatly to peoples lives in just a short period of time through various things. Like promoting of entrepreneurship.
