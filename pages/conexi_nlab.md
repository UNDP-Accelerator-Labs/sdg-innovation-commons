> Description

ConexiónLab is an ecosystem of digital and innovative solutions implemented by the NGO Conexión, aimed at enhancing access to education and promoting job creation and entrepreneurship for young people in vulnerable situations.


ConexiónLab generates digital transformation in rural areas of Bolivia and Latin America by using digital solutions for training in digital skills, utilizing a digital platform, community connectivity models, and belonging to a digital community, JunteX.

ConexiónLab encourages the active participation of women in the initiative, promoting their leadership and digital entrepreneurship initiatives to immerse them in economic empowerment processes that improve their income and that of their region.
