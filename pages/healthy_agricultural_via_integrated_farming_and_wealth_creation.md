> Title of the innovation

Healthy agricultural via integrated farming and wealth creation

> Brief description of the innovation

Integrated farming may be defined as a  form of agriculture aimed at minimizing the use of inputs from outside the farm by implementing a variety of production enterprises, long and diversified crop rotations, crop residue or animal excreta restitution to the soil.
Integrated farming is one of the most organic and healthy way of farming. It's  doesn't damaged the crops, instead it makes  soil more fertile and reduces on the cost of buying fertiliser.
