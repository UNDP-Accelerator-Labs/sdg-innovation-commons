> Brief description about the identified local solution:

The vital home is the first project in Iraq that aims to provide the best medical and nursing services at home through licensed professionals. It also provides free consultation service by calling customer service for urgent cases. This project relies on the principle of telephony and the use of GPS technology to reach patients.
