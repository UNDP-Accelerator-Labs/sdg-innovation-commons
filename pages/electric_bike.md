> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem & solution description)

He makes an electric bike using locally available resources such as already used steel and batteries to make it affordable. The bike uses electricity and not fuel to make it environmentally friendly. The innovation is aimed at helping disabled people with easy mobility.
