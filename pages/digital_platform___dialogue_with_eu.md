The project CSO Dialogue – Platform for Structural Participation in EU Integrations aims to facilitate meaningful and structured participation of the civil society that expresses the views of citizens, to impact key sector reforms in the EU accession process. The platform offers a opportunity for structural participation of the civil society in institutional consultation mechanisms, thereby increasing availability of information and support for participation in policy-making related to the EU accession process.   The platform has the following functions:  1. conducting research to measure involvement and participation of the civil society; 2. organizing events and public consultations; 3. monitoring the work of sector working groups by developing analyses and reports; 4. designing visual charts on key conclusions from work of sector working groups in order to improve information dissemination to citizens about this process; 5. production of video materials, educational videos, tutorials, and the like; 6. drafting a declaration on transparency of the negotiations process; 7. streaming meetings of the Council for Cooperation between the Government of RNM and Civil Society; 8. organizing annual conferences around the issue of civil society participation in sector policy creation and implementation
