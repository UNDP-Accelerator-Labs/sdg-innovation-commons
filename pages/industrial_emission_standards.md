> Type of the Solution

Regulation

> Affected Sector

Industry

> Description of the solution

Regulations that set limits on the amount of air pollutants that industrial facilities can release into the atmosphere. These standards aim to protect public health and the environment by controlling emissions from various industrial processes.

> Socio-economic effects

Improved air quality, reduced respiratory illnesses, potential economic benefits from cleaner air
