![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2023/blobs/solutions/818f9929e1477c275903019382ae145e.png)


Transition plans are agile approach to identify a strategy that contributes to the integrated management of waste and solid waste from local governments.  This process allows us to outline actions and prioritize them within a strategic framework that accelerates the strengthening of a legal and institutional framework, citizen participation and improvement of municipal capacities.  


It is a method that can be replicated in different contexts and with different work teams. In addition, it is possible to train other people so that they can develop the same methodology with more people
