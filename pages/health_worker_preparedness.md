> Description of the solution

Health workers are often the first line of defence during an extreme heat event. Health workers such as community health volunteers, doctors, nurses and emergency medical technicians need to be trained to recognize the signs and sysmptoms of heat-related illnessnes in order to correctly diagnose and treat.
