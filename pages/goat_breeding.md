> Title of the innovation

Goat breeding

> Brief description of the innovation

The project is a youth based project aimed breeding goats by crossing improved breeds with local selected breeds. This project aims to provide life saving skills by engaging in agricultural production which goat rearing. The project will also enable participants to have hands own skills which help a young generation to survive on their own.The breeding of goats is profitable and can provide much needed income. The milk from goat is also nutrious and can also provide meat which currently fetch more in Dr congo
