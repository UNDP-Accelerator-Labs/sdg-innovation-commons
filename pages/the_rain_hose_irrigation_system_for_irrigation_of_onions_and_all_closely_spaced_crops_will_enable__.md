> Brief description of the innovation

Rain Hose is an affordable spray irrigation technology. It is a replacement for Sprinkler Irrigation System. It is easy to install and maintain. Rain Hose is flexible hose with pattern of drip holes. These drip holes are made with nano punching technology to ensure uniform flow of water. Rain Hose is suitable for closely spaced crops, onion, vegetable crops, leafy vegetables, groundnut etc.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

I will supply this Product in bulk to Agro dealers who are my primary target through out Zambia. The Agro dealers will retail the product to small scale farmers who are the end users. The small scale farmers will use the product and produce more food to sale through out the year to super markets and marketeers. This kind of value chain will enable me to generate income, the agro dealers will generate income, the farmers will generate income and the super markets and marketeers will generate income. The challenge prize will help me get more stock from my manufacturer in India and in turn increase revenue. The product is enviromentally friendly as it can be recycled after 6 years of use.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed to help small scale farmers irrigate their crops efficiently and effectively reducing energy and labor costs whilst increasing yields. A prototype exists and we have been selling the product for the past 2 years now.

The Zambian agriculture sector is currently yearning for innovations in agriculture in order to produce more food for the country and the region. The current market has responded very well to the innovation.
