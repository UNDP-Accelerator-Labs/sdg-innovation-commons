> Innovation Solution Description

Organic Agricultural Methods

> Qualitative Results of the Innovation Solution 



1. Improved soil health: By reducing the use of harmful chemicals and pesticides, Serendipalm has helped to improve soil health. This has resulted in increased soil fertility and better crop yields.
2. Access to markets: Serendipalm has helped small-scale farmers to access markets for their products. This has helped to increase their incomes and improve their livelihoods.

> Quantitative Result of the Innovation

 1. Increased crop yields: According to Serendipalm's website, farmers who have adopted organic farming methods have seen a 15-20% increase in their crop yields.
 2. Increased incomes for farmers: Through its support of small-scale farmers, Serendipalm has helped to increase their incomes. According to a report by Fairtrade International, Serendipalm paid an average premium of 22% above the market price for palm oil, which has helped to support local communities.
