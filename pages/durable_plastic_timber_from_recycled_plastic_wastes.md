Solution owner:  EcoAct- Tanzania

EcoAct recycle plastic wastes into durable plastic timber and poles. This technology enable to reduce the deforestation since poles and planks can be used the same way as normal wooden timber but still perform much better since are;
i) termite free
ii) Do not rot
iii) Long lasting 
iv) Affordable 
v) once you want to replace them, you can sell back to producers as raw materials.
