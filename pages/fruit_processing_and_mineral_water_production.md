> Brief description of the innovation

Fruit processing into various products such as pure fruit juices dry fruits jams syrup
and process and package mineral water
we process fruits using organic methods and  solar drying of fruits which are packaged with prolonged shelf life

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

through the processing of fruits and packaging it it helps the out grower farmers with ready market for their fruits and also creates employment for the youth and women in the communities. as a company it brings revenue through profits made from sales. we sale our products to supermarkets chain stores,municipal markets wholesale and retail shops.
we encourage organic farming by converting our waste into manure and annimal feeds.

> Technical Feasibility

the innovation begins from the fruit collection to organic processing of the fruits into various products.
the proposed implementation plan  is to buy from out grower farmers then process using organic methods then package and distribute to consumers of all levels

> Anticipated Social Impact

it creates more income for the farmers and also creates jobs on thier farms 
it also creates jobs with factory and distribution workers.
the inovation is relevant to the current market in that it reduces wastage of fruits and provides healthy drinks and snacks as alternative for non healthy beverages and snacks
