> Title of the innovation

Seba Agro marketing Limited is registered entity that wants to engage in Agro Marketing and process. This will be done by away of doing an out grower scheme in Rice production and finally adding value to it by polishing the commodity.

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation  has a total of 3 facilitators who are very experienced in the field of Agriculture Marketing. 
The  innovation will be carried out under  a company (Seba Agro Marketing limited)   formed and registered to be fully engaged in agriculture marketing and processing mainly in the following commodities; Rice and Groundnuts.
Rice is top on the agenda where we want to promote upland rice growing by a way of an out grower scheme. This is in a quest to boost rice production in the province. In this out grower scheme , our target groups are the women, the disabled and the youths both male and female  these  we feel are more vulnerable. The company wishes to also to add value to the crop by processing the rice, Sunflower and ground nuts, Sunflower  into cooking and groundnuts into  peanut butter. This will require the purchase of Processing machines for all these processes. 
polishing machine will be procured to add value to the rice , oil processing machine will also be required. The innovation also requires the purchase of  Peanut butter making machine so that there is value addition to the groundnuts. 
This innovation is looking at empowering women and the youths in a way of offering agriculture inputs and employment opportunities  because all these processes require labour.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation is quite viable in that  there that many  institutions that can support  for its growth. the likes of chain stores, boarding schools hospitals lodges and may more where this innovation can rely on. 
The Rice that will be grown will be purchased from the farmer groups that will be formed and  be sold to  Chain stores at whole price, Boarding secondary schools, Hospitals, marketeers, and individuals for home consumption. Some of the rice will be exported to neighboring countries to raise more income.
The groundnuts will be processed into peanut butter  while the  Sunflower will be processed into cooking oil  and its by product,  the cake, to be sold to companies that make animal feed. Cooking oil will be sold locally by a way of opening retail outlets in various areas in order to raise income. This, we believe, will offer continuity to other businesses in the Districts and Eastern Province at large. on other hand, Chain stores  will benefit immensely.  because they will be off takers in the this business. All in all the Innovation shall have retail outlets across the the Province in addition to offtakers.

> Technical Feasibility

The Innovation will be managed as follows: 
1. The CEO will be the overall overseer of the whole operation
2.Below the CEO, there shall be the Agriculture Manager, who will also be in charge of all the operations through the supervision  of Extension officers that will be offering technical advice to farmers how to care for their crop fields.  These shall be two. These Extension officer shall be technocrats  in Agriculture to be able to effectively advise farmers on technical issues to enhance high productivity. Then below the Extension Officers there Buyers to buyers, who will be picked from among the farmers to be buying the commodities, and these shall  use bicycles as their mode of transport while Extension officers will be on motorbikes.
3. Then comes in the Accountant who will be in charge of Finances who shall have minimum qualification of up to ACCA level one.
4. The smooth running of the machinery to be guaranteed,  the innovation shall employ machinists with trade certificates.
Farmers will recruited in groups of Twenty and we hope to have 30 groups . these shall be supplied with seeds in each commodity mention above to grow but at  reasonable prices, since these shall be procured from seed companies. farmers shall be made to sign contracts not to sell the produce to outsiders.  Even the issuance of the seeds shall be documented. 
These farmer groups shall be monitored by the Extension Officers and offered technical advice.
The Innovation through Seba Agro Marketing, will procure a light track to transport the commodities once collected from the farmers.  
After processing the the produce, shall package the products  to taken for sale either to Offtakers or Outlets.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Firstly it will provide employment to the youths in the areas as employment opportunities are not easy to come by.  There will food security as farmers will be able to improve their yields as extension services will be readily available through the government extension workers and the field staff that will employed. These shall work hand in hand . However, the innovation will require the purchase of a Rice Polisher to enable it process the paddy rice it will buy from the farmers. The Rice Polisher will also be accessible to the community to polish their rice for either home use or business at very affordable charge, this further  improve will food security in their homes.
