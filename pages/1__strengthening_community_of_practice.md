## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights) 

1. Strengthening Community of Practice is a good way of building sustainable partnerships;
2. Despite the shared values it is still challenging to unite private sector companies into community and encourage them to contribute into development agenda.
3. Dialogue platforms are crucial to facilitate integration of development topics into agenda of local governments 

> Considering the outcomes of this learning challenge, which of the following best describe the handover process? (Please select all that apply)

- Our work has led to a significant change in public policy at a national or local level
- Our work has been picked up by UNDP or the government and has now expanded geographically in our country
- Our private sector partners have expanded our joint work through their own resources in our country or internationally

> Can you provide more detail on your handover process?





1. Pro-active joint advocacy and fruitful collaboration with diverse partners contributed to the decision of the Cabinet of Ministers to adopt the law and launch the Park of Creative Industries making Kyrgyzstan first country in the world with such unique regime for creators and innovators.

2. We scaled the platform geographically to engage more creators and innovators into the community including those from rural area. 


3. The platform was successfully integrated into existing UNDP Kyrgyzstan projects fostering the agenda of creative economy into core programming. 


4. Scaling of the  platform is introduced into the integrated UNDP's offer on creative economy being used for resource mobilization purpose.
