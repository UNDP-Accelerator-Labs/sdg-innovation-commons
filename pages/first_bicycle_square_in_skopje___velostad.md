This space is a new functional space for Skopje cyclists. The bicycle Square, in addition to the holders for safe bicycle parking and bicycle service station, also includes a wardrobe for quick change, cabins for long-term parking of bicycles, a drinking water fountain and appropriate urban equipment that further humanizes this space.
