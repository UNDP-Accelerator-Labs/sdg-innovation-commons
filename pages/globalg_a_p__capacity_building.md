> Description of the solution

GLOBALG.A.P. is a farm assurance program promoting safe and sustainable agricultural practices worldwide. Its capacity-building initiatives focus on training farmers, agribusinesses, and stakeholders in best practices for food safety, sustainability, and market access. The program includes workshops, training sessions, and resources tailored to local agricultural contexts.
