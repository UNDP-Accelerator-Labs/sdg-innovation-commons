> Brief description of the innovation

Mulenga Mugabeni General Dealers is a neighborhood stationery store and office supply located in a centralized area in - between a busy area of Kabwata .

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Our Stationery store will generate income through printing Services, Office equipment sales. This business idea will help Schools around kabwata with ease of access to school materials. Most schools around Kabwata access their stationery from printing services that are beyond their location.. Most Stationery suppliers have high fees making it hard for them to order School items.

> Technical Feasibility

My innovation is under Education... The store will help schools have easy access to our store without having trouble to travel longer distances

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will create jobs for more youths around Kabwata
