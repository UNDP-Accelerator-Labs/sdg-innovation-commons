> Description of the solution

The Heat Health Action Plan (HHAP) was finalized and adopted by the Government of Macedonia. Heat Action Plans can be done by establishing a heat monitoring system, informative campaigns, the mobilization of health care professionals, volunteers, social workers and trained caregivers in the surveillance and management of individuals with known vulnerabilities or stand alone.
