The project is a pioneer in Guatemala, seeking to be facilitators of tools, knowledge and technologies to take advantage of the plastic that is thrown away in landfills. Through different alliances, the project has managed to create a municipal recycling center in Mixco, in addition to being an environmental education center.&nbsp;
