The Gambia Accelerator Lab Annual Report
AUGUST 23, 2022
The Gambia Accelerator Lab Annual Report captures the work of the Accelerator Lab in supporting the country office do development differently by fostering greater integrated initiatives to address new challenges such as the unprecedented COVID-19 pandemic and other emerging global concerns like the impact of the war in Ukraine. This is seen in the partners they work with, the solutions they map and use, and through the concept of experimentation using a portfolio approach.
