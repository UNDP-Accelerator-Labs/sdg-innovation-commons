> Description of the solution

The xFarm Academy Advanced course is an online platform that offers training in digital agriculture. It covers topics such as precision farming, data analysis, and the use of technology in agriculture. The course is designed for farmers and other professionals in the agricultural industry who want to learn how to use digital tools to improve their operations.
