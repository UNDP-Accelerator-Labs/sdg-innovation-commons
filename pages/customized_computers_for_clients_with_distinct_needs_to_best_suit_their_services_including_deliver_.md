> Title of the innovation

Customized computers for clients with distinct needs to best suit their services,including deliveries of such services.

> Brief description of the innovation

Due to different needs of clients where Information technology (IT) is concerned, there are lots of people who struggle to assemble the necessary tools needed to suit their service delivery for these clients. So instead of congesting their office spaces with lots of gadgets, this unique service of "Customized computers" comes into play,as it uniquely packages the tools into one piece that can be made readily available to everyone in need at an affordable price/amount.
