> Title of the innovation

ENHANCING THE LIVES OF PERSONS WITH DISAABILITIES THROUGH INCLUSIVE EDUCATION

> Brief description of the innovation

Enhancing the Lives of persons with Disabilities through an Inclusive Education is an Innovation that I have come up with and implemented in order to make majority of persons with Disabilities access education. Being a developing Country,Zambia has got alot of persons with Disabilities that are ILLITERATE and not able to be Independent in lives.My Innovation has impacted their lives through an inclusive education program that I have embarked on in the Eastern Province of Zambia specifically Sinda District.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

My innovation will add value to the persons with Disabilities in Sinda District of Eastern province of Zambia through provision of quality Education to persons with Disabilities. It will also add value to those youths with Disabilities that have various entrepreneurship  skills  such as tailoring, carpentry,electrical engineering and many more.My innovativation will help to revamp and boost these skills by helping the youths with Disabilities with capital in Sinda District.

IMPLEMENTATION PLAN.
