Cholera outbreaks in Zambia mainly affect Lusaka and the other densely populated peri-urban areas. The outbreak is mainly due to unsafe drinking water and faecal contamination. The affected areas are usually high density areas which mostly do not have running water, mainly have pit latrines and shallow wells where the water can easily be contaminated. 

The Innovation seeks to detect, prevent and cure cholera cases faster. 

Benefits: 

      ·       Promotion of local pharmaceutical industry

      ·       Early detection of cholera.

     ·       Prevention and cure of cholera 

     ·       Reduced Cholera related deaths
