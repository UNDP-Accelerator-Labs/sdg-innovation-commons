Telegram chatbot that helps you to arrange a free pick-up of your household recyclable waste. In such case you can make sure that the waste is recycled (all waste from the regular is sent to landfills) while you can point location of the trash bag and time for pick-up.

[https://web.telegram.org/#/im?p=@EcolaAssistBot](https://web.telegram.org/#/im?p=@EcolaAssistBot)
