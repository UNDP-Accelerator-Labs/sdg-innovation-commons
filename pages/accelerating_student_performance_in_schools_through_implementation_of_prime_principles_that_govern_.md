> Title of the innovation

ACCELERATING STUDENT PERFORMANCE IN SCHOOLS THROUGH IMPLEMENTATION OF PRIME PRINCIPLES THAT GOVERN STUDENT BEHAVIOUR AND THINKING.

> Brief description of the innovation

The "ACCELERATING STUDENT PERFORMANCE IN SCHOOLS THROUGH IMPLEMENTATION OF PRIME PRINCIPLES THAT GOVERN STUDENT BEHAVIOUR AND THINKING" is a practical student program that is aimed at helping students in schools overcome the difficulties in learning. One of the prominent factors that has contributed to student poor performance in schools is the lack of awareness of self-imposed principles that help attain school goals. This initiative, therefore, will help address the said factor.
