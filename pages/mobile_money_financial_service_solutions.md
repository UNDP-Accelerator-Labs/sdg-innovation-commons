> Qualitative Results of the Innovation

1. Increased Financial Inclusion: Paga platform has provides a simple and accessible way for people to access financial services, even in remote areas where traditional banking services are not available.
2. Access to Credit: Paga partnered with a number of financial institutions to provide loans to users. By leveraging data from Paga's platform, these financial institutions are able to make informed lending decisions.
4. Increased Economic Growth: Paga provides a simple and accessible way for people to access financial services.
5. Improved Efficiency: Paga has also improved the efficiency of financial transactions in Nigeria. The platform has enabled people to carry out transactions quickly and easily, without the need for cash. This has helped to reduce the costs and risks associated with cash transactions, and has improved the overall efficiency of the financial system in Nigeria.
