> Title of the innovation

Kaoma Library

> Brief description of the innovation

With the coming in of the corona virus in 2019, student are having a challenge to cope with their studies while at home. mainly because of the unconducive environment at home. So Setting up national libraries in every district will address this challenge and offer a social amenity. However in this modern era these libraries cannot only have books but also they should have computers and internet which will be an add advantage for anyone who wants to do research.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The idea of setting up at least a national library in every district won't just empower the students but also create employment and revenue as the users of these library would have to pay very affordable subscription fee per month or in the case of once of users an affordable user fee per day.

> Technical Feasibility

Hypothetically book donors are around the corner, five desktops per district will do. one reauter, tables, bookshelves, chairs and an affordable venue favourable located for everyone.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Empower students access to easy conducive environment for their research and studies which are aimed at improving the social, economic, political state of the nation. Looking at the current numbers of student back at their homes from school to continue with online learning the current market looks promising.
