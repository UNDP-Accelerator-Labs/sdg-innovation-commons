## 

Çiğdemim Association (a 24 year old community) aims to show that organic-natural product cultivation in the countryside can also be done in the city center in the urban garden they created in 2012, to raise awareness in the society, to share basic information about natural product cultivation, to gain skills, and to help children and young people gain the habit of growing products, carries out its activities to promote the plant species.
