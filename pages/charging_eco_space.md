> Type of the Solution

Community-led Renewable Energy Initiative.

> Affected Sector

Energy, Education, Public Policy, Community Development

> Description of the solution

The project in Kutaisi, supported by RICDOG and 350.org, involves installing energy-generating bicycles in public squares to power devices and promote renewable energy. This 6-month initiative also includes community events to enhance ecological culture and raise awareness about climate change impacts.

> Socio-economic effects

Fosters energy independence, educates the public on sustainable practices, and enhances community resilience.
