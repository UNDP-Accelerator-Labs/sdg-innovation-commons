> Title of the innovation

Health Food &amp; Efficient Food Budgeting

> Brief description of the innovation

A software application that helps promotes healthy eating and efficient expenditure on food. Let me give  an example, the application provides a variety of healty recipes that can be made local ingredients. Furthermore, it gives the prices and nearest location of where the you get your ingredients

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Since the application will be free, the major source of income will be internal-app advertisements for companies.

I will be working with local businesses and other businesses in order to get updates pricing of ingredients and also add their location on the map, which will allow for users of the application to know where to get the items required from. Hence, bring customers to them and increasing their income

My target market is the youth, especially students who really need to know how to budget and eat healthy. And anyone who cooks 

The prize will make grow the business by allowing the application to operate internationally
