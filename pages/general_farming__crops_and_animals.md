> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

My business is aimed at supplying local people with the to be supplied to our neatest market which is kafue. This I believe will help to curb nutritional challenges among the local people and will provide jobs for the local youths.

> Technical Feasibility

In the near future IAM planning to grow commercial which I believe is possible once enough money is raised. And I would like to diversify into rabbit production and fish farming so as to supply the local people with protein which i believe they lack so much.
