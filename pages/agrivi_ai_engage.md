> Description of the solution

Agrivi AI Engage is an AI-powered virtual assistant designed to help farmers. It provides personalized advice, answers questions, and offers recommendations based on real-time data and expert knowledge. By using this tool, farmers can improve their decision-making, increase efficiency, and ultimately boost their yields.
