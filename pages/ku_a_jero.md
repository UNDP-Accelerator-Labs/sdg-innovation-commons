> Project

Moiru

> Partners

National Innovation Strategy, Presidential Delivery Unit. National SDG Comission

> Description

This "Heads of households Association," helps women and men with limited resources, vulnerable to their situation of poverty, often extreme. The Creation of Micro-enterprises, and a productive community farm, where women can acquire the products of this place and be self-sufficient from their own production. As required by the Association, the beneficiaries must have a savings account.
