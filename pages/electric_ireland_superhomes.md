> Type of the Solution

Subsidy scheme

> Affected Sector

Households

> Description of the solution

As an obligated party under the Irish energy efficiency obligation scheme (EEOS), Electric Ireland has set up a one-stop shop that provides energy efficiency measures such as installing heat pumps or a whole house retrofit including a renewable energy air source heat pump, high-standard insulation, air-tightness and advanced ventilation for indoor air quality. To finance the services, energy-poor customers can access grants from the Sustainable Energy Authority of Ireland.

> Socio-economic effects

Improved health, increased energy affordability due to lowered consumption
