> What is the Title for this solution?

Cement Mixer Forest Fire extinguisher 

> How would you describe this solution in under 300 words? 

Forest Fires becoming more and more prominent with little to no resources to fight blaze from government the people of North Lebanon championed a new way to get water into inaccessible steep or dangerous areas caught in fire. Using a cement mixer attached to a water to pump water in a surgical manner.
