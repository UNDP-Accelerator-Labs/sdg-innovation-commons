Featuring a terrace and a shared kitchen, Barta Valley Tourism Camp is conveniently situated in Madaba, 27 km from Ma'in Hot Springs and 32 km from Dead Sea Panoramic Complex & Museum. This tented camp provides free private parking and a 24-hour front desk.

Towels and bed linen are provided.

A halal breakfast is available daily at the tented camp.

Umm er-Rasas is 32 km from Barta Valley Tourism Camp, while the Greek Orthodox Basilica of Saint George is 42 km away. The nearest airport is Queen Alia International Airport, 60 km from the accommodation.
