> Description of the solution

This solution aims to enhance the comprehensive prevention and control ability of forest fires and improve the effectiveness of forest management and protection by applying technologies such as Internet of Things perception, Ndvisensors, artificial intelligence, and spatio-temporal analysis, an all-weather, high-frequency, large-scale three-dimensional intelligent monitoring network integrating multiple means such as satellites, drones, thermal imaging videos, and manual patrols is constructed; a forest fire prevention big data pool including basic spatial databases, forestry thematic databases, and protection databases is constructed; and a forestry and grassland intelligent visualization platform with functions such as fire monitoring and early warning, fire situation judgment, fire fighting command, situation analysis, disaster loss assessment, and fire supervision is constructed.
