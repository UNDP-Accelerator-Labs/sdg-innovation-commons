> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovations will help generate an income in that once the application is launched we will be able to have an in app purchase or subscription that can vary from monthly to 7 yearly payment plans. 

It will benefit other businesses in that clinicians/doctors or psychiatrists to be able to have regular and more stable patients. The people who use the application will also be benefiting in reaching goals of less times spent in admission/hospitals and thereby can generate a higher income and be more productive.

The challenge prize will help us launch the application on various platforms (Huawei, Apple and Android/Google) and help us reach the world market.

> Technical Feasibility

The applications (mental health &amp; personal growth app) is designed to improve the environment aiding to less stress experienced during the pandemic. Health will be improved and new skills can be acquired. 
We are not developing what already exists but would like to enhance existing ones and introducing new appealing means to tackle a problem in society.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The mental health and personal growth of the current generation deeply engrossed with social media is deteriorating. The innovation focuses on having a counter attack on the detrimental effects of peoples mental health.
The innovation is vital in order to improve the performance of the generation leading the country forward.
