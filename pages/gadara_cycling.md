## Solution holder: Ala'aldeen Al-Akash

https://www.instagram.com/gadara_cycling/

https://www.traveljordanian.com/explore/wellness-and-ecotourism/gadara-cycling?lang=en

Discover the hills of Umm Qais, while cycling through its villages and farmlands overlooking the Golan heights and the Jordan Valley. Your experienced local guide - Ala'aldeen - will take you on a route that contains a combination of flat farmland and easy climbs.

The routes availability vary depending on location, weather, and season.
