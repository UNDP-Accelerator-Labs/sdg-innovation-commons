> Title of the innovation

Help rural schools with cost effective classroom and housing.

> Brief description of the innovation

In rural Zambia. Most schools lack proper rooms to be used for learning and teaching. Without redtape and institutional inefficiencies, we can help build more classrooms and teachers houses using pre fabricated materials and turnkey technologies.
