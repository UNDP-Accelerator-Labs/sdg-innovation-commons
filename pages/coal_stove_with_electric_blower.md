> Observation &amp; Description: What is the innovation about? What need does it answer? What does it do?

Coal Stove with Electric Blower - a prototype coal blower with an electric blower as accessory. The operator does not need to monitor and have continues blowing since the accessory does the trick.

> Need: What need or challenge does this answer?

It serves as a solution to the unending monitoring and blowing of stove. It also serves as an answer to the burning inefficiency of the coal.

> User: Who is the solution for? How does it answer the need of the user? Why does it matter to the user?

It will be used by cooks in restaurants, carenderias, and even homes. It will give ease to the operator and makes cooking more easier, less hassle. It is significant since it will improve kitchen operation.

> Insight(s): What does this observation mean?  What have you learned from the observation?

Typical equipment which can be seen in homes and firms can be improved more by innovation. These equipment can be modified through changing the design or adding more accessories to improve its function.

> Opportunities: What are other opportunities where the solution can be applied?

The prototype can be replicated and adapted by other communities and business firms who are in the food sector, this can be used to improve efficiency and operations and also for those who wish to improve their kitchen errands.
