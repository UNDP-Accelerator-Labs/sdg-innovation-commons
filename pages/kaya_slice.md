This innovation changed the texture and form of kaya jam (coconut egg jam) into slices. Kaya slice can be eaten on its own or with bread. This innovation makes storing and preparation of the jam easier. Moreover, through innovations and application of food technology, the fishy smell of jam has been solved and it also increased the shelf life of the product.
