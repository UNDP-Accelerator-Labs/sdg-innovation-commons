> Title of the innovation

Avigo Finace. An inclusive finance platform for the Zambian MSME.

> Brief description of the innovation

Avigo Finance is a technology enabled application and web based platform that facilitates short-term working loans and financial literacy provider for micro, small and medium  (MSMEs) in the urban agricultural value chain. We aim to empower fruit vendors, groceries stores, small fast-food outlets, cereals shops and other food vendors by use of digital technology to offer  tools and resources, built to address Poverty's interconnected root causes in a scalable and sustainable way.

We are equipped with intimate understanding of immediate financial needs of our customer base. We seek to reorganize the agricultural value chain by empowering urban agribusiness vendors (demand side) so that they can unchain the rural farmer (supply side) for an efficient ecosystem and elevation of everyday realities. We make business growth to be as clear as glass .

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Avigo Finance empowers MSMEs (fruit vendors, grocery store, small fast-food joints, cereals shops and other food vendors) through short term financing that is paramount in overcoming short term challenges and enabling long term growth. Our credit facilities will allow you to keep the lights and actively continue to cultivate your business by ensuring that it is well stocked at all times.  Avigo Finance short term financing will help facilitate recovery, keeping your business on its feet and moving forward in cases of business emergencies. Our revenue drivers are 
1.short-term business loans,with only up to 15% interest rate paid monthly.
2. AvigoBook App subscriptions, a comprehensive AvigoBook application that securely allows you to keep records of all your business activities,  manage your business inventory, sales activities, business expenses, keep records and generate reports to monitor business performance. We anticipate a huge demand for our services as the number of MSMEs continue to increase in Lusaka, the prize money will help us reach more MSMEs  in the region and foster job creation and sustainable business growth

> Technical Feasibility

We are developing the application with Flutter 2.2 user interface toolkit for mobile, web, desktop and embedded devices. Our platform will be expressive and flexible user interface with quick ship features with a focus on native end user experience. Layered architecture that will allow for full customization which will result in incredibly fast rendering and expressive and flexible application design. We are currently developing a prototype and we confident of securing funding to develop the actual Application. Our implementation plan follows through vigorous stages from budgeting to  developing a timeline of 3-6 months, lead time for app testing and availability for downloads and  monitoring the results.
