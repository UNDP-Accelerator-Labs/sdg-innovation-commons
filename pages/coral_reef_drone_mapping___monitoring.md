Blue Bot: Underwater coral reef mapping and monitoring system using drone technology, artificial intelligence and machine learning. Blue Bot will survey reef areas down to 200 ft., quickly categorize and analyze data, increase the marine conservation asset pool for the Caribbean and create visual content that can be used for promotional, educational, stock or entertainment purposes.
