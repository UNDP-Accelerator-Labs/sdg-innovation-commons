Zeca Nfad lives in Bissora of the Oio Regio. Although geographically close the capital, it lacks in terms of local institutions including banks. Using his tech savyness Zeca serves the community by selling and fixing electronics at an affordable price. He also provides mobile money services which is the main source of money transactions in the region as no banks are available and Western Union closed down.
