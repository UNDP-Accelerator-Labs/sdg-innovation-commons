Circular Economy Roadmap in Da Nang city
FEBRUARY 14, 2023
The Circular Economy Roadmap for Da Nang city has been developed by the City’s People’s Committee with the support from the UN Development Programme (UNDP).
The roadmap will be implemented until 2030 in association with the 10-year national action plan. It focuses on communication; education; training; and capacity building, and prepares the premise for the implementation of pilot projects and models in the prioritized areas of the circular economy and replicate these models to the entire economy.
The tasks and solutions set out in this Roadmap will help Da Nang city achieve 05 general goals of circular economy development by 2030:
1. Environment
Reduce exploitation and use of non-renewable resources and water resources
Extend the use time of materials, equipment, products, goods, and components
Reduce waste and minimize adverse impacts on the environment
2. State management
Enhance capacity and raise awareness, knowledge and skills about circular economy
3. Economy and businesses
Improve the productivity and profit of businesses and the economy
4. Infrastructure and technology
Develop a circular economy based on digital technology and transformation
5. Society
Create more green jobs and improve living quality
