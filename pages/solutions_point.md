> Brief description about the identified local solution:

University students in Iraq suffer from the lack of practical experience and the practical aspect of the academic curriculum
In general, and in graduation projects, especially because of the gap between the old academic style and the needs of the labor market, the weak self-development of students and the teaching staff, due to the electronic entertainment temptations for students and the lack of responsibility for the teaching staff under the pretext of receiving monthly dues in a consistent manner and not related to additional activities. 
The proposed value of the project

First: the basic value:
1 - A shared electronic space that brings together private sector companies and university teaching staff
And university students and graduates.

Second: Added Values:
1 - Easy to access (website enabled mobile app).
2 - Free for students and faculty.
3 - The content of the common space is scientifically practical and stems from the core of the labor market.
4 - It is considered a means of development in the practical side of the teaching staff in universities.
5 - The first electronic space in Iraq that brings together the above-mentioned parties.
6 - Increasing the chances of graduates getting a job in the participating companies.
7 - An opportunity for students to work on graduation projects close to the job market.
8 - Reducing the cost of finding solutions to problems or challenges facing private sector companies as a result
Scientific projects resulting from students, which will lead to partnerships between them in the future, however
This is at the cost of time.
9 - It contains a database of the first of its kind for projects and companies.
10 - Providing internship for students and alumni with the participating companies
