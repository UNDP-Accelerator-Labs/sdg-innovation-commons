> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem & solution description)

The stove addresses the problem of lesser heat production from other available charcoal stove and uses readily available Paddy husk thus reducing the operational cost. The stove also helps areas where the products need to be kept warm for long hours. It is a cylindrical stove, which a perforated charcoal chamber inside. There is gap of 3-5 inches gap, depending on the size of the stove, between the out encasing wall and the perforated cylindrical chamber inside
