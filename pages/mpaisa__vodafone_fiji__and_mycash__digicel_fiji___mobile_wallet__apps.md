Mobile wallet apps allow customers to transfer money to other users within Fiji without fees, receive money transfers from friends and family abroad, deposit and hold money in the app with no fees, make bill/ remote payments, make payments at selected outlets using a QR code—all without the need for a bank account. This is especially convenient for those living in rural and remote areas (provided they have network coverage).
