> Brief description of the innovation

The Business
The The Culture Food Resort and Outside Catering is a local enterprise which is registered with the Registrar of Companies. The enterprise is owned by Mrs. Fridah N. Chileka &amp; Mr Nchimunya S. Chileka. The business’ main services are provision of Zambian Traditional Cuisines to both local and international customers. The enterprise also provides outside catering services to households intending to host parties and dinners, organizations and companies intending to host workshops, seminars and other events and for families intending to host special parties such as wedding ceremonies.  
Marketing Consideration
The Enterprise’ main products are; Zambian Traditional dishes such as Nshima with Lumanda, Kapenta, Offals, Dry Fish, Village Chicken, Mundyoli etc . The Business shall therefore sale its products to People around Lusaka mostly those working in the long acres and mass media areas. The business shall also target tourists visiting Zambia as a way of enhancing food tourism in Zambia. The Business’ main niche is that currently there is no Typical Zambian Traditional Restaurant which is able to organize Zambian dinners for all types of people.
Economic Benefits of the project
The Project has the following Economic benefits: 
It will initially create more than 5 jobs and the number may increase as the project equally grows. 

Utilization of locally available resources thereby providing a ready market for suppliers of food products.

The government shall benefit from the projected taxes and pay as you earn from workers.

The project shall contribute to technology enhancement in the targeted five provinces.

The project shall contribute to the enhancement of tourism in Zambia

5. Products/services description
5.1. Major Activities
Provision of Traditional Foods
The Restaurant shall cook delicious Zambian Food Staffs common in all the ten provinces. The restaurant shall be providing assorted traditional food staffs to meet the need for complete meals, amongst the food staffs shall be; Cikanda, Maize Meal, Cassava and Millet Nshima  saved with different forms of local relish.  Local Drinks such as Munkoyo, Tobwa, Jenge, Chibwantu etc.
Provision of Outside Catering
Outside services will be provided to people hosting parties such as weddings, kitchen parties, Chilangamulilo, Matebeto etc. The services shall also be extended to companies and organisations in need of special food staffs.  



Financial Analysis	
Grant/Equity analysis
The business is seeking an investment of ZMK 120,575 to finance its start up for a period of 4 months of which it shall be able to attain its projected sustainability.
Project Profitability
The projected profit and loss statement, which is based on 80% capacity utilization, shows that the Project’s gross profit margin is 47% and the net profit margin is 31.8%. This means that for every ZMK 431,760 of sales, the project’s anticipated gross profit will be; ZMK 201,960   whilst the net profit could be; ZMK 137,460 Both margins are comfortably high and go to show that the project is financially viable. 
Cash Flow Balances
Throughout the 12 months, the projected cash flow reveals positive cash balances which clearly show that all things be equal, the project would be able to sustain its operations and influence a diversification process. In this business plan we have projected that the closing balance for the business as at December 2019 shall be ZMK  137,460

7. Market potential
7.1. Major Markets
As already stated in table 1, the project’s target market comprises the working class, local and international tourists and families from within Lusaka. The major market shall be derived from the nearest businesses and companies in the Eastern Part of Lusaka. 
7.2. Marketing/Communication methods
	Competition
The Enterprise’s key competitors are basically Restaurants and women providing food staffs using vehicles.  These competitors are already known by the market and sale most of their products cheaply. The key weakness of these competitors is they are dominant in fast foods of western or Indian origin and do not provide typical Zambian dishes. In Zambia now, you have to visit a household and make an older for you to have a taste of typical Zambian foods.
Advertising and promotion
The Culture food resort restaurant and Outside Catering proposes to promote and advertise its business activities using very simple and practical methods such as:
Ensuring that its services and products are continuously on demand by trying as best as is professionally possible to always offer high quality Traditional Foods at the same time building more local innovations.

Ensuring that there is prompt fulfillment of customers´ orders.

Ensuring that there is a possibility of discounts facilities for bulk and repeat customers. 

Extending credit facilities in some well examined cases including the opening of bill accounts for some known reliable customers.

Ensuring that there is always a way of getting feedback from costumers through “personal selling” i.e. going round to all key costumers to source for orders and seek their comments. 

Making sure that a good Bill Board is made to increase visibility of the Restaurant.

Updating our website regularly and offering a pictorial depiction of our products and services

Setting up the restaurant in a welcoming traditional way with interior decorations

Making the restaurant closer to the media thereby increasing its visibility.

The Executive Director
The Executive Director shall be the Policy maker for the business and shall be in charge of the overall administration of the enterprise which includes making final decisions on payments and ensuring that sales are recorded and well accounted for. They shall approve any diversification plans for this business and ensure that capital is sourced for the start up and operations of this business.
Head Chef
The Head Chef shall lead the Kitchen Brigade for the restaurant and shall plan menus in accordance with the target market. He/she shall supervise all members of the kitchen brigade
Cooks
Shall perform functions as directed by the chef and shall be in charge of cutlery management and ensuring that the levels of hygiene are maintained in the kitchen. The cooks shall make requisitions on all needs in the kitchen
 Head Waiter
Shall be in charge of the front office for the business, shall prepare bills and receive payments on behalf of the restaurant, shall ensure that the environment is clean and conducive for the customers. Shall delegate duties to the hand men.  

Hand Men
Shall perform duties as delegated by the head waiter
