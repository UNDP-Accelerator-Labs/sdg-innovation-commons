> Title of the innovation

David village chicken and meat supplies is local company that intends to sell village chicken local and export outside Zambia.

> Brief description of the innovation

David village chicken and meat supplies is a Zambian owned company that intends to sell and export chickens and other meat products. We believe eating health can prolong life thus village chicken and unprocessed meat products are the best

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Started with little capital gotten from family and friends to sell village chickens. The target is families and restaurants, who need chickens and meat products on a daily basis for consumption

> Technical Feasibility

First two years to market and win customers by marketing village chicken. Then in the next five years becoming leading supplies of village chicken and meat supplies local and internation. This will be done by door to door sell, radio, social media and online website
