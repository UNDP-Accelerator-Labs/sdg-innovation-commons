> Title of the innovation

Rise And Walk Zambia ( Rawtza)

> Brief description of the innovation

Rise and walk is a Zambian youthful group with the Aim or Inculcating moral values into youths, discouraging gangs and so on. This is done by Music, poetry, Movies and etc.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We organise actors, Musicians, writers who are talented and when a song, Movie, Book is produced it is sold on the market. This can assist writers, musicians, actors and everyone into arts by improving their Talents as long as they are part of us. Our target is the youths, because we know that a destroyed youth has a destroyed future. Hence we ensure the minds of youths are Changed. We do not just look at arts but also look at ways in which we can raise Entrepreneurs. This definitely has great value on the environment. Imagine a society with no gangs but only those who are morally upright, such a society can only bring about peace and love. Like we say " Rawtza, Building a nation of love"

> Technical Feasibility

Our Implemented plan is to start. The best way to start is to start, with funds or with no funds. We intend to start with a small group of people targeting especially Notorious communities E.g Chibolya and so on.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This can improve the lives of others with no doubts. Imagine a group that digs someone's talents, someone's capabilities and so on then show it to the world. Imagine a group that aims at Educating others through movies, arts and so on, this can definitely improve peoples lives. The proposed plan is very relevant, to the market because we will not only produces good and sound youths but also produce great ideas on the market when it comes to Arts, Entrepreneurship and so on.
