> Province

Thumeza is an innovative next generation logistics solutions provider built for enterprises. Using our flagship service Intransit, enterprises coordinate the movement of their loads and the supply of transporters, through the use of up to date data on transporter availability and dynamic route pricing. Using the peer to peer system, our enterprise clients get access to accountable and affordable solutions spanning from last mile to freight.
