The Effect of Spatial Design on Gender Norms and Roles: Jerash Camp
MAY 18, 2023
Through 2021 and 2022, the Accelerator Lab Jordan and the Society for Aid, Improvement, and Bridging (SAIB) designed and ran an experiment to study the effect of spatial design on gender norms and roles in two houses in the Jerash refugee camp in Jordan. The experiment was designed according to the published research of Zaid Awamleh, cofounder of SAIB, entitled: ‘A multi-method Behaviour Settings Analysis in a Protracted Refugee Camp in Jordan.
