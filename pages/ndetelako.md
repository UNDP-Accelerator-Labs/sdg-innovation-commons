> Brief description of the innovation

A social e-commerce fulfillment platform who’s vision is to provide world class business tools aimed at taking the African product entrepreneurs from survival to scale.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Ndetelako once developed fully will make money through:
1. 10% commission of every item sold through the platform. 
2. Charged ads through the social feature of the platform. 
3. Storage fees from sellers who decide to store products with Ndetelako. 
4. Last mile delivery charges

> Technical Feasibility

Our innovation is designed to bring familiarly to how SMEs do business in Zambia. Over 80% of retail sellers conduct most of their business on social platforms such as WhatsApp, Facebook and Instagram which are great but that’s only one part of order fulfillment. With the ndetelako app we are looking to create, we want to fuse e-commerce features with social platform features to create the ultimate business tool for African product entrepreneurs

> Anticipated Social Impact

With ndetelako, sellers will no longer have to pay hefty rental and labor fees to run their businesses. With the platform and physical fulfillment centers, sellers can now just sit back and watch their business run. On the other hand Buyers will not face data overload and know which sellers are reliable and who are not through the social feature of the app to be developed. In summary Ndetelako = Amazon + Facebook
