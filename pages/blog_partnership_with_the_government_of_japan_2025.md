Partnership with the Government of Japan 2025 May 30, 2025 The Government of Japan is a key partner in UNDP Kyrgyzstan’s mission to build a fairer, more inclusive, and sustainable future for all - supporting the achievement of the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, national development priorities of Kyrgyzstan, where people and planet thrive in balance. As one of UNDP Kyrgyzstan’s key partners and contributors to its programmes, Japan has provided over USD 20 million since 2018, advancing human security, green transformation, and resilient development. This partnership plays a pivotal role in enhancing climate resilience, empowering youth, promoting responsible business practices, and driving digital innovation. Japan also contributes to UNDP’s core resources, enabling agile and impactful support in advancing socio-economic development, democratic governance, strengthening institutional capacities, fighting climate change, and supporting communities in building sustainable livelihoods.
