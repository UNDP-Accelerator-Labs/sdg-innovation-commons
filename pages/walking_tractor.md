> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This tractor can help farmers in lowering there expenses of maintenance , it doesn't require a lot of people to operate it's easy to use even with one person and easy to learn how it operates .

> Technical Feasibility

This tractor is designed in such a way that it can be used as multipurpose , once done cultivating it can be used as a water pump for the crop's
