Clandestino Labs, founded by Juli and Nahuel, is Panama’s first design studio transforming discarded plastic into art and furniture. Their innovative approach aligns with the principles of eco-design, where waste materials are reimagined into functional and aesthetic pieces. Their mission is to drive a circular economy by minimizing waste and maximizing the reuse of resources. Clandestino Labs’ work not only reduces plastic pollution but also fosters environmental awareness through sustainable design solutions, making them pioneers in eco-friendly artistry in Panama.

Clandestino Labs transforms plastic waste into functional art, reducing plastic pollution while creating sustainable products. Their scalable model, combining eco-design with circular economy principles, demonstrates how localized recycling initiatives can become widespread solutions. By fostering community involvement and promoting sustainable consumption, their innovation has significant potential to expand both regionally and globally, promoting environmental awareness and reducing waste.
