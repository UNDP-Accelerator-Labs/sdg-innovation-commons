Mizan Credit Score - is a system for measuring credit risk for businesses and individuals.  Our credit risk assessment and rating platform that analyzes traditional and alternative borrower data to arrive at a standardized credit score. The platform enables the large sections of society that are currently unbanked to build their profiles using technology that can be readily accessed. Additionally, customers will acquire further access to finance through applications integrated with financial institutions and payment systems. The Platform allows lenders to have full information about borrowers and allows borrowers to gain financial education and access.”

 Key features

 · Standardized credit scores

· Integration with Credit Reference Bureau, Tax Database and commercial registry

· Self-assessment and publication of own credit score

· Searchable using TIN, business name, or address

· Accessible by microfinances, banks and other lenders and financial institutions
