## Solution holder: Sari Al Husseini

Embark on an exciting biking adventure in Jordan with Cycling Jordan! Choose from a selection of pre-arranged cycling activities or let them create a tour exclusively for you. Personalize your experience by selecting the sights you want to see, the duration of your bike ride, and your fitness level, while the Cycling Jordan team takes care of the rest! Join a group of fellow bikers on an organized excursion and maximize your approximately one-week vacation in Jordan. Discover the country's stunning scenery on two wheels as you ride through some of the most scenic sections of the Jordan bike trail, witness breathtaking vistas, and engage with local communities.

Each tour provides a one-of-a-kind cycling or mountain biking experience. Cycling Jordan offers a diverse range of single-day tours to various destinations, offering unique experiences and unexpected attractions for bikers of all fitness levels. Whether you're searching for an extreme adventure or a family-friendly day trip, Cycling Jordan has something for everyone!
