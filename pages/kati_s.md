> Title of the innovation

Kati’s

> Brief description of the innovation

Kati’s Taxi Services is a taxi services company that will be positioned in Solwezi  to fully satisfy its customers. We will make sure of providing very quality services to our customers both in Solwezi and Zambia. Here is a list of our services offerings:Provision of luxury sedan services, and Taxi services which will include leasing of the taxi as well as other taxi-related services.
The business of Kati’s Taxi will be transportation. Not just taxi service, but package delivery. The taxi service will be located in Solwezi Northwestern.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Not only is there a strong and growing industrial base in the region, the city has been quietly developing for quite some time. New projects are being planned and built daily and the entire Solwezi property is expanding at a very fist rate. This new developments have made most people to move to the area in search for green pastures (jobs) hence an increase in demand of new means of transportation. The taxi business has the potential to generate about K36,000 per year and  K3,000 in a month. 
Taxi will be used as a substitute for private vehicles by passengers who use the service for convenience reasons and also raise money from delivery of goods to various places.
