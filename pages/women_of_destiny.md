We rehabilitate and empowers sex-workers to become self-relient.
Many young girls and women are being engaged in prostitution not because they want, but due to the high rate of poverty in their homes, they find themselves at the streets so that they can sustain their families.
Women Of Destiny provide these women with Rehabilitation, Empowerment and Sensitization of prostitutes to become successful job creators and be able to sustain themselves and their families.
