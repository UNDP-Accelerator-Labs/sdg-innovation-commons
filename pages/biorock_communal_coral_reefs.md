## 

Using biorock technology, the Grenada Coral Reef Foundation in partnership with the Fisheries Division is implementing a communial coral reef restoration initiative where community volunteers from the local areas are trained on coral reef restoration using biorock artificial reef technologies. The artificial reefs have been propagated with rescued coral fragments from adjacent reefs.
