Could ancestral Morocco, Prickly pear (Opuntia) cactus oil build a name in the beauty industry while lifting local women from poverty?

How does this high quality, natural beauty ingredient  while providing a sustainable lifeline for African women
 and their families.Extracted from the flower of the prickly pear, cactus oil unpack moisturizing and healing properties that beauty industry could use. Moroccan women traditionally like it for its healing and protective properties. It takes about a ton of prickly pears to get a liter of cactus oil. 
Local women usually organize in coops to harvest and produce this unique natural moisturizer that is packed with essential fatty acids, vitamin E and it also has powerful antioxidant and revitalizing powers. Less greasy than other oils, it penetrates quickly into the epidermis and is suitable for all skin types.
