> What is the Title for this solution?

Kwikpak Shelter for the displaced

> How would you describe this solution in under 300 words? 

KWIKPAK offers a practical emergency shelter model that is structurally safe and sound, while also offering its occupants security from harsh weather conditions. It has been developed using basic materials. The design is collapsable allowing ease in transport and set-up.
