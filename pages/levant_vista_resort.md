## Solution holder: Tariq Al Momani

Located amidst the picturesque landscapes of Ajloun, the Levant Vista Resort beckons travellers seeking a sustainable yet comfortable retreat. This enchanting haven seamlessly blends into its natural surroundings, providing guests with a serene and eco-friendly accommodation experience.

The resort boasts a collection of charming wooden cabins, thoughtfully designed with sustainability in mind. Inside, guests will find modern amenities that ensure a comfortable stay, while still maintaining a strong commitment to eco-conscious practices.

Nature enthusiasts will delight in the resort's proximity to pristine hiking trails, winding through the captivating forests and hills of Ajloun. Embarking on these trails, visitors can immerse themselves in the beauty of the natural landscape, breathe in the crisp mountain air, and encounter rich biodiversity that thrives in this region.

For those seeking a delightful outdoor culinary experience, the resort provides ready-equipped areas for BBQs and picnics. The designated areas are designed to blend seamlessly into the environment, ensuring that the experience remains sustainable and respectful of the surroundings.
