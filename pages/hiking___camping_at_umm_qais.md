## Solution holder: Ahmad Al Omari

Hiking at Umm Qais allows your soul to reconnect with nature while allowing your mind to experience learning about the culture and history of a place once unknown to you.

With Ahmad Al-Omari, a local guide who was born and raised in Umm Qais, you will go on a hike to learn about the area's history, culture, environment, flora diversity, and beautiful landscapes.

A camping experience is also available and is ideal for adventurous travelers, as you will help set up the camps, prepare the food, and expand the campfire.

You will have a one-of-a-kind night filled with breathtaking scenes as you look out over the spectacular Tabariah lake and Golan heights!
