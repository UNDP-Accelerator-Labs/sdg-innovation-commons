SRI (SYSTEM OF RICE INTENSIFICATION) is a method of planting which increases yield but reduces emissions because 40% less water. It has been tested and is successfully doing it. Progressive innovations includes bringing in fish, duck rearing to be part of SRI ecosystem. Currently done by one of our grantees called Forever Sabah.
