> Title of the innovation

Improved Village Chickens rearing

> Brief description of the innovation

The rearing of improved village chickens which will be sold on the market.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

A lot of farmers have concentrated much on Broiler chickens, this leaves out gap for village chickens which is of high demand. If the business is set up, will be growing and selling village chickens on the Local and commercial markets eg malls. With the help of incubators to fast the process of increasing the chickens. Eggs will also be sold on the market.

> Technical Feasibility

There is just the need of building poultry houses and fencing the surrounding, Water source which are near here and tanks, incubator and electricity.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Eggs will be readily available to local people and also village chickens will be available at all times.
It will create employment to local people and also teach local people the ways of growing chickens.
