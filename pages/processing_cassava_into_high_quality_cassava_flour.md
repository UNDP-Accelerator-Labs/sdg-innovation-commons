> Title of the innovation

Processing cassava into high quality cassava Flour

> Brief description of the innovation

To be processing locally grown cassava into a safe well packaged cassava Flour and make to it an alternative source of staple food using cassava.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

.The project identified a gap in availability of safe high quality well packaged cassava Flour on the market that is commonly used as in our country.
.The product will target all individuals that have preferences for cassava Flour as opposed to mealie meal wether as a results of health nutrition benefits, cost or as an alternative.
.The project will generate revenues via sells of the products in both formal and informal markets.

> Technical Feasibility
