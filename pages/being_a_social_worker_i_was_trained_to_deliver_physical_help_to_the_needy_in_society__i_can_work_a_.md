> Title of the innovation

Being a social worker I was trained to deliver physical help to the needy in society. I can work as a probation officer, vulnerable, and as an officer in Dmmu at the office of the president,but due to the unemployment levels that the previous regime had it was not possible for some of us to be accorded such opportunities. With regard to this my education allows me to be an entrepreneur hence given an opportunity to be accorded the capital to run my own business I can do hardware business of which I had an opportunity to learn from Indians for 12 years. Therefore as a widower the government grants should be targeted to people like me for us to have the chance to sustain for the children.

> Brief description of the innovation

By doing hardware business with the experience that I have I believe this business will go Miles knowing that building Is inevitable for everyone living in the urban areas knowing that sustainable development can only be realized when massive infrastructure is built in this country. When this happens that will mean that jobs will be created for other Zambians in need those that have humble education background for a better citizenry for as to share the national cake evenly by virtue of being a Zambian citizen.
