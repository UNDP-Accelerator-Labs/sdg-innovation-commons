> Title of the innovation

Commodities exchange market

> Brief description of the innovation

Easy access to commodities such as agricultural products around the country, easy exchange and easy modes of exchange for commodities

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Act as the middle man and allow for smooth exchange

> Technical Feasibility

Test it out on a one city by one city scale, give availability of a commodity for an individual in one city to exchange with a different commodity or any monetary form without external costs and factors

> Anticipated Social Impact

It will help individuals or companies trying to access scarce commodities in their part of the country to be able to access them without moving around given the current pandemic.
It has been implemented in most developed and developing countries.
