> Description of the solution

This solution focuses on monitoring and enforcing a ten-year fishing ban in key river basins in China, such as the Yangtze River. It leverages digital technologies to ensure sustainable fishing practices, conserve biodiversity, and maintain ecological balance. It uses following technologies                 AI Surveillance: Cameras with AI monitor and track illegal fishing in real time.
IoT Sensors: Monitor water quality and fish populations, integrating data for analysis.
Big Data: Analyzes activity patterns and environmental impacts, offering actionable insights.
Command Systems: Real-time alerts and video feeds support quick decisions and agency coordination. It ensures effective enforcement, and sustainable resource management  and supporting real-time decision-making.
