> Description of the solution

The Smog Free Project, led by Daan Roosegaarde, is an innovative environmental initiative that includes the Smog Free Tower, the world's first smog vacuum cleaner. This project aims to improve urban air quality by creating zones of clean air in public spaces, and it engages communities through educational campaigns and collaborative design workshops.

> Socio-economic effects

Enhances urban livability by improving air quality, raises environmental awareness, and fosters community engagement in sustainability practices.
