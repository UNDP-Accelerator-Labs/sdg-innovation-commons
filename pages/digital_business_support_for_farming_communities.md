> Description of the solution

Cosmic focuses on enhancing farming efficiency and sustainability through digital solutions. The project offers free information and specialist workshops, as well as 1:1 advice, support, and training to help build digital confidence and enable farmers to better manage the day-to-day running of their farms.It provides training in farm management tools for record-keeping and planning, guidance on online marketing to promote farm businesses, and support for adopting advanced technologies like drones and sensors. Additionally, it emphasizes environmentally friendly farming practices, helping farmers improve productivity while maintaining sustainability.
