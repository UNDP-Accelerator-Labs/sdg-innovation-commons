SVTECH is a software development company that is aimed at improving the ease of doing business and building reliable technological solutions. Their mission is to make a qualitative difference to the lives of millions of people and organizations in Eswatini and across the continent by empowering them with the power of information telecommunications technology.  SVTECH’s goals are to:  1. Empower businesses and individuals to keep them ahead.  2. Be a leader in market share, quality, revenue, growth and gross margins.  3. Add value to stakeholders, our investors, customers, partners and associates 4. Build on Eswatini’s unique advantages.  Services and software solutions currently under SVTECH’s portfolio include the following:  Smart Card Printing, Network Installation and management, hardware repair and refurbishing, bulk SMS platform, online school platform, retail point of sale, biometric clocking system, and  online survey application. SVTECH recently developed a mobile application that will help agriculture stakeholders manage farmer’s data. They have put the functionality to send bulk SMS's to farmers to notify them about opportunities and information they will need to know about the agriculture industry. The application will also enable Eswatini Agricultural Union (ESNAU) to link farmers to financial services. Farmers are able to pay for input subsidy and further manage their individual farms.
