> Brief description of the innovation

The Sampay IDEA project is seeking funding from the National Innovation Initiative and its partners to finance a fully developed ecommerce hub. Our innovations are:

1.	Sampay Agent
We have structured a profit-share with Samafrica Online Zambia for operators of the Sampay Mobile Money Business.
2.	Sambezi
We have developed ecommerce marketplaces meant to help SMEs fast-track their ability to get online especially during this Covid-19 crisis.
3.	Samdelivery
We have developed a complete shipping aggregation services that will encompass the existing business and create employment for new people through parcel handling and delivery.
4.	SamChilimba
A complete Village Banking Solution that allows all Village Banking groups security and communication for safety and support. It has been developed by interviewing numerous village banking groups and offering and new and cheap solution so we can emphasize this Group building solution for our whole population.
5.	Sam4Women
We have developed an online literacy and development program to emphasize the development of Women in business and life skills and with correct funding we will also create a loyalty and bonus system for the more skills that they learn and develop on our platform.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The goal of the I.D.E.A Project is to enable Zambians to benefit from the potential in e-commerce. The job creation opportunities are vast and include progress towards achieving sustainable development goals: poverty reduction, financial inclusion, job creation, digital migration, equal access for women and youth etc.

The main objectives include: 
1.	Providing a measurable increase in product/raw material offtake especially from the rural areas.
2.	Enabling poor communities to have access to markets. 
3.	Providing access to money 
4.	Providing free technology platforms and skills to small &amp; medium business to give them a fair advantage in ecommerce.
5.	Providing employment to women and the youth in various communities and providing technology and financial literacy.
6.	Create employment through product delivery (On-foot deliveries, Bicycles, Motor Bikes etc.)
7.	Create opportunities for brands to grow with free access to marketing and payment tools
8.	Up-scaling potential for the emergence of reliable courier companies
9.	Complimenting demand for local produce using ICT

> Technical Feasibility

Key participants and beneficiaries in this system are local producers and sellers, cash exchange agents, delivery companies and buyers of local products. 
The project seeks to run a profitable business by providing and participating in an economic ecosystem that is effective and self-sustaining. In order to create and grow economic activity and sustain it, the project addresses the key factors of supply, demand, money transfer and transportation. 

Sambezi is the go-to place for a wide variety of products. A one-stop online market place where different products can be purchased and put up for sale. 
Sampay is a payment system that facilitates online payments and transactions. It is incorporated into Sambezi to allow for buyers and sellers to securely and conveniently transfer money.
Samdelivery provides a solution for both delivery companies and people intending to send parcels. The mobile app provides a user with instant access to delivery companies available in their area.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The I.D.E.A Project is designed to help Zambians by creating and sustaining economic activity by providing an e-commerce solution that satisfies all key business aspects including an online market, money transfer, delivery services and village banking. Successful implementation of the project will result in job creation in the respective sectors.  
In addition to this, the Sam4women program will ensure that we are attending to our responsibility of ensuring equal opportunities for all by providing access to quality education for the women in Zambia. Sam4women will provide the necessary support and skills necessary in the 21st century.
