> Type of the Solution

Urban planning

> Affected Sector

Transport

> Description of the solution

A strategy for transforming public spaces throughout the city, under a new, healthier and fairer model that supports local relations. Traffic is restricted in strategic areas while active mobility, connection to public transport, recreational uses, green spaces, social cohesion and a green and innovative economy are promoted.

> Socio-economic effects

Health, Social cohesion, Local economic development
