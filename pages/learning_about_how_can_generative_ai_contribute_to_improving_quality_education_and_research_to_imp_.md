## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights) 

- Generative AI can be used to personalize learning; 

- Generative AI can be used to create and update educational content ; 

- Generative AI can help to shape creativity of students and researchers to drive innovation

> Considering the outcomes of this learning challenge, which of the following best describe the handover process? (Please select all that apply)

- Our work has not yet scaled
- Other

> Can you provide more detail on your handover process?

The handover process is still underway, because this learning was prospective and our hope is that the ministries in charge of education and research in Cameroon and even beyond will appropriate the results of this reflection in order to provide guidance on the use of artificial intelligence in education and research in their country.
