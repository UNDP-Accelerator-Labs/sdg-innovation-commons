## 

An Individual entrepreneur encourages to reduce waste production in daily life, specifically single-use plastics. She has led this social movement #kahvemtermosta that invites people to use their own mugs in coffee shops. Today, more than 80 coffee shops across Turkey provide incentives to their customers who want to take their coffee with their own mugs.
