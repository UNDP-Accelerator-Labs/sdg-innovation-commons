> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem & solution description)

A lot of students have been struggling with their studies due to persistent blackouts. The Kalichangu desk was specifically designed to help students in their studies. The desk contains an air conditioner, solar panel, phone charger, and bulb. its space can be expanded to accommodate more books.
