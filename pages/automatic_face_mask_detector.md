Low-cost Face Mask Detection Device can detect whether or not a person is wearing a face mask. The camera captures images of those entering public spaces and detects whether they are wearing face masks. This device, when installed in public spaces such as market places and restaurants, helps to better streamline the flow of customers entering public spaces.
