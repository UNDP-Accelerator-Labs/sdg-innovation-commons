Madaba Knowledge Product
FEBRUARY 29, 2024
Discover the potential of Madaba, Jordan's tourism scene with the UNDP Jordan Accelerator Lab. This publication outlines our innovative efforts to unlock Madaba's tourism prospects. Through collaborative activities, insightful discussions, and data analysis, we uncover opportunities and challenges. With actionable recommendations, we aim to enhance visitor experiences, bridge service gaps, and promote domestic exploration. Join us in unveiling Madaba's hidden gems and fostering sustainable tourism growth.
