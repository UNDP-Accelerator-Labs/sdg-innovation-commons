PINK is a social enterprise partnership that aims to produce & supply menstrual products that are affordable and environmentally conscious. Products are supplied through a network of community agents, enabling easier access while creating meaningful part-time employment.  In the process , we endevour to change behaviour, encouraging independence & build confidence in their community.
