The Role of Digital Mobile Money in Catalyzing Financial Inclusion: The Case of Cameroon, South Sudan and Zambia February 24, 2025 This publication presents the collaborative efforts of the UNDP Accelerator Lab network, focusing on advancing digital financial inclusion through research and development. The initiative was part of a global framework led by the UNDP Accelerator Labs, with active participation from the South Sudan Country Office, alongside labs in Cameroon and Zambia. The primary objective was to generate comparative knowledge and insights on digital mobile wallets across these three countries.
