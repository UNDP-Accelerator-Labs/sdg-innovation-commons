Accelerator Lab Guinea-Bissau Annual Report - 2022
MARCH 27, 2023
The 2022 Annual Report of the Guinea-Bissau Accelerator Lab highlights our second year of progress. The report encapsulates our learning cycles, collaborations, insights, and lessons learned, which are essential to our commitment to bottom-up innovation and sustainable development in Guinea-Bissau. If you have any queries, concerns, or areas of collaboration, please contact us via info.acclabgw@undp.org
