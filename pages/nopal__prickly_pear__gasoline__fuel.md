## Using the waste of prickly pear plantations to transform public transport from fossil to bio-fuel vehicles.


![Missing alt text](https://undp-accelerator-labs.github.io/Archive_SDG_Commons_2021/blobs/solutions/5d78fb28d1d09c36e6b03d0d6d701b0a.png)


Biogas from prickly pear has the same performance in vehicles as gasoline, but is 40% cheaper, besides the environmental benefits of not using non-renewable energy sources. According to the promoters, prickly pear biogas has proven more effective than sugar or corn biogas.
