> Title of the innovation

IMPROVED DRAINAGE SYSTEMS IN CHIBOTE COMPOUND KALULUSHI

> Brief description of the innovation

Chibote Compound is one of the compound that is situated South of Kalulushi.Its population is approximately 3500 plus. The majority of the people's income depend on subsistence farming. There is pool sanitation and road infrastructure for people use pit latrines made of sacks and this is a harzard to health especially during rain season.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We are a group of youths that are God fearing and affiliated to The United Church Of Zambia.Many of the youths are unemployed and they are the ones to implement the project right from the initial to the end.we have competent people that will help carry out the project to the end.The youths are the owners of the project for they are the ones who understands the social economic impact they face.

> Technical Feasibility

> Anticipated Social Impact

The innovation will improve on the health of the people in the community and bring about investment to the community. for it will attract businesses and establishment of other institutions that will be of help to the community. There has been fear to establish businesses due to poor road infrastructure in our community.
