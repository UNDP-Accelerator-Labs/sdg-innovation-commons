> Title of the innovation

Krazy muzungu farms and mineral resources

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation will be set in serenje to build 72 fish ponds, build 7 poultry houses 
The 72 fish ponds will be divided into 6 meaning in every month we will be offloading 12 fishponds to the market in reducing the shortage of 80, million tons of fish on the zambian market
Each fish pond will be holding 60,000 of fish that will be ready on the market
Poultry houses will also contribute to the shortage of chicken on the zambian market of 4million 
We have wide local market in the big chain stores and on international we have DRC, Rwanda, angola, burundi and kenya in need of the tilapia fish that'll be grown by us
We currently have one thousand hector of land that will be ultilized in planting of trees to reduce on deforestation
They will be job create towards youths, training center, distribution and one stop market for farmers
