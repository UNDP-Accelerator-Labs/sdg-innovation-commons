> Title of the innovation

Agro Mobile Services

> Brief description of the innovation

Farmers are a major target country wide, the sole purpose is to link farmers to customers and also help farmers save for the coming season, update farmers on issues concerning farming inputs which include e.g fertilizer distribution,seeds etc. seasonal farming idea and diversification of crops growth through sensitization program using free sms and adverts.Unify farmers country wide and spread of data surrounding climate change and Global warming. Enhance a mobile agriculture brokerage by linking sellers of agricultural produce with buyers and get commission for it. Its vision is to become a new banking platform for many living in rural /Urban areas without access to formal banking services.
