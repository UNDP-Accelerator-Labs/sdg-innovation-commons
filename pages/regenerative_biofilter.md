> Description of the solution

Green City Solutions, with its biotechnology that combines the capabilities of mosses with the latest Internet of Things technology, has developed the world's first regenerative biofilters which helps people in particularly polluted urban areas to get fresh air and thus more healthy and quality of life. Innovative natural systems are the ideal complement to established urban greening. Where trees have no room to root and the care of green strips fails, Green City Solutions‚Äô products offer a measurable and space-efficient improvement in quality of life and stay. The core product is an air filter and cooler based on moss. The biotechnology is energy-efficient, generates no excess waste, and is 99% recyclable.
