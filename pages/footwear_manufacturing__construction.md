> Title of the innovation

Footwear manufacturing, construction

> Brief description of the innovation

We are safety footwear manufacturers and a Construction company

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We need equipment which will enhance production to meet the target of 300 pairs per day going up. Our target is defense forces, security firms, pupils, mines and ordinary people. Our pricing is lower than our major competitors. Digital marketing, social media, sponsor contest and literature will serve as marketing tools in our model. We shall employ 50 at start.

> Technical Feasibility

We need to intensify our shoe production in Zambia to cater for all classes of people from low class to high class which will attract local and international market. We need to work on quality, delivery time, value, guaranteed customer satisfaction and good environment. We need capital in order to achieve these abjections.

> Anticipated Social Impact

As a bulk shoe Manufacturers, we aim at cutting down the process of asking for part payments from customers by having available commodities and be able to deliver on order agreements. We shall employ more skilled and unskilled workers who will be trained the skill for their survival which we are already doing with the standing MOU with donbosco technical institute students who are doing tailoring and design.
