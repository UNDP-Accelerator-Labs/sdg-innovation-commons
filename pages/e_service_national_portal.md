> Description

The designated website of National E-Services Portal (hereinafter: Portal) is an electronic platform, available at https://uslugi.gov.mk, which allows citizens of North Macedonia to receive information about public services and to utilize e-services from competent authorities and other entities that provide e-services via the Portal.


 The Portal is established and managed by the Ministry of Information Society and Administration (hereinafter: MISA), which is responsible for the Portal’s availability and technical operation, as well as systems. The portal offers different services for the citizens of North Macedonia categorized by different categories: life events, alphabetic order, competent authority and search tool. The website is working as an umbrella for the services and directs the users to the competent authority's website. 


There are 839 services available on the Portal and 60731 registered users. - Digitized/Digitalized process
