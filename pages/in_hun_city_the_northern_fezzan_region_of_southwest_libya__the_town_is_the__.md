In Hun city the northern Fezzan region of southwest Libya. The town is the capital of the Jufra district.  Hun volunteer announced the start of water heaters recycling project, were they recycle heaters and re-use it again as colorful waste bins installed in front of hospitals, schools, mosques, and, on the sidewalks of streets and some public roads. the project found a great interaction from the community were many people donated expired water heater to the project.
