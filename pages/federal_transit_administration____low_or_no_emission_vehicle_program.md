> Description of the solution

The FTA Low or No Emission Vehicle Program (Low-No) provides funding to state and local governmental authorities for the purchase or lease of zero-emission and low-emission transit buses and related equipment, as well as the construction or leasing of facilities to support these vehicles. This initiative aims to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and improve air quality in urban areas, supporting sustainable and environmentally friendly public transportation solutions.

> Socio-economic effects

Reduces greenhouse gas emissions, lowers public health risks associated with air pollution, supports green jobs, and promotes sustainable urban development.
