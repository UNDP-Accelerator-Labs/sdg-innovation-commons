> Brief description of the innovation

Collecting of industrial waste, sorting them out into biodegradable, non biodegradable and biomass. The last two are usually sold to the cement companies as alternative fuels to coal.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

It will generate income as the recycled products can be resold as fuels to companies that need them and the biodegradable can be used to generate biogas which can be sold as eating gas to gas stoves.
It will help companies that have a challenge disposing off hazardous waste and will benefit those companies looking for alternative fuels to coal. 
The prize will help in expanding and buying machinery to carry out the process. environmental management, employment to the locals, helping companies increase alternative fuel to caol utilization.

> Technical Feasibility

Technical
