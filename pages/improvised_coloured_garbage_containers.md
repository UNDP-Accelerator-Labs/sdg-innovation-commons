Improvised coloured plastic containers, suitable to facilitate the separation of waste for recycling. Although the use of several coloured plastic containers has not been enforced in Angola, the owner of a store around Alemanha informal market, used empty plastic oil bidons to replicate the original coloured garbage containers for the separation of solid waste.
