> Description of the solution

Drones equipped with cameras capture aerial images and videos of fields, forests, and rangelands. Farmers use this technology to monitor crop health, analyze soil conditions, optimize irrigation, and manage forests and livestock. This data-driven approach improves efficiency and reduces costs.
