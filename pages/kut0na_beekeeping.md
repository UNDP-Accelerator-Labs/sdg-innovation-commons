> Brief description of the innovation

Kutona beekeeping and its products is an agribusiness operating in the agricultural industry. It was born due to household poverty, Honey has been cited to have a number of health  and nutrition and pharmaceutical benefits. As such everyone is rushing for the sale of the organic honey overlooking the honey comb therefore as Kutona beekeeping we thought it would be a great innovation if we start working with the beekeepers on an off take basis or just buying from them the honey combs which we processes using simple machines to beeswax. Beeswax is the creamy coloured substance used by bees to build the comb that forms the structure of their nests therefore this makes it clear on how pure beeswax. Beeswax is a very valuable product that can provide a wealth while income in addition to honey.  One kilogram of beeswax is weaith more than one kilogram of honey. Unlike honey beeswax is not a food product and its simpler to deal with it does not require careful packaging which make it easy for storage and transport. We sale the beeswax in its natural form after the production but we intend to start adding value to it by producing cosmetics,cadles.
