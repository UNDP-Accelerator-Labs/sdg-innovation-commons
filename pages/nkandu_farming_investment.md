> Title of the innovation

Nkandu Farming Investment

> Brief description of the innovation

Nkandu Farming investment will provide high quality agricultural produce of all variety. All products will be produced from agricultural produce such as cows, pigs,  wheat, goats,  chicken, maize, ground nuts and many more

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The products will be sold locally at affordable prices to local marketers and the people of Zambia and these goods will also be sold outside the country to our neighboring countries. 
We will invest in employees through on going skills training and we will also empower local farmers by buying and their products and processing the products
Nkandu Farming investment will embrace innovation through incorporating new production  methods and technological advancements to continue providing best looking best performance exo friendly products and services

> Technical Feasibility

Nkandu Farming investment has two farms which will we develop, this development will need will bring infrastructure for animals infrastructures like piggery,  goats houses and growing of agricultural goods like maize, soya, wheat 
After the animals have mass produced these will be sold off to local marketers and the people of Zambia through our outlets and our distribution lines. 
After the crops are cultivated some of the crops  like maize will be sold to the government in order to increase the food reserve and others crops will crops will be processed and sold. 
The revenue will be used to increase our productivity  and make our company grow and turn it into one of the biggest in Zambia

> Anticipated Social Impact

Nkandu Farming investment will create employment for the Zambian people and will also empower local farmers by creating forums to teach local farmers on how to improve there agricultural produce and providing Zambians with affordable food products like eggs, meat etc
