> Title of the innovation

The innovation is an idea of eradicating poverty in different communities and the country at large. Its agenda is to create an equal opportunity for all.

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation will require a cooperate structure that will engage different stakeholders like the unemployed youths and those that have not attained a single education at any level. We shall teach them on different survival skills and life sustainable solutions that can elevate their way of living.  We will teach them and  empower with machinery such as peanut grinding machines and some will work on the sanitation aspect. People will be empowered to clean and do other services that are payable.. We shall create groups that we shall empower on the transport sector.. The drivers and conductors will have a monthly earning to avoid the hand to mouth character.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Income will be generated in phases of duties..  Creating the transport system will bring income to the people. The people in the gabbage collection and sanitation will generate their income from the residents and it will become mandatory for residents to work with our team. > Technical Feasibility

The innovation is designed to be a management structure that will demand total discipline and fiscallity among stakeholders.  It will include other single or micro structures that will work and be authorised by relevant authorities for easy communication and proper execution of duties.
