> Title of the innovation

Gardening and Timber processing

> Brief description of the innovation

Timber processing is one of our project done with the youth group. We processing different types and sizes of timber according to the costumer specification not only that we also venture into carpentry and joinery we make different types of school desks and chairs according to the specifications. Not only as KYC aka Kalulushi Youth Club we venture into agriculture specifically in gardens we have started growing tomato and cabbege using drip irrigation system.
