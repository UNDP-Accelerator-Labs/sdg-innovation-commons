Lack of proper waste disposal mechanisms has led to littering of single use plastic in Wajir town, located in the Northern part of Kenya.

One resident in this town has devised ingenious ways of recycling single use plastic water bottles to mitigate the challenges brought about by poor means to dispose the bottles by recycling them. He has made his fence out of the plastics and uses the same to harvest rainwater.
