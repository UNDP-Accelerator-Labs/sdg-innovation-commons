> Title of the innovation

Patkeyz feed processing cooperative center

> Brief description of the innovation

Cooperative main business is the production or making animal feeds. Management of enterprise is based on the four pillars. These are: good milling and storage building, good quality feed ingredients, best but least cost formulas and selling of livestock stock feed at a profitable price. The major objective is to obtain a profitable production of animal feeds by minimizing input costs while maximizing the returns.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The product shall be packed in well labeled bags and nutritional content specified on the label. This nutritional content shall be done periodically be analysed by ZABS and UNZA school of food technology and human nutrition. It will be priced at an economical price affordable by both small scale, medium scale and commercial livestock farmers. Our target market is the local fish farmers in the province and neighboring country Democratic Republic of Congo and we aim to educate our farmers on the benefits of feeding balanced commercial feeds to fish.

> Technical Feasibility

We plan to work with only 12 workers for the first three years which are the managing director (1) feed mill manager (1) general workers (6) security officers (2) sales personnel (2) which brings up to the total payroll of k180,000 from the badget of k 660,049.65 ZMK. An outlet retail shop shall be opened in town. For the boarding schools and colleges practicing fish farming, the products shall be either delivered by the cooperative on a fee or collected by the buyer. For the working site we wish to apply for a small piece of land from the government.

> Anticipated Social Impact

We shall create employment to many people and provide sufficient raw products from local farmers enhancing market for them and adding nutritional value to the community.
