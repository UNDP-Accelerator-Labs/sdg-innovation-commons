> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

I Grow maize during summer to help take care of my family and create business for marketeers, this farming of fresh maize has an additional value on the environment because it encourages youths to work hard and produce products for themselves and supply to other people.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is not something big, I have a pump at home which works with power... The pump has an outlet were the pipe is connected which takes water in the tank once the tank is full then I start watering the maize.

> Anticipated Social Impact
