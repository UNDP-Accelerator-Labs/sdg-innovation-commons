A man in Blantyre re-using buckets used for selling cleaning detergent into efficient water heaters which are safe and requires low electricity. He has experience an accident with one of his children due to the usual water heater and had been looking for ways to make durable /thick water heater. He has produced a number of these heaters which he has also shared with his relations in his village which has recently received electricity under the government led rural electrification programme.
