> Type of the Solution

Regulation

> Affected Sector

Industry, Agriculture, Transport

> Description of the solution

Policy designed to control air pollution in the fall and winter seasons in two major cities, Beijing and Tianjin, and prefecture-level cities nearby.

> Socio-economic effects

Improve Air Quality

> Type of Measure

- 2. policy/regulation measure

> Type of sub-measure

- 5. (none)

> Who led the solution

- local government

> Timescale of implementation

- short-term (up to 1 year)

> Other Notes

Main tasks:


(1) Promote the major projects of the "14th Five-Year Plan": promote the ultra-low emission renovation projects in the iron and steel, cement and coking industries; promote the comprehensive treatment of VOCs; and promote the treatment of loose coal.


(2) Promote the comprehensive treatment of boilers and kilns: promote the elimination and renewal of outdated coal-fired boilers and kilns; promote the comprehensive treatment of boilers and kilns; and strengthen the control and management of disorganised emissions.


(3) Continuing to carry out comprehensive treatment of mobile sources: accelerating the adjustment of transport structure; promoting the elimination and renewal of old and high-emission vehicles; increasing the supervision of mobile source emissions to meet the standards; strengthening the comprehensive enforcement of automotive oil products.


(4) Enhance the management level of air surface sources: strengthen the comprehensive control of dust; strengthen the comprehensive utilisation of straw and the ban on burning; and steadily promote the control of ammonia emissions from livestock and poultry farming.


(5) Effective response to heavily polluted weather: strengthen the regional joint prevention and control; the implementation of performance rating differentiated control.


(6) Creating a number of atmospheric management benchmark enterprise.
