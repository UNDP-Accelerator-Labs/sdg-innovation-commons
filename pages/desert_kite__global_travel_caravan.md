## 

Trailer (Desert Kite) is a trailer that is driven by traction behind the vehicle. It is the latest modern innovation to serve the owners of trips and camping...which has been specially equipped for all camping needs abroad. It is equipped with large storage areas to hold luggage, camping kit, powerful charging battery, solar energy unit, in addition to night lighting units, a refrigerator to store food, a large water tank and a mobile kitchen. The trailer was also equipped. With offroad wheel systems and a tent on top of the trailer (for more details, see the explanation below)
