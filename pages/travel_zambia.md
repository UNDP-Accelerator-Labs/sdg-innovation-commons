> Title of the innovation

Travel Zambia

> Brief description of the innovation

Travel Zambia is a mobile application that introduces Zambia to the world. It gives details such as maps, pictures, stories about the tourist destinations and many other important details.
Travel Zambia promotes tourist of Zambia.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Travel Zambia not only helps the us(the creators) generate income, it also helps a lot more local people. With a lot of lodges, hotels, motels listed on the application Travel Zambia makes money by charging these hospitality organizations for exposing them to more customers. Local tour guides also generate an income by listing their services on the platform and get connected to tourists(both local and international).
Other way of generating income is through advertising.

> Technical Feasibility

Travel Zambia is designed in such a way that it is open and transparent. Helping tourists get to any place in the country without any problems. It also uses machine learning algorithms to learn tourist behaviors and help them get to the places they want in return also helping the hospitality industry get the most out the tourist. Travel Zambia also helps tourists with full information even before traveling to a particular area.  There's no prototype yet but with a few app developers and app designers it can be done.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Travel Zambia helps generate income for local people who act as tour guides. we have a lot of people in our local communities who know so much in those places and can be used as your guides. Help generate income for translators.
