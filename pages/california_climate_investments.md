> Description of the solution

California Climate Investments utilizes proceeds from the state's Cap-and-Trade program to fund projects that reduce greenhouse gas emissions, strengthen the economy, and improve public health and the environment. It supports a wide range of initiatives across various sectors, aiming to create a sustainable and low-carbon future for California while prioritizing benefits for disadvantaged communities.

> Socio-economic effects

Enhances public health, reduces greenhouse gas emissions, creates green jobs, improves air quality, and supports economic growth in disadvantaged communities
