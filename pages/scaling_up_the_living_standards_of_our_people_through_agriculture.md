> Title of the innovation

SCALING UP THE LIVING STANDARDS OF OUR PEOPLE THROUGH AGRICULTURE

> Brief description of the innovation

It will be an agribusiness where by we shall set up an agro shop which will be supplying all agricultural implements.
Buying of land which will be used to grow different crops and vegetables hence supplying our local markets with fresh vegetables.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Taping into agribusiness,we shall make sure sure we set up an agro shop which will be located in town,we shall also make sure to have a land where we will be growing different crops and vegetables.we will make sure our innovation to have 2 impacts on the people.
(1) Making sure we supply our farmers with all agricultural implements such as seeds, fertilizers, chemicals e.t.c at  competitive and affordable prices.
(2) The land which we shall procure will be used to grow different crops and vegetables hence providing our marketeers with fresh vegetables such as rape, chinese cabbage, tomato e.t.c, you know it doesn't make any sense for us in mansa to be flooded with tomatoes from as far as mbala and mkushi.
We shall make sure we have a good relationship with the already existing business to offer them a sounding competition in terms of doing business.
Since we shall be new on the market our main challenge will be selling our selves to our clients hence we shall embark on massive advisement through radios,bill boards, social media platforms or any other platform which will be available.
Our innovation will also focuse on environmental protection,we shall make sure that natural resources are protected and finally but not the least we shall use our knowledge and influence to educate farmers on good agricultural practices.

> Technical Feasibility

First find a reliable farm land where we can grow different crops and vegetables, buying agricultural implements then tap into agribusiness with all our energy and resources to make sure that in 2months time of our comecement of our company we are able to supply our products to local markets.By the time our company will be turning 4months old we will be able to supply our products outside luapula province such as kasumba Lesa and if we can manage we can even go beyond boarders.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Ensuring that there's enough food grown for everyone in the country, supplying our markets realiable food which is grown in a clean environment.avalaibility of food on markets at a competitive price.
