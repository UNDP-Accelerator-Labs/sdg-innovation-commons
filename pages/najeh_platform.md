> Brief description about the identified local solution:

Najeh is an interactive educational platform that connects tutors to students, through which the tutor can upload the lectures and registers on the platform, then create various tests for his students. This will support the graduates from education colleges to start practicing their specialty and gain more experience that allow them to gain he required skills for the market.
