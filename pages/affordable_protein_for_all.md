> Title of the innovation

Affordable protein for all

> Brief description of the innovation

The project will a small to medium small holding farm. On the farm we plan on keeping layers, broiler chickens, village chickens goats and pigs. To supervise project I ll will build a farmhouse were my family and I will stay. We plan on growing our own feed to lower the costs of production and effect these cost savings in our pricing. Traditional waste products from the animals such as intestines, skin and feet will be turned into food for pets such as dogs on the farm. Extra will sold to the immediate community.

The project will provide affordable sources of protein from the eggs, chickens, goats and pigs. We plan on using the waste products from these animals such as intestines, feet and skin of these animals to create affordable food for pets such as dogs.
