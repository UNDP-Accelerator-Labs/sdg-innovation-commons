> Brief description of the innovation

Mudaipa General Dealers is a registered as a sole proprietorship business entity owned by ALFRED MOYO who was born on 25th April, 1989 of Mudaipa farms Chief Zumwanda, Lumezi district in eastern province of Zambia.
The business will be located at Lumezi trading Centre along Chipata – Lundazi road at Lumezi Zambia National Service camp turn off. This is about 150km from the provincial headquarters Chipata and about 35 km from the Lundazi district council (BOMA).
Mudaipa General Dealers is a newly opened business which deals in general groceries and agricultural services in the newly created Lumezi district. The main aim of the business is to become a leading and progressive suppler of Onion Flakes and Powder and well packaged vegetables to local restaurants, lodges and super markets and providing accessible markets for onion and vegetables farmers in eastern province of Zambia. 
The business therefore will be dealing in the following products and services

> Technical Feasibility

FUNCTIONAL DESIGN OF PROCESSING PLANT
The production of onion flakes and Onion Flakes and Powder is the results after onion dehydration, which has a significant in further processed onion products. These two kind of products are resistant to long distance transport, good storage and easy to use. And both of them need to be dried, having no much different processing technology, but Onion Flakes and Powder needs crushing and screening process to finish the production process.
The mix of unit operations for producing onion flakes and Onion Flakes and Powder is as follows; 

Desirable product 	Raw Materials 	Mix of unity Operations 
Onion Flakes 	Fresh onions 
	i.	Sorting/Grading
ii.	washing
iii.	Slicing(Cutting)
iv.	drying(dehydrating)
v.	packaging of flakes 
Onion Flakes and Powder 	Fresh Onions 	
i.	Sorting/Grading
ii.	Washing
iii.	Slicing(Cutting)
iv.	drying(dehydrating)
v.	Grinding(Pulverizing)
vi.	Packaging.

The flow chart Processing of Onion Flakes and Powder
1.	Raw Onion
2.	Grading, Stem Cutting and Peeling
3.	Slicing (Size reduction 1/8 – 1/4" thickness
4.	Tray Drying of Onion Slices (50-700C 8-10 hrs)
5.	Onion Flakes 
6.	 Pulverizing (Grinding of onion flakes)
7.	Onion Flakes and Powder
