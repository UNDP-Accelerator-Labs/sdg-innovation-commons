## Solution holder: Rateb Al Rabadi

Khallet Al-Saadeh, a remarkable sustainable eco-tourism camp nestled in the heart of Ajloun, where nature and culture intertwine to create an unforgettable experience. This eco-friendly haven not only offers comfortable accommodation facilities but also showcases lands of olive tree farms.

Immersed in the tranquility of the surrounding landscape, guests can unwind in eco-friendly accommodations that seamlessly blend with the natural environment. By choosing to stay here, visitors contribute to the preservation of Ajloun's pristine beauty. Adding to the allure of the camp, guests have the unique opportunity to explore the bountiful lands of fruits. Throughout the seasons, visitors can engage in the joyful act of fruit picking, selecting ripe and juicy treasures from a variety of orchards. The experience is not only fulfilling but also offers a deeper connection to the land and its abundant offerings.

Beyond the breathtaking views, the camp provides a culinary adventure that celebrates Jordanian traditions. Delightful aromas waft through the air as guests indulge in a feast of traditional delicacies prepared with locally sourced ingredients.
