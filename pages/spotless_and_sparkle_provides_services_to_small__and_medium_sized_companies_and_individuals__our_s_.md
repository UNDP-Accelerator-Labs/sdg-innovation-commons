> Brief description of the innovation

Spotless and Sparkle provides services to small- and medium-sized companies and individuals. Our services include office and house cleaning services, hiring of bridal wedding garments, traditional healthy Zambian (in future African dishes) dishes; all the above are door to door services, for the business aims deal with untapped market who customers are mostly busy working persons who have no time to cook healthy meals for themselves and family, those who have demand full jobs to that they fail or too tired to do house cleaning and brides who want to limit the process of window shopping for their dress with limited time and budget
