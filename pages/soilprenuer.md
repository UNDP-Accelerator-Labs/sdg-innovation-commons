> Title of the innovation

Soilprenuer

> Brief description of the innovation

Soilprenuer is a startup which intends to help nurture young entreprenuers to take advantage of the unlimited wealth creation ventures in agriculture using modern technology through training, funding and provision of required tools for success.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Sponsored training, share on profits and interest on loans.

> Technical Feasibility

The project will incorporate online classes, market monitoring tools, and easy production estimating tools the require less technicality in agriculture and ICT.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The innovation will provide sustainable income to youths and the nation at large directly and indirectly while reducing food wastage.
