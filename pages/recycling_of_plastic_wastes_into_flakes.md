Solution owner: Liberatha, a very young girl from Dar es salaam Tanzania

She formed her own company  called Libe Green Innovation Co. Ltd. The company is dedicated to developing sustainable solutions to plastic pollution in the country through plastic waste collection, segregation, treatment and recycling into useful flakes consumed by manufacturing industries. The company has so far employed 10 people and has recently launched a new initiative called after event waste collectors.
