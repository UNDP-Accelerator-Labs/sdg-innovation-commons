> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem & solution description)

Tikikil stove is used for cooking. It uses firewood as fuel which is continuously fed into the combustion chamber. The stove has a cylindrical inner clay liner as combustion chamber, covered with galvanized sheet metal on the outside. The 4cm thick liner has internal diameter of 11cm and is 23.5cm high. At its bottom is an 11cmX11cm opening as fuel and air inlet.
