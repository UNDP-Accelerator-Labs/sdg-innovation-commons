> Affected Sector

Multiple sectors including manufacturing, energy, transportation, and construction: cleaner production processes, renewable energy technologies, sustainable urban mobility solutions, and energy-efficient building practices

> Description of the solution

The Sustainable Innovation Fund by Innovate UK provides financial support to businesses across various sectors to develop innovative solutions that drive economic growth while reducing environmental impact. It aims to help companies adapt to the challenges posed by climate change, focusing on projects that promote sustainability, resource efficiency, and low-carbon technologies.

> Socio-economic effects

Promotes sustainable economic growth, supports business innovation, reduces environmental impact, and creates green jobs.
