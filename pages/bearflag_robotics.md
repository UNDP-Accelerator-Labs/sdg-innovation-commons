> Description of the solution

Bear Flag Robotics is a company that develops autonomous driving technology for farm tractors. By equipping tractors with advanced technology, Bear Flag helps farmers automate tasks like tilling and planting, increasing efficiency and reducing labor costs. Their technology also provides valuable data and insights that can help farmers optimize their operations and improve crop yields.
