> Title of the innovation

Bicycle Trailer

> Brief description of the innovation

The  bicycle trailer is a small contraption attached or hitched at the back of an ordinary bicycle.  This is to enable people to carry small loads of groceries or farm produce in an easy manner. When not in use or not needed the trailer is unhitched from the bicycle.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Can be useful for charcoal burners, farmers and people running small grocery stores in the community.  Will reduce the cost of transporting goods to and fro.

> Technical Feasibility

The design is a fabrication in nature requiring mild steel tubings , a set of two bicycle wheels assembled together by welding and use of bolts and nuts etc.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Very relevant as most of our population can not afford cars or vans etc. Pedal power only.
