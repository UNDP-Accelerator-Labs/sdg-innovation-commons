> Title of the innovation

Goat farming business

> Brief description of the innovation

Goat farming business is profitable,the market for goats is high and continue to increase. Goat farming business is a diversified business,Goats produce dairy  and  meat which is the source of proteins required for the good health of the people .

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation of goat farming business will generate money by selling the goats,the target market is kasumbalesa where the potential offtakers are the Congolese people from  the democratic republic of Congo .the price for goats at kasumbalesa is very good and will make the business to grow and thrive without facing any challenges. This innovation adds value to the environment because goats produce excretes which can be used as manure ,this manure can be applied to different plants.

> Technical Feasibility

This innovation is designed by building a kraal which provides shelter for the goats and provides security  to the  goats at night. It takes one to two months for this innovation to be implemented provided the resources are available.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation will improve the lives of others because two or three people will be employed and also the manure from the goats Will be used by other people in their fields and and gardens to provide nutrients to their crops hence having good yields and earn an income which will sustain their living.
