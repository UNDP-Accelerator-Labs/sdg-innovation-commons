> Brief description of the innovation

Manufacturing of petroleum.
The innovation is to manufacture petrol from garbage plastics. And be used in the energy sector.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will produce extreme income once our petrol is manufactured we tend to sell it to our targeted clients which are petroleum distributors. And it will create hundreds of jobs for other youths

> Technical Feasibility

It consist of collection of garbage plastics and heated up at a higher temperature using our burners. Proposal implementation plan is to have a plant where we can manufacture petrol for commercial used country wide and other countries.

> Anticipated Social Impact

My innovation will improve lives by creating hundreds of jobs and reduce garbage plastics in our communities since we will be buying garbage plastics from the community. Our innovation is proposed to current market because our product will be manufactured locally.
