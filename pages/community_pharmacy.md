> Title of the innovation

Community Pharmacy

> Brief description of the innovation

The Community Pharmacy is aimed at providing the sorrouding community within Lukanga and sorroinding areas with quality pharmaceutical related services cost effectively.  It will play a significant role in promoting, maintaining and improving the public's health.
Being located in the heart of the community will give us an opportunity to gain a unique understanding of the health needs in the community through  daily interactions with patients and customers, as such, services provided will be based on the community needs

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The income will be generated through direct selling of pharmaceuticals, coametics and ahrochemicals, and through provision of consultative servises among other things.

> Technical Feasibility

The Community Pharmacy will comprise three units:
1. The front shop
2. The dispensery
3. The bulk storeroom

The community pharmacy will provide services in Pharmaceuticals, cosmetics and agrochem services. The place has already been secured and is cerrently undergoing partitioning and shelving among other facelifting requirements, and is already registered with PACRA. Once the shelving is complete, there will be need to put in place pharmacy equipments such as Airconditioning units, the refridgerator among other items. After that, there will be nedd  to engage ZAMRA for inspection of the premises followed by registration with ZAMRA, ZRA and the local City Council

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation will supplement the government's efforts in improving the public's health and wellbeing as follows:
1. Health improvement at individual level by providing health care services aimed at management and prevention of common ailments
2. Health improvement at community level through health education aimed at closing gaps in health inequalities
3. Supports career development for upcoming pharmacists by creating an environment conducive for training through internship programs.
4. Develop relationships with various stakeholders by creating a friendly platform where divergent views can be expressed, and that way wewill be able to learn some of the expectations from the public.
