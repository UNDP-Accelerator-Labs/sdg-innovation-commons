> Brief description about the identified local solution:

An online platform that links between the experts in agriculture, the private sector and workers in this field in Iraq to spread awareness, give tips and help farm owners to know how to use the new methods of Irrigation that uses less water. It also helps young entrepreneurs to share ideas and opinions.
