> Description of innovation

LoopHoop is looping in handloom weavers, artisans, small manufacturers together with technology driven marketplace. This is a marketplace platform which ensures making authentic and pure handloom and handicraft products available to clients at value for money prices. The endeavor is to endorse the heritage handlooms and handicrafts of India and convert them into contemporary wearable fashion. TheyÂ support resellers , women who wish to work from home. They have generated employment for working mothers who work from home . They have future plans of bringing in virtual reality to their website for better client services
