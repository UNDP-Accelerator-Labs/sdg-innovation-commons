Innovator: Judi Ramses Nieves

This is a very efficient and economical, durable and heavy-duty charcoal stove. It runs on very little charcoal where the heat is controllable with the air vent. It is a good alternative to smokey wood stoves or expensive LPG stoves. It is cheap, portable and can last for years.
