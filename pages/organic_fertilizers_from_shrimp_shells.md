Chitosan Egypt is a Pioneer EG + MENA biotech company expert in manufacturing high competence chitosan and Chitosan solutions for all sectors. The start-up was born from one of the UNDP Egypt innovation lab's Hackathons. Chitosan Egypt is specialized in manufacturing a raw material called chitosan which they use in farms to convert conventional farming to organic farms, getting rid of more than 500 tons of shrimp shells waste yearly in Egypt by making organic agri products, also helping more than 300 families in Fayoum governerate whose only income comes from shrimp peeling.  https://chitosaneg.com
