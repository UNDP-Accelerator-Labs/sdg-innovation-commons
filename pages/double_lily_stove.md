Innovator: Jimmy Wanemay

This is a twin lily stove moulded in steel that uses charcoal as its fuel. It is cheap, portable and cooks’ food quickly. It has a cylindrical chamber for airflow, and a placeholder for charcoal as well as a placeholder for pots of all sizes.
