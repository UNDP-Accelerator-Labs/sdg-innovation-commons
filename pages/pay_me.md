The service was launched on 01-06-2016 in the city of Benghazi and several other cities across Libya, including (Tripoli - Tobruk - Al Bayda - Darnah - Jallo - Ojlah - Ajkhira - Ajdabiya) where the service was very popular, especially in light of the lack of cash availability.

Pay me service has used many commercial and service activities, including different markets and food stores - meat - vegetables and fruits - pharmacies - clinics - clinics - analysis laboratories - clothes - shoes - auto parts - travel and tourism agencies - and many other different activities
