Transport

> Description of the solution

Support bicycle, pedestrian, and other transportation projects that help clean the air by reducing motor vehicle emissions by dedicating approximately $800,000 annually to projects across the city. 
The revenue comes from a $4 per vehicle surcharge collected by the California DMV on motor vehicle registrations in the nine-county Bay Area region.
