> Description of the solution

This solution features an operation dashboard for data analysis and sales management. It can address challenges in agricultural product sales due to data variability and complex entities. It offers a tracing mechanism, shows scheme effects like hot-selling goods analysis, and has advantages such as increasing benefits, upgrading the industry, reducing risks, and efficient design.
