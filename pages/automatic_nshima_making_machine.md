> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This machine will generate money because it will be very much needed in populated institutions such as boarding schools and hotels which need Nshima to be prepared for large number of people.

> Technical Feasibility

The machine uses electrical and mechanical engineering to automate the process of Nshima preparation. The main parts are 
1. Motor
2. Arduino Uno
3.Heat source
4. 2 pots .
Nshima making can be divided into 2 stages.
First stage the water is warmed to 60% and a signal is sent to an Arduino to switch on the motor and the mealie meal is added to make a mixture. The motor is turned off and the mixture is left to cook.
Second stage starts when the mixture or paste is headed to 130Â° and a signal is sent to the Arduino to switch on the motor and allow the mealie meal into the pot.  When the Nshima is well mixed and is hard enough the process is automatically stopped and the user is alerted of the end of the process.The ac motor control is called Pulse width modulation .
