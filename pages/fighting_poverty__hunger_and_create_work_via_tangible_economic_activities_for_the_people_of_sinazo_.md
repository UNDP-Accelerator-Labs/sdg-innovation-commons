> Title of the innovation

Fighting poverty, hunger and create work via tangible economic activities for the people of sinazongwe district through real diversification/modernisation in Agriculture sector and as well as in manufacturing and processing.

> Brief description of the innovation

M. SIANKUKU MEAT PROCESSOR AND RESTAURANT, was officially registered in 2009. It's owned by Youths and in partnering with other youths across the country. Our innovation is centered on buying and rearing of village chicken and Goats in order to produce the much needed meat and sausage . Youths  and Women will be engaged in full time jobs and others who are unskilled will be also engaged in other equivalent jobs. Additional, other youths will be connected to become out growers of V/Chicken and Goats. We are going to have 4 Zones, special for our facility. Two zones for V/ Chicken on separate areas and two zones for Goats on separate  places in order to supplement the supply demand . The facility centre will have field officers working in conjunction with District Vet Officers and Agriculture District Team in order to combat any out break of certain diseases.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Our business Will generate profitable income for us through sales from the manufactured products, such as preserved meat, smoked sausage , live goats, Chicken, premixed livestock feeds. The innovation will create jobs, wealth and improve the General living standards for the people of sinazongwe and the entire Zambia. The innovation will assist small business owners to be partners in supply of Village Chickens and Goats with good incentive.
The price of village chicken.and goat is very good and most the prices are always determined by the suppliers because the products are on demand in the market arena.
On commercial basis the demand is too huge therefore, industries like ours is key and vital to be implemented.
We have local markets such as local people of sinazomgwe ( specific in donating farm), Maamba and Nkandabbwe mines in sinazongwe, chain store's in Zambia, mines in Zambia, hotels in lusaka and many more other reliable institutions.
All goats that will be seen suffering from skinny diseases to the community  will be bought at the agreed prices and taken to the isolate centre for proper treatment , until they become well for market.
Unwanted pumpkins, watermelon, and cucumbers will be used as low materials to produce local premixed livestock feeds.
The management team will be equal to the task as most of them will be university graduate.

> Technical Feasibility

Our innovation is designed to ensure that income generation is achieved on collectively both the business and the community. Therefore, the said facility will have have offices for administration, management, field officers and other supportive staffs . Each field officer Wil have a specific task to do in their respectively zones.
The second, year of operations will have resources to purchase a hummer meal machine  so that, Mealie-meal can be sold locally and outside, and the remaining meal number 3 Will be used for manufacturing of livestock feeds for the same business. Chickens and Goats deaths rate will be monitored .

> Anticipated Social Impact

Our innovation will transform the lives of people from poverty to wealth in sinazongwe district. First, people of sinazongwe already have basic requirements on how to raise village chicken , goats, pigs , sheep and ducks etc.
The hunger.and jobs problems will be the thing of the past for sinazongwe  people because, the innovation program will consume a lot of youths, women and men into serious business. The community will benefit from the local revenue which Will be facilitated across the business.
Drastically in the second year of operations the lives of people will change for better because every household will have village chicken and Goats to supply to our facility and be paid cash instantly and they will be able to pay school fees for children and manage other basic needs in their lives.
