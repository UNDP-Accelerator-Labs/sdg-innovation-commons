> Description

This platform is a site that gathers various businesses on one stop. The association E-commerce in North Macedonia has created this webpage that allows the users to see all digital tools and services offered by all members. Various businesses are able to sell, provide service, and reach out to potential costumers through this site. For example, they also offer a section 'Pay bills" where the site takes you to various phone, electricity  companies, as well as public services such as water bill, or property tax.
