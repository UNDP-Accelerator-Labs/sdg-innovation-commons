Timor-Leste is a informal group working in creating wooden hand-made shrines
for praying. The group led by four members and has been operating for four
years. Roberto Ximenes, 40 years-old has passion in making shrines and has been
his main income activity. His design is referenced to Gothic Architecture style
in combination to Timorese forms of traditional housings and materiality.
