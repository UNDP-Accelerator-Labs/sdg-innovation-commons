Smart irrigated Akarima is an integrated system which comprises of the family house, a recreational area and a garden producing a variety of foods including vegetables, fruits and medicinal plants for home consumption or sale. Smart irrigated Akarima has been found to play an important role in improving food security for the resource poor rural or urban households in developing countries to avoid malnutrition.
