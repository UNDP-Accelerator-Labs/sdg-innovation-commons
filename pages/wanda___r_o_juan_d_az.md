Marea Verde is a non-profit organization working with communities in Panama since 2018 to
address the critical issue of plastic waste, particularly in rivers—the primary source of
pollution affecting the Panama Bay. Their groundbreaking project, the Wanda system,
combined with La Casa de Wanda (LCW), showcases a unique integration of eco-design,
plastic repurposing, and public engagement.
