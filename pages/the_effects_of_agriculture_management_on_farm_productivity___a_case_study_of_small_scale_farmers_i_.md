> Title of the innovation

The Effects of Agriculture Management on Farm Productivity - A Case study of Small Scale Farmers in Zone three (3) Chipata

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation is the a system platform that addresses the management of agriculture among small scale farms in Zambia. The structure way of small scale farmers is mostly not considered as business, thus affecting production growth in Zambia. Therefore, the innovation builds a platform of business mindset in small scale production in growing their farming business than managing their crops growth and sales from traditional mindset of production to profitability and economical contribution growth.
