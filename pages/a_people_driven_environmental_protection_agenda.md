> Title of the innovation

A PEOPLE DRIVEN ENVIRONMENTAL PROTECTION AGENDA

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation proposes policies that can enhance the protection of the environment to stop the adverse effects of climate change. The innovation is two fold and this includes: the proposal to constitute a community based environmental protection committee to oversee the use of the environment in conjunction with experts technocrats; and the creation of the Community/Institutional environmental protection tribunals and institutions that will hear and determine environmental protection concerns before they go to the courts.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation
