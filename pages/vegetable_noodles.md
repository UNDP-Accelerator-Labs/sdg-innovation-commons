The noodles made from vegetable (pumpkins, mustard, spinach, carrots) offers a healthier option to conventional noodles (lined with alkaline, natrium hydorside and natrium silicate and high in carbohydrates). The noodle is high in Ferum which is crucial for brain development. The aim is to reduce chemical infusion that often causes growth and cancerous cells.
