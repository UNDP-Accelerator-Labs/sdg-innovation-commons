Girls and education
SEPTEMBER 22, 2020
The infographic focuses on girls 'access to education in three countries (Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan, Afghanistan), main barrier’s to girls' education and the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on education around the world.
The infographic is created within “Supporting the Economic Empowerment of Afghan Women through Education and Training in Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan” project. This project is funded by the European Union and implemented by UNDP.
