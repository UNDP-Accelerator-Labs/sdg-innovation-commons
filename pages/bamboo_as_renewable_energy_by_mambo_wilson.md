Reafforestation. This project therefore aims at greening northern Uganda through promotion of bamboo plantations, their value addition, and sustainable utilization to meet cooking and heating fuel needs of the people. Bamboo was chosen because of its high growth rate and biomass yield able to grow in most parts of the country which when sustainably managed and used for other valuable products besides cooking fuel shall reduce deforestation
