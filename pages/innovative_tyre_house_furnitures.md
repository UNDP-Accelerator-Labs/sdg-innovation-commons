> Brief description of the innovation

The Innovative Tyre house furniture is an idea that recycles used tires into furniture, they can be used as garden chairs or house hold furniture.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovative idea can generate income  through the sell of the furniture to the market, mukuba tree is very expensive that should be used to produce these furniture so by the use of these tires can be a very cheap method that generates income, this method can also help other individuals that do not have capital to to order woods or mukuba tree, the target market for this program is those that buy furniture or household goods.
