> Title of the innovation

'Find It Zambia' Website for finding missing persons, property and documents

> Brief description of the innovation

A local website/app to aid the search for missing persons and retrieval of missing goods and documents. members of the public can share the missing people and property  with specific details and using a search engine other users will be able to find missing loved ones, property and documents

Idea will greatly benefit Zambian community by helping them easily locate missing loved ones, property and critical documents in an attempt to aid the growing number of missing people and un- recovered items
