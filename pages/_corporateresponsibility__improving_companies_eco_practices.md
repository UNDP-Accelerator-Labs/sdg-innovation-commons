Centrarse &nbsp;is the organization promoting CSR in Guatemala, with 18 years of experience. Led by visionary entrepreneurs and driven by an executive team committed to working with companies and all public and private sectors, promoting sustainability as a business transformer. Through different programs, it encourages and advises different companies to improve their practices and to start having a correct environmental responsibility.
