## Solution holder: Hashem Al Shmayleh

Diving with Hashem Shmayleh in Aqaba is not just a thrilling adventure but also an educational experience that focuses on understanding and appreciating the local culture and marine life. Shmayleh's expertise as a certified diving instructor ensures that visitors are well-prepared and safe throughout the dive trip, while his passion for sharing knowledge about the area's traditions and sea life enriches the overall experience. With stunning marine life and shipwrecks to explore, diving with Shmayleh is an unforgettable adventure for all skill levels.

In addition to providing a thrilling diving experience, Hashem Shmayleh also focuses on sustainable tourism practices and responsible diving. He emphasizes the importance of respecting the marine environment and minimizing the impact of diving on the delicate ecosystems of the Red Sea.

As part of his commitment to responsible tourism, Shmayleh also participates in regular beach cleanups and encourages his clients to do the same. He believes that by educating visitors about the importance of conservation and sustainable tourism practices, we can help to preserve the natural beauty of Aqaba's Red Sea for future generations to enjoy.
