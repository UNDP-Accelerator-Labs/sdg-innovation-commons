> Title of the innovation

Internet Cafeteria to be dealing in supply of stationery and office equipment, printing, photocopying and internet provision

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation seeks to contribute to the advancement of technology use of computers and the internet in the provision of education and computer literacy. The main objective is to provide stationery services to the community which is in very short supply. We seek to provide excellent internet, printing and photocopying services and also serve as a stationery shop to be supplying office equipment and goods.
Besides that, we hope to provide financial services and serve as agents for ZANACO (Xapit), Multichoice paypoint and distributors, zoona, MTN, Airtel and Zamtel mobile money providers and other retail services that will improve daily human needs of the local populus.

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Once established, the business will offer quality and exceptional printing services to ensure customer satisfaction leading to customer retention. The business will employ not less than 10 people once in full operation. We will offer quality t-shirt printing, branding and graphics designing to bring the services closer to the people. The nearest where these services are offered is in Mansa which is 300km from Nchelenge.

> Technical Feasibility

The innovation is already registered with PACRA with the registration certificate that includes the nature of business allowed under the registered business name attached. Currently, we are in the implementation stage where we are raising funds to procure the necessary equipment and machinery for the full implementation of the project.
The business has experienced advisors and managers who are well knowledgeable about the industry and it's operation. As soon as all the resources are available, the business will begin to operate to its full capacity.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The business will provide the community with an excellent one stop shop for all their internet, printing and stationery needs. With the emerging eLearning, internet provision will be key to the education of many and education materials will in circulation and need for printing services is steadily increasing. We seek to be the solution to this emerging challenge.
