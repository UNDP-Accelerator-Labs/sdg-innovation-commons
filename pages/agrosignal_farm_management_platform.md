> Description of the solution

Maintains operational plans and schedules: automates all reporting processes related to working the land, setting staff shift schedules, creating individual and group plans and reports for equipment operation, resource allocation, cargo movement, and security. When integrated with the 1C system, waybills are automatically generated in 1C based on data on completed work and the output of machine operators, which is automatically loaded from the AgroSignal system.
