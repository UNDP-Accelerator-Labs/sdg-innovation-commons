> Title of the innovation

FROM WOMEN TO NATIONS- Let hard work soar above everything the future can be

> Brief description of the innovation

Empowering women in agriculture throug keeping of livestock and food processing, this will result into closing the gender gap in food production. In most rural areas of zambia women take up the role of farming and food production but still living in extreme poverty conditions because of lack of knowledge in farming for business and resources so as to develop their families and communities. 
From women nations is the innovation that will see that every women evolved will have entrepreneur knowledge and farm for business to inspire the next women to venture into agriculture and grow together. 
When women run their own farms and have knowledge on the new technology that turn raw materials into finished products, will bring about  a huge difference in gender gap for development and hunger reduction in the nation and financial independence

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Front women to nations will generate income by keeping livestock and making sausages from the animals and also selling them live
The manure from the animals will be used to grow vegetables which will be supplied to local and supermarkets and they will benefit by always having fresh locally grown vegetables and increase their sales. Another target market is at household level where will be able to have outlets where people can easily access our products and create job opportunities in the outlets
The business will have competitive prizes because it will be based on cost saving ideas like the use of manure and growing our own animals for food processing 
This will bring value added to the environment because they will be zero use of chemicals meaning more healthier food

> Technical Feasibility

The whole goal of the innovation is to encourage women in farming and business 
It will take the ministry of livestock and farming to help with the knowledge on how to effectively do farming for business and other stakeholders helping women to be financially independent. The livestock will be sourced from the local villages. 
The targeted women are the unemployed because they will always be available 
The whole point of this innovation is to meet the needs of the unemployed women 
The innovation will extend throughout zambia because the part of the profit will be used to instablish more farming land get more women involved

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation will improve the lives of women living in extreme poverty conditions and help their families and communities and also create employment opportunities as the business grow
The proposed innovation is relevant to the current market because sellers will be able to access cheaper locally grown food and help inthe reduction of food prises
