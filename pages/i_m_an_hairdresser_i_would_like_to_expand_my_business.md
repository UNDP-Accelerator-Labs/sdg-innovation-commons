> Title of the innovation

I'm an hairdresser I would like to expand my business

> Brief description of the innovation

I want to expand my saloon business because I want it to have both a saloon a a barber shop since I just work from home therefore I would like it expand so that I can involved even others so that we can work together and prevent joblessness

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Since we'll be offering a wide variety of services like hair-platting and haircutting therefore this will benefit both men and women of Zambia
This challenge price will help me to rent a shop and have a specific place to be found moreover this will help me and my fellow youths to concentrate on things that are useful this will help us to abstain from harmful drugs like marijuana, alcohol drinking etc

> Technical Feasibility

For this business to be implemented properly i need to have mirrors, sets of barbing machines, spirit, towels, shampoo, dryers,blowers, toners, stools/ garden chairs, set of rowlers, wigs( Brazilian hairs, Peruvian hairs, styling gel, creams, hair conditioners,set of combs, nail fylers fridge, etc so that we can be offering quality services to the people

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation will improve my life,  the lives of the people that we'll be working together with and the people we'll be offering our services to, I mean this innovation will help me and my fellow youths to work hard in improving our financial situations and lift the burden off our parents and our guardians shoulders
