> Title of the innovation

integrated small holder farming for sustainable development and job creation.

> Brief description of the innovation

This Project will mainly focus on the youths especially the women and adolescents who have no capital and training to venture into farming for the development of our communities and poverty reduction

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

income will be generated through farming of daily needed vegetables, seasonal crops, fish, goat, pigs and poultry farming at a small scale to be expanded bit by bit as the project grows.

> Technical Feasibility

the project is designed to pass on the knowledge to the youths by training them easy farming techniques and empowering them with capital from the sales they make during training.in addition. Constant monitoring of their project will be done by the experts to ensure that the clients are able to developed their farms as a business so that they can also empower others.

> Anticipated Social Impact

This innovation will improve the lives of the youths who are going to be involved by making them work for their money and enjoy the fruits of their labor which will not only keep the youths focused on productivity but also provide the much needed revenue for the country at large. The products coming from these small holder farms will not only be sold locally but will be able to be exported to other countries after adding value to them.
