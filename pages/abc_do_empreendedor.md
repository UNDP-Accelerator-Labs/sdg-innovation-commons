## ABC do Empreendedor is a portal created to support entrepreneurs. Guides entrepreneurs to better plan and organize their business.

It provides tools that will help and guide entrepreneurs on the best way to start their business, considering all the important factors, whether financial, legal or management.

The portal is aimed at all types of entrepreneurs: new, newly started or already established, and provides a range of services in the same space: Business Plan, Feasibility Studies, Company Constitution, Model Documents, Virtual Accounting, Business Plans Marketing, Credit/Financing.

In addition to the above services, it supports entrepreneurs to find co-working spaces and multicredit.
