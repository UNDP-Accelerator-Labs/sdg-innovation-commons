> Title of the innovation

Eco friendky sleepwear.   The Allseasonsdelicate  brand hopes to venture into sleepwear made ournof african prints from diffrrent parts of africa.

> Brief description of the innovation

This innovation was birthed after much research in the sleepwear industry that mainly use modern 
  western,oriental prints but found very little african prints. I found that the african content  has diverse prints which would bring a new niche in the sleepwear market.
The other reason is the type of fabrics being used for sleepwear manufacturing.  These included silk, cotton and polyester. I would like to explore eco -friendly materials that support a drive to sustainable production which then saves our planet.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

This innovation would generate income for myself and others as there will be job creation across africa, use of  social media and the internet for sales and marketing, support local textile factories across africa. The venture will also support girl child education on the continent.
