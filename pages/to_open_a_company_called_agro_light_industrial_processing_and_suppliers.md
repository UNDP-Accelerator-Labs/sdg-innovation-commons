> Title of the innovation

To open a company called Agro light industrial processing and suppliers

> Brief description of the innovation

My innovation aim at growing and processing and supplying agricultural products at an affordable price so that everyone can afford to buy and and eat so as to remain healthy 
To reduce the prices of food items that have gone too high due to low supply

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

For me it will generate income because I will not be buying raw materials I will be cultivating on my own  so it means more profits
For others it will improve the existing business because they will be ordering the finished products at a cheaper price and make more profits
The value addition of my innovation on the environment is that it will be participating in keep Zambia clean,green and healthy
By planting trees, participate in cleaning the town and supplying food items at an affordable prices so that everyone can afford to have three meals per day and live healthy because a healthy country is a wealthy country
