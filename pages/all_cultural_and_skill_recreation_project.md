> Title of the innovation

All cultural and skill recreation project

> Brief description of the innovation

All cultural and skill recreation project is an innovation that is going to deal with girls and women that will generally recruit, train then empower them via the goods and  services that the project will offer mental and physical training from health to physical well-being.
First and foremost it will offer mental health for the females on how to deal with various health challenges that girls and women go through such as  mental illness in women, pelvic informatory disease , sexually production health and economic empowerment and social security in women through the skills that will be offered .

A platform that will not only provide health services to girls and women but also create jobs after training for the  females so as to deposit a spirit of independence  and courage in females with or without the highest level of education to all living with and without disabilities so as to have a common goal to be game changers.
