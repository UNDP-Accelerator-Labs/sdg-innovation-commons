> Title of the innovation

Practical Financial Solutions

> Brief description of the innovation

Practical Financial Solutions is a business whose Core purpose is to provide a platform that enables everyone establish a genuine, viable and sustainable business in order to reduce poverty, and create wealth.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The business will provide services meant to assist clients establish businesses and reduce unemployment/create supplementary income for the employed. The services will attract a charge.
Target market: 
1. Unemployed youths
2. Civil servants
On the copperbelt.

> Technical Feasibility

The business will offer the following services;
1. Ideal business identification
2. Business plan formulation
3. Business registration
4. Management of start-ups

> Anticipated Social Impact

1. The business will provide services that will enable our customers establish well planned and managed business, and therefore, reduce unemployment and create well.
2. The low income working class earners will have supplementary income and will caution poverty levels.
