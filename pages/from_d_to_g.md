This Solution is trying to spread the joy of planting and let people to share their plants location by simply placing a tree emoji on the map and this will show him/her how much oxygen she/he helped producing by planting this tree. Also there is a technical advices on how to plant and some recommendations to grow the plants in a healthy way. This solution will motivate the people to learn from each other and to increase the awareness of people towards the importance of planting. This Quick-win platform will leave a good impact on the peoples behavior and this is just the beginning for the upcoming features.
