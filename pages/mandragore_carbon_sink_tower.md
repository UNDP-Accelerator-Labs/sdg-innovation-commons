> Description of the solution

A design based on the mandrake plant and packed with many innovative energy-saving and carbon-capture technologies and strategies. It uses the best passive heating and cooling techniques to condition the interior space. It incorporates as many natural materials as possible and a literal forest of plants and trees

> Socio-economic effects

improve energy efficiency, reduce carbon emissions, increase biodiversity
