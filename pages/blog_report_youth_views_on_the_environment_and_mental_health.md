Report: Youth Views on the Environment and Mental Health
MARCH 16, 2023
In collaboration with the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP) Accelerator Lab for Barbados and the Eastern Caribbean, U-Report Barbados launched a poll asking youth for their views on the impacts of climate change on mental health. The poll was released in October 2022 after a series of dialogues between the two parties.
