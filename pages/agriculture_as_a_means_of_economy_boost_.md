> Title of the innovation

Agriculture As a Means Of Economy Boost.

> Brief description of the innovation

This Looks at how Agriculture boost the economy by engaging fully on large scale farming of almost all the products needed by the local and international market.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

When Agriculture is done at a large scale can produce grains and other agricultural products to feed the nation and the neighboring countries since Zambia has a good weather system to help grows variety of crops. The land is very fertile and government policies are conducive to help the farmers.

> Technical Feasibility

When the equipment will be distributed to the people involved and all the necessary commodities are supplied, it is expected that Zambia is the best and most reliable country to supply all the products to the international market either finished or unfinished products.

> Anticipated Social Impact

The society will have access to enough food, financial sustainability, promote good health among the players as well as help in the sponsoring of the education to all within the catchment areas of the project.
