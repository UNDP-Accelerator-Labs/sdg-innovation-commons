# Irrigating Urban Greenery

The CONWAT is a device designed to irrigate greenery in the urban areas by collecting water released as a result of the condensation process from the air conditioners installed in buildings. Then using collected water to irrigate parks and yards around the building.  


The device functions in the following phrases:  collecting water and transmitting it to plants;  ensuring the operation of the hardware/software of the Electronic System of that device.
