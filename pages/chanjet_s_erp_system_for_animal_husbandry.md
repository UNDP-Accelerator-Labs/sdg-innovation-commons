> Description of the solution

This system has features including:
Financial Management: Good Accounting for data handling and Yidai Zhang for proxy accounting, supporting multiple methods and generating statements.
Procurement Management: ERP module for comprehensive functions with Good Business for inventory, planning, and supplier management.
Sales Management: Good Finance and Business for automated sales and strategy formulation.
Production Management: Good Business module for production process digitalization.
Supply Chain Management: Good Finance and Business module for connecting and coordinating production, sales, and resources.
