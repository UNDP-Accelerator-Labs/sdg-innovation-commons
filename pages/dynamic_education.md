> Title of the innovation

Dynamic Education

> Brief description of the innovation

Dynamic education blues the lines between subjects, teaches students how topics are related and, more importantly, teaches students critical thinking. The objective to put the Zambian educational system on the map and to prepare students for leadership.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Our nation needs an educated populace to succeed in life and as a nation. An educated workforce achieves higher economic growth and, hence, national prosperity.

> Technical Feasibility

Apply nationwide teacher training programs that are assessed formatively and summatively. Change the role of teacher and student, change the academic standards/curriculum and change the property taxation system to that it funds local/regional government schools. Government schools must be able to compete with private education.

> Anticipated Social Impact

A phenomenal educational system is an agent for change. It improves livelihoods, health, social stability, and long-term economic growth. It enables upward socioeconomic mobility and thus, key to escaping poverty.
