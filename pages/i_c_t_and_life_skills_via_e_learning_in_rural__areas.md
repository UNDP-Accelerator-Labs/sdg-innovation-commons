> Title of the innovation

I.C.T and Life Skills Via E-learning In Rural  Areas

> Brief description of the innovation

We are in the fourth industrial revolution and the Youth and Women need skills especially in I.C.T. For this reason, we  provide I.C.T and Life Skills for self-development,personal-development, skills development and capacity building Via E-Learning in Rural Areas to the Youths and Women.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The project will generate income through tuition fees by providing training in both I.C.T and Life Skill courses. Additionally, we shall realise revenue by provide I.C.T consultancy and I.C.T Services. 
Our target market is the Youths and Women for this reason the challenge prize will leverage our short falls in  to accrediting to T.E.V.E.T.A, affiliating to I.C.T professional bodies and acquiring more computers so as to provide our services to a large number of the Youths and Women.

> Technical Feasibility

This innovation is designed in such a way that we shall empower I.C.T skills and Life Skills to the Youths and Women later integrate the aspect of I.C.T in Innovation and Entrepreneurship, Agriculture and S.T.E.M education.
Furthermore, for us to effectively implement it shall be done quarterly. In the first and second quarter we shall train 300 students. The income generated will be 50% ploughed back, 30% of it will be used affiliate to T to acquire land and farm and lastly 20% will be invested in Collective Investment Schemes.
The last quarter we shall increase the enrolment to 500 through aggressive marketing and thereby increasing the cash flow for the grow of the business.

> Anticipated Social Impact

We are in the fourth industrial revolution and the Youth and Women need skills especially in I.C.T. to empower them for self-development, personal development, skills development and capacity building.
