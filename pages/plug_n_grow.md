> Description / Mission Statement

Plug’n’Grow fills the “technology development” gap in the soilless agricultural value chain. This gap entails the process of prototyping the produce and designing the sustainable and efficient technology needed for it to grow soilless. They give their client a system that produces the vegetables with the lowest cost available in hydroponics. Plug’n’Grow’s role is to assist agri-companies to reduce the cost of production while raising the quality of produce. They consult on the production from the technical aspect to the cost and pricing.
