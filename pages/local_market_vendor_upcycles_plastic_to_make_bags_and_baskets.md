A local market vendor sells bags and baskets made out of upcycling plastic. Vanuatu has a world-leading single-use plastic ban that came into effect in 2018, banning non-biodegradable plastic including bags and polystyrene containers. Upcycling plastic uses minimal natural resources and saves plastic waste from ending up in landfills.Â
