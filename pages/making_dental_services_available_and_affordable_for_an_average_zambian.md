> Title of the innovation

MAKING DENTAL SERVICES AVAILABLE AND AFFORDABLE FOR AN AVERAGE ZAMBIAN

> Brief description of the innovation

There is an increase in the number of oral medical complications due to late detection of Dental cases.
This is as a result of the high charges of Dental services making it difficult for an average Zambian to access the service in time. Through this initiative, which we have already in the process, we seek funding to enable us to make Dental services available and affordable to the community.
