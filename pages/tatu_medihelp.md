> Title of the innovation

Tatu MediHelp

> Brief description of the innovation

Yatu MediHelp is a Mobile App/Ussd based medical consultancy service that aims at increasing access to quality Medical Services especially for people living in rural areas of Zambia via both ussd and Mobile Application.This will be done by connecting patients to medical experts thereby reducing the numbers of death due to long distance traveled by patients just to access medical help. The App/Ussd platform also allows patients to schedule appointments with medical personnels at the comfort of their own homes. Our goal is to provide easy/fast but quality Medical services.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

YatuMediHelp will have a k3 registration fee to access the service.

> Technical Feasibility

Yatu MediHelp is designed in a way that a patient can access medical help via 2 distinct platforms 
(a) Ussd (b) Mobile App 
For ussd a patient dials a given ussd code.for mobile app one simply downloads it on their phone.
Both platforms gives direct access to patients interactions to medical personnels.A prototype is yet to be designed.our target implementation plan is to partner with mobile network provider for the Ussd platform and putting the Mobile App on Google Play Store.

> Anticipated Social Impact

1. The innovation will reduce the time patients spend on ques trying to access medical services.
2. It will help patients access quality Medical service in that all Yatu MediHelp affiliated medical personnel will be medical experts.
3. Easy and fast medical assistance to patients.
4. Good quality of health in that patients will have fast access to medical help. 
5. Access to medical help from the comfort of their own homes.
