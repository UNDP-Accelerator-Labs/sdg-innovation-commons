The Municipality of Veles introduces a new system of electronic services which aims to facilitate access to all services we provide in accordance with our competencies.  Access to documents, reports, official messengers are done through this portal. The dashboard is displaying the information as well as the forms for all municipal services.  The system includes access to all the services, documents, urbanistic plans, realized projects and council video meetings in the municipality of Veles. - Digitized process
