Powered by UNDP Accelerator Labs in Cambodia, these dashboards that are a collection of visualizations and insights which seek to explain and dissect the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic in Cambodia. Globally, the pandemic has had not only devastating health effects, but has triggered cross-cutting impacts in economic sectors such as tourism, manufacturing, and service delivery.Â 


These dashboards showcase trending survey data and geolocation data, collected before and throughout the pandemic. The insights garnered from these dashboards can help citizens, policymakers, decision-makers, NGOs, and CSOs to understand the varied and far-reaching effects of the pandemic on men, women, business owners, MSMEs and any intersections between those groups.
