> Description of the solution

This project aims to revolutionize wine production by establishing a system that improves the grape intake process and quality grading. It uses video cameras at grape intake points for enhanced grape quality grading based on video footage, streamlining and improving key winemaking steps. It also enhances traceability by linking the final product (wine) back to the vineyard, it represents a significant advancement in the wine industry, leveraging technology to improve efficiency, quality, and traceability.
