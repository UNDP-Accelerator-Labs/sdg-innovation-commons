> Qualitative Results of the Innovation

Cross-Border Cultural Festivals: In some borderland areas, cultural festivals have been organized to promote cross-border cultural exchange and understanding. The Lake Turkana Cultural Festival, held annually on the border between Kenya and Ethiopia, celebrates the cultural diversity of the region and promotes peaceful coexistence between neighboring communities.
