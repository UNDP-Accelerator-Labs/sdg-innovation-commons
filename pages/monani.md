## Support program for pregnant women, by sending SMS with advice and useful information to accompany your pregnancy.

This platform works as a pre- and post-natal counseling center. Every pregnant woman can register for free and receive every week, according to her gestation period, suggestions, and tips on a healthy and risk-free pregnancy.
