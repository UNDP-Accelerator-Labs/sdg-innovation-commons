> Description

Ecoarami seeks to provide technical assistance, training services, infrastructure, and inputs for agroecological farming. It is a single-person brand of products and services that offers assistance and accompaniment to Peasant and Indigenous Communities from an Agroecological approach. We provide advice on: production planning and marketing; elaboration and commercialization of biofertilizers, facilitation of workshops.
