> Description of the solution

ONDO is a cloud-based system that automates irrigation, fertilization, and climate control for various crops. This system helps farmers save time, reduce resource usage, and minimize human error. By providing remote access and 24/7 support, ONDO empowers farmers to optimize their operations and increase profitability.
