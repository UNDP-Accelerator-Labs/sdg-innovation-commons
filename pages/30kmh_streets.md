> Description of the solution

Campaign calling for limiting speeds to 30 km/h where people walk, live and play, as streets that promote safe walking and cycling can reduce car dependency and harmful vehicle emissions that contribute to climate change. Barcelona has been taking more steps to apply the 30 km/h limit in 75% of the city‚Äôs streets, including main roads.

> Socio-economic effects

Reduce harmful vehicle emissions, reduce fuel consumption, help prevent road traffic deaths, 
improve traffic flow, promote physical activity
