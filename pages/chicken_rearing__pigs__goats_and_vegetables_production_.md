> Brief description of the innovation

Possibilities for Vulnerable Youth development was formed in 2019 who's vision is to empower the vulnerable Youth with the knowledge and life skills to enable them withstand the pressure and challenges faced with in the future. Our objectives is to make sure all youth have a great future and be able to stand on their own for their development .

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We currently aquired Land of size about 2 Hectors were we are going to construct and expand our production to cater for all the youth around the area. By so doing we will be able to create employment and food security to for all.our market target is local and outside Zambia eg Congo DR through mpulungu harbor which just near Kasama our area of operations.
