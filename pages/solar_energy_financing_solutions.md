Solar Energy Financing Solutions

> Qualitative Results of the Innovation

1. Renewable Energy Access: M-Kopa's solar energy systems provide households with access to renewable energy, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and decreasing greenhouse gas emissions.
2. Reduced Deforestation: By providing households with an alternative to wood and charcoal for cooking and lighting, M-Kopa's solar energy systems may contribute to reducing deforestation rates in the region.
3. Improved Health: By reducing exposure to indoor air pollution from traditional cooking methods, M-Kopa's solar energy systems may improve health outcomes for households in the region.
4. Economic Benefits: M-Kopa's pay-as-you-go model enables low-income households to access energy services that they might otherwise not be able to afford, potentially increasing economic resilience in the face of climate change impacts.
5. Community Building: M-Kopa has also partnered with organizations such as the Red Cross to distribute solar energy systems in refugee camps, providing energy access to vulnerable populations and potentially building community resilience to climate-related shocks.

> Quantitative Result of the Innovation

1. M-Kopa reports that it has prevented 1.5 million tonnes of CO2 emissions since its inception in 2011.
2. According to M-Kopa, its customers have saved over $300 million in energy expenses since 2011.
 3. M-Kopa has provided over 1.5 million households with access to electricity since 2011.
 4. According to a study by Dalberg Advisors, the use of M-Kopa's solar energy solutions has led to a 26% reduction in the incidence of respiratory illnesses in Kenya.
