> Title of the innovation

Innovation in value addition and promoting a health community through application of nuclear technology

> Brief description of the innovation

Investing in gemstone mining to create employment and reinvesting proceeds in a robust and dynamic nuclear medicine treatment center.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Mining gemstones and cutting/ polishing will generate income to invest in setting up a nuclear medicine centre.The prize will enable to mechanise the mining project to increase production and employ more citizens.The proceeds will then be invested in developing centres for nuclear medicine centres for treatment and detection of various ailments.

> Technical Feasibility

Employment creation through gemstone mining and reinvesting proceeds in application of nuclear technology to detect and treat disease.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Employment creation and investments in health by applying nuclear technology to treat and detect diseases.The relevance lies in employment creation and lessening disease burden
