> Qualitative Results of the Innovation Solution 

1. Increased adoption of precision agriculture: Agronovate uses precision agriculture techniques, which increased crop yields, reduced waste, and improved efficiency. 
 2. Improved access to finance: Agronovate partnered with financial institutions to provide farmers with access to finance. The company has also introduced a digital platform that enables farmers to access financing without collateral. This has increased the availability of funding for farmers and has helped to reduce the financial barriers to innovation and growth in agriculture.
3. Increased market access: Agronovate has a digital platform that connects farmers with buyers. This helps farmers to sell their products at better prices and increase their market access. 
4. Improved soil health: Agronovate's soil testing services provide farmers with information on the nutrient content of their soil, enabling them to apply fertilizers and other inputs more efficiently. This has resulted in improved soil health and increased crop yields.
5. Increased employment: Agronovate has created jobs for young people in Nigeria through its various initiatives. The company has trained and employed youth and women to provide digital extension services to farmers, operate drones, and provide technical support to farmers.
