> Title of the innovation

I am running a farm program called the mud agri-triad it comprises of growing legumes,maize, piggery, cattle, chickens and veggies.

> Brief description of the innovation

My innovation is an Agri cycle which starts with growing legumes and maize for feeding my cattle and pigs, and these are only meant for meat processing, since I run a butchery in petauke and katete. I wish to have my sells point through out eastern Province and expand the market to other provinces, besides that I want to grow pawpaw and orangesto take advantage of the juice plant which will be operational soon it is situated in katete.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

My innovation is based on the demand of meat products which I intend to expand and employ more people. There is great potential in the meat industry and I am already in it and it's really working out well.

> Technical Feasibility

To expand the already running business

> Anticipated Social Impact

Already I have 8 workers and I need to employ more and give jobs to the locals, they also help there families
