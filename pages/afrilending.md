> Title of the innovation

AFRILENDING

> Brief description of the innovation

AfriLending (UBER but, FOR CASH) -  is the only African Disruptive peer to Peer lending and borrowing platform with instant “On the platform” Mobile money Payments implemented for remissions and disbursements to help people quickly share and/or lend money to nearby people in need of financial help with little to no physical interactions.

It is a location-based (GPS) technology platform that allows for both individuals and institutions to lend to anyone in need of urgent financial help. 

From payments of funds to the management of business finances and transactions, AfriLending comes with tools that enable anyone to start a lending business at a click of a button by connecting them to thousands of nearby customers.


AfriLending Aims at contributing to Africa's economic growth by enabling serve the unmet financial needs of those segments of the population which are not the core target segments of traditional Bank and Corporate financial services models through decentralization of lending and borrowing processes. And, most importantly, to accelerate and help achieve the 2017-2021 National Sustainable Development plan No. 7

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

With over 50, 000 active loan businesses in Zambia; having each business, a minimum of 10 active paying clients with smartphones; This technology currently makes and will be generating its revenue from transaction commissions carried out on the platform from the loanee (borrower) or Lender.

As a benefitting result for both the lenders and their customers, through improved, secure, and decentralized lending; AfriLending relieves businesses from spending thousands of money on costly and crowded online advertisements while creating direct, eased, none costly/free, guaranteed client acquisition processes and automated KYC processes for increased revenue generation.

The above statements are supported by the rise in recorded transactions during the first week pilot of the innovation launch. contexted summing up to over ZMW250,000.00 with accumulative interests/profits of over K70,000. This proved the fact that hundreds of citizens are looking for convenient methods of accessing financial help during times of urgency/need.

The prize to be received will act as a foundational anchor in helping the innovation reach out to serving thousands and/or millions of Africans, through promotional content. And, critically accelerate the foundational building of the business team in helping achieve and accomplish the intended solution goals.

> Technical Feasibility

AfriLending Platform has currently been built by its founder using the following open-source technologies; PHP, MYSQL, JAVASCRIPT, HTML5 &amp; CSS3 Web Technologies (For the web platform), and JAVA being a primary language powering the mobile Application. 

These foundational technologies have made it possible for the AfriLending platform to be instantly, easily accessed, and used by everyone with access to the internet, via www.AfriLending.com website with support for both computers and mobile phones.

> Anticipated Social Impact

AfriLending FinTech technology primarily aims at bridging the gap of financial access and cut down the cost of acquiring financial assistance, by giving people the chance and opportunity to be financially empowered and supported during their most urgent, stressful, and/or needy times of the hour. 

It's the answer to the underbanked populations of the continent. A perfect cashless technology to power, empower and promote currently rising small businesses and neighbor-to-neighbor lending activities.
