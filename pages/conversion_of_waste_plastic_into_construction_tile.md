The proposed Project will 
(i) envisages the establishment of a plant to produce Plastic tiles with a capacity of 180,000 pieces per annum 
(ii) will recycle 574 kg of waste plastic per day to produce 600 Pieces of tiles per day.
(iii) Focus on Waste Plastic management and conversion of waste plastic into a valuable Construction Tiles 
(iv) Pilot scale tile plant with community-based efforts by converting waste Plastic into useful Construction Tiles
