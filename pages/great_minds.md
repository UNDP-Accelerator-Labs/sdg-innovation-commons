> Title of the innovation

Great Minds

> Brief description of the innovation

This is an innovation of producing Educational, Traditional and International videos through movie Acting/photo shooting. Having successfully completed a programme of instruction in video production at Zambia Institute Of Mass Communication Education Trust (ZAMCOM) year 2010, it has made me professional in shooting, editing and producing Standard Digital Videos.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

It will generate income through producing videos and photo shooting. It will also help others to have income learn and polish up their skills. The market target are the TV Stations namely the Multi Choice . It will help businesses grow through advertisements. It will change the mind set of people especially the youths that they are suppose to be doing something profitable and beneficial for them and their families.

> Technical Feasibility

It is located in Chimwemwe Kitwe. The requirements are: the computers, cameras, shooting lights and the tripled stands to mention but a few.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Been a youth chairperson of the UPND Buntungwa Youth Wing Chimwemwe Kitwe from 2019 till date, I know this will help not only the youths but even little kids and the the elderly people because most of the mothers are widows. It will play a bigger role of teaching and helping the community at large.
