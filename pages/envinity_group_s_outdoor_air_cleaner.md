> Description of the solution

The Giant Dutch Vacuum Cleaner, developed by Envinity Group, is designed to significantly improve air quality by filtering out fine particulate matter from the urban atmosphere. It can clean more than 280,000 cubic feet of air per hour, capturing 100% of fine particles and 95% of ultra-fine particles, which are known to be harmful to human health.

> Socio-economic effects

By improving air quality, the project aims to enhance public health and reduce the health-related costs associated with air pollution.
