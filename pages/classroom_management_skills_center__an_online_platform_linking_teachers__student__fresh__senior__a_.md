> Title of the innovation

Classroom management skills center. An online platform linking teachers (student, fresh, senior &amp; retired teachers)

> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation aims to improve pupils performance through building teachers' efficacy. Teachers once equipment with modern pedagogy of classroom management on real time and share their daily basis experiences will enable them handle pupils in a manner that facilitates learning.

The 21 Century skills demand for pupils in areas of ; innovation, creativity, collaboration, problem solving among other skills requires vigorous educator who are capable of creating classroom environment that allows pupils to develop these skills.
With more interest, is the rural and remote pupils of today who are highly detached from their peers in urban areas. 

In interest of resources, teachers are argents of change. Hence, equipping educators who can pay for this knowledge unlike pupils who are the beneficiaries of this innovation.

It is my believe that to every drop-out there's a teacher responsible.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Through participation fees of educators and purchase of the software (classroom management skills center). To some degree the hardware such as computers or smartphones.

> Anticipated Social Impact

* Rural pupils academic performance will be improved.
* Teachers' efficacy and confidence will be high.
* More innovation from a thousand of millions of pupils who will benefit.
