> Brief description of the innovation

We are a community oriented organisation that targets youth through sports and empower them through discipleship and academic program.  We raise financial resources through fundraising and through our initiatives in farming programs

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We are doing farming business and we have started with chicken for ( layers and meat) in chingola. Our target market foe this is for wholesale and retail to the interested companies and individuals. Upon realising the income ,we put the money to implementing the programs (mobile clinic in livingstone, school sponsorship program in choma , and soccer program in livingstone, choma and chongola, as well as wages for our staff and other administrative costs)
The value this activities add the environment in that they surely create employment. But more so we prepare the youths to be a responsible citizen foe the country.

> Technical Feasibility

We target youths from the age of 6 going upwards. Our implementation is to see that these kids become empowered through their interests and academics.
