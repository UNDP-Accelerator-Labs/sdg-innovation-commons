Think Plastic, which is a social media campaign to raise awareness of people on plastic usage
and environmental pollution in Cambodia. Think Plastic, has more than 97,000 followers. Sotheavy produced more than 80 videos on topics related to plastics and pollution. Each of Sotheavy’s videos encourages and guide people to discuss environmental pollution caused by plastics, reaching thousands of people across Cambodia.
