> Title of the innovation

Climate change, through beekeeping it involves keeping bees, production, marketing

> Brief description of the innovation

We are keeping bees, processing of bee products, packaging and marketing, the aim of the project is create employment , protect the environment by discouraging, indiscriminately cutting of trees as the source of Nectar for bees, reduce rulal poverty levels etc

Keeping bees , involves, manufacturing of beehives, which can be sold and bring in income, selling bee products like honey and beeswax  will bring income for business and the rest of employees ,bees are the major pollinators hence help maintain the environment, also bees depends on trees therefore will discourage the cutting of trees for honey production
