> Brief description about the identified local solution:

Knowledge Gate has big interest in supporting students and universities within its vision for better education. 
We focus in Student Labs on linking students from scientific fields with qualified tutors, whether academic professors or peer educators as this is an opportunity for distinguished students to help other students and make living while developing their teaching and training skills. 
Student Labs provide the students with: 
High quality education at affordable fees. 
Up-to-date education methodologies and in-person and online tutoring.
Curricular and extracurricular activities that enhance their academic field of study.
Market-based knowledge in their field and opportunities to get practical experience that supports their academic experience.
