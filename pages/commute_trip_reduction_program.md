> Type of the Solution

Policy Intervention &amp; Awareness Raising, Financial Incentives

> Affected Sector

Transport

> Description of the solution

Programs that encourage employees to choose alternatives to single-occupancy vehicles for commuting, such as carpooling, vanpooling, biking, walking, or using public transportation. Programs may include awareness campaigns, employer incentives (parking cash-out, transit subsidies), and financial benefits through tax breaks.

> Socio-economic effects

Reduced traffic congestion, improved air quality, public health benefits, potential for tax savings
