Disinfecting booth 

> Solution description



●It sanitizes the whole body  and shoe soles with spray from 12 points and
uses electricity and water power pressure to spray disinfectant solutions
●No hand contact is needed to
operate the machine.
●Can be used for public spaces,
hospitals, crowded areas and public transport stations.
●Can be built in two days all  out of local materials 
●It is built in different sizes
including motion sensors and an optional wheel chair ramp
