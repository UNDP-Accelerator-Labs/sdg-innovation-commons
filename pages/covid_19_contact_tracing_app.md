TRACE: TRACE was one of the submissions made to the UNDP Barbados and Eastern Caribbean COVID-19 #IslandHack Hackathon challenge. TRACE is an app boasts of cutting edge peer-to-peer contact tracing capabilities which could become a standard weapon in the fight against COVID-19, by tracking down infections and containing outbreaks.
