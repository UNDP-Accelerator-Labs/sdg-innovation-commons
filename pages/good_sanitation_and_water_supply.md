> Title of the innovation

Good Sanitation and Water supply

> Brief description of the innovation

To provide sustainable water development and good sanitation to the community.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

The innovation will generate income for me and others in such a way that will be working on projects that requires our services (plumbing, welding, water connections and waste disposal) in both maintenance works and new buildings where need be.
I work on delivering good and quality works at an affordable charge target both public institutions, private sectors and at individual level.
I hope that my innovation will reduce the rate of environmental pollution by good watse  disposal and having sustainable sanitation development in the community.

> Technical Feasibility

My innovation is designed in such a way that it requires full technical trained personnel at least five and unskilled men women. There are also technical support which are required in form of machinery, workshop, tools and safety attire. My skill is to provide plumbing works, metal fabrication and installation of water tanks, good disposal of waste matters.

> Anticipated Social Impact

My innovation is to provide good water quality and sustainable sanitation development to the community, e.g by construction of pit latrines  toilet in rural areas, drilling of boreholes and installing water hand pumps.
