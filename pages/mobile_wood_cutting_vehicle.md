This is an informal wood cutting vehicle that is used by informal service providers in the North Macedonia. Usually teams of 2-3 people are offering the service, and spot the households where there are woods in the yard for the upcoming winter season. The service is provided for cutting and chopping the woods.  Payment done in cash only.
