> What is the purpose of the solution? (brief problem & solution description)

Problem: High prices of fuel  decreased number of public transpiration vehicles  Solution: Dual-made a prototype of a solar tuk-tuk that goes with Qamishli's sunny weather and that has a less environmental footprint.  the Solar taxi (Tuk-Tuk) is used as public affordable transportation. The Tuk-Tuk is made locally using locally available materials. It consists of, an electric motor, wheels, metal body, battery bank, solar panel, and shade made from fabric.
