> Title of the innovation

CAPITAL MARKETS

> Brief description of the innovation

this is a business based on global capital markets, which will involve buying and selling secondary shares taking profits when a a particular company raises in share value. participant's/recruits are to play on global standards, with more basis to the standards of economically established countries hence raising the living and working standards of those in a small economy country like zambia.
capital market's are also an unpopular venture locally, with only one dominant player being the Lusaka stock exchange, after raising capital trading on global market's like NASDEC, NYSE. we are going to set up an alternative trading system locally that will compete with LUSE ours will provide easy access to share purchase and selling capabilities for local individuals and also educate on the importance of investing in company shares, the easy access to trading of share's will be be achieved by building up and promoting a digital platform.
participating on capital market requires market knowledge, theirs a system set to provide education to the recruits.
