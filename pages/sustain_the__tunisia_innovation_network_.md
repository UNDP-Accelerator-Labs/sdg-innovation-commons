Our challenge is that The “Tunisia Innovation Network” is set to expand into universities with a special focus on AI.

> Background: What is the history of your challenge? What is causing or driving it? Who is involved? How does the current situation look like? What undesired effects does it produce?



This network is one of the flagship programs of the Lab, the challenge of 2024 was the animation, sustainability and codification of the network.

Through monthly Calls, online training with international consultants, and two physical meetings, one of which was with government representatives, as well as immersion in the UN environment, the network is now ready to begin collective experiments with the aim of incorporating innovation into public policies.
