> Title of the innovation

Developed an eLearning portal with more than 35,000 pupils online and more than 5,000 play store downloads. We have innovated to provide eLearning services to people with hearing challenges.

> Brief description of the innovation

We are a Tech company that develops education learning Softwares and other operational Softwares and apps for firms in Zambia. Our focus market is Zambia and the SADC region. Our customers are mostly in Zambia and are parents and pupils from G1 to G12 &amp; GCE. Our eLearning innovation has more than 35,000 pupils online and we are different from other Edtech innovators due to the fact that we are the only firm with an MOU with the Examinations Council of Zambia and Zamtel and the ministry of education.
We have further developed an eLearning portal for pupils with hearing challenges in Zambia.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- yes

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

We generate revenue through charging $1/month to our users. We further generate revenue through selling our customized tablets. our target market is school pupils, schools, and parents in Zambia. The prize will help improve our business through developing version 3(includes eLearning services for the deaf) of our software and adverts.

> Technical Feasibility

we have digitised past exam questions with digital solutions. We have a prototype( https://www.smartrevision.co.zm/)

> Anticipated Social Impact

Now more than ever it is critical that students are able to study remotely. The Smart Revision is offers a smart, quick and reliable means to revise and get ready for those attempting to sit for ECZ final examinations in grades 7, 9 and 12. With Covid-19 cases still going up, and schools closing more frequently than ever, our eLearning services have become more vital to more than 35,000 pupils in Zambia.
