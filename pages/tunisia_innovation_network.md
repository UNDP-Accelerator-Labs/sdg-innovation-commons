## Learnings on your challenge

> What are the top key insights you generated about your learning challenge during this Action Learning Plan? (Please list a maximum of 5 key insights) 

1) The innovation landscape in Tunisia is very fragmented.

2) Public Policies poorly use of innovation.

3) Confusion between innovation applied to Development &amp; invention.

4) Innovation practioners cover many fields and areas in Tunisia.

5) Innovation techniques are more used by civil society more than public policies

> Can you provide more detail on your handover process?

we launched a call through socua networks for innovators in Tunisia. We managed to select more than 30 actors that we put together. Currently, collaborations are emerging between them with the aim of impacting public policies
