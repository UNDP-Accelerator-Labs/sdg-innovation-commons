> Description of the solution

ChotuKool is an innovative approach to tackling the problem of food shortage in India. Architecture Sunderraman Gopalan and his team turned the traditional product which provides affordable and attractive food storage solution for people who had never used a refrigeratore. Chotukool is a 45-litter plastic container that can cool food to around 8 to 10 degrees on a 12 -volt battery. It uses a thermoelectric or solid state cooling system.
