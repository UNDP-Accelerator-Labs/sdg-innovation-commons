> Title of the innovation

Vision Impact Training Project

> Brief description of the innovation

Vision Training Impact Project is an idea of Vision Network Foundation a not-for- profit organisation based in Mansa. It seeks to empower women with life survival skills in food preparation and housekeeping to make uneducated women employable as house helps and start small businesses.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Mansa town has no maid centre and working mothers complain about where to find maids and when they find them they have challenges with the conduct and performance. Those looking for maids will know where to find trained maids and our women will be empowered with survival skills. The women will not pay anything to VINEF because their training will be sponsored. Training period is four months.

> Technical Feasibility

This project intends to train five hundred women from Senama and Maiteneke high density areas of Mansa. The instructors in cookery, entrepreneurship and housekeeping will be hired by VISION NETWORK Foundation. we will need catering equipment and other training materials. Period to train women will be four months. After training the Foundation will find jobs for the women and help to negotiate for their wages.

> Anticipated Social Impact

single mother headed homes will have an income. The dependency of women on men will reduce and abuse of women will likely go down. Nutrition at household level will improve.
