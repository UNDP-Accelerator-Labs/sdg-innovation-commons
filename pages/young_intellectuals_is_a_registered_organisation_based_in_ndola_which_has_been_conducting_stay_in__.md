> Brief description of the innovation

Who We Are
Young Intellectuals Organisation is a registered society in Zambia whose aim is to be an educational advocate by inspiring young people to stay in school and explore the potential benefits that education offers.
Our Mission
We aim at empowering young people with the necessary skills required for them to excel academically through a transformed belief system and a value-driven life.
Our vision
To see responsible and visionary young people who believe in the importance of school and its ability to transform their lives.
Our Values
In all our endeavors we strive for honesty and transparency. Decisions are made and guided by a commitment to social justice as well as to a long-term commitment to the communities we serve. We aim to undertake our responsibilities with care, integrity and love.
