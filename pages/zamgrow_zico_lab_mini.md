> Title of the innovation

Zamgrow Zico Lab Mini

> Brief description of the innovation

The Zamgrow ZICO minilab is a portable device that is used to measure and test an environment in order to advise the user(farmer) on whether it is conducive for farming activities and advise what possible crop, seed or fertilizer is conducive for the climate via soil, humidity light intensity and atmospheric temperature for the best of yields and harvest with 98% accuracy.

> Technical Feasibility

The ZICO minilab has numerous features that perform different tasks that achieve the above listed things which are PH sensor for testing the soil PH and nutritional value of soil, humidity to test the moisture content of the soil and the level , temperature sensor to test the atmospheric temperature of the area in order to know the compatibility of the crops desired and light sensor to test the level of light intensity therefore deducing the crop conducive for the given area and also can provide more info E.g. whether solar panels can be used based on the light intensity for energy purposes etc.
