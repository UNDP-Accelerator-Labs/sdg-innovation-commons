Emergency sex education via Mobile
Ask Without Shame is a non-profit social enterprise providing emergency sex education via mobile to improve and promote sexual health. It is a free and anonymous service to answer questions regarding sexual matters without judgment through android App, SMS, WhatsApp and a toll free hotline. Medical experts are available 24/7 to assist youth with the right information. Ask Without Shame has responded to over 30.000 users, is employing eight young sexual and medical experts and has received international awards such as the appsafrica Award for Social impact or the African Women Award.
