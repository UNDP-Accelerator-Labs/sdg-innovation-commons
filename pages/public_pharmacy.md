> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Almost all clinics and hospitals in our locality luck basic and essential drugs, thus opening up a pharmacy is ideal as the market is readily available. The act will not only benefit me, the community as well will benefit from this essential drugs, the government with benefit as we'll supplement their efforts.

> Technical Feasibility

I wish to open up pharmacis around around different parts of our country, which will supply different types of drugs at offordable prices. My aim is not to enrich myself, but for the majority of the citizens to benefit from this initiative.

> Anticipated Social Impact

We want to bring competition to other medicine  sellers by selling drugs at reduced prices, in so doing other traders will be forced to sell their products at reduced prices, thus the majority of the citizens will benefit from this. And quality of life will be improved to all.
