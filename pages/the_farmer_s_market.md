> Title of the innovation

The farmer's market

> Brief description of the innovation

Farmers market is an online site that provides a market place for local farmers to sale their products to a wide audience of customers that includes chain stores and millers creating a very competitive market environment to make sure that farmers get the money that puts real value to the products,it will also provide free information to the farmer's about the weather,market prices for certain products and innovations and technologies in the agricultural sector.
This will also ensure that the chances of our farmers being swindled are zero.
The farmer's market will match the farmer with the right inputs and the right planting time for the crop.
Farmers will be able to buy farm inputs from argodealers with online stores and ensure that they get the product they paid for.

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

*Advertising spaces that will be paid for by users looking to reach a wider customer audience for their products in form of popups on the site.
*Website store spaces that companies can purchase on the site.
*Commission fees that will be earned from transactions made from millers and chain stores

> Technical Feasibility

*It will require a basic e-commerce website design and layout for the market part.
*The information section will require a basic blog or social room design to help our farmers share information.
*The weather data section will access forecasts and other climate and weather data from the national Metrologic department.

> Anticipated Social Impact

*It will provide our local farmers with a larger customer audience that will create a very competitive market environment that will ensure that the farmer gets the what they deserve for their products.
*With the free climate and agriculture information provided,it will ensure that the local farmers are aware of climate change and improve their farming practices to help improve the environment.
*Through sensitisation and information farmers will be able to learn about our crops they can venture into and access a possible good mine that is best suited to their climate and region
