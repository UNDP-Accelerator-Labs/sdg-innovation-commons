Tool used to know different aspects regarding the generation, collection and final disposal of waste in the different municipalities where the transition plan was worked. This allows to have a base to know the needs of the municipalities and the points in which it can be improved.  

This predignostic must be filled by people with greater knowledge of the subject and the state of the municipality, to ensure that the answers are as close to reality as possible.
