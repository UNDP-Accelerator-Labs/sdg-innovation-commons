> Brief description of the innovation

The innovation is targeting women by forming women groups of  the range of 15-20 members each group and these women are in the rural part of the district of Kalomo. Composition of the members is open to all community members but mostly the focus are the poor of the poorest which includes the Disabled, People living with HIV &amp; AIDS and many other. Our target and main focus are children who are coming from these families, to ensure they have food , clothes, able to go to school and promotion of girl child to be in school and do sensitization meetings on child marriages , Teenage Pregances and and ensure promotion of re-entry programs for those girls that had fallen pregnancy at an early or tender age.
The women would do self help project through saving and lending concept where women individually do businesses to supplement at household level.It is during this time when social development pillars are attained, Economically and politically sensitized on leadership and governance issues including gender Equality .Involvement of traditional leaders is cardinal during such gathering in order to facilitate development in the areas as well,by facilitating linkages to service providers  who can support in the area of access to clean water, farm inputs , health services , education services etc

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Through the same innovation the organization is promoting wise investments such as Oil expellers in order to generate income for continuity of the projects as well as running poultry &amp; Piggery run.The farmers will bring sunflower , soya beans and ground nuts to produce cooking oil which has market in both rural and urban rural , not only cooking oil will be generated but also feeds for poultry,piggery will be produced to feed the animals, when we generate more , we can supply to other commercial farmers surrounding Kalomo town ,for these feeds are on high demand more especially sunflower cake for diary animals.
The animal drops for piggery and chicken run will be supplied in the gardens and fields which is a good source of manure and it  makes the soil to be more fertile or becoming virgin land.
The challenge prize will help the business to grow in that it is going to assist our organization to buy good and quality machinery which will assist us to produce more oil and feeds at a fast rate than it is now where we hire machinery and the produce becomes low than the demand.

> Technical Feasibility

The design of the innovation is through the women groups where they will do the saving and lending concept. The focus in here are 3 pillars that is Economically, Socially, and Politically.Promotion of wise investments among the same groups,including promotion of value addition to the individual businesses so that school going children are able to go to school and have basic needs at household level.
Awareness campaigns on child marriages,teen pregnancies, etc
A wise investment of oil expeller to raise more income and expand the project to at least 5 communities from 2 communities.Facilitate linkages to other service providers in order for the women and other community members access good and quality education services, health, road network,clean and safe water for drinking for all. 
Feeds for piggery and chicken runs will be promoted  as well and vegetable growing in order to have food on the table for all and generate individual income from individual businesses. Collaboration with other key stakeholders will be key in order to archive  our goal and it also promotes transparency and accountability to the service deliverly

> Anticipated Social Impact

Social impact anticipated will be that many women in the rural areas will be motivated to do individual businesses and generate income at household level,GBV cases will reduce because most community members will be busy with businesses, children will have access to school, and other basic needs,Women will participate in governance issues as well as becoming people who can speak for the voiceless in the communities. 
 Access to clean and safe water, road network , health services will be attained through linkages.
Traditional leaders will be equipped with Human rights information which is fundamental to all human beings dispite the way one looks like and ensure women who have been deprived of assets in various households will be able to access and own properties such as land and livestock.ect
