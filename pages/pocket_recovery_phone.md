Pocket Recovery Phone is a local solution aimed at protecting data and privacy of Smart phone users. It also serves as an early warning or alert system when someone's phone is taken away or distant from them. The solution addresses the following challenges:


-Customer Privacy Protection
-Supports reduction in the rate of theft and loss of smart phones
-Provides an efficient way for the customer to find their data in an event of loss or theft of their smart phones.

A solution comprised of three modules;
-An Electronic key ring
-A mobile Application and
-A Geolocation Platform
