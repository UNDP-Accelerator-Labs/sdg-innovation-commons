## Money fellows

Trusted and Convenient online Money Circles

Over 4 million users use the platform and each user has to sign a legal contract before the circle starts.

Money Fellows is the evolution of Money Circles, it’s:

- Always available at a click of a button.

- Payout values that reach 100K EGP.

- Installments start as low as 500 EGP/month

- Ability to join two Money Circles at the same time.

- Not bound by your current social circle or geographical location.

- Private. Nobody knows when you are getting your payout.

- Secured. Everyone signs a legally binding contract.

- Various Pay-in and Payout methods.
