MoveEt delivery and logistics system was developed with objective of improving (introduce digitized management) the existing delivery and logistics which is generally fragmented and manually managed in inefficient and ineffective manner.
MoveEt Delivery and logistics is a system serves suppliers to deliver their goods efficiently and timely while fully controlling the movement of the transport system using GPS system, thus improving the efficiency of their transport system. The system enables to select the best route to deliver goods to customers. The system had been established with the objective of optimizing the business activities through efficient utilization of transport means.
Move.ET service includes
1. MoveET Rental Fleet Management System:
MoveEt provides a robust system to Enterprises to manage their vehicles. This system enables the Enterprise to see detail information about the delivery and track the vehicles on map. MoveEt installs the system on the computer of the customer and provides support services such as system maintenance, training and upgrading the system.

MoveEt Enterprise is a delivery management system controlled by MoveEt, enabling customers (Enterprises) order a delivery using a web portal or call center. The Vehicles are managed by
MoveEt where MoveEt uses its own cars or verified car owners
(Drivers) who qualify for the enterprise delivery requirement.
3. MoveEt Public:
The MoveEt Public is a delivery system managed by MoveEt prepared for the public user to manage a logistics and delivery. This system includes various verified vehicle drivers to manage the delivery service. A user can use a mobile application or a call center to make the order (request the service).
The initiative is sustainably replicable because All users shall be charged for the service of logistics and delivery as they use of the system. At this time the system has already been developed, tested and is being loaded on smart phones of drivers, ready for use.
