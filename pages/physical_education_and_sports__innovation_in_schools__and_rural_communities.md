> Title of the innovation

Physical Education and sports  innovation in schools, and rural communities

> Brief description of the innovation

To ensure inclusiveness especially in rural communities in talent tapping and identification in schools and rural communities

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

If well managed and planned,  this innovation can be a source of income in the sense that people can be charged to come and watch these games, members to contribute a certain fee monthly and generation of income through cooperative activities like fish farming,  bee keeping etc.

> Technical Feasibility

Though not yet established,  our goal is to build  a fence pitch in the rural area and other  supporting buildings where people can be coming to watch games at a fee.

> Anticipated Social Impact

Through talent identification,  this innovation can produce players for big clubs even at national level in different sporting activities there by advancing  the lives of those with in identified talents.
