> Type of the Solution

Green Taxation, Financial Incentives, Subsidy

> Affected Sector

Transport

> Description of the solution

Introduced a series of policies such as purchase subsidies, tax exemptions, charging infrastructure and exemptions from the lottery to promote the sale of new energy vehicles and reduce vehicle emissions.

> Socio-economic effects

Reduce the use of traditional fossil energy, reduce air pollution, adjust the national energy structure, promote the upgrading of industrial structure
