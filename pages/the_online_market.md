> Title of the innovation

The Online Market

> Brief description of the innovation

Application software that can allow people to search for a product and find the nearest seller selling the product they are looking for and also allow those selling to find the closest buyer.

> Do you have a prototype for your innovation?

- no

> Commercial Viability of the Innovation

Income will be generated through subscriptions, service charge fees, and advertisements on the platform.

It'll quicked sells for existing businesses and allow those businesses which are not well known or well marketed to have visibility as they be appearing in the search results if they are the ones closest to the app user.

The target market are business owners especially those selling goods and offering services. 
The challenge prize will help the business grow by enabling us to well market the app and acquire all necessary equipment for the smooth running of the application such as servers, cloud services, etc.

This innovation impacts the environment by minimizing the costs spent by individuals to move from one place to another searching for a particular product by easily connecting them to the closest possible solution, therefore, also reducing on unnecessary burning of fuels if the individual is driving.

> Technical Feasibility

This innovation is an application software still under development able to run on mobile phones and other gadgets that support android applications. 

The goal is to have a fully running application software by 20th November 2021 to be first deployed on the local Zambian market and then later on the global market.

> Anticipated Social Impact

It can help users easily find the nearest product or service they want to utilize.
It can also help users to reduce unnecessary transport costs and also reduce the stress of not knowing where to find a product or service you are looking for.
It can also greatly impact businesses by helping them have projected sales as they are able to first communicate with potential clients through the application software first before the actual transactions.
