Iraqi Innovators is a social good startup with a mission to change the narrative about Iraq through quality content, training Iraqi copywriters and supporting the creative industry. Also, this online platform provides the available opportunities to the Iraqi youth in order to engage in developing the entrepreneurial eco-system in Iraq. This platform led by Iraqi women is documenting the entrepreneurial activities and events in Iraq. in addition to that, this platform provide different media services.
