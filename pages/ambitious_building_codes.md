> Description of the solution

To cool a city, there is need to adopt and enforce ambitious building codes for new buildings and renovations. Energy savings is through building energy codes programs. These programs are proven mechanisms for improving the efficiency of buildings around the world, impacting new
construction as well as major renovations.
